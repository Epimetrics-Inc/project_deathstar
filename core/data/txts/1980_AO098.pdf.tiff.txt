<subject>SUBJECT: Placing the newly created Management Information System Division temporarily under the Philippine Population Project II, Office of the Minister effective immediately</subject>

<body>
In view of the critical stage of negotiations for a loan from the World Bank for the Second Philippine Population Project which is tied up with the creation of the Management Information System Division, organizationally attached to the Disease Intelligence Center, the newly established unit is hereby placed temporarily under the supervision and control of the Project Coordinator, Philippine Population Project II, Office of the Minister, effective immediately and until further orders.

Accordingly, all administrative and technical matters involving the said Division including those on personnel transactions, vouchers, requisitions, payrolls and other financial documents, shall bear the recommendation or approval as the case maybe, of the Project Coordinator.

Likewise, such applicable appropriations, if any, records, property, supplies and equipment of the defunct Management Information System under the Project Management Staff shall be transferred to the new Division. To maintain the continuity of its special functions, such personnel from the former office as may be necessary and qualified shall be given priority consideration for appointment to meet on time periodic World Bank and other offices requirements.
</body>
