<subject>Supplementing previous Administrative Orders issued regarding authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria</subject>
<body>
	This order is being issued to further supplement Administrativ Order No. 81, s. 1980 and the supplementing orders thereto issued implementing Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health No. 2, Batas Pambansa Blg. 866, fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria at any given time including undelivered purchases so as to include Loreto Municipal Hospital, Agusan del Sur in the amount of P20,000.00
	The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the submitted request.
