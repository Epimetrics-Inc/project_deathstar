       In consonance With Batas Pambansa Blg. 866 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, DBB Municipal Hospital Dasmarinas, Cavite, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30, 000.00) worth of drugs and med1c1nes that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is3 chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

       It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321, authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be maintained shall be followed 
	The ceiling hereby fixed is buried on the submitted request.
