<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority for regional administrative officers to approve applications for leave of absences up to sixty (60) days, except those of provincial and city health officers and chiefs of hospitals which are heretofore shall be approved by the regional health directors</subject>

<body>
Effective December 8, 1975, all regional administrative officers are hereby authorized, subject to the provisions of Rule XVI of the Revised Civil Service Rules, to approve after the words "By authority of the Secretary of Health" all applications for leaves of absences not exceeding a period of sixty (60) days, with or without pay, of all personnel in the regional health offices, provided that their services can be spared without detriment to the interest of the public service, except as follows:

a. Appliations for leave of absence of provincial and city health officers and chiefs of hospitals, and
b. Requests for commutation of salary incident to leave which shall be approved as usual by the regional health director in accordance with previous delegation of authority.

To further expedite personnel transactions, regional administrative officers are hereby authorized to approve clearance certificates supporting the applications for leave of absence covered by this authority.

This Order, having been issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
