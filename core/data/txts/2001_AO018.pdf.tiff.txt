<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines in Payment of CY 2001 Hazard Pay</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to the provisions of Sec. 7.1.5 of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of ILA. 7305 otherwise known as the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers; which prescribes the schedule of implementation of the hazard allowance rate for the period 1999 to 2003, for CY 2001 health workers shall receive 15 % of the monthly basic salary.

However, Fiscal Discipline Measures must be adopmd per Budget Circular No. 2001-11-1 dated March 6, 2001 under Administrative-Order No. 5 issued on February 28, 2001 Ten percent (10%) of the basic salary shall be initially released and the five percent (5 %) differentiation may be given at the end of the year depending on the availability of savings

In line with the provisions of the "said issuances, the following are the guidelines for the computation and payment of the current year’s hazard pay:

1. All health workers including mammal and camal employees with Salary Grades 19 and below shall reeeive a monthly hazard allowance equivalent to 10% of their monthly basic salary.

2. All health workers with Salary Grades 20 and above shall receive the equivalent of. 10% of a preset benchmark amount based on SG-19 Step 8 per month.

Part time employees are entitled to one half (1/2) of the mount enjoyed by full time employees. Consultants are not entitled to this beneﬁt.

This Order shall be. implemented effective January 1, 2001.
