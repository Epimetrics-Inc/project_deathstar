<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 174 s. 1972</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT : Placing under the administrative control and supervision of the Regional Health Directors all dental health personnel in the field of the Bureau of Dental Health Services.</subject>

<body>
For the purpose of fixing responsibilities and effective today, al dental health personnel in the field of the Bureau of Dental Health Services, are hereby places under the administrative control and supervision of the Regional Health Services.

In this connection, attention is invited to Department Circular No. 147, series of 1968, relative to the delineations of authority of the Regional Health Officers, with respect to the field dental services under the Bureau of Dental Health Services.

This Order, being issued in the interest of the public service, is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
</body>
