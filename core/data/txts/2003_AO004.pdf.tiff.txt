<subject>SUBJECT: Forms of Bid Security, Performance Security, Advance Payment Guaranty and Project Guaranty for GOP-Funded Infrastructure Projects of the Department of Health</subject>


<body>

As prescribed under PD 1594 on the Guidelines for the Implementation of Government Projects, as amended May and July 2000, the original copy of the bid shall be accompanied by a bid security in an amount equivalent to the Approved Budget For the Contract. Also, the. faithful performance of the contractor under contract for any government infrastructure project shall be guaranteed by the posting of a performance security, advance payment guaranty and project guaranty.

Only the following forms of bid security, performance security and guaranty shall be allowed for contracts for GOP-funded infrastructure projects of the DOH:

A. Bid Security

1. Cash, Manager's Check, Cashier's Check, lrrevocable Letter of Credit, or Bank Draft — One Percent (1 %) of the Approved Budget for the Contract to be bid, as published/ advertised.

2. Bank Guarantee — One and a Half Percent (l 1/2%) of the Approved Budget for the Contract to be Bid as published / advertised.

This shall be valid for a reasonable period as indicated in the Instruction to Bidders but in no case later than One Hundred Twenty (120) Calendar Days following the opening of bids.

B. Performance Security

1. Cash, manager’s check, cashiers check, irrevocable letter of credit issued by a reputable private local bank, bank draft - Five percent (5%) of the total contract price; and

2. Bank Guarantee issued by a reputable private local bank - Ten percent (l0%) of the total contract price

This shall be at least co—terminus with the final completion of the project.

C. Advance Payment Guaranty equivalent to the amount of advance payment

1. Irrevocable letter of credit issued by a reputable private local bank.

2. Bank Guarantee issued by a reputable private local bank

This shall be valid until the total advance payment has been recouped. The amount of advance payment guarantee may be reduced at any time by the amount recouped, in which case the contractor shall replace the old guarantee with a new one. in the reduced amount.

D. Project Guaranty in an amount equivalent to ten percent (10%) of the total contract price including costs of Variation Orders, if any.

1. Manager’s check, cashiers check, irrevocable letter of credit issued by a reputable private local bank, bank draft

2. Bank Guarantee issued by a reputable private local bank.

This shall be valid for a period of one (1) year after completion and final acceptance of the project.

All these forms shall be posted in favor of the Department of Health.

Further these shall he kept by the DOl-l in accordance with government accounting, budgeting and auditing rules and regulations.

Under this Order, the instructions to bidders/ tender documents of infrastructure projects shall be amended to explicitly mention the above allowable forms of performance security and guaranties and specific requirements thereto.

This Order takes effect immediately.
