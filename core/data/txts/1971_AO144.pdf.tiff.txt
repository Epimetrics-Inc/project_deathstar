<docnum>NO. 144 s. 1971</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Amending Administrative Order No. 12, series of 1966, relative to the decentralization of psychiatric services.</subject>

<body>
In order to attain more realistically the objective of the program for the decentralization of patients in the National Mental Hospital as envisioned in Administrative Order No. 12, series of 1966, the said Administrative Order is hereby amended to reflect the following policies:

1. The direct administrative control and supervision of the regional mental hospitals or psychiatric units shall be the duty of the respective Chiefs of Hospitals concerned, subject to the general supervision of the Regional Health Offices concerned.

2. Advisory services and follow-up activities may be secured from the National Mental Hospital, only when requested by the Chief of Hospital concerned thru the Director of Medical Services and authorized by the Secretary of Health.

This Administrative Order shall take effect on June 1, 1971.
</body>
