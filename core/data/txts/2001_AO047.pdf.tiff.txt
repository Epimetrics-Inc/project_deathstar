<subject>SUBJECT: Revised Guidelines on Delegation of Authority for certain Administrative Transactions in the Centers for Health Development (CHD's) including Retained Hospitals</subject>

<body>
In view of the implementation of the DOG Rationalization and Streamlining Plan pursuant to Executive Order No. 102, s. 1999, and in order to streamline operations, fast-track action on official transactions, documents and communications, and define area of responsibility, authority to certain officials in the Center for Health Development including Retained Hospitals to take appropriate action thereon, is hereby delegated.
