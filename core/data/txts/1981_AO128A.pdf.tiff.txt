<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 128-A s. 1981</docnum>

TO: All Regional Health Directors and Chiefs of Hospitals under the Ministry

<subject>SUBJECT: Revised Schedule of Hospital Fees and Charges.</subject>

<body>
Due to the spiraling cost of all commodities, there is an urgent need to revise Administrative Order No. 41, s. of 1962 which is no longer realistic to the present time, and upon recommendation of the MOH-NEDA, Interagency Coordinating Committee on the National Hospital Development Program, the following revised schedule of hospital fees and charges are hereby prescribed for all the different categories of government hospitals under the Ministry of Health.

This Order takes effect upon approval.
</body>
