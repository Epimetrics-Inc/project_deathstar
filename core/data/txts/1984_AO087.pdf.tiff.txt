<body> To:  The Bureau Directors, Regional Health Directors, Project Directors, Chief of Offices/Services/Hospitals directly under the Office of the Minister of Health, Unit Heads and others concerned </body>

<subject> Deferment of Paragraph VI.I of MOH Administrative Order No. 71, series of 1983, on Licensure Fee of P20.00 per bed</subject>

<body>
    In view of the tight financial condition of hospitals in the country due to escalating costs of current expenditures and capital facilities, it is hereby directed that the implementation of paragraph VI. I of Administrative Order No. 1, series of 1983, on licensure fee of P20.00 per bed had to be deferred as duly approved by the Cabinet.
	Accordingly, publication of this deferment is being made in accordance with the provisions of Section 10 of Ministry Order No. 6-83 of the Ministry of Finance on the Rules and regulations implementing Batas Pambansa Blg. 325.
	All concerned are hereby advised to be guided accordingly.
