I. RATIONALE

The Department of Health is mandated to provide quality health care especially the poor. Crucial to effective health care delivery are competent officials to implement the plans and programs of the Department.

The third level positions Directors of Centers for Health Development and Central Office, Chief of Hospitals and Medical Center Chiefs are positions of management level. Appointees to these positions should meet the minimum qualification standards set by the Civil Service Commission as well as the additional qualifications set by this Department.

To ensure that the most suitable and qualified candidates are selected and appointed, the following Qualification Standards and Evaluation Criteria for application to the above-mentioned positions are hereby established.
