<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 12 s. 1998 dated March 24, 1998 re: Kabataan 2000 - Health Outreach Program Implementing Guidelines</subject>

<body>

Section V.2 on the Qualifocation to Apply for the Program and Section VII.1.1.2.a on
the Evaluation of the Application of Administrative Order No. 12, are hereby amended to
read as follows:

V.2. Must have a monthly family income of not more than five thousand seven hundred pesos (P5,700.00) for a family of six or a per capita income of P95 per month based on the 1997 National Statistics Coordinating Board (N SCB) Annual Per Capita Poverty Threshold of Families; or based from the latest prevailing regional per Capita Poverty Threshold issued by the Regional NSCB office.

VII.1 1.2 a. Preference shall be given to students who studied in public elementary and high schools, state colleges or universities.

Other provisions that are not affected by this amendment shall remain in effect.

This Order takes effect immediately.
