<subject> Amending Administrative order No. 74, dated December 22, 1983, dealing on delegation of authority to officials in the field units to approve requisitions and purchases of orders. </subject>

<body>

	In the interest of the serv1ce, and in order to expedite transactions in the various field units of Ministry Health, Section II of Administrative Order No. 74 dated December 22, 1983, is hereby amended to include public bidding among the modes of procuring supplies and materials. The authority of officials to approve transactions to public bidding shall be equivalent to the maximum amount granted them under the SOO system, prov1ded, however, that authority to conduct public bidding shall be secured from proper authorities.

   

This Order supersedes all issuances inconsistent herewith.
