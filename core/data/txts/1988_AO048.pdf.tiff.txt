<subject> Subject: Amendment of Item No. 5 under Paragraph 3 of Administrative Order No. 65 dated January 5, 1980, as amended.</subject>


<body>
	Considering the avowed aim and purpose of the department policy laid down in Administrative Order No. 65, as amended by Administrative Order No. 79 both series of 1980, and as further amended by Administrative Order No. 18, series of 198, it has become desirable to further clarify the nature or kind of appointments intended to be covered by the prohibition being enjoined as a matter of policy. Accordingly, the provision of Item No. 5 under Paragraph Three of Administrative Order No. 65, as amended, is hereby modified to make it read as follows:

‘5. This prohibition shall cover not only original appointments but also appointments which cover or involve transfer, reinstatement or reemployment in the hospital or office, whether the status of the appointment be permanent, temporary, casual, or contractual’

All other provisions of the Administrative order No. 65, as amended, shall remain in force and effect.
This Amendatory Order shall take effect immediately.
