<docnum>NO. 183 s. 1972</docnum>

<subject>Subject : Delegation of authority to Chiefs of Special Hospitals and the Chief of the Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital to approved Emergency Purchase of below ₱5,000.</subject>

<body>
In the line with the policy of the administration to facilitate action on government transactions by eliminating red tape, authority is hereby delegated to the hiefs of the Special Hospital to approve requisitions and purchase orders covering emergency purchases of below ₱5,000, under the clause "By authority of the Secretary of Health". In the excercise of this authority, the Chiefs of Hospitals concerned shall see to it that the need for the supplies covered by the emergency purchase is exceptionally urgent or absolutely indispensable to prevent immediate danger to, or loss of, life and/or property, or to avoid detriment to the public service.
</body>
