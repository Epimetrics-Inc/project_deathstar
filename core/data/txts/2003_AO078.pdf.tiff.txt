<subject>SUBJECT: Addendum to A.O. No. 5 s. 2003 dated January 10, 2003 Re: Guidelines and Procedure for the Institutionalization of the Department of Health Drug Consignment System entitled Guidelines on the Retention and Use of Hospital Income pursuant to Special Provision No. 6 of the DOH Appropriations under R.A. 9206 FY 2003 General Appropriations Act</subject>


<body>

In view of the issuance of Joint DOH-DOF-DBM Circular No. 2003-1 dated July 16, 2003 entitled “Guidelines on the Retention and Use of Hospital Income” pursuant to Special Provision No. 6 of the DOH Appropriations under RA. No. 9206, FY 2003, General Appropriation Act and the provisions of Administrative Order No. 5 s. 2003 dated January 10, 2003, Section F—2 Fiscal Management, shall be amended to exclude “hiring of contractual personnel” chargeable against income derived from consigned drugs and medicines.

Likewise, DOH Department Circular No. 230 s. 2002, dated September 30, 2002 also mentions the hiring of contractual Pharmacists under No. 3 of the observation and clariﬁcation of the Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management (DBM), which emphasized that “hospital income may be validly used "for hiring contractual pharmacists, provided said personnel to be hired shall be paid against the regular Maintenance and Other Operating Expense (Other Services) of the hospital.”

Please be guided accordingly.
