<subject>SUBJECT: Further delegation of authority to Regional Directors to make emergency purchases of drugs and medical supplies.</subject>


<body>To expedite the provision of drugs and medical supplies to the field, the authority of Regional Directors to make emergency purchases of said items is hereby increased from P10,000 to P25,QOO a month. To meet an emergency which may involve the loss of, or danger to, life and/or property, they are authorized to make emergency purchases of drugs and medical supplies in a monthly amount not exceeding four per centum of the annual agency expenditure program for supplies and materials (Sec. 20, page 604, Batas Pambansa Blg. 131). Reports of said purchases shall
Be submitted to the Office of the Minister.
	This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
