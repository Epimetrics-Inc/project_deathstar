<subject>
SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by Badian District Hospital.</subject>

<body>

       In consonance with Section 2, Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa 640, Badian District Hospital, Badian, Cebu; is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Pl5,000.00 worth of drugs and medicines in its stock for resale chargeable against-its maintenance and other operating expenses.

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office -of the Budget and Management and GOA regulating the use of revolving fund to be created out of the authorized ceiling shall be followed.
