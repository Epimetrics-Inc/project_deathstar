<subject>SUBJECT: Amending pertinent portions of Administrative Order No. 4-B, s. 1981 relative to delegation of authority to officials of Special Hospitals, Regional Health Offices, Hospitals and Sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
To relieve regional directors and chiefs of hospitals from the task of signing routine transactions in their respective agencies and allow them more time for management function, pertinent portions of Administrative Order No. 4-B dated August 20, 1981 is further amended to read as follows:

I. Approval of Disbursements -

Officials authorized to approve disbursements:

1. In Special Hospitals

a. Chief of Hospital
- no limit
b. Assistant Director (For hospitals with positions of Assistant Director)
Chiefs of Clinics (For hospitals with no Assistant Director)
- up to ₱10,000.00 per disbursement
- no limit, in the absence of the Chief of Hospital
c. Administrative Officer
- no limit for transactions pertaining only to salary payrolls and vouchers and remittances to GSIS, BIR, MEDICARE and other government agencies.
- up to ₱5,000.00 per disbursement, in the absence of the Assistant Director or the Chief of Clinics, as the case may be

2. In Regional Health Offices

a. Regional Health Director
- no limit
b. Assistant Director (For Regional Health Office with duly appointed Assistant Director)
Chief, Technical Division (For Regional Health Office with no Assistant Regional Health Director)
- up to ₱10,000.00 per disbursement
- no limit, in the absence of the Regional Health Director
c. Finance Officer
- up to ₱5,000.00 per disbursement; and
- no limit for transactions pertaining only to salary payrolls, vouchers and remittances to GSIS, BIR, MEDICARE, and other government agencies.
d. Administrative Officer
- no limit for transactions pertaining to salary payrolls and vouchers and remittances to GSIS, BIR, MEDICARE and other government agencies.

3. In Hospitals and Sanitaria

a. Chief of Hospital
- no limit
b. Authorized Official (Next ranking official to be designated by the Chief of Hospital/Sanitarium)
- up to ₱5,000.00 per disbursement
- no limit, in the absence of the Chief of Hospital/Sanitarium.
c. Administrative Officer
- up to ₱3,000.00 per disbursement
- no limit for transactions pertaining only to salary payrolls and vouchers and remittances to GSIS, BIR, MEDICARE and other government agencies.
- up to ₱5,000.00 per disbursement, in the absence of the Chief of Hospital and the designated officer-in-charge.

II. Approval of Requisitions and Purchase Orders -

Officials authorized to approve requisitions and purchase orders:

1. In Regional Health Offices

Administrative Officer
- up to ₱5,000.00 per requisition for supplies and materials for housekeeping purposes only.

2. In Hospital and Sanitaria

Administrative Officer
- up to ₱5,000.00 per requisition for supplies and materials for housekeeping purposes only

III. Approval of Travels of Personnel

Officials authorized to approve travels of personnel:

In Hospitals and Sanitaria

Regional Health Director -
- all travels of hospital/sanitaria personnel under his jurisdiction.

This Order supersedes all other Orders inconsistent herewith and shall take effect immediately.
