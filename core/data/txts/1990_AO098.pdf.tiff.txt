<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines on Advertisement and Promotion to Implement the Generics Act of 1988 (RA 6675)for multi-active ingredient Fixed Dose Combination Products</subject>

<body>

Section 1: Definition of Terms 
		
		Fixed Dose Combination (FDC) products  are multi-active ingredient pharmaceutical preparations containing two or more pharmacologically active ingredients in a single formulation or dosage form.

Section 2 : Applicability of pertinent provisions of AO 65 s. 1989
		All pertinent provisions of AO 65. s; 1989 on
Advertisement and Promotion to Implement the Generics Act of 1988 sha11 apply to multi-active ingredient Fixed-Dose Combination products.

Section 3: Effectivity
 
		This order shall  take effect two weeks after its
publication in two newspapers of general circulation, but the actual mandatory use in advertisements, of the new approved Generic Name or Official Name for the Multi-active ingredient
Fixed Dose Combination Product - which shall follow the approved generic label of the product - will start August 1, 1991
