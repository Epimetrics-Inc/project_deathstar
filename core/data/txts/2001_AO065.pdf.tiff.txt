<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of P500.00 Gift Check or In-Kind for Central Office Health Officials and Employees for Calendar Year 2001</subject>


<body>

Budget Circular No. 2001-02 dated November 12, 2001 contained the Presidential Instruction and Pronouncement as embodied in the Memorandum dated June 26, 2001 from the Head Presidential Management Staff relative to the Granting of Extra Christmas Bonus or Similar Cash or Non-Cash Benefit for Government Personnel as long as the Department/agency has available funds to pay the said benefit.

In light of the foregoing and in the spirit of the yuletide season, all opfficials and employees at the Central Office are hereby entitled to a Gift Check or In-Kind Items equivalent to P500.00 each chargeable against any available savings of this Department subject to the usual accounting and audit rules and regulations.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
