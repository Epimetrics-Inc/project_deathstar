<subject>Granting of Provisional Accreditation to Pharmaceuticals Suppliers</subject>

<body>

In order to ensure the continuous supply of drugs and medicines, provisional accreditation is hereby granted to pharmaceutical suppliers that renewed/applied for accreditation on or before December 31, 2000.

The said companies listed in Annex A are hereby allowed to participate in bidding activities with the Department of Health , effective immediately. This provisional accreditation is valid until June 30, 2001, unless revoked earlier and does not cover the specific batches of violative products specified in Department 
Memorandum No. 27, s. 2001. 

Henceforth, no payment shall be released pending release of satisfactory laboratory analysis by the Bureau of Food and Drugs (BFAD) on all supplies delivered.

All other policies and regulations inconsistent with this Order are hereby revoked.
