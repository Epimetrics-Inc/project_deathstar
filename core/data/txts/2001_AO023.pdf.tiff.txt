<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 178 s. 2001</subject>


<body>
 
Administrative Order No. 178 s. 2001 re “Prescribing Guidelines in the Granting of Longevity Pay to Deserving Public Health Workers pursuant to Section 6.3 of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations of R. A. 730”, is hereby amendedsuch that Item No. 6.0 shall be read as follows:

“6.0 For initial implementation, computation of the benefit shall be based on actual monthly basic salary"

Further, for purposes of computing the longevity pay, the following are being added as provisions:

1. The authorized vacation leave without pay of not more than fifteen (15) Days shall not be considered a gap in the health workers' service record;

2. That in the case of personnel who were already separated from the service due to resignation/retirement/AWOL, are entitled to claim only up to the time they were separated.

As thus amended, all other stipulations of the above-mentioned Administrative Order, which are consistent with this amendment, still stand in effect.
