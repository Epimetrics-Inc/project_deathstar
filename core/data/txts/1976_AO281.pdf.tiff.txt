<subject>SUBJECT: Renewal of the designation of P/Lt. GREGORIO G. LAZARRAGA, JR., of the Davao del Sur Integrated Police Force, Police District I, City of Davao, as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspector.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to the authority vested in me as Secretary of Health, concurrently ex-officio member and Chairman of the Dangerous Drugs Board, to enforce the provisions of Section 27 of Republic Act No. 3720 and Section 12 of Republic Act No. 953 in relation to pertinent provisions of Republic Act No. 6425, as amended, the designation of P/Lt. GREGORIO G. LAZARRAGA, JR., as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspector effected thru Administrative Order No. 225 dated October 16, 1974 and Administrative Order No. 254 dated August 22, 1975, is hereby extended up to and including December 31, 1976 unless sooner revoked subject to the same conditions specified in the original designation above-referred to.
</body>
