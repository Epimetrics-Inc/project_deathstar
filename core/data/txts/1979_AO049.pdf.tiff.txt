<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of the provisions of Administrative Order No. 286 relative to the signing and countersigning of TCAA Checks by Officials in Special Hospitals.</subject>

<body>
Following the provisions of CCA Circular No. 79-26 (8), Section II-1 and 2 of Administrative Order No. 286 are hereby amended to read as follows:

II Signing of TCAA Checks

1. Officials authorized to sign checks:
a. Cashier - to sign checks up to ₱2,000.00
b. Administrative Officer - to sign checks up to ₱20,000.00
c. Chief of Hospital - to sign checks up to ₱100,000.00

2. Officials authorized to countersign checks:
a. Chief of Hospitals - to countersign checks up to ₱20,000.00
b. Administrative Officer - to countersign checks up to ₱2,000.00

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
