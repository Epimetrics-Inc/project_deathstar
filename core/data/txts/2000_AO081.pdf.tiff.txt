<subject>SUBJECT: Department of Health Guidelines for Board of Investments Registration of Health Care Projects</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to the authority vested on the Secretary of Health under Section 3, Chapter I Title IX, Book IV of the Administrative Code of 1987 and as per Memorandum of Agreement entered into between the Department of Health (DOH) and the Board of Investments (BOI), the following guidelines are hereby issued for the endorsement of health care projects needing Board of Investment registration.

The Department of Health shall endorse applications for BOI registration of health care projects. A technical evaluation committee shall be created within the DOH to review and evaluate these applications, following these guidelines, and to recommendendorsement or non-endorsement by the Secretary of Health.

The guidelines shall be as follows:

1. The health care projects cover the following facilities

a. hospitals
b. diagnostic center
c. therapy centers
d. clinical laboratories
e. ambulatory health care facilities
f. health facilities for mentally ill
g. other facilities providing health or health related services

2. The facility must use brand-new and up—to—date equipment with an FOB cost of at least 2M pesos.

3. The project must use health care technology that has been proven scientifically to be effective and safe.

4. The facility must provide the same quality care to indigent and pay patients.

5. A quality assurance program must be established providing for periodic evaluation and review by appropriate DOH units.

6. Applicants for DOH endorsement shall follow the following procedures:

6.1 File a letter of request, addressed to the Secretary of Health
6.2 Attached the following documents.

a. Background information on applicant
b. Detailed description/feasibility study
c. 1) List of equipment
   2) Specification and quotation/cost of each equipment item
d. Documentation of efficacy and safety of the technology/equipment, if necessary.
e. Sworn document stating that all information and data submitted are true and correct.
f. Certificate of authorization for signing official in document 6.2 e. For a corporation, this certificate shall be a resolution of the Board of
Directors. For a sole proprietorship, this shall be the owner of the ﬁrm.

6.3 Follow-up after 15 working days

For immediate compliance.
