<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

<subject>SUBJECT: Renewal of the designation of Major ROMAN T. PANTINO, SR. PA (INAC), District Intelligence and Liaison Officer for Agusan del Norte-Sur District and Narcotics Officer, RASAC Northern Mindanao Region, as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspector.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to the authority vested in me as Secretary of Health, concurrently ex-officio member and Chairman of the Dangerous Drugs Board, to enforce the provisions of Section 27 of Republic Act No. 3720 and Section 12 of Republic Act No. 953 in relation to pertinent provisions of Republic Act No. 6425, as amended, the designation of Major ROMAN T. PANTINO, SR. as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspector effected thru Administrative Order No. 245 dated May 14, 1975, is hereby renewed effective immediately and to continue only up to December 31, 1976 unless sooner revoked, subject to the same conditions specified in the original designation abovereferred to.
</body>
