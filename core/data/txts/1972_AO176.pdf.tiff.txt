<subject>SUBJECT : Modifying Administrative Order No. 166, dated February 16, 1972, delegating authority to approved applications fot leaves of absence of officials under the Department of Health.</subject>

<body>
In view of the compulsary retirement of the Chief, Office of Administrative Services, and during the transition period of reorganization, the authority delegated to the Chief, Office of Administrative Services, Department of Health, to approve applications for leaves of absence specified in Administrative Order No. 166, dated February 16, 1972, shall now be exercised by the Officer-in-Charge, Office of Administrative Services.

As thus modified, Administrative Order No. 166 stands.
</body>
