<subject>SUBJECT: PROJECT IMPLEMENTATION ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE WOMEN’S HEALTH AND SAFE MOTHERHOOD PROJECT (WHSMP)</subject>

<body>

The following Project Implementation Arrangements ﬁor the Women’s Health and Safe Motherhood Project are hereby issued to ensure effective implementation of the said project: 

1. The Department of Health is responsible for the over-all direction for project implementation nationwide.

2. The Project Steering Committee (PSC) shall provide broad policy directions on project implementation.

3. The Project Management Office (PMO) shall provide over-all responsibility for project management and technical performance.

4. The Component Management composed of four (4) program managers shall be responsible for developing implementation guidelines and technical supervision  on their respective components

5. The DOH Integrated Regional Field Units Shall provide assistance to Local Government Units (LGUs), adopt project guidelines to regional conditions and undertake implementation of regionwide activities.

6. The Office for Management Services (Finance Service) ensures that Government of the Philippines’ (GOP) rules and regulations are adhered to with respect to financial, budgetary, and accounting concerns of the project.


Attached are the organizational structures for  the over-all managerial and technical direction of the project for immediate reference.
 
This Order shall take effect immediately.
