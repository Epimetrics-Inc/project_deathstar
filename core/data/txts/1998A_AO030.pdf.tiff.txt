<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of Food Assistance and Emergency Allowance to all Officials and Employees of the Department of Health for CY 1998</subject>

<body>
  
Pursuant to Section I, Rule XI of the Omnibus Rules, implementing Section 31, Book V of Executive Order 292 and other pertinent Civil Service Laws, the grant of Food Assistance and Emergency Allowance as employee welfare beneﬁt is hereby authorized to all Department of Health (DOH) officials and employees at the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, regional health offices and hospitals including specialty hospitals.

This beneﬁt shall not be more than SEVEN THOUSAND PESOS (P7,000.00) which shall be given to each official and employee of the DOH regardless of salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, temporary or contractual) chargeable against savings realized from released allotment of continuing and current appropriations- current operating expenses (PS and MOOE) or sub allotments (except from CIA, CDF, FAPS and Calamity Fund) of the agency and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations. 

To be entitled to this beneﬁt, an employee must have rendered at least four (4) months of continuous service from January 1, 1998 to December 31, 1998, provided that an official or employee who has rendered less than four (4) months of service shall be entitled to an amount equivalent to the actual service rendered as follows:

In case of part—time employees, they shall be entitled to one-half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full-time employees.

Exemptions to this beneﬁt are (1) the consultants and/or experts who are hired for specific purposes and services; and (2) the PSYP trainees.

Strict compliance by all concerned with the provisions of this Order is enjoined.
