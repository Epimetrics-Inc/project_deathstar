<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 16-A s. 1998, Guidelines on the Conduct of Medical Mission in DOH Hospitals or by DOH Hospitals.</subject>

<body>

In view of the Department of Health reorganization, Administrative Order No. 16-A s. 1998, Guidelines on the Conduct of Medical Mission in DOH Hospitals or by DOH Hospitals is hereby amended to include the following:

Evaluation and approval of proposals for Medical Mission that are to be conducted in the regions outside of Metro Manila by Department of Health hospitals and/or local organizations shall seek approval from the respective Regional Health Director.

Records and reports shall be submitted to the Regional Health Office Copy furnished the Office for Health Facilities.
