<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority to Regional Finance Officers.</subject>

<body>
To facilitate financial transactions in the regional health offices, authority is hereby delegated to regional Finance Officers to approve disbursements not exceeding ₱2,000 for any transaction. In the absence of the regional directors, they may approve salary payrolls, remittances to the GSIS, and inter-agency transactions in excess of ₱2,000.

The Finance Officers are also authorized to approve Requests for Obligations of Allotments and to countersign checks and Treasury Warrants in amounts not exceeding their authority to approve disbursements.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby confirmed and made of record.
</body>

By authority of the Secretary of Health:
