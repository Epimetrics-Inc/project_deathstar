<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 10 s. 2000 re Guidelines on the Department of Health Accreditation for Drug Suppliers.</subject>

<body>

Section V.5.5.3 of Administrative Order No. 10 s. 2000 is hereby amended to read as follows: 

All manufacturers, drug importers and drug traders shall have Quality Assurance Unit. However drug importers may enter into a
Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) with the Philippine Analytical Laboratory Inc. (PALI), Philippine Institute for Pure and Applied Chemistry (PIPAC) and Industrial Technology Development Institute for the analysis of their products.

All other provisions of the previous Order remains in effect and full force.
