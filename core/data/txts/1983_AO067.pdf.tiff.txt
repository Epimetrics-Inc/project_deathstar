<subject>   SUBJECT: Amendment to certain provisions of Administrative
Order No. 45 s. 1982 to facilitate the reporting of trust fund disbursements to construct the Barangay Health Stations funded by Loan 923-PH.</subject>

<body>
To facilitate the reporting of disbursements made out of the trust fund for the construction of Barangay Health Stations by Disbursing Officers of Provincial and District Hospitals the provisions of Sections 2. 2. 6 and 2. 2. 7 of Administrative Order No. 45, series of 1982, are hereby amended to read as follows:

“Section 2. 2 6 - Disbursing Officers of Provincial and District Hospitals who- disbursed the trust fund shall prepare the required "Report of Disbursements by Disbursing Officers" on a quincena basis in four (4): copies to be distributed as follows:

Original plus 2 copies - Accounting Unit of the Provincial or District Hospital.

 1 copy — File of the Disbursing Officer

	The Accounting Unit of the Provincial or District Hospital shall verify the disbursement report, enter the disbursements in the Journal of Disbursing Officers. Original copy of the report (RDDO) together with the supporting documents (vouchers, stubs, invoices, purchase, order, labor payrolls, etc.) shall be forwarded to Hospital Auditor. Duplicate copy of the RDDD duly noted by the Auditor shall be forwarded by the Accounting Unit of the Provincial or District Hospital to the Project Coordinator, Ministry of Health, Manila, monthly. This report shall be used by the NEH Accounting Division in preparing the adjustments to "Prepayment Account”

Section 2.2.7 – The Municipal Health Offices shall submit to the Project Coordinator, Ministry of Health Infrastructure duly accomplished by authorized representatives.

2. Copy of the resolution by the Barangay Council to support the construction and maintenance of the Barangay Health Station;
3. Copy of the memorandum of agreement between the Municipal Primary Health Care Committee and the Barangay Council specifying the mode of government’s project)
4. copy of the deed of donation as soon as the Barangay Health Stations are completed.
	These documents shall be submitted as soon as the Barangay Health Stations are completed.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
