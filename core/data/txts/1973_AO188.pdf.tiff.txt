<subject>SUBJECT : Implementation of Presidential Decree No. 61, dated November 16, 1972, on Physical Security of Installations, Documents and Materials.</subject>

<body>
TO : Bureau and Regional Health Directors, Chiefs of Office, Chief of Hospitals, Heads of Office and Projects, and all others concerned.

Pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 51, dated November 16, 1972 and Letter of Implementation No. 52-B dated January 17, 1973, you are hereby directed to provide fire extinguishers and other security in all levels of your office for the physical security of your installations, documents and materials.

In this connection, it is desired that reports on the implementation of this order must be coursed thru Engineer Jose M. Morales, Chief Security Officer of the Department.

This Order, being issued in the interest of the public service, is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
</body>
