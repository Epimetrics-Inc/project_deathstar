<subject>SUBJECT: Approval of disbursements for the repair and rehabilitation project, Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan.</subject>

<body>
To expedite the completion of the repair and rehabilitation project of the Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan, approval of the transactions, connected therewith is hereby delegated to Dr. Tranquilino Elicaño Jr., member, Record of Governors, subject to the usual auditing regulations.

Dr. Elicaño shall make regular reports to the Board on the status of the project.
</body>
