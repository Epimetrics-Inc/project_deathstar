<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 10. s. 2000 re Guidelines on the Department of Health Accreditation for Drug Suppliers.</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 10 s. 2000 is hereby amended to exclude Section V.5.5.1. and increase the paid up capital of drug suppliers/companies required under Section V.5.5.4. to FIFTY MILLION PESOS (P50M).

Section VII of the said Order is also amended to read as follows:

Certificate of Accreditation shall have a validity period of two (2) years from the date of issue subject to recall/suspension revocation, after due hearing and proper notice by the Accreditation Committee through its Chairman.

All other provisions of the previous Order are still in effect and full force.
