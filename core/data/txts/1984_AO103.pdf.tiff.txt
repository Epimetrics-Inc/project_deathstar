<subject> Subject: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by Research Institute for Tropical Medicines, Alabang, Muntinlupa, Metro Manila</subject>

<body>


In consonance with Section 2, Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa 640, Research Institute for Tropical Medicines, Alabang, Muntinlupa - Metro Manila, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Two Hundred Forty Thousand Pesos (P240,000.00) worth of drugs and medicines in its stock for resale, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating-expenses.
       It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget
Circular No. 321 authorizing cash ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be created, as provided under said circular and COA regulating the use of revolving fund to be created out of this authorized ceiling shall be followed.
