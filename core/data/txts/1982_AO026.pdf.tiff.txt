<subject>SUBJECT: Modifying Administrative Order No. 4-D and 11,both series of 1981 relative to approval of financial transactions, signing and countersigning of TCAA checks in Special Hospitals.</subject>

       <body>In the interest of the service and in order to institute internal control system among hospitals under the direct supervision and control of the Office of the Minister, Administrative Orders No. 4-B and 11 both series of 1981 are hereby modified to include the Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan and the Central Luzon Sanitarium within the scope of said issuances.
              Accordingly the authority granted to officials of Special _.,
Hospitals shall similarly apply to officials of both Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan and the Central Luzon Sanitarium to approve disbursements and sign Requests for Obligations of Allotment, to approve Requisitions and Purchase Orders and to sign and countersign TCAA checks in accordance with the limitations under Administrative Orders No. 4-B and 11, both series of 1981, copy attached.

	This Order shall take effect immediately and supersede or modify all issuances inconsistent herewith.
