<subject>SUBJECT: Subject: Changes in the guidelines for Regional Bulk Procurement.</subject>

<body>
	In order to further improve the implementation of the Regional Bulk Procurement the following changes in the guidelines are hereby adopted:
1) Municipal hospitals are to be excluded in the Regional bidding for Bulk Procurement but said hospitals shall utilize the price index of the bidding in the procurement of their needs.

2) Purchase Orders shall be picked up by the suppliers either from the IPHO or RHO whichever is most convenient to the end-uses and the suppliers.
3) A cut-off date will be set for the supplier to pick0up the Purchase Order.
4) A definite date will be set for the suppliers to get the checks in payment of their deliveries
5) The bidding will be for a period of four (4) months.
6) Lead time for the conduct of the Regional Bulk Procurement Bidding should be determined which should include the following processes:
	a) Publication of invitation to bid (2 consecutive days)
	b) Issuance of bid documents (2 weeks)
	c) Presentation of Drug or Device registration for certification (2 weeks)
	d) Prebid Conference (1 day)
	e)Bidding day (separate day for each package)
	f) Evaluation of results of bids
	g) Issuance of Price Schedule/Award/P.O.

7) A provision in the General Conditions should be included in the Qualifications of Suppliers to this effect:
“Suppliers from other regions, including NCR, should have a duly designated resident representative in the Region to whom all notices relative to this bidding can be served, in order to be qualified to participate.”

This Order shall take effect immediately.
