<body> 

This Administrative Order is being issued to amend Section 5 - F of Administrative Order No. 109 s. 2002 which will now read as follows:

E. Monitoring / Evaluation Through Contractor’s Performance Evaluation System

1. As soon as possible and by virtue of separate Department Order, the DOH shall form a Contractor’s Performance Evaluation System — Implementing Unit (CPES-IU) and assign Contractor’s Performance Evaluation (CPE) Evaluators, who will be responsible for adopting the guidelines set by the NEDA Infracom for Contractors Performance per Section IV of the Implementing Rules and Regulations of PD 1594 as amended July 2000,:

2. The CPES-IU shall oversee the evaluation of a contractor’s performance at least twice during the construction period. First, when the project is almost 50% completed and immediately upon completion of the project or when the contractor has claimed 100% completion of the project.

3. A Contractor’s Performance Evaluation Report shall be sent promptly to the Secretary of Health and to the Construction Industry Authority of the Philippines, Department of Trade and Industry.

4. The CPES-IU shall be composed of the following:

a. Unit Supervisor — He/ she shall act as the head of unit

b. Technical Personnel — They shall assist the head in the performance of the unit 5 technical function

c. Clerk — He/ she shall perform all the unit's administrative and coordinative activities

5. The CPES-IU shall be under the direct technical supervision of the Director III, National Center for Health Facility Development.

Other provisions of the said Administrative Order shall remain valid and in effect.
