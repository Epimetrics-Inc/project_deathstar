<subject>SUBJECT: Amending Section 6 and relevant provisions of the Terms of Reference (TOR) of the National Codex Organization (NCO) annexed to the Joint DA—DOH Administrative Order (DA-A0 No 01 $2008/DOH—AO No. 2008—0030) issued on October 22, 2008.</subject>



<body>
WHEREAS the Department of Agriculture designated the Office of the Undersecretary for Policy and Planning, Project Development and R&D as Codex Contact Point in order to strengthen its support to the National Codex Organization (NCO); 

WHEREAS the Department of Health recognizes the importance of the CCP in linking the NCO with the DA as well as with the FAO—WHO Codex Secretariat and host government Secretariats of Codex Meetings, in view of the effect of information on this link on the implementation of NCO activities; 

NOW THEREFORE, WE the respective Heads of the Department of Agriculture and the Department of Health, by virtue of the powers vested in us by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby instruct the following amendments to the Joint DA-DOH Administrative Order of 2008 (DA-A0 No 01 $2008/ DOH-AO No 2008—0030) and amendments resulting to relevant sections in the Terms of Reference which is annexed to the said Joint Administrative Order. 

SECTION 1 Amendment of Section 6 of the Joint Administrative Order of No 01 Series of 2008 DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE and No. 2008—0030 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH changing the name of the Codex Contact Point to the Office of the DA undersecretary for Policy and Planning, Project Development and R&D from that of the DA Bureau of Agriculture and Fisheries Product Standards. 

SECTION 2 Amendment to Sections 6.5.1, 7.5.1 and 8.5.5 in the Terms of Reference annexed to the above Joint Administrative Order changing the name of the Codex Contact Point to the Office of the DA Undersecretary for Policy and Planning, Project Development and R&D from that of the DA Bureau of Agriculture and Fisheries Product Standards. 

SECTION 3 Repealing Clause — All Administrative Orders, Rules and Regulations and other Administrative issuances or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Joint Administrative Order are hereby repealed and modified accordingly. 

SECTION 4 Effectivity — This Order shall take effect immediately. 


Done in the City of Manila this 16th day of April in the year of our Lord two thousand and ten.
