<subject>SUBJECT: Accounting for Foreign assistance granted to Family Planning Projects.</subject>

<body>

The Family Planning Project of the Department of Health is receiving several grants/aid from various foreign financing institutions. Under the agreements signed with these institutions, a Finance staff office/division under the Family Planning Service is hereby created to handle the financial operations of these funds. Under this set up, disbursement of these grants will be under the administration and supervision of the Family Planning Services Director who shall be primarily responsible and accountable for any violation of existing budget, accounting, auditing and other applicable rules and regulations regarding the management of foreign and/or local grants and aids.

	In view thereof, the Financial Service, Office of Management Service of this Department, is hereby directed to turn over all records pertaining to these funds to the administrative office of the Family Planning Service.
