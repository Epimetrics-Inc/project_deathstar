<subject>SUBJECT:  Authority to provide transportation, subsistence, and quarters to volunteer medical specialist groups.</subject>

<body>
In connection with the Volunteer Medical Specialist Program of the President, authority is hereby granted to provide the volunteers with transportation, subsistence, and quarters chargeable against the funds of the regional hospitals involved. The expenses may be paid in Manila, to be billed against the regional hospitals, to facilitate the fielding of the volunteers, subject to the usual accounting and auditing regulation.
</body>
