	To ensure a smooth flow of transactions relative to disbursement/accounting of funds allotted for the construction of 915 Barangay Health Stations ﬁnder the amended Loan Agreement No. 923 PH (Population Project II) this Administrative Qrder is thereby issued for the guidance of all concerned.

1. General Guidelines

	1.1 Releases of allotments for the construction of Barangay Health Stations Shall be taken up in the Accounting Division, Min1stry of Health.

	1.2 The Budget D1v1sion of the Ministry of Health shall prepare the the Request of Obligation (ROA) to cover the cost of construction of a Barangay Health Station.

	1.3 Disbursement Voucher in favor of the Provincial Health Officer/Chief District Hospital nearest to the chosen location of BHS shall likewise be prepared by the Budget Division (MOH}, to cover the remittance of the allotted fund for the construction of the Barangay Health Station

	1.4. The Accounting Division of the Ministry of Health shall obligate the funds and process the disbursement vouchers. The transfer of cash allotment shall be treated in the books as “Prepayment”.

	1.5 Check hall be issued to the credit of account of the Provincial Health Officer/Chief District Hospital. The deposit shall be treated as trust fund in the books of said Provincial/District/Hospital.

       1.6 Incurrence of expenditures of the funds shall be in accordance with the disbursement procedure, discussed in 2.2 hereof. 
       1.7	Expenditures incurred for the construction of the Barangay Health Stations shall be paid thru checks drawn by the Cashiers concerned against trust deposits.

2. For disbursement of funds for construction material

	2.1 The barangay captain/BPHCC Chairman assisted by the CWIO prepares the requisition and issue voucher to cover all materials needed in the construction based on the MOH specification.

       2.2 The provincial Health Officer/District Chief of Hospital approves the requisition and forwards papers to the Supply Officer of the institution wherein funds for the purpose are accounted for.
       
	2.3 The Supply Officer concerned prepares the canvass papers and purchase orders or the materials needed to be approved by the PHO/District Chief of Hospital.

	2.4 The Barangay Captain/BPHCC Chairman receives construction materials in the presence of a Commission on Audit Representative and signs Cert. No. 1 of the disbursement voucher prepared by the supply officer concerned. Attached Annex C shall be signed by the Barangay Captain/BBPHCC Chairman upon receipt of the construction materials.
	
       2.5 The Hospital Administrative Officer of the Institution handling the funds, recommends the approval of the voucher by signing Certificate No. 3.
       2.6 The voucher is forwarded to the Provincial Health Office/ District Hospital Accountant who signs. Cert. No. 4.

       2.7 The Chief of the Prov1nc1al/D1strict Hospital approves disbursement voucher by signing Certificate No. 5 and forward same to COA

       2.8 The Provinc1a1 Health Office/District Hospital cashier prepares check in accordance with existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations Ito pay claimants.


3. Procedure for disbursement of funds for payrolls of construction workers.

	3.1 The Barangay Captain/BPHCC Chairman signs labor contracts and forwards contract to PHO/District Chief of Hospital for approval
	3.2 The Barangay Captain/BPHCC Chairman certifies in the payroll that services have been duly rendered. The hospital Administrative Officer recommends the payment thereof.
	3.4 The claim is forwarded by the Administrative Officer to the PHO/District Hospital Chief for Approval.
	3.5 The cashier of the Provincial Health Office/District Hospital prepares the check to pay the laborers after pre-audit.
	3.6 The provincial/District Hospital cashier prepares a report of disbursement on a “quincena” basis to the Project Coordinator. Said disbursement shall likewise be supported by voucher and supported by check stubs, three copies of each of invoices, purchase orders, requisition issue voucher and contracts with suppliers if there are any. Creditor portion of the voucher shall be signed by payee.
	3.7 The Regional Provincial Coordinator shall submit the Project Coordinator, Ministry of Health, the certification of completion by the CWIO within 15 days after the turn-over. This document as verified by the Auditor shall support the adjustment to the prepayment account so as to charge the expenditures against the proper account.
	This Order shall take effect immediately. Strict compliance with the above guidelines is enjoined of all.
