<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 19 s. 1995 dated December 28, 1995</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 19 s. 1995 dated December 28, 1995 is hereby amended so as to include the following who will be entitled to issuance of the cellular phones and pagers:

1. All DOH Central Office key persons involved in health emergency management;

2. All designated Stop DEATH Program coordinators of the DOH Retained hospitals and Regional Health Offices

The following are the guidelines for the utilization and maintenance of the units:

1. All monthly rental fees arising from the use of the cellular phones and pagers issued to the personnel involved in the Stop DEATH Program shall be charged against the funds of the Stop DEATH Program of their respective units;

2. All personnel below the level of a Director ( Step DEATH Coordinators ) who have been issued cellular phones shall have a fixed ceiling rate of P 1,000.00 except those personnel in charge of the Stop DEATH Program. Any excess from the allowed amount shall be charged to their personal accounts. The concerned agency should provide a scheme or procedure so as to certify that all calls arising from the use of the units are official in nature.

All other provisions under Administrative Order No. 19 s. 1998 shall remain in full force and effect.
