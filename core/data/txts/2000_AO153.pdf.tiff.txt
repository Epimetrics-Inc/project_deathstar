<body>  

In the light of the requests of Drug Manufacturers, Drug Traders and Drug Importers and in line with the thrust of making drugs affordable, available and accessible on a timely basis, this Department allows Accredited Drug Manufacturers, Drug Traders and Drug Importers to avail the services of Drug Distributors, subject to the following conditions:

1. Only one distributor shall be authorized by the manufacturer/ importer/ trader to transact business with the DOH "unreadable text" geographic area.
2. The distributor shall not engage subcontractors to do business in its behalf;
3. Drug Manufacturers, Drug Importers and Drug Traders in appointing the authorized distributor shall be jointly and severally liable for any transaction with the DOH;
4. The authority for Drug Distributor to transact business with the DOH shall be accredited by the DOH.
