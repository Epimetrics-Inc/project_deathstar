<subject>SUBJECT: Clariﬁcation on the implementation of Adm. Order Nos. 178, s. 2001 dated January 30. 2002 and 23, s. 2001. dated June 4, 2001 on entitlement to Longevity Pay under the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers (RA 7305) and its Imolementinq Rules and Regulations (IRRs).</subject>


<body>

For a uniform implementation of the entitlement to Longevity Pay under the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers (RA 7305) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRRS), certain provisions of AO Nos.178 and 23, both series of 2001 shall be interpreted as follows:

1. On the period of entitlement — The entitlement to Longevity Pay shall be continuous from April 17, 1997 (5 years from the approval of RA 7305 on April 17, 1992) and onwards for all Public Health Workers who have complied with the requirements.

In like-manner, any PHW who has rendered ﬁve (5) years of service at any time from April 17, 1992 and has complied with the requirements is entitled to longevity pay computed from the time the 5-year is completed and onwards on a continuous basis.

2. On other requirements for entitlement— For purposes of entitlement to Longevity Pay, the performance of below Satisfactory, the ﬁnding of guilt in any administrative or criminal case and the authorized leave without pay exceeding ﬁfteen (15) days shall disqualify the public health workers to receive the Longevity Pay only for the period covered by such disqualiﬁcation/s.
