<subject>SUBJECT : Creation of a Product Evaluation and Registration Division in the Food and Drug Administration</subject>

<body>In the interest of the service and in accordance with Administrative Order No. 5 s. 1981 strengthening the Food and Drug Administration, a Product Evaluation and Registration Division is hereby created in the FDA to assume the function of the existing New Drug Unit, Drug Registration Unit and such other units engaged in the evaluation and registration of products under the FDA jurisdiction.

       The Acting Food and Drug Administrator shall temporarily assume the duties and responsibilities as Head of Division.

       In this connection, the Acting FDA Administrator shall recommend to the undersigned the re-assignment of personnel, if necessary, in order to judiciously carry out its objectives and functions.


       Accordingly, all pertinent records of the combined units shall be transferred to the newly created-division.

       This Order shall take effect immediately and supersedes all issuances inconsistent herewith.
</body>
