<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of Headquarters of Western Visayas and Central Mindanao Traveling Skin Clinics to Mambusao, Capiz and Cagayan de Oro City, respectively.</subject>

<body>
In order that the leprosy survey of the provinces of Capiz and Aklan, and the northern provinces of Mindanao may be started and completed, and a more effective implementation of the total leprosy control program of the country attained, the Headquarters of the Western Visayas Traveling Skin Clinic now located in Bacolod City is hereby transferred to Mambusao, Capiz. The Medical Officer-in-Charge of this Clinic is hereby directed to proceed immediately to Mambusao and establish its Headquarters thereat, bringing with him all the equipment, materials and records necessary in this undertaking.

Likewise, the Headquarters of the Central Mindanao Traveling Skin Clinic now located in Kidapawan, North Cotabato, is hereby transferred to Cagayan de Oro City, preparatory to the start of the leprosy survey of Misamis Oriental.

The Regional Directors of Regional Health Office Nos. 5 and 8 are hereby instructed to circularize this Order to all concerned under their respective regions.

This Order, being in the interest of the public service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
