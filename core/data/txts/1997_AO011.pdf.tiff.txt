II. USE OF FUND:

1. An amount of P 45,000,000, net of 10% reserve, released chargeable against A.III.k-Regional Assistance Fund for Drugs and Medicines of Provincial and Municipal Hospitals shall be sub-alloted to Regional Field Office broken down as follows:

<image>table_1.png</image>

2. The said fund shall be utilised for the purpose for which it is intended. Per query with the Department of Budget and Management, Provincial Hospitals referred thereto is inclusive of District Hospitals and Municipal Hospitals and also includes medicare community hospitals.

3. From the net amount received, the Director concerned shall allocated the funds for each hospital under his/her jurisdiction on the basis of a criteria set deemed appropriate to the region.

4. The procurement of drugs and medicines shall be based on the consolidated RIVs submitted by the different hospitals which is within their allocated budget.

5. The RHO shall inform hospitals under their jurisdiction that their requests (RIVs) should be within the lists attached as Annex I of the guidelines. Drugs to be procured outside the list needs approval from DOE-Central Office.

6. The Director concerned is hereby instructed to undertake bulk procurement activity through open competitive public bidding.

7. Report of Liquidation of funds shall be submitted to the Finance Service of the Department of Health, Manila within 30 days after the activity and a copy off which shall be forwarded to the Procurement and Logistics Service.

8. All Disbursements shall be subject to the existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations. 


This order takes effect immediately.
