<subject>SUBJECT: Additional delegation of authority to Mr. ANTONINO P. SAN JUAN, Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development to act on routine matters</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 1 dated July 14, 1981, insofar as delegation of authority to Mr. ANTONINO P. SAN JUAN, Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development is concerned, is hereby further modified to include the approval of requests to use the facilities of the MOH Sports Center and the MOH Badminton Courts made by other offices of the Ministry, various government agencies or private organizations.

In approving such requests, Mr. San Juan shall be guided by the following instructions:

1. Authority to use any of these facilities to any particular office, league or organization, shall be limited to a period of one month and for certain hours and days only in order to accommodate other legitimate requests;

2. The requesting office or organization shall be held responsible for the proper upkeep and use of the facilities and premises during the period covered by the authority;

3. Those designated to take charge of the MOH Sports Center and the MOH Badminton Courts shall be consulted before approval of any request to use these facilities is granted for in orderly scheduling of the offices or organizations concerned.

4. The Ministry reserves the right to preserve order and security within its premises under the provisions of G.O. No. 67 dated October 8, 1980, amending G.O. No. 65 dated September 13, 1980 of the President of the Philippines.

Approval of such requests shall be made "By authority of the Minister of Health" which shall be placed above that of his name. This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
