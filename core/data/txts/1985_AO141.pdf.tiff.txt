<subject>Subject: Amending Adm. Order No. 139 dated December 10, 1985, granting Barangay Health Workers Free Medical Care Effective December 1, 1985</subject>

<body>
	In his letter dated December 16, 1985, His Excellency, President Ferdinand E. Marcos extolled the Barangay Health Workers for unselfishly devoting their time and efforts in the service of the Filipino people, and  the vital role they play in the effective implementation of the Primary Health Care approach to health programs at the level of the Barangay.

	In compliance with the President’s directive, all Barangay Health Workers and their legal dependents shall be entitled to medical care in main health center or hospital facility of the Ministry of Health
	The term “Free Medical Care” shall mean the following benefits:
1. Free medicines subject to the availability thereof in the hospital pharmacy or RHU
2. Free room and board.
3. Free diagnostic services as requested by the attending physician depending on the facilities of the hospital RHU facility.
. No professional fee.

As amended, Adm. Order No.139, s. 1985 stands in effect.
