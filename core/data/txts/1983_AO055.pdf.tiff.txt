<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No; 26-C series of 1982</subject>

<body>Administrative Order No. 26-C, series of 1982 is hereby amended so as to correct the omission in the last sentence of Column II No. 4 of page 24, to read as follows:


       "x x x x x as preceptor coming from the dental college, he or she shall be given an orientation of the hospital policies, rules and regulations for a period of one week before the semester or before the actual affiliation of dental students starts”.

       As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 26-C, s. l982, stands in effect.
