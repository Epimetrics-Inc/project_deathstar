<subject> Subject: Delegation of Authority to Undersecretary TOMAS P. MARAMBA, JR. to approve proposals for projects to be undertaken by NAMRU jointly with the Department of Health or other agencies in the Philippines</subject>
 
 

<body>

In the agreement between the Government of the United States of America and that of the Republic of the Philippines effective June 5,  1981, the Department of Health was designated as the responsible agency for the Philippines while the Naval Medical Research Unit Two (NAMRU—2) designated for the U.S. Direct liason authorized between these two (2) agencies. NAMRU—2 may participate in the conduct of such joint medical research projects of diseases, endemic and epidemic in the Philippines as mutually agreed upon by the two agencies. Other collaborative scientific and medical studies may be conductd as agreed uon between NAMRU-2 and the Department of Health

       To facilitate the approval of and scientific studies, the Undersecretary for Standards and Regulation is hereby delegated the authority to grant such approval. The Undersecretary may call upon any official or unit of the Department and may request assistance from academic institutions, professional associations or non—governmental associations in evaluating the proposals submitted by NAMRU—2 investigators.



Accordingly, the Undersecretary of Health shall approve such research proposals as follows:

”APPROVED by the authority of the Secretary of Health

TOMAS P. MARAMBA, JR., M.D., M.H.A.
Undersecretary of Health
for Standards & Regulation”
Such approved projects shal1 follow the terms and conditions of the aforecited agreement on the activities of NAMRU—2 including the submission of periodic progress reports and results of all research conducted in the Philippines and clearance from appropriate agencies of the Philippine government prior to publication.


This Order shall take effect immediately.
