<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 318 s. 1977</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to the Executive Director, Schistosomiasis Control Council.</subject>

<body>
In order that the Schistosomiasis Control Council can effectively perform its functions, the Executive Director, Executive Committee of the Council, is hereby authorized to approve disbursements, requisitions and other transactions in connection with the performance of his fiscal responsibilities, in accordance with Part II, Chapter I, Article II-3-C of the Integrated Reorganization Plan as implemented by Letter of Implementation No. 8, dated March 1, 1972.

The provisions of Administrative Order No. 285, s. 1976, authorizing agency heads of the Department of Health (Central Offices) should be observed in exercising this authority.

For the purpose of carrying-out the foregoing functions and responsibilities, the Council shall utilize the services of the Financial and Administrative Division of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service of this Department.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
