<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of site of the Davao Mental Hospital from Davao City to the Regional Hospital Compound in Tagum, Davao del Norte.</subject>

       <body>In line with the integration of health and medical services in the Ministry of Health, regional mental hospitals shall be placed under the administrative supervision and control of regional hospitals. For this reason, as much as possible, they should be made a contiguous part of the regional hospital complex.
       Accordingly, the site of the Davao Mental Hospital which is located in Davao City, is hereby transferred to the Davao Regional Hospital Compound in Tagum, Davao del Norte.

       This Order takes effect immediately,

 
</body>
