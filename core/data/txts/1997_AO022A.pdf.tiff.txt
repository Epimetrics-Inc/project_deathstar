<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to some provisions of Administrative Order No. 40—A s. 1996 re: Delegation of Authority</subject>

<body> 

In order to facilitate the processing and approval of financial transactions of the Department of Health some portions of Administrative Order No. 40—A are hereby supplemented and amended. Amendments include modiﬁcations in and/or inclusion of some procurement-related transactions and corresponding signatories.

The transactions are as follows: 

1. Public Biddings, Limited Source Bidding and Repeat Order

2. Direct Contracting, Negotiated Procurement and Local Shopping

3. Disbursement Vouchers of POs, Job Orders and Contracts 

Other provisions/stipulations of Administrative Order No 40—A.s. 1996 still stand in effect. A Department Order which will provide details in effect supplementing this Order, shall be issued.

This Administrative Order shall take effect October, 1997.
