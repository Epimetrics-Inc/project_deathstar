<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines for the implementation of the appropriation granting additional compensation of ₱50.00 a month in lieu of quarters, subsistence and laundry allowances to rural health physicians effective January 1, 1981</subject>

<body>
Upon representations of this Office, an additional compensation of ₱50.00 a month in lieu of subsistence, quarters and laundry allowances, has been included in the appropriations of the Ministry of Health for Calendar Year 1981 under Batas Pambansa Bilang 80 for rural health physicians.

The following guidelines, therefore, are hereby issued to the regional health directors for the proper implementation thereof in accordance with pertinent memorandum circulars of the Ministry of the Budget:

1. The additional compensation of ₱50.00 per month in lieu of subsistence, quarters and laundry allowances, are hereby granted to rural health physicians effective January 1, 1981.

2. The grant of additional compensation of ₱50.00 a month shall not include any day/days when a rural health physician is on leave of absence with/without pay or when on official business outside of his regular station for which he receives per diems.

Strict compliance therewith is hereby enjoined.
