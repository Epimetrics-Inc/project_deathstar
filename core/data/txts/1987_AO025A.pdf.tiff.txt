<subject> Subject: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by BESAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL, Besao Mountain Province.</subject>

<body>
In consonance with Executive Order No. 87,5ection 2, Special Provision of the Department of Health, by BESAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL, Besao Mountain Province, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of One hundred fifty Thousand Pesos (Pl50,000.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

       It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 32l, authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed.
