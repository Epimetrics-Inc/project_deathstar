<subject>Subject: Amendment of Administrative Order No. 18 s. 1993, Subject: Guidelines for the Establishment and Operation of Revolving Funds for the Bureau of Food and Drugs</subject>

<body>
	The provision of Administrative Order No. 18, s. 1993 specifically SPECIFIC GUIDELINES: III. ACCOUNTING SYSTEM AND PROCEDURE: is hereby amended to read as follows:

	“SPECIFIC GUIDELINES:
	III. ACCOUNTING SYSTEM AND PROCEDURE:
		The accounting system and procedure on the establishment and operation of the Revolving Fund for BFAD shall be in accordance with Commission on Audit Circular No. 91-349 dated January 18, 1991 and other pertinent rules and regulation prescribed by fiscal agencies of the government.
