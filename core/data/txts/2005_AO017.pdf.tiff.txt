<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 182 s. 2004. re: Revised Policies and Guidelines on the Administrative Issuance System (AIS) in the Department of Health (DOH)</subject>


<body>

In view of the issuance of Administrative Order No. I82 s. 2004. dated December 2- 2004 which provides that Department Orders. specifically instructions on the use of funds. mandatory savings and sub—allotted funds shall he coursed through the Health Policy Division, HPDPB For review. Section VI-A—l is hereby amended such that said Orders shall pass through the Finance Service For review and approval.

As thus amended. Administrative Order No. 182 still stand in effect.
