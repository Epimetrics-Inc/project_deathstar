<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines and Policies Governing Affiliates in Psychology in the Ministry of Health.</subject>

<body>
1. Hospitals authorized by the Ministry of Health to accept Students majoring in Psychology as student affiliates in Clinical Psychology must have the following requirements:

a. A hospital with an organized Psychological Services Department with qualified psychologists to teach and supervise students majoring in Psychology.

b. The number of students for a specified period will be in proper proportion to the number of qualified staff which shall be 4 students to one psychologist in Acute and Diagnostic Areas, Rehabilitation Areas and Long Term Treatment Areas.

c. Shall provide qualified psychologists to guide, teach, supervise and evaluate student's performance at the ratio of 1:4.

d. Shall provide necessary supplies and materials for the learning experiences of the student affiliates.

e. During the stay of the student affiliates in the designated hospitals, the hospital will not be held responsible for whatever happens to them in case of accidents, etc.

2. Affiliating schools offering majors in Psychology must be recognized and duly accredited by the authorities of the Ministry of Education and Culture before applying for affiliation of its students majoring in Psychology in a teaching and training hospital of the Ministry of Health.

3. Affiliating schools shall pay or replace any damages or losses of test materials by the faculty and students of the schools and shall enforce that the practicum of each student be set at a maximum of twenty (20) hours per week. It shall see to it that all students for practicum in Clinical Psychology must have finished the theoretical aspect for this work: They must be in their senior year and must have taken specifically Psychology of Personality, Clinical Psychology, Abnornmal Psychology, Psychometrics, Statistics and Projective Techniques.

4. The schoold affiliating with the Ministry of Health Hospitals shall, preferably, provide a Preceptor to teach and guide affiliating students in their clinical training. Assignment in Clinical areas and utilization of clinical materials shall be made in six (6) months before the start of the Students' training.

5. All efforts must be made to establish a linkage mutually acceptable between the Affiliating School and the Ministry of Health Agency.

6. The affiliating school shall pay the hospital fifty (â‚±50.00) pesos per student per month. Payments shall be made within the first five (5) days of the month.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
