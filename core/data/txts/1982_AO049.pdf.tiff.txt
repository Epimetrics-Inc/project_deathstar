<subject>SUBJECT	 : Guidelines on the Use of Single Side Band Radio Transcievers and VHF/UHF FM Radio.</subject>

       <body>To ensure the orderly purchase/possession and proper use/operation of Single Side Band, Radio Transceivers and VHE/UHF FM Radio, this Administrative Order is hereby issued for the guidance of all concerned,

1. A11 requests to purchase/possess and operate SSB Transceivers and VHF/UHF-radio should be coursed through the Acting Chief, Administrative Service of the Ministry of Health {Administrative Order No. 35 s. 1982) together with the justification and study conducted by the requesting party

       2. Only Regional Health Office, Provincial and Regional Hospitals are allowed to direct link to the Main Office (Manila) Station.

       3. District Hospitals and Rural Health Units should course their messages to the respective Regional Health Offices or Provincial Hospitals as the case may be. 

       Strict compliance with this order which takes effect immediately is enjoined. 
 	All other Orders contrary hereto is considered revoked or rescinded.

</body>
