<subject>SUBJECT: Prescribing procedures in the signing and countersigning of checks and approval of payrolls and vouchers for payment of salaries, traveling expenses, and other disbursement chargeable from the NISIP-I and SIRDP funds of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service (SCRS), this Ministry.</subject>

<body>
In line with the recommendation of the World Bank Mission (Technical Report I - Philippines, July 1977 and SIRDP Staff Appraisal Report No. 2627 - PH, dated November 12, 1979, in connection with the implementation of the Schistosomiasis Control Components under SIRDP and NISIP-I), that the regional administrative capacity of the SCRS, this Ministry, be strengthened by establishing a functionally autonomous headquarters in Leyte, and to effect the expeditious processing and payment of claims chargeable against the funds of the NISIP-I and SIRDP funds, the following procedures should be strictly followed:

I. Approval of Disbursement.

The Schistosomiasis Project Deputy, concurrently hereby designated Field Manager, NISIP-I, shall approve disbursements and sign treasury warrants, requests for obligations of allotments and/or countersign checks in any amount.

The services of the Hospital Administrative Officer I, Bookkeeper I, both of the Schistosomiasis Hospital, the Disbursing Officer and other personnel assigned in the Schistosomiasis projects at Palo, Leyte, may be utilized in carrying out the administrative function of the project.

All General Vouchers and payrolls and other disbursements should be signed by the Hospital Administrative Officer I (Certificate No. 2) and approved by the Schistosomiasis Project Deputy (Certificate No. 3).

II. Reponsibility of Approving Officials.

The approving official shall be accountable for the following:

1. Proper utilization of funds including:

a. Incurrence of obligations to conform with the purposes of the allotment and procurement to be undertaken in accordance with approved procurement program;
b. the incurrence of expenditures not to exceed authorized allotments; and
c. that adequate funds are provided to finance all the activities of the projects.

2. Proper interpretation of related existing fiscal policies, rules, regulations and observance of prescribed procedures.

III. Filing of Bond

The duly designated approving official shall file with the Bureau of Treasury as accountable Officer in accordance with existing regulations.

At least two (2) copies of all orders and/or issuances made by the Schistosomiasis Project Deputy resulting herefrom or in compliance with these directives should be furnished the Executive Director of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service in Manila, for information and record purposes.

This Order shall take effect upon issuance.
