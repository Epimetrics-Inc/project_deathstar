OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY
Manila

<date>05/28/1971</date>

<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 146 s. 1971</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Re-delineation of Area F boundary to include areas under Cagayan de Oro Malaria Unit.</subject>

<body>
In the interest of the service and for a better coordination between the Malaria Units and Area Offices of the MES and the Rural Health Units and Regional Health Offices concerned, effective July 1st, 1971, the boundary of Area F is hereby re-delineated to include areas presently under the Cagayan de Oro Malaria Unit.

This Order, being made in the interest of the service, is hereby made of record.
</body>
