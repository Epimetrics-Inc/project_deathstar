<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority for Dr. ANTONIO O. PERIQUET, Undersecretary of Health for Hospital and Facilities Services, to approve and sign matters involving infrastructure projects.</subject>

<body>
	Dr. ANTONIO O. PERIQUET, Undersecretary of Health for Hospital and Facilities Services, is hereby delegated the authority to approve and sign infrastructure plans (blue prints) and program of work.

	Accordingly, Dr. Periquet shall approve and sign infrastructure plans and programs of work as follows:

“APPROVED: By the authority of the Secretary of Health”
ANTONIO O. PERIQUET, M.D.
Undersecretary of Health
For Hospital & Facilities Services”

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
