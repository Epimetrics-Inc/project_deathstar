<subject>SUBJECT: Forms of Bid Security, Performance Security, Advance Payment Guarantee and Project Guarantee for GOP-Funded Infrastructure Projects of the Department of Health</subject>


<body>

As prescribed under the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of Republic Act 9184 (An Act providing for the Modernization, Standardization and Regulations of the Procurement Activities of the Government and for other Purposes) Administrative Order No. 4 s. 2003 re: Forms of Bid Security, Performance Security, Advance Payment Guarantee and Project Guarantee for GOP4 funded Infrastructure Projects of the DOH (letter D on p. 2) is hereby amended as follows:

D. Project Warranty in an amount in accordance with the following schedule:

1. Cash deposit, cash bond or letter of credit - Five percent (5 %) of the total contract price

2. Bank Guarantee - Ten percent (10%) of the total contract price

All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 4 remain in full force and effect.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
