<subject>SUBJECT: Implementation of Malaria Vigilance in Pilot Areas As Per Plan of Action Attached</subject>

<body>
To ensure adequate malaria vigilance in areas currently under pre-maintenance and to maximize utilization of health manpower and develop a comprehensive health delivery system in the periphery through an integrated approach, pilot projects on malaria integration will be conducted in the provinces of Ilocos Sur and Marinduque to be implemented by the Malaria Eradication Service and the General Hea1th Service in accordance with the Plan of Action for the Pilot Project on Malaria Integration which is hereto attached and made part of this administrative order.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
