<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81, 88, 94, 96-A, 100, 100-A, 104-A and 109, all series of 1980, and Administrative Order No. 118, 120, 123, 135 and I-B, series of 1981; Re-authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
This order is being issued to supplement Administrative Orders No. 81, 88, 94, 96-A, 100, 100-A, 104-A and 109, all series of 1980, and Administrative Order No. 118, 120, 123, 135 and I-B, series of 1981, fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hosp1tals at any given time including undelivered purchase to include the hospitals named hereunder.

The ceiling hereby fixed are based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines.
