<subject> Subject: Accreditation and licensing functions of private clinics for overseas employment purposes to be discharged by the Bureau of Medical Services effective September 1, 1984</subject>

<body>

       Under the MOH-MOLE agreement, the accreditation and licensing of clinics which will conduct the physical and medical examination of workers for Overseas employment and vested in the Ministry of Health. For purposes of expediency, these functions were discharged by the Bureau 01 Medical Services and the Physical and Medical Examination Division, administrative Service, Office of the Minister.

       In accordance with the provisions of Executive Order No. 851, dated December 2 ,1982, the functions of accreditation and licensing of private clinics shall be discharged only by the Bureau or Medical Services.

This Order takes effect September 1, 1984
