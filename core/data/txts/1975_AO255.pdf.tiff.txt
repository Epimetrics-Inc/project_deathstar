<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 255 s. 1975</docnum>

TO: The Technical Staff, Bureau Directors, Chiefs of Offices, Project Directors, Regional Health Directors, Provincial Health Officers, City Health Officers, Chiefs of Hospitals, and other concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Policies, Guidelines and Standards on Dental Health Services</subject>

<body>
In accordance with Letter of Implementation No. 8 relative to Part XVI on health and other pertinent provisions of the Integrated Reorganization Plan and for effective supervision, control and coordination of the dental health program with the total health program of the Department of Health and the Regional Health Offices, the updated policies, guidelines and standards prescribed by the Bureau of Dental Health Services are hereby promulgated and officially established for the strict compliance by all concerned.

Section 1. Policies on Dental Public Health
Section 2. Organization and Functions of the Bureau of Dental Health Services
Section 3. Organization and Functions of Dental Services in the Regional Health Offices
Section 4. Working Relationships Between the Department of Health, Bureau of Dental Health Services, Other Staff Bureaus and Offices of the Department of Health, Regional Health Offices and Other Field Offices
Section 5. Delineating Lines of Authority and Responsibility
Section 6. Channels of Communication
Section 7. Standard Methods of Recording and Reporting
Section 8. Minimum Standard Requirements for Rural, Hospitals, Public and Private Schools and Industrial Dental Services
Section 9. Recruitment, Selection, Appointment, Promotion, Distribution and Assignment of Dental Personnel
Section 10. Dental Training Policies
Section 11. Budget Preparation for Dental Services
Section 12. Requisition, Procurement and Distribution of Dental Equipment, Supplies and Materials
Section 13. Dental Research Projects
Section 14. Evaluation Procedures

The applicable provisions of Administrative Order No. 118 as contained in the Standards Manual, BDHS of 1970, which are not inconsistent with the provisions of this Administrative Order, shall continue to be in full force and effect. All other previous orders inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are hereby rescinded, or otherwise amended accordingly.

This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
