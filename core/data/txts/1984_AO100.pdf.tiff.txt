<subject> SUBJECT : Delegation of authority to Provincial Health Officers/Officers in Charge of Integrated Provincial Health
Offices to approve applications for maternity leave of absence of married women employees holding positions in the integrated health and medical units of the province effective September 1, 1984. </subject>


<body>
Pursuant to the provisions of Section 16 of P.D. 851 dated December
2, 1982, and in order to facilitate action on routine personnel matters that will enhance administrative efficiency at their level, authority is hereby granted to Provincial Health Officers or Officers in Charge of Integrated Provincial Health Offices to approve applications for maternity leave of absence of married women employees holding positions in the integrated health and medical units under their supervision, including computation of salary incident to such maternity leave, subject to the following conditions:

l. That when necessary, any initial application for maternity leave of absence to be approved with full pay under this authority shall be supported by a copy of the applicant's approved appointment or service record, particularly in the case of vertical programs integrated with the health office.

2. That, when applications for maternity leave of absence is approved with half pay, deductions shall be immediately effected in the payro11 of the Corresponding office. '

3. That leave applications to be approved under this Order shall bear the following phrase above that of the signing official:

"By authority of the Minister of Health"
<image>figure_1.png</image>


This Order takes effect September 1, 1984.
</body>
