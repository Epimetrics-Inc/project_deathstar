<subject>Subject: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 74, s. 1983</subject>


  
<body>
To expedite the bulk procurement of construction materials
for the immediate construction of barangay health stations (BHS) damaged by typhoon “Nitang” in the province of Surigao del Norte, Region 10, and. the Approval of Cebu and Bohol, both of Region 7, Administrative Order No. 74, s. 1983 is hereby amended insofar as Item II. Approval of Requisitions and Purchase Orders under 3. In provincial Office:
	a. Provincial Health Officer
	-up to P100,000.00 per order for emergency purposes of construction materials.
	This Order shall be valid and to cover only the construction of BHS in the above province under the Population Project II and shall take effect immediately.
