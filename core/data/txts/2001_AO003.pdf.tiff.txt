<subject>SUBJECT: RECALLING AO NO. 99 S. 2000 PROVIDING FOR THE BANNING AND WITHDRAWAL OF THE FIXED-DOSE COMBINATION DRUG PRODUCT LORATIDINE + PSEUDOEPHEDRINE (CLARINASE) FROM THE MARKET</subject>

<body>

After a careful review of current available information and data, AO 99 s. 2000 which called for the banning and withdrawal of Fixed Dose Combination drug product Loratidine + Pseudoephedrine (Clarinase) from the market is hereby recalled and declared of no force and effect and instead the guidelines on the Registration of Fixed-Dose Combination products promulgated under AO 96 s. 1990 is reiterated.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
