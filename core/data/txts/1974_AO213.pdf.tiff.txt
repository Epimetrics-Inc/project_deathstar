<docnum>NO. 213 s. 1974</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Suspension of the Implementation of Administrative Order No. 211 dated April 8, 1974.</subject>

<body>
In view of the need for further study on the delegation of authority for the approval of disbursements, the implementation of Administrative Order No. 211 dated April 8, 1974 is hereby suspended. The delegation of authority previously made to the Acting Budget Officer II of Regional Health Office No. 8 is also hereby revoked.
</body>
