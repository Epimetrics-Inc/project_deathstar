<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of SONA Incentive Bonus to all Officers and Employees of the Department of Health (DOH) for CY 2002</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to Section I, Rule XI of the Omnibus Rules, Implementing Section 31, Book V of Executive Order No 292 and other pertinent Civil Service Laws and in view of the DOE vital contribution towards the attainment of government objective of building a strong republic, the grant of SONA Incentive Bonus as employee welfare benefit is hereby authorized to all DOH officials and employees in the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, centers for health development and hospitals including specialty hospitals to help them overcome present economic difficulties, boost their morale and deepen their commitment and dedication to public service.

With the present economic crisis, this benefit is a timely expression of the DOH's recognition of its unwavering support and obligation to government service and in keeping with the spirit of Christmas.

This benefit shall not be more than FIVE THOUSAND PESOS (P 5,000 00) which shall be given to each officials and employees regardless of salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, temporary or contractual), chargeable against savings realized from released allotment of continuing and current appropriations, current operating expenses (PS and MOOE) or sub-allotments of the agency and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

To be entitled to this benefit, an employee must have rendered at least a total or an aggregate of four (4) months of service in 2002 and are still in the service as of October 31, 2002. Those who have rendered less than four (4) months of service from January 1, 2002 to October 31, 2002 and still in the service as of October 31, 2002 shall be entitled to the SONA Incentive but pro-rated as follows:
