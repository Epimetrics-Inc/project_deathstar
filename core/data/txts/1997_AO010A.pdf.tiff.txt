<body>

SECTION 15 — ACCREDITATION FEES:


Subsection 15.3 ”A penalty of FIVE HUNDRED PESOS (P500.00) for late renewal shall be charged in addition to the renewal fee of hospital/clinic of the application is filed during the next three (3) months after anniversary date” is hereby repealed as it is no longer applicable.

Subsection 15.4 “The certificate of accreditation shall be considered lapsed if not renewed within three months after expiration. Subsequently, any application shall be subject to an application fee” is hereby amended to read as follows

“THE CERTIFICATE OF  ACCREDITATION, IF NOT RENEWED SHALL BE CONSIDERED LAPSED AFTER THE DATE OF EXPIRATION. SUBSEQUENTLY, ANY APPLICATION SHALL BE SUBJECT TO AN APPLICATION FEE.”


SECTION 21 - RENEWAL


Subsection 21.1

"Application for renewal of certificate of accreditation shall be filed within sixty (60) days prior to the date of expiration. A penalty shall be paid if the renewal is filed after the anniversary date in accordance with Section 15” is hereby amended to read as follows:

APPLICATION FOR RENEWAL OF CERTIFICATE OF ACCREDITATION SHALL BE FILED WITHIN SIXTY (60) DAYS PRIOR TO THE DATE OF EXPIRATION. 

All sections of A0 85—A s. 1990 which have not been amended by this AO shall remain in effect and in force.

This amendment shall take effect on July 1, 1997.
