<subject>SUBJECT: Authority for selected personnel of the Center for Health Development to attend the Post-Graduate Course on Environment and Occupational Toxicology Administration and Management at the UP-National Poisons Control and Information Service/College of Medicine-Manila from July 1-December 31, 2001</subject>


<body>

Authority is hereby granted to the following personnel of the Center for Health Development to attend the six (6) month post-graduate course on Environmental and Occupational Toxicology Administration and Management at the UP-NPCIS/College of Medicine-Manila from July -  December 31, 2001.

<image>table_1.png</image>

Under this Order, the above-mentioned personnel are entitled to collect annual transportation expenses, per diems in going to Manila and return after the training, monthly training allowance based on Department Memorandum No. 25 s. 1995 and book allowance chargeable against the sending agency, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations. Likewise, their tuition fees had been covered by the existing Contract of Service with the UP-NPCIS.

Furthermore, the above-mentioned personnel shall submit a narrative report including their re-entry plan to the CHD Director within thirty (30) days after completion of their training program.

By Authority of the Secretary of Health:
