<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of the provisions of Administrative Order No. 296, s. 1976, relative to the signing and countersigning of TCAA Checks by Regional Health Officials.</subject>

<body>
Following the provisions of COA Circular No. 79-26(E), Sections I-C and 1-D of Administrative Order No. 296 are hereby amended to read as follows:

Section I-C - Officials authorized to sign TCAA checks:

1. Regional Health Director - to sign checks Up to ₱100,000.00.
2. Admintstrative Officer III - to sign checks up to ₱20,000.00 for transactions not subject to pre-audit per COA Circulars Nos. 76-26 and 79-26 (E).
3. Cashier II - to sign checks up to ₱3,000.00.
4. Disbursing Officer - to sign checks up to ₱l,000.00.

Section I-D - Officials authorized to countersign checks:

1. Ragional Health Director - to countersign checks up to ₱20,000.00 for transactions not subject to pre-audit by C0A representatives (COA Circulars Nos. 76-26 and 79-26 (E).

2. Finance Officer - to countersign checks up to ₱3,000.00;
- in the absence of the Regional Director, to countersign checks up to P20,000.00.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
