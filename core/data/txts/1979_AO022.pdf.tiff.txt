<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 19, dated April 19, 1979</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 19, dated April 10, 1979, authorizing Mrs. FELISA C. DUNASCO to handle ccash and check disbursements and collections of the Field Operations under the Office of the Minister including the Rural Health Practice Program, is hereby modified such that all the other accountable officers in the Collection  and Disbursements Section, General Services Division, Administrative Service, are hereby authorized to sign the stipend checks of all the underboard medical and nursing graduates under the Rural Health Practice Program in view of the large volume of checks involved and the time limit for their release.

As thus modified, Administrative Order No. 18 stands.
</body>
