<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines and Procedures on Accreditation of DOH Suppliers for Medical/Surgical/Dental/Laboratory Supplies and Materials</subject>

<body>

The accreditation of suppliers is an essential screening and gatekeeping mechanism to ensure that only qualified bidders can transact business with the Department of Health and ultimately perform to the best interests of the Filipinos. With the experiences in the implementation of the Modified Procurement System, results of monitoring and evaluation showed that the accreditation needs to be strengthened through changes and modifications in the various levels of the process.

The objective of this Order is to establish terms and conditions, processes, activities and responsibilities of parties involved in the accreditation processes at the Central Office.


I. STRUCTURE

Accreditation Committee for Medical/Surgical/Dental/Laboratory Supplies and Materials shall be composed of a Chairperson, Vice-chairperson and at least three (3) members; and a Secretariat.

1. The committee must have at least one (1) member with financial analysis expertise, preferably an accountant, who shall be designated as the financial analyst. He/she shall evaluate the financial capability and determine the financial category of the supplier.

2. Other members of the committee shall be tasked to do technical evaluation (production and service capability of the company) and site inspection, as the need arises.

3. There shall be at least two (2) members of the Secretariat. They shall maintain the records of policies and procedures of the committee.


II. TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Accreditation of suppliers for Medical/Surgical/Dental/Laboratory Supplies and Materials by the Central Office shall be valid and acceptable to all RFOs and retained hospitals.

2. The Chairperson of the committee shall have the authority to hold cash advance. The cash advance shall be used for gasoline during site visits and for supplies and materials that the committee needs which are not available from the PLS. Funds shall be chargeable against the Procurement Systems Development of PLS.

3. Only suppliers of Medical/Surgical/Dental/Laboratory Supplies and Materials are covered by this accreditation including suppliers of medical/dental device with a unit cost of less than P10,000.00
