<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO A.O. No. 21-A ENTITLED “MANAGEMENT OF THE DOH LINGAP PARA SA MAHIHIRAP PROGRAM FUND UNDER R.A. 8745 OTHERWISE KNOWN AS GENERAL APPROPRIATIONS ACT OF 1999”</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 21-A s., 1999 entitled “Management of the DOH Lingap Para sa Mahihirap Program fund under R.A 8745 otherwise known as General Appropriations Act of 1999” is hereby amended as follows: 

1. Section A.2 Medical Assistance Fund
This Section should include private hospitals aside from government hospitals as identified by the legislators but on emergency cases only. Release of the funds will be through a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) between DOH and the private hospital concerned. Private Drugstores may also be included. DOH, Central or concerned Regional Offices, shall execute a MOA with the drugstore and the fund shall be used to provide only generic drugs/medicines listed in the Philippine National Drug Formulary (PNDF) and prescribed only by government physicians to indigent patients referred by the legislator/s.

2. VII. General Fund Management
A. Assistance Fund
The following provision should be added:
“Upon the discretion of the legislator, the Regional Field Office (RFO) may transfer the amount allocated for the legislator in kind. As such, the RFO shall be the one to purchase the needed drugs, medicines and supplies for the identified hospitals, RHUs and BHSs”.

3. VI. Fund Allocation
The Sustansya Para sa Masa Matching Grant under the Public Health Package should provide for a P500,000 allocation for each Congressman and P2,000,000 for each Senator for LGUs, NGOs and other organized groups of their choice as grant funds for Community-based Nutrition Projects. Project Proposals shall be submitted to the concerned RFOs for review and approval prior to the release of funds to the concerned Project Proponents (LGUs, NGOs and other organized groups).

All other provisions in A.O. 21-A s., 1999 shall remain in effect.
