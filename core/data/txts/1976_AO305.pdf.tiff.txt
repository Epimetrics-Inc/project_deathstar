<docnum>NO. 305 s. 1976</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to certain officials under the Department of Health on matters involving approval of leave applications.</subject>

<body>
Effective December 16, 1976, the Directors of the National Family Planning Office and of the National Nutrition Service, the Executive Director of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service, and the Administrator of the Food and Drug Administration are hereby authorized, subject to the provisions of Rule XVI of the Revised Civil Service Rules (R.A. 2260), to act on, approve and sign after the words "By authority of the Secretary" all applications for vacation, sick and maternity leaves of absence with and/or without pay (except leave without pay beyond 60 days) of officers and employees under their respective jurisdiction, provided, in case of vacation leave, their services can be spared without detriment to the interest of public service, and provided further that in all cases of leave applications, the required papers in support thereof are in order.

Requests for commutation of salary incident to leave of officials and employees in the above-mentioned offices shall continue to be acted upon by this Department, and applications for leave without pay in excess of sixty (60) days shall likewise be submitted to this Department for appropriate action.

In connection with the foregoing authority, the Chiefs of the aforementioned offices shall secure from the Personnel Division of this Department the leave credits of their respective personnel.

Appliation for leave of absence of officers and employees of the said offices received in the Department before December 16, 1976 shall continue to be acted upon in this Office, but those that may be submitted after that date shall be returned to the Office of origin for action conformably with the terms of this Order.
</body>
