<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority of officials in the Mariveles Mental Ward under the Central Luzon General Hospital.</subject>
<body>
	To expedite financial transactions in the Mariveles Mental Ward located in Mariveles , Bataan, now placed under the Central Luzon General Hospital under the Executive Order No. 851 dated December 2, 1982 and Batas Pambansa Bilang 640, the following delegation of authority is hereby made temporarily to avoid disruption of the services.

	I. Approval of Disbursements
		1. Officer-in-charge of the mental ward
			-no limit
		2. Officer-in-charge of the administrative service
			-up to P10,000 per transaction, but no limit for transactions pertaining only to payroll for salaries, vouchers and remittances to GSIS, BIR, MEDICARE, Pag-ibig and other government agencies
	II. Approval of Requisitions and Purchase Orders
		1. Officer-in-charge of the Mental Ward
-up to P50,000 for food stuffs and per requisition for supplies, materials, drugs and medicines thru the Supply Coordination Office and thru SOO
-up to P20,000 per order for local emergency supplies and materials, drugs and medicines
-up to P5,000 per order for emergency repair of motor vehicles, office and medical equipment
2. Officer-in-charge of Administrative Service
	-up to P10,000 per requisition for supplies and materials for housekeeping purposes only.
	III. Signing of Checks
		1. Officer-in-charge of Administrative Service
			-no limit
		2. Cashier
			- up to P5,000
			0up to P10,000 in the absence of Officer-in charge Administrative Service.
	IV. Countersigning Officials
		1. Officer-in-charge of Mental Ward
			-no limit
		2. Resident Physician (Senior)
			-no limit in the absence of the Officer-in-charge of the mental ward.
	This order supersedes all other Orders Inconsistent herewith and shall take effect immediately.
