<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO SECTION ADMINISTRATIVE 0RDER NO. 156 SERIES OF 1971, DATED SEPTEMBER 23, 1971</subject>

<body>
Section 1 – The provisions of Section 4, Administrative Order No. 156, series of 191, dated September 23, 1971, are hereby amended to read as follows:

	“Section 4 – A fee of Two hundred pesos (P200.00) shall be charged for every license issued by the Bureau of Research and Laboratories to open and operate a blood bank or processing laboratory or to distribute human blood (Outlet) whether for free or for sale. This license shall be subject to renewal within the last two (2) months of each year upon payment of renewal fee of one hundred pesos (P100.00). A grace period of two (2) months is allowed subject to a fine of fifty pesos (P50.00) within the first two months of the succeeding year. After the grace period, the license will be considered to have lapsed and a new license shall be applied for.

Section 2 – This amendment shall take effect upon approval and publication as required.
