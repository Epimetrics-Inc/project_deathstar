<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing previous Administrative Orders issued regarding authorized ceiling o£ drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>This order is being issued to further supplement Administrative
Order No. 81, s. 1980 and the supplementing orders thereto issued implementing Section 9, Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pamhansa Blg. 40, fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria at any given time including undelivered purchases so as to include western Kalinga
District Hospital, Balbalan, Kalinga, Apayao in the amount of P30,000.

       The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines.

</body>
