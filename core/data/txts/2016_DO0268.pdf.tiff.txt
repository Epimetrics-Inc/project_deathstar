<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines for the use of Hardhats and Headlights</subject>

<body>
I. RATIONALE 

Administrative Order No. 168 s. 2004 which is the National Policy on Health Emergencies and Disasters, aims to develop goals, strategies, plans and policies for ensuring an efﬁcient and effective system for managing emergencies and disasters. To do this, the DOH through Health Emergency Management Bureau (DOH— HEMB) is distributing Hardhats and Headlights to all employees of DOH- Central Ofﬁce for emergencies and disasters. 

The safety Hardhats and Headlights shall be distributed to each ofﬁce so that each personnel/staff of the ofﬁce will have the hardhats and headlights to be used in case of emergency like an earthquake. 

II. OBJECTIVES 

To serve as a guide in the distribution of Hardhats and Headlights. 

III. SCOPE AND COVERAGE 

This Order shall cover all DOH-Central Ofﬁce employees. 

IV. GENERAL GUIDELINES: 

1. One DOH employee — one safety hardhats and headlights provided. 
2. The Hardhats and Headlights should not be taken home. 
3. The Hardhats and Headlights shall be displayed in the most accessible place within reach of individual personnel for emergency. 
4. After emergency use, it will be returned to the ofﬁce. 
5. Maintenance and aftercare shall be the responsibility of the ofﬁce supervisor (inventory, accounting and cleaning) 

V. EFFECTIVITY: 

This shall take effect immediately once approved.
