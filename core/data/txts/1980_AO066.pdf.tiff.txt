<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority to the designated officials of the Philippine Population Project I (MOH).</subject>

<body>
PURPOSE: This Order authorizes the designated officials to approve financial transactions and communications related to the liquidation operation of the Philippine Population Project I (MOH).

I. Officials authorized to approve disbursements:

a. Executive Director - ₱100,000.00 per transaction.
b. Deputy Director - Not to exceed ₱20,000.00 per transaction.
c. Minister of Health - No limit.

II. Officials authorized to obligate funds and sign ROA's:

a. Executive Director - ₱100,000.00
b. Deputy Director - Not to exceed ₱20,000.00
c. Minister of Health - No limit.

III. Officials authorized to sign TCAA checks and Treasury Warrants:

a. Executive Director -
TCAA Check - up to ₱100,000.00 per check
Treasury Warrant - No Limit
b. Deputy Director -
Treasury Warrant - up to ₱100,000.00
c. Head, Disbursement and Collection Section -
TCAA Check - up to ₱50,000.00 per check
d. Disbursing Officer -
TCAA Check - up to ₱5,000.00 per check
e. In the Absence of the Project Coordinator -
The Deputy Director shall sign:
-TCAA check up to ₱100,000.00
-Treasury Warrant up to ₱100,000.00
f. Minister of Health -
-Treasury Warrant - No limit.

IV. Officials authorized to countersign TCAA checks:

Executive Director
Deputy Director
- Both to countersign up to ₱20,000.00 per check in accordance with COA Circular No. 76-26 and 79-26 (E).

V. Approval of Requisitions, Purchase Orders and other transactions:

- Minister of Health - Any or all requisitions, purchase Orders and Contracts.

VI. Issuance, Distribution of Supplies, Materials and Equipment in stock:

- Executive Director - To approve all issues or schdules of distribution of the various items in stock in accordance with the Project Plan.

VII. Officials to Approve Travel Orders:

- Executive Director - to approve any or all travel orders not to exceed a period of fifteen (15) days and related to the liquidation operations of the Project, chargeable against the said Project funds.

IX. Re-Delegation:

The authority herein delegated shall not be re-delegated.

X. Effectivity

This Order shall take effect from January 1 to June 30, 1980.
