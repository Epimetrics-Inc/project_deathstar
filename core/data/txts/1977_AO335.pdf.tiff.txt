<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 335 s. 1977</docnum>

TO: The Technical Staff, Bureau Directors, Regional Health Directors, Chiefs of Offices & Hospitals, Heads of Projects and Units, and all others concerned

<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 319, series of 1976</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order 319, series of 1976 relative to the inclusion of Hospital Dentists as member of the Medical Staff for purposes of sharing with the Medicare Medical Staff Common Fund is hereby amended upon recommendation of a committee created by the Undersecretary of Health which quotes:

"All duly appointed hospital dentists are part and parcel members of the organized medical staff organization and are to be governed by the policies, rules and regulations affecting said organization."

All concerned are advised to be guided accordingly.

This Order shall take effect October 1, 1976.
</body>
