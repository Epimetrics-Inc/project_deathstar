<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 308 s. 1977</docnum>

TO: Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors and Officers-in-Charge of Regional Health Offices duly designated by the Secretary of Health

<subject>SUBJECT: Additional delegation of authority governing appointments to positions of the 2nd level in further compliance with Letter of Instructions No. 448.</subject>

<body>
In further compliance with the provisions of Letter of Instructions No. 448 dated August 18, 1976, strengthening the administrative capacity for regional operations, Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors and Officers-in-Charge of Regional Health Offices duly designated by the Secretary of Health are hereby delegated additional appointing authority over original and promotional appointments and reinstatements to positions of the second level which include professional, technical and scientific positions and which involve professional, technical or scientific work in a non-supervisory or supervisory capacity from Range 43 to Range 50 (alphabetical list of positions from R-43 to R-50 in the second level attached). The guidelines to be observed in the appointments of personnel to these positions of the said level shall be the same as those enumerated in Sec. I-A-b of Administrative Order No. 299 dated September 22, 1976. Likewise, Secs. 2 to 5 of the said Administrative Order No. 229 shall be applicable thereto. Accordingly, the provisions of Administrative Order No. 301 also dated September 22, 1976 on the subject: Delegation of authority to create a Regional Committee on Evaluation and Protests, for purposes of expediting the resolution of evaluation and protest cases against appointments to first level positions up to R-50, shall include the resolution of similar cases against appointments to second level positions up to R-50.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
