<subject>SUBJECT : Authority of Hospitals to sell medicines and drugs at cost plus 10% margin</subject>

<body>
In accordance with the authority wested in the government Department Heads under section 541 of the Revised National Accounting and Auditing Manual, Chiefs of Hospitals under this Department are hereby authorized to sell to pay patients the medicines and drugs of their respective institutions at cost plus a margin of ten percentum (10%). 

It is understood that this authority is applicable only to cases embraced under the provisions of the Philippine Medical CAre Act of 1969 (R.A. 6111). All concerned are hereby enjoined to act accordingly.
</body>
