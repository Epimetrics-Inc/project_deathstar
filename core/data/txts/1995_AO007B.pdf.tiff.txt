<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of Administrative Order No. 33-A. S. 1994 re: Rules and Regulation Governing the Licensure and Regulation of Dialysis Clinic</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to the Executive Order No. 5, s. 1986 and consistent with E.O. 119, the following sections of DOH Administrative Order No. 33-A, s. 1994 are hereby amended to strengthen and improve the licensing and regulatory functions of BLR over dialysis clinics independent of a hospital.


Section 7.1.1.1 [At least one of the medical staff of the clinic shall be a member of the medical staff of a tertiary hospital in the locality to facilitate referral of complications that may arise from the dialysis procedure].

7.1.1.2 [If the above is not possible the clinic shall have a memorandum of agreement with a tertiary hospital for complications that may arise from the dialysis procedure. Ambulance service shall be readily available for the transport of the patients].

THE REQUIREMENTS THAT “ONE OF THE MEDICAL STAFF OF THE DIALYSIS CLINIC MUST BE A MEMBER OF THE MEDICAL STAFF OF A TERTIARY HOSPITAL IN THE LOCALITY” AND “A MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT WITH A TERTIARY HOSPITAL” ARE NO LONGER REQUIRED IN ORDER NO TO IMPEDE EQUITABLE DISTRIBUTION OF DIALYSIS SERVICES.


Section 7.1.2 [Registered Nurse shall have Certificates on a Special Training in dialysis for not less than 25 days].

THE CERTIFICATE OF TRAINING IN DIALYSIS FOR THE NURSES WILL BE CERTIFIED BY THE RENAL NURSING ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (RENAP) WHICH ACCREDITS THE DIALYSIS NURSING TRAINING PROGRAMS CONDUCTED BY THE DIFFERENT INSTITUTIONS.


Section 7.1.3 [Medical Technicians shall have certificate of dialysis training and at least 1 year experience in same field]

CERTIFICATES OF TRAINING REQUIRED FOR THE MEDICAL TECHNICIAN AND A NURSING AIDE COURSE OR 2 YEARS COLLEGE STUDIES FOR THE MIDWIVES OR NURSING AIDES ARE NO LONGER NECESSARY. THERE ARE NO TRAINING PROGRAMS FOR SUCH OTHER THAN IN-SERVICE TRAINING IN THE COURSE OF EMPLOYMENT.


Section 7.1.9 [From the very nature of free standing dialysis units, most patients are stable and prefer dialysis chairs to beds. There should be an additional phrase: “or suitable dialysis chairs”]

SUITABLE DIALYSIS CHAIR SHALL ALSO BE USED INSTEAD OF BED GUARD RAILS, IF PREFERRED BY THE PATIENT DURING THE DIALYSIS PROCEDURES.


Section 7.2.1.8 [Suction Machine]

SUCTION MACHINE IS AN ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT FOR EQUIPMENT.


Section 7.3.2.1 [Dialysis room with air conditioning unit]

AN AIR CONDITIONING UNIT IN THE DIALYSIS ROOM IS AN OPTIONAL REQUIREMENT.


This Order amends the above mentioned Sections of Administrative Order No. 33-A s. 1994 and supersedes previous issuances that are inconsistent herewith.

Effectivity: This amendment shall take effect fifteen (15) days after publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
