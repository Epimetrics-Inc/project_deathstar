<subject>SUBJECT: Revocation of Administrative Order No. 117, dated June 1, 1970, fusing the Tagum and Sta. Cruz Malaria Units.</subject>

<body>
After assessing the activities of the Malaria Units in Tagum, Davao del Norte and Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur, it has been found that the technical objective of malaria eradication will be jeopardized with the fusion of the said two (2) Units. In view thereof, Administrative Order No. 117, dated June 1, 1970, effecting the fusion, is hereby revoked effective immediately.

This Order is hereby made of record.
</body>
