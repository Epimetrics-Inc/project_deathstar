<subject>Guidelines in the Utilization and Replenishment of Cash Advance of Provincial Health Officers in the account of P1,000.00 to be availed in the implementation of Akbayan sa Kalusugan Project in Regions 2, 8, 9, 10, 11 and 12</subject>
<body>
	1. Cash advance amounting to P1,000.00 granted to each of the Provincial Health Officers of Regions 2, 8 to 12 in pursuance of Ministry Order No. 41-A, s. 1985, chargeable against the ASK Funds of the Nutrition Service is intended to reimburse the expenses of the consignees, either the Municipal Health Officers/Public Health Nurses, in transporting and handling the rise from the NFA warehouse to the project sites of the ASK Project.
	2. Pursuant to Section 618 of the Revised Administrative Code, the Provincial Health Officers as Special Disbursing Officers shall – 
	a) maintain a separate cash book for the project;
	b) record all disbursements made out of the cash advance, related to the project
	c) be ready for audit or cash examination at all times.
	3. The cash advance shall be operated on the imprest system.
	4. Replenishment of the cash advance shall be made upon preparation and submission by the Provincial Health Officers of the following documents:
		a) Summary of charges made from the cash advance;
		b) Duplicate copy of the authority to issue rice from NFA provincial branch and the authority to withdraw rice from the NFA warehouse, duly signed by the consignee;
		c) Properly filled up vouchers and untampered receipts;
		d) Statement of Account Current.
	5. Replenishment as an exemption to P.D. 1445, Section 89 shall be made as soon as a minimum of one-third of the total cash advance has been disbursed, the details of  which should be reflected in the accountable personnel’s cash book for ready inspection at all times.
	6. At the end of the year, it shall be fully liquidated. Any balance shall be refunded and a new set of cash advance shall be issued at the start of the year
	This Order takes effect immediately upon proper designation of Provincial Health Officers as Special Disbursing Officer.
