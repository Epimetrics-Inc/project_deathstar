<subject>SUBJECT: Revised Guidelines for Delegation of Authority for certain Administrative Transactions in the Centers for Health Development (CHD’s)</subject>

<body>

In view of the implementation of the DOH Rationalisation and streamlining Plan per Executive Order No. 102, and in order to streamlining operations and fast-track action on official documents and communications, authority for certain officials to approve transactions is hereby delegated. This delegation is within the framework of EO 102, other executive order and the General Accounting and Auditing Manual. Further, this Order thereby supersedes stipulations in all other Orders on “Delegation of Authority” and all other Orders inconsistent herewith.
