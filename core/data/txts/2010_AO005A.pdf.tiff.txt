<subject>SUBJECT: Admendment to Administrative Order No. 2010-0005 Entitled Establishment of the National Ceneter for Pharmaceutical Access and Management (NCPAM) under the Office of the Secretary of Health</subject>

<body>
Executive Order No 649 dated August 8, 2007 and Excecutive Order No 649 -A dated April 10, 2008 both issued by the Office of the President, authorized the retention of the Philippine International Trading Corporation (PITC) under the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) and the transfer of the PITC Pharma Inc. (PPI) to the Department of Health and designating the Secretary of Health as the ex-officio Chairman thereof. In view of these developments, page one, paragraph three(3) of Administrative Order No 2010-0005 dated January 8, 2010, is hereby amended, modifying the name Philippine International Trading Corporation (PITC) into PITC Pharma Inc. (PPI). 

All the provisions above - mentioned Administrative Order are all in order and in effect. 

Please be guided accordingly
</body>
