<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 14 dated May 10, 1996</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 14 dated May 10,1996 is hereby amended to the effect that the Chairman of the Selection/Promotion Board for positions in the first and second level of the career service in the DOH Central Office shall be the Assistant Secretary or the Bureau/Service Director in the Office concerned instead of the Undersecretary.

The two representatives of the rank-and-file employees one from the first level and one from the second level shall be chosen by the duly registered/accredited employee association in the department or agency. In cases where there are no employee associations in the department or agency, the representatives shall be chosen at large by the employees through a general election to be called for the purpose.

As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 14, s. 1996, stands in effect.
