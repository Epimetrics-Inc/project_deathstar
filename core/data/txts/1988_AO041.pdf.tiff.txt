<subject>Subject: Designating the Guimaras District Hospital as the Provincial Hospital of Guimaras Sub-Province.</subject>


<body>
	

In line with the integration of health and medical services in the Department of Health, the GUIMARAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL is hereby designated as the provincial hospital of Guimaras Province. As such, it is placed under the administrative supervision and control of the Provincial Health Officer of Guimaras.

This Order takes effect immediately
</body>
