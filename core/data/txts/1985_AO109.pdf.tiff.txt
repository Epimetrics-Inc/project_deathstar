<subject>SUBJECT:	Amendment to Administrative Order No. 16-C s. 1981</subject>

<body>
In line with the provisions of P.D. 1424 and pursuant to the provisions of Administrative Order No. 60s. 1979, Dr. RIMANDO C. SAGUIN is hereby appointed as member of the specialty section of Orthopedic Surgery vice Dr. Rogelio B. Fable.
	The composition of the specialty section of Orthopaedic Surgery will thus read as follows:

	Section Chairman – Dr. Jose M. Pujalte
	Members	-Dr. Sergio Pineda
			-Dr. Rimando C. Saguin
			* Chairman of Specialty Board or his representatives

	The above-mentioned officials of the Ministry of Health, in addition to their regular duties, shall help assess capabilities of designated Teaching and Training hospitals under the Ministry of Health, participate in the Accreditation of Residency Training Program and submit recommendation to the Minister of Health. They shall conduct qualifying examinations as scheduled by this office and report the result thereof.
	The specialty section shall hold office for a period of three years with no prejudice to reappointment. The invited members from the Specialty Board or his representative may be changed yearly, depending on the respective organization concerned.
	As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 16-C s. 1981 stands in effect.
