<subject>SUBJECT: UTILIZATION OF FULL TIME MEDICAL SPECIALIST ITEM FOR APPOINTMENT OF A PART TIME MEDICAL SPECIALIST</subject>

<body>
The Ministry of Health is aware that the number of qualified Medical Specialist in Ministry of Health Hospitals is insufficient because of lack of takers of vacant Full Time Medical Specialist item. While there are available qualified Medical Specialist practicing in the locality of government hospitals who are interested to extend their expertise to government hospitals, they hesitate to apply to the position because of the requirement of servicing full time. To attract therefore these qualified Medical Specialist into the government service, the Chief of Hospitals and Regional Health Directors are hereby authorized to recommend their appointment as part time Medical Specialist utilizing a full time Medical Specialist item in the hospital plantilla.

This Order, being necessary in the interest of the service is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
</body>
