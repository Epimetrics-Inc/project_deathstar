<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

TO: All Chiefs of Teaching and Training Hospitals under the Ministry of Health

<subject>SUBJECT: Attendance to a meeting with Medical Manpower Development Committee of the Ministry of Health</subject>

<body>
All Chiefs of Teaching and Training Hospitals under the Ministry of Health are hereby directed to attend the meeting with the Medical Manpower Development Committee to discuss the accreditation of their respective residency program. The meeting will be held in Manila on November 10 & 11, 1980 during the Annual Convention of the Government Hospital Association of the Philippines. All participants shall bring with them for submission signed official copies of the following:

l. Accomplished form for accreditation of residency program for each department to be accredited. Attached Form No. l.

2. Listing as of October, 1980, by departments, requested data on trainers i.e. Medical Specialist/Consultants, official title, plantilla number, MOH/Specialty Board certification, date of appointment. Attached Form No. 2.

3. Listing of all available resident items by departments with date of appointment of the presently occupied positions.

4. List of training residents by departments in their last year of residency and their date of completion.

This Order, being made for the interest of public service is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
</body>
