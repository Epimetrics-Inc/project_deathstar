<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines on the application of Five (5) days mandatory forced leave.</subject>


<body>

Rule XVI, Section 25 of the Civil Service Law and Rules provides that, “all officiais and employees with ten (10) days or more vacation ieave credits shall be required to go on vacation iea ve whether continuous or intermittent for a minimum of five (5) working days annually”. This provisions is set under the following conditions:

(a) The head of agency shall, upon,_prior consultation with the employees, prepare a staggered schedule of the mandatory annual five-day vacation leave of officials and employees provided that he may in the exigency of the service cancel any previously scheduled leave

(b) The mandatory annual five-day vacation leave shall be forfeited if not taken during the calendar year. However, in cases where the scheduled leave has been cancelled in the exigency of the service by the head of agency, the scheduled leave not enjoyed shall no longer be deducted from the total accumulated vacation leave

(c) Retirement or resignation from the service in a particular year without completing the calendar year does not warrant forfeiture of the corresponding leave credits if concerned employees opted not to avail of the required five-day mandatory vacation leave.

(d) Those with accumulated vacation leave of less than (10) days shall have the option to go on forced leave or not. However, officials and employees with accumulated vacation leave of fifteen (15) days who availed of monetization for 10 days under Section 22 hereof, shall still be required to go on forced leave.

In order to effectively implement these provisions without disrupting Department of Health (DOH) Operations, all Directors, Chief of Hospitals and other heads of offices under the DOH shall ensure:

1 the preparation of the schedule of 5-day forced/mandatory leaves for all employees by their respective Administrative Officers/designated staff:

2. the submission of this schedule to respective Personnel Section/Division by April 15 for this year. However, for succeeding years submission of the scheduled 5—day forced/mandatory leave shall be 15 days before the end of the year;

3. that filing of application for forced leave by the employees shall always be based on this schedule;

4. that application for forced leave ..shall be filed in. advance or whenever possible, five (5) days before the effective date of the leave;

5. scheduled leaves shall be cancelled by the head of office in the exigency of the service;

6. that forced leaves scheduled for the month of December can no longer be cancelled;

7. that in cases of approved cancellation, the Personnel Section/Division be notified within 2 weeks after the desired scheduled leave. Failure to do so shall be interpreted by the Personnel Section/Division as availment by the employee of the scheduled leave;

8. that cancellation of scheduled forced leave shall be allowed only up to (3) three times.

All other issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed/rescinded.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
