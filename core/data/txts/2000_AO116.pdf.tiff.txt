<subject>SUBJECT: Amending A.O. No. 77 s. 2000 dated 6 July 2000, “Requiring All Physicians, Dentists and Veterinarians to Issue Ordinary Prescriptions for Prescription Drugs in Duplicate”</subject>

<body>

In view of the problems encountered in the implementation of the provision of A.O. No.77 s.2000 requiring the issuance of duplicate copies of ordinary prescription forms, said provision is hereby deleted.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
