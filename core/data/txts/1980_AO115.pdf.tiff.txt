<subject>SUBJECT: Amending the date of effectivity of merit increases in the Ministry of Health to July 1, 1980</subject>

<body>
In order that deserving officers and employees in the Ministry of Health may avail of the benefits of the selective merit increases authorized under Budget Circular No. 286 and CSC Memorandum Circular No. 3 s. 1978, the full year rating period described in Administrative Order No. 95-A dated July 22, 1980, shall cover the two grading semesters ending June 30, 1980.

Accordingly, merit increases to deserving officials and employees covered by the OCPC pay plans in the Ministry of Health shall take effect July 1, 1980 instead of January 1, 1980.

As thus modified, Ministry Order No. 95-A stands in effect.
</body>
