<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

TO: All Bureau Directors, Project Directors, Chiefs of Offices/Services/Special Hospitals under the Office of the Minister, the Technical Staff, Unit Heads and others concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Integration of all Security Services of the different Bureaus, Projects, Offices, Units and Programs in San Lazaro Compound and all Special Hospitals in Metro Manila and the same to be placed under the functional direction and control of the Civil Security Office.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to Article VI of Presidential Decree No. 51 dated November 16, 1972 and Letter of Implementation No. 52-B dated January 17, 1973, re-establishment of Security Units that shall provide for the security of personnel, installations, classified documents and materials, etc., it is hereby directed that the security services within the San Lazaro Compound and all Special Hospitals in Metro Manila shall be placed under the functional direction and control of the newly activated Civil Security Office of this Ministry. This integration will look into the central planning, coordination, and implementation necessary for the effective and efficient supervision of all security and intelligence activities as provided for under the aforementioned decree and implementing order.

Accordingly, all Bureau Directors, Project Directors, Chiefs of Offices/Services and the Chiefs of the San Lazaro Hospital, Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital and Medical Center, Dr. Jose Fabella Memorial Hospital, National Orthopedic Hospital, National Mental Hospital and National Children's Hospital, are requested to turn over the security personnel, equipments and records under their respective offices to the Chief, Civil Security Office, this Ministry, who shall henceforth exercise immediate supervision and control over them.

All orders, regulations or memoranda which are inconsistent herewith are hereby superseded or modified accordingly.
</body>
