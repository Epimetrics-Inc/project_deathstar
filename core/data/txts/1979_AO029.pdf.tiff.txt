<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to sign Noticec of Salary Increase under the National Budget Circular No. 305 dated April 30, 1979.</subject>

<body>
In order to effect the early release of the notices for the salary increase authorized under National Budget Circular No. 305 dated April- 30, 1979 of the officials and employees under the Ministry of Health, the following delegation of authority is hereby ordered:

1. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the officials and employees of the Minister, Bureau of Health Services, Bureau of Medical Services, Bureau of Dental Health Services, Health Education and Training, Disease Intelligence Center, Regulation and Control of Radiation, National Cancer Control Services, and Dermatology Research and Training, whose salary ranges are 67 and below, shall be signed by the Acting Chief, Administrative Service, and those whose salary ranges are 68 and above, shall be signed by the Deputy Minister of Health;

2. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the officials and employees of the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, and the Bureau of Quarantine, shall be signed by the respective Directors of said Bureaus;

3. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the officials and employees of the Malaria Eradication Service, Nutrition Service, and the Family Planning Office, shall be signed by the respective Directors of said Projects;

4. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the officials and employees of the Food and Drug Administration, Schistosomiasis Control and Research, and the Schistosomiasis Control Council, shall be signed by the Administrator and Executive Directors, respectively, of the said Offices;

5. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the officials and employees under Regional Health Offices, shall be signed by the Regional Health Director/Acting Regional Health Director concerned. In the absence of the Regional Health Director/Acting Regional Health Director, the Administrative Officer III of the Region concerned shall sign the notice;

6. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for officials and employees in the National Training Hospitals operating under the immediate supervision and control of the Minister of Health, shall be signed by the Chief of the Training Hospital concerned, while those of the Chiefs of the aforecited Hospitals, shall be signed by the Minister of Health; and

7. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for the Directors of the National Cancer Control Services, Dermatology Research and Training and the Regulation and Control of Radiation, shall be signed by the Minister of Health.

The Notices of Salary Increase shall invariably carry the words "By authority of the Minister of Health" which shall appear over the signature of the signing official.

It is understood that the basis for computing the salary increase/adjustment shall be the actual salary of the official or employee concerned as of April 30, 1979.

Sufficient copies of the Notice shall be prepared, one copy to be retained by the Office or Agency concerned, one copy each to be furnished the Accounting Division and the Personnel Division, this Ministry, one copy for the Budget Commission, one copy for the Civil Service Commission, one copy for the Commission on Audit, one copy for the GSIS, and one copy for the OCPC.

This delegated authority shall be effected and good only for the Calendar Year 1979.
