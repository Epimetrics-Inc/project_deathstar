<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of Department of Health National Infectious Disease Advisory Council (NlDAC)</subject>


<body>

In the interest of service and pursuant to the memorandum of Understanding between the Philippine Department of Health (DOH), US. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention(CDC), and New Tropical Medicine Foundation, Inc, (NTMFI) dated December 14,1999 regarding the Infectious Disease Component of the USAID grant for Strategic Objective: The Threat of HIV/AIDS and other selected Infectious Diseases Reduced, the National Infectious Disease Advisory Council (NIDAC) of the Department of Health is hereby created.

The NIDAC is a national, multisectoral body with authority and responsibility to recommend, operational strategies and policies to improve implementation of infectious disease prevention and control programs at the primary, secondary and tertiary levels of the health system.

The NIDAC will closely coordinate with the Head of the Operation Cluster who will ensure that all policy decisions and activities of the National Infectious Disease Advisory Council are consistent with the policies and strategies of the DOH.

The NIDAC functions as implementation problem solver, strategic planner, quality assurance guardian, and advocate at various stages in the operational use and development of policy, standards, and guidelines. The committee may organized/a working committee on a specific infectious disease on a project to project basis. The NIDAC will have freedom and resources to accomplish its work independently and above vested interests of politicians and commercial entities. Speciﬁc functions include:

a. Commission evidence-based review on the implementation of policies, standards, guidelines for speciﬁc infectious diseases

b. Prepare improvement strategies and develop policies to address the current program issues and recommend these to OSEC for ofﬁcial issuance to all concerned

c. Advocate for compliance to policy, standards and guidelines at various levels of the health system

d. Provide technical advise to all stakeholders involved in infectious disease.

e. Provide recommendations regarding ﬁnancing of infectious disease programs

The Department of Health National Infectious Advisory Council shall meet every third Friday of the month or as necessary. Representatives from BIHC, CDC, NTMFI, USAID, and project staff will be invited to participate in NlDAC meetings. All expenses incurred during the meeting of the NIDAC shall be charged to IDSCP budget until September 30, 200 . Thereafter, the National Center for Disease Prevention and Control shall shoulder all related costs.
