<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 345 s. 1978</docnum>

TO: The Chief, Administative Service, the Chief, OHEPT, and the Chief, Information Division.

<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of the Library from the Information Division to the Office of Health Education and Personnel Training.</subject>

<body>
Effective today the Library is transferred from the Information Division to the Office of Health Education and Personnel Training.

The Librarian shall continue her work in the Library. Steps will be taken to officially transfer the position of Librarian to OHEPT. In the meantime all the supplies and materials needed by the Library shall be furnished by OHEPT. The Chief, OHEPT, shall have supervision and control over the Librarian and shall be responsible for the improvement of the Library.
</body>
