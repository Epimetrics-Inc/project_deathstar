<subject>SUBJECT: RESTRICTED USE OF BORIC ACID (BORACIC ACID) AND SODIUM BORATE (BORAX)</subject>

<body>
In the interest of consumer protection against the toxic effect of boric acid and sodium borate, the following regulation is hereby promulgated:

1. Sale of borax, teething preparation, cosmetic products (such as talcum and dusting powder) and other preparations intended for infants and children under 3 yrs. of age containing boric acid or sodium borate is hereby prohibited.

2. All products containing boric acid or sodium borate intended for external use by adults shall bear in bold and conspicuous manner the warning: "FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY. DO NOT APPLY TO BROKEN OR RAW SKIN, OR LARGE AREA OF THE BODY".

3. The use of Boric Acid or Borax in products intended for oral use or consumption is likewise prohibited.

4. Products not in accordance with this regulation shall be deemed adulterated and/or misbranded within the meaning of the provisions of the Food, Drug & Cosmetic Act.

This regulation shall take effect 30 days after publication in the Official Gazette.
