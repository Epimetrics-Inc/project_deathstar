<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81, 88, 94, 96-A and 100, all series of 1980: Re-authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
This order is being issued to supplement Administrative Orders No. 81, 88, 94, 96-A and 100, all series of 1980, fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be maintained in stock by hospitals at any time including undelivered purchases to include the hospitals named hereunder.

The ceiling hereby fixed are based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines.
