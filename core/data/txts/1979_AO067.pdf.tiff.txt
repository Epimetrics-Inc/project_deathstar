<subject>SUBJECT: Policies, rules and regulations governing the transfer of supervision and control of Schistosomiasis Control activities in Regions IX & XII to the Regional Health Offices concerned.</subject>

<body>
WHEREAS, on March 1977, Proclamation No. 1628 was issued declaring autonomy in Southern Philippines;

WHEREAS, on 25 July 1979, Presidential Decree No. 1618 was issued implementing the organization of the Autonomous Regions;

WHEREAS, the establishment of the Autonomous Region will enhance the attainment of peace and order, the acceleration of socio-economic development and the resettlement of displaced persons and families in Regions IX and XII;

WHEREAS, consistent with the provisions of autonomy, it would be desirable to effect the turnover of responsibility for undertaking development activities, particularly at the community level to the Autonomous Region; and

WHEREAS, it is likewise consonant with the concept of autonomy to grant such powers and authority to the Autonomous Regions as would enable them to adopt and implement regional policies and legislation which are appropriate to their particular needs and social and cultural values.

NOW THEREFORE, pursuant to section 79 (B) of the Revised Administrative Code, the following policies, rules and regulations governing the transfer of supervision and control of Schistosomiasis Control activities in Regions IX and XII to the respective Regional Health Offices concerned are hereby prescribed;

Section I. The Schistosomiasis Control units operating in Region IX and XII under the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service are hereby placed under the Regional Health Offices concerned except those which are engaged in the implementation of foreign—assisted projects pursuant to Section 41 of P.D. 1618.

Section II. All records, equipment, supplies and materials belonging to the Schistosomiasis Control units assigned in Region IX and XII shall remain under their responsibility upon completion of the turn—over of properties as provided for under the provision of Section 645 0f the Revised Administrative Code, as amended.

Section III. The Executive Director of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service shall extend full cooperation and assistance to the personnel of the Regional Health Offices concerned who will be assigned to take charge and exercise direct and immediate supervision and control over the Schistosomiasis Control acitivities in the different provinces, cities and municipalities comprised within the geographical area of their respective field of operations.

SEction IV. The Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service shall continue to <missingtext> </missingtext> that salaries of locally appointed personnel to be assigned to the aforesaid units shall be borne by the concerned Regional Health Offices and Provided further, that personnel salaries and operational expenses of any additional units that maybe created by the Regional Health Offices shall be paid out of funds from the Regional Health Offices concerned.
 
Section V. The Regional Directors concerned shall periodically submit to the Executive Director of the SCRS financial reports on the amount that will be sub—allotted to the Region as required by existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

Section VI. The Executive Director and his staff in the Control Office shall continue exercising technical supervision and inspection over the Schistosomiasis Control Units concerned. Likewise, the above Units shall continue submitting copies of periodic reports to SCRS on the progress of the campaign.

Section VII. This Administrative Order shall take effect on 1 January 1980.

Section VIII. All provisions, orders inconsistent herewith are hereby modified accordingly.
