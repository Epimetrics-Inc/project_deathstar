<subject> Subject: Authority by the Undersecretary, Acting as Chief of Staff; To Issue Implementing Instructions In Furtherance of Policies and Programs of the Department of Health</subject>





<body>

In order to improve further the pace and direction of implementation, the Undersecretary of Health, serving as Chief of Staff of the Office of the Secretary, is hereby authorized, in addition to prior delegations, to issue implementing instructions to regional health offices and department field units, in furtherance of policies and programs of the Department of Health.

       Implementing instructions shall be in the form of numbered memoranda citing the decisions of the Secretary or the Executive Committee For Field Operations from which such instructions arose. Such issuances shall be deemed official and. equivalent; to 1department orders.

       The Undersecretary and Chief of Staff shall sign authority of the Secretary of Health or the Executive Committee for Field Operations as the case may be.

This order takes effect immediately.
