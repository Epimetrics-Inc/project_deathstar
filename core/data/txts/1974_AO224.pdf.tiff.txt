<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 224, s. 1974</docnum>

TO: Chiefs of Training Hospitals, Regional Training Centers, Regional Training Laboratories, and others concerned

<subject>SUBJECT: Amending Administrative Order No. 210, s. 1974, regarding student affiliation.</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 210, series of 1974, which amended previous Administrative Orders relative to the guidelines for the implementation of student affiliation with Training Hospitals, Regional Training Centers, Regional Training Laboratories, and other agencies under the Department of Health, is hereby further amended as follows:

Physicians including part-time consultants, nurses, pharmacists, medical technologists, administrative officer, cashier, clerk, auditor, medical records librarian, and accountant are hereby authorized to receive compensation at rates to be fixed by the Secretary of Health upon recommendation of the Chiefs of Hospitals and/or heads of agencies concerned in accordance with standards prescribed by the Department.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
