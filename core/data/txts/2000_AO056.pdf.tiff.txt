<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of the “Doctors to the Barrios" (DTTB) Program from the Community Health Service to the Health Manpower Development and Training Service.</subject>

<body>

To ensure a more comprehensive and integrated approach in ensuring the adequate supply and equitable distribution of health human resource in the country, the “Doctors to the Barrios” (DTTB) Program shall be transferred from the Community Health Services (CHS) to the Health Manpower Development and Training Service (HMDTS). The management of the DTTB by HMDTS shall commence by January 2001. While in transition however, coordinating activities between CHS and HMDTS shall take place immediately to complete the transfer.

This Order shall take effect upon approval.
