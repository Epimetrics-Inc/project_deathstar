TO: All Regional Directors, Regional FP Medical and Nursing Coordinators, Staff members of the Family Planning Unit in the Regional Health Offices, and others concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Prescribing procedures in the signing and countersigning of FP checks and approval of payrolls and vouchers for payment of incentives of Lay Motivators, representation and/or transportation allowances of field personnel, and other expenses and disbursements chargeable from the Family Planning Project Funds.</subject>

<body>
In line with the decentralization plans of the National Family Planning Office, this Department, primarily aimed at the expeditious processing and payment of claims for representation and/or transportation allowances of field personnel, incentive pay of Lay Motivators and other authorized expenses incurred by the Regional and Provincial Health Offices under the former, chargeable against the funds of the National Family Planning Office, this Department, the following procedures should be strictly followed:

(1) Checks of ₱200.00 and below in value should be signed by the FP Disbursing Officer, who may either be a regular appointee or a designee, and countersigned by the FP Field Representative (Medical Specialist);

(2) Checks over ₱200.00, but not more than ₱500.00 should be signed by the FP Field Representative (Medical Specialist I), and countersigned by the Regional Director, or, by the FP Medical Coordinator of the Region, in the former's absence or if assigned or delegated to regularly perform such task for and in behalf of the Regional Directors;

(3) All checks above ₱500.00 should be signed by the Regional Director, or, in his absence, by the FP Field Representative (Medical Specialist I), and countersigned by the Region Auditor (upon prior representation with the letter official), or his duly authorized representative.

All personal vouchers and payrolls of <missingtext> </missingtext> other disbursement <missingtext> </missingtext> by the Administrative Assistant (Certificate <missingtext> </missingtext> and in behalf of Representative Officer the FP <missingtext> </missingtext> Representative (Medical Specialist I) in (Certificate <missingtext> </missingtext> or and in behalf of the Director of the National Family <missingtext> </missingtext> Office.

Regional Health Offices concerned are further informed that the following <missingtext> </missingtext> the regular members of the staff of the <missingtext> </missingtext> Unit in the Regions:

(1) One (1) FP Field Representative (Med. Specialist I);

(2) One (1) Administrative Assistant I;

(3) One (1) Accountant I;

(4) One (1) Disbursing Officer; (only five (5) positions are available which will be distributed to five selected Regional Health Offices)

(5) One (1) Accounting Clerk II; and

(6) One (1) Clerk I.

At least two (2) copies of all orders and/or issuances made by the Regional Health Offices resulting here from or in compliance with these directives should be furnished the Director of the National Family Planning Office in Manila, for information and record purposes of the letter office.

Strict compliance to the above by all concerned is hereby enjoined.
