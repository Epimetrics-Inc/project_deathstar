<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to sign notices of salary adjustments to Chiefs of Offices and Special Hospitals, Line Bureau Directors and Regional Health Directors</subject>

<body>
In order to facilitate the grant of merit increases to personnel in the regional health offices, line bureaus and special hospitals, the heads of offices concerned are hereby delegated the authority to sign the notice of merit increase subject to the following conditions:

1. That the list of outstanding and deserving employees of each bureau, office, project service, special hospital or regional health office shall first be submitted to the Minister for his approval. The list shall specify the nature of outstanding service performed by the particular employee in the first (outstanding) category.

2. That no employee with a pending administrative case shall be recommended for merit increases.

3. That funds allocated to the particular office recommending the merit increases are sufficient to cover the number of personnel involved.

4. That the corresponding salary increase will be duly reflected in the basic salaries of the personnel concerned for the next calendar year.
