<subject> Subject: AMENDMENT OF ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 68-A, S. 1989 RE: REVISED RULES AND REGULATIONS GOVERNING THE REGISTRATION, LICENSURE AND OPERATION OF HOSPITALS IN THE PHILIPPINES.</subject>

<body>Pursuant to Section 15 of R. A No. 4226 and consistent with E.O. 119, Sections 8.2, 15.2, 17.2, 20 and 21.2 of DOH – Administrative Order No. 68-A, s. 1989 are hereby amended in order to strengthen and improve efficiency of the BLR in issuances of hospital licenses

Section 1	Any applicant hospital found not complying with the technical requirements for hospital licensure of the category appl1ed for shall be issued a license appropriate to what the hospital has complied with. Provisional license shall no longer be issued.

Example: A secondary category hospital applying for a license to operate can only be issued such a license if all the technical requirements have not been complied with. Non~comp1iance with all the technical requ1rements shall downgrade the category of the hospital to primary.

Section 2	A non-refundable license fee shall be charged for an application for renewal of license to operate a hospital during the first three (3) months before the expiry date of the hospital license.

Section 3	Each specific license shall be effective for one year end1ng on the date specif1ed in the license

Section 4	Application for renewal of license shall he filed within three (3) months before expiry date of the hospital license. Only complete applications with supporting documents shall be accepted.
   

Section 5  This order amends Administrative Order No. 68-A, s. 1989 and supersedes  previous issuances that are inconsistent herewith.
