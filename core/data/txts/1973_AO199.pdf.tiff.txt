<subject>SUBJECT : Delegation of authority in the Regional Health Office No. 10, Cagayan de Oro City.</subject>

<body>
To facilitate action on administrative matters in the Regional Health Office No. 10, Cagayan de Oro City, the following officials of the said Office are hereby delegated to exercise the authority and its corresponding responsibility as indicated for them hereunder and for which they shall be held responsible:

1. Mr. MOISES R. PERALTA, Personnel Officer II, MES

(a) To approve disbursement involving amounts not exceeding ₱2,000.00 in a single disbursement;

(b) To sign treasury checks in the amount for which he is authorized to approved disbursements;

(c) To sign request for obligations of allotments in the amount for which he is authorized to approve disbursement; and

(d) To approve salary vouchers and inter-government transactions as well as treasury checks corresponding thereto without limit.

2. Mr. ROSENDO P. AQUINO, Budget Officer II

(a) To approve disbursements involving amounts not exceeding ₱500.00 in a single disbursement;

(b) To sign treasury checks in the amount for which he is authorized to approve disbursement;

(c) To sign request for obligations of allotments in the amount for which he is authorized to approve disbursement; and

(d) To approve salary vouchers and inter-government transactions as well as treasury checks corresponding thereto without limit.
</body>
