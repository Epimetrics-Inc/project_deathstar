<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of a team to work with a counterpart Budget Commission team on the Government Equipment Inventory Project.</subject>

<body>
In compliance with the request of the Acting Commissioner of the Budget, a team composed of the following employees is hereby created in the Office of the Secretary, to work with a counterpart team of the Budget Commission on the Government Equipment Inventory Project.

Amador Esleta - Chairman
Michael Lopez - Member
Napoleon Tecson - Member
Sisenando Dimaliuat - Member
Melanio Tuazon - Member
Florante Anastacio - Member

The team shall render periodic progress report to the Office of the Secretary through the Chief, Financial and Management Service. It is authorized to avail of the assistance of any official or employee of the Department in the pursuit of the project.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby confirmed and made of record.
</body>
