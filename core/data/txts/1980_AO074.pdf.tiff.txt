<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of a Construction Unit to take charge of the construction activities in the Ministry of Health.</subject>

<body>
Effective immediately and for proper coordination and implementation of construction activities in the Ministry of Health, a Construction Unit is hereby created.

Engr. DELFIN R. GONZALEZ, in addition to his regular duties as Chief, Division of Environmental Sanitation Service, Bureau of Health Services, is hereby designated as Chief of the newly organized Construction Unit and shall perform the following functions:

1. Implement the construction component of the Second Population Project;
2. Assume the responsibility for pending construction programs of Population Project I under the Project Management Staff (PMS);
3. Exercise direct supervision of technical staff and other personnel of the Construction Division of PMS who have been retained to complete construction backlog of the Project;
4. Coordinate with different units/offices of the MOH and other agencies in connection with all construction activities;
5. Submit reports of Construction Unit activities to the Part "A" and Part "B" Project Coordinators of Population Project II, to the Acting Executive Director of Population Project I and other officials concerned in construction activities of the MOH.

The operations of the Construction Unit will be funded under the budget for Population Project II until such time that the Unit is fully integrated into organizational structure of the Ministry of Health.

Pending approval of the proposed positions of technical staff in the Special Budget for the Construction Unit under Population Project II, Engr. Gonzalez shall be assisted in the implementation of the various functions enumerated above by Engr. Pablo Imperio, Engr. Clemente Furagganan and Engr. William Illustre in addition to their regular duties in the Division of Environmental Sanitation Division.
