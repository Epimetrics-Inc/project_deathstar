I. RATIONALE

In view of the implementation of Executive Order 40, New Government Accounting System and the DOH procurement and financial management reforms, Section C in pages 8 and 9 of the Administrative Order No. 154 dated 16 November 2000 on the "Revised Guidelines for the Delegation of Certain Administrative Transactions in the Central Office, DOH" is hereby amended.

III. GENERAL PROVISIONS

1. The Officials and Personnel who are functionally identified and described in the foregoing are required to affix their Signature/initial on the original copy of the documents so mentioned and on the. corresponding replicate copies to assure fidelity, accountability, the exercise of due diligence and completed staff work.

2. The Chairperson of the COBAC shall be authorized to issue COBAC orders in assigning the Chairperson and Members from among the pool of experts identified as Technical Working Group for Eligibility and Evaluation per specific transaction to be evaluated.

3. The Chairperson of the COBAC shall likewise be authorized to sign Department Orders, below the phrase "By Authority of the Secretary of Health" to compel Officials and key Personnel of the DOH not explicitly mentioned in previous germane Orders, to render their expertise and opinion which are deemed vital to the operations and decisions of the Committee.

4. All decisions of the COBAC shall be acted upon collegially and must be signed by at least three (3) members, including the Chairperson.

IV. FINAL PROVISIONS

a. Repealing Clause - All issuance inconsistent with this Order are hereby rescinded or accordingly modified.

b. Separability - If any provision in this Order, or application of such provision to any circumstance, is held invalid, the remainder of this order shall not be affected thereby.

c. Effectivity - This Order shall be effective November 15, 2002.
