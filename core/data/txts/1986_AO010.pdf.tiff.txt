<subject>
SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by DENNIS MOLINTAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Daclan Bokod, Benguet.</subject>


<body>
In consonance with Batas Pambansa Big. 879 Section 23 Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, the DENNIS MOLINTAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Daclan Bokod Benguet is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Twenty Thousand Five Hundred Pesos (21,500.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses;

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321; authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed.

The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached request.
