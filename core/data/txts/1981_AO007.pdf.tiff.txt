<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to Regional Health Directors and Officers in Charge of Regional Health Offices designated by the Minister of Health to approve the dropping from the rolls of the service of such officials and employees appointed by him for cause as defined by Civil Service regulations</subject>

<body>
TO: The Regional Health Directors, Provincial and City Health Officers and Chiefs of Hospitals'

In order to expedite the transaction of official business at the regional level, and pursuant to the provisions of LOI 448 and 895, Regional Health Directors and Officers in Charge of Regional Health Offices duly designated by the Minister of Health are hereby delegated the authority to approve the dropping from the rolls of the service of officials and employees appointed by him for cause as defined under pertinent Civil Service regulations and subject to the guidelines spelled out by this Office under previous administrative issuances.

Papers approving the dropping from the rolls of the service of any official or employee shall be returned to the office or field unit concerned through the Regional Office of the Civil Service Commission, for confirmation and appropriate action. Certified true copies of such papers should invariably be attached to the appointment papers of the proposed appointees to the positions concerned, for ready reference and guidance of the CSC Regional Office.
