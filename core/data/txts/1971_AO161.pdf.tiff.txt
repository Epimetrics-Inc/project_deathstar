<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of jurisdictional coverage in the Malaria Operation over the provinces of Misamis Oriental and Bukidnon, including Wao only of Lanao del Sur, from Iligan City Malaria Unit to Cagayan de Oro Unit.</subject>

<body>
Effective January 1, 1972, by virtue of the feasibility and ease with which to undertake the malaria operation within the coverage of Malaria Unit No. 25 in Cagayan de Oro City over the provinces of Misamis Oriental and  Bukidnon, including Wao only of Lanao del Sur, the jurisdiction of Malaria Unit No. 26 in Iligan City over those places is hereby transferred to Cagayan de Oro City Unit.

This transfer is hereby made of record.
</body>
