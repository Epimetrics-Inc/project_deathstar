<subject>SUBJECT: Authorized Signatories of Disbursements Vouchers(DV) of goods procured, delivered and distributed by the central office</subject>


<body>

The following DOH ofﬁcials are hereby given the authority to sign the disbursement vouchers of the following transactions relative to the procurement of goods at the central ofﬁce:

1. Payment to suppliers of goods procured through the Central Ofﬁce Bids and Awards Committee(COBAC) and Procurement Division, and are delivered to and distributed by the Materials Management Division (MMD) regardless of amount and requesting ofﬁce.

<image>table_1.png</image> 

2. Pre-payment of common ofﬁce supplies and equipment, and all other goods to be procured through Procurement Service, Department of Budget and Management regardless of amount and requesting ofﬁce.

<image>table_2.png</image>  

3. Pre-payment of pharmaceutical products, vaccines and other immunobiological products to be procured through the WHO, UNICEF, GDF and other similar international bodies regardless of amount.

<image>table_3.png</image>  

4. Forwarding, brokerage and related services necessary to accept and distribute procured goods by the MMD regardless of amount and requesting ofﬁce

 <image>table_4.png</image>  

All concerned ofﬁces specially the Material Management Division, Procurement Division, and the National Center for Disease Prevention and Control are mandated to undertake complete staff work before processing each transaction. All other issuances inconsistent with this delegation of authority is hereby repealed.

This order takes effect March 12, 2004.
