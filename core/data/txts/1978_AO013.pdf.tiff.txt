TO: Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors and Officers-in-Charge of Regional Health Offices.

<subject>SUBJECT: Implementation of the approved standards classifying hospitals by categories as primary, secondary and tertiary levels of health care, and the new licensing standards.</subject>

<body>
In accordance with the approved standardization and categorization of hospitals, as well as the new licensing procedures, the quality of hospital service will undoubtedly be much improved and health care delivery in the country will become more effective. The implementation, therefore, of these standards as enumerated hereunder has become imperative.

- Standards for Diagnostic and Treatment Capabilities
- Standards for Basic Technical Instruments/Equipments
- Standards Requirements for Physical Facilities by Beds
- Hospital Staffing Patterns by Beds and Teaching anf Training Capabilities
- Methodology of Licensing Hospitals

Accordingly, the required implementation is hereby authorized to take effect on January 1, 1979. Strict and prompt compliance with this Order is enjoined.
</body>
