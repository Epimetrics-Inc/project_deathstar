<body>Item No. 2 of Administrative Order No. 16 dated October
24, 1981, concerning the grant of an additional compensation
of P50.00 a month in lieu of subsistence, quarters and laundry to rural health physicians is hereby amended as follows:

" 2.'The grant of additional compensation of P50.00 a month in lieu of subsistence, quarters and laundry shall not be granted to any rural health physician who will be on leave of absence without pay for a whole month or more.  

       It is further directed that the said privilege be already incorporated in the preparation of appointments of new rural health physicians for facilitative purposes.
