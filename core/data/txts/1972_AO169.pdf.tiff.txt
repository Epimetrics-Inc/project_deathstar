<subject>SUBJECT : Delegating authority to the Project Director of the National Comprehensive MCH-Family Planning Project Office to discharge the administrative functions indicated hereunder</subject>

<body>
Effective immediately and in the interest of punlic service, the authority to discharge the following administrative functions is hereby delegated to the Project Director of the National Comprehensive MCH-Family Planning Project Office. This delegated authority is limited and will apply only to transactions of the said Project Office.

SUPPLY MATTERS

1. Matters relative to negotiated contracts for services or for furnishing supplies, materials and equipment involving an amount of ₱30,000.00 or less; and

2. Contracts requiring payment for services not yet rendered and/or for supplies and materials not yet delivered where the contract amount is ₱30,000.00 or less.

All orders or parts thereof previously issued by this Office inconsistent herewith are hereby modified, amended or rescinded, as the case may be.
