<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to sign Notice of Salary Adjustment under Budget Circular No. 240 dated July 22, 1974.</subject>

<body>
In order to effect the early release of the Notices for the 10% salary increase authorized under Budget Circular No. 240 dated July 22, 1974, of all officials and employees under the Department of Health, the following delegation of authority is hereby ordered:
  
1. The "Notice of Adjustment" for those from Chiefs of Divisions and up under the Bureau of Health Services, the Bureau of Medical Services, the Bureau of Disease Control, the Food & Drug Administration, and the Offices (Proper) of the Undersecretary for Health and medical Services and for Special Health. Services, shall be signed by the Secretary of Health; while those from below the aforementioned Chiefs of Divisions shall be signed by the Chief, Administrative Service;

2. The "Notice of Adjustment" for the officials and employees of the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, the Bureau of Quarantine and the Bureau of Dental Health Services, shall be signed by the respective Directors of the said bureaus;

3. The "Notice of Adjustment" for the officials and employees of the Office of the Secretary of Health (Proper); those in the various units under the Office of Administrative Service; those under the Office of Health Education and Personnel Training; those under the Disease Intelligence Center; those under Specia1 Health Projects not operating under any Office, Bureau or Regional Health Office, shall be signed by the Chief, Administrative Service;
 
4. The "Notice of Adjustment" for the officials and employees under the Malaria Eradication Service, shall be signed by the Project Director;

5. The "Notice of Adjustment" for the officials and employees under the Regional Health Offices shall be signed by the Regional Health Director concerned. In the absence of the Regional Director, the Administrative Officer of the Region concerned shall sign the Notice;

6. The "Notice of Adjustment" for officials and employees in the Training Hospitals operating under the immediate supervision and control of the Secretary of Health, shall be signed by the Chief of the Training Hospital concerned.

In authorizing the payment of additional salary adjustment the provisions of Budget Circular No. 240 dated July 22, 1974, copy attached, as well as the provisions of the Civil Service Law and rules, shall be strictly observed and complied with.

It is understood that the basis of computation for the 10% adjustment is the basic salary of the official or employee concerned as of June 30, 1974, and that it is likewise understood that NO adjustment of salary was made effective July 1, 1974 unless the same was effected by the issuance of a regular appointment.

Sufficient copies of the Notice shall be prepared, one copy to be retained by the Office or Agency concerned; one copy to be furnished the Accounting Division, this Department, one copy the Personnel and Records Division, this Department, one copy for the Budget Commission, one copy for the Civils Service Commission, one copy for the Commission on Audit, one copy for the Government Service Insurance System and one copy for the WAPCO.

Sample copies of the "Notice of Adjustment" are hereto attached for ready reference.

This delegated authority shall be effected and good only for the fiscal year 1974-75.
</body>
