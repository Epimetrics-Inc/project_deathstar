SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by DO SALVADOR BENEDICTO

MEMORIAL OSPITAL, La .Carlota City.</subject>

<body>
In consonance with Betas Pambansa 819.879 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, the DON SALVADOR BENEDICTO MEMORIAL HOSP TAL, La Carlota City, Negros Occidental, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30,000. 00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in= stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Off1ce of Budget and Management National Budget Circular No. W321 authorizing ceiling: for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed. 

	The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached request.
