<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing previous Administrative Orders issued regarding authorized selling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>This order is being issued to include Wao District Hospital, Wao, Lanao del Sur, 1n the amount of P13,000. 00 among the hospitals listed in Administrative Order No. 81, s. 1980, and the supplements thereto, implementing Section 9, Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa Blg. 40, (Section 3 of B.P. 230), which fixes the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria at any given time including undelivered purchases.

 	The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption- or drugs and medicines
