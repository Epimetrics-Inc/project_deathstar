<subject>SUBJECT : Cause for Suspension or Revocation of Permit or License Issued By The Food and Drug Administration to Operate a Drug Establishment.</subject>

<body>
1. Republic Act 3720, otherwise known as Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act states among other things that the policy of the State is to regulate the manufacture, sale and traffic of drugs to protect the health of the people. For the implementation of the above policy with respect to dangerous drug and under authority of Section 26 (a) of the same Act, this regulation is hereby promulgated.

2. Violation of any Dangerous Drug Board Regulations pertaining to the sale and traffic of dangerous drug by any person who owns or operates, or is employed in, a retail drugstore or a drug establishment is sufficient cause for the Secretary, upon recommendation of the Food and Drug Administrator, to suspend or revoke his permit or license to operate issued by the Food and Drug Administration.

3. This regulation shall take effect upon approval.
</body>
