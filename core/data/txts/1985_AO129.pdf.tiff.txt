SUBJECT : Banning and withdrawal of FLOSINT TABLETS (INDOPROFEN) from the market.</subject>


       <body>
	   While the anti-inflammatory efficacy of Indoprofen which is marketed as FLOSINT has been established through the years, recent preliminary results of long—term studies on the drug led to a further toxicological investigation.

       Contrary to previously established studies, recent researches and further studies conducted in various animal species indicate that prolonged tract of the treated animals. Instances of the presence of tumors were also found. 


ORDERS

1. In the interest of consumer protection, FLOSINT TABLETS (INDOPROFEN) is no longer registrable.

2. Henceforth, the registration of the above—mentioned product is hereby recalled and cancelled.

3. All existing stocks from drugstores, hospitals, etc. should be recalled by the importer/distributor and destroyed in the presence of BFAD Inspectors.

This Order takes effect immediately.
