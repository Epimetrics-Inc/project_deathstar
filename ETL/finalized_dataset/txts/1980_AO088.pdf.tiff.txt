<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81 dated March 26, 1980 Re-authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
This order is being issued to supplement Administrative Order No. 81 dated March 26, 1980 fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be maintained in stock by hospitals at any time including undelivered purchases, to include the hospitals named hereunder.

The ceiling hereby fixed are based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines.
