<subject>SUBJECT: VOLUNTARY WITHDRAWAL OF ASTEMIZOLE (HISMANAL) FROM THE MARKET</subject>

<body>

Astemizole which is marketed as (Hismanal) is a drug used in the treatment of allergic conditions.

The company, which produces the above named drug, has voluntarily withdrawn the product from the Philippine market and in other countries because of its being associated with serious cardiac adverse events when used inappropriately with contraindicated drugs.

In view of this voluntary withdrawal and in the interest of consumer protection, it is hereby ordered that the Bureau of Food and Drugs shall no longer allow the registration of products containing Astemizole. The manufacture, importation, distribution or sale of any drug product containing Astemizole shall be deemed illegal and punishable under R.A. 3720, the “Foods, Drugs, Devices and Cosmetics Act”. 

This Order shall take effect immediately.
