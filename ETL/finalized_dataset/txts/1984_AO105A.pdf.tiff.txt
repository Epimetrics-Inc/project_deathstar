<subject>Subject: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by Isidro C. Kintanar Memorial Hospital, Argao, Cebu.</subject>

 
<body>
       
In consonance with Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa 640, Isidro C. Kintanar Memorial Hospital, Argao, Cebu, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Fifteen
Thousand Pesos (P15,000.00) worth of medicines and drugs in its stock for resale, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

	It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget
Circular No. 32l authorizing cash ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be created shall be followed.



</body>

<body>
This has reference to the request of the Chief Isidro C. Kintanar Memorial Hospital, Argao, Cebu for an authority to have a quarterly ceiling in –text missing- in the amount of P15,000.

Please be informed that the role of the (Office with respect to the subject fund is to monitor and evaluate the status of the operations or funds for drugs and medicine and to act on request for increase in the ceiling previously authorized by the Ministry of Health. Section 3.2.1 of National Budget_Circu1ar No. 321 dated February 6, 1980 vested to the Minister of Health the responsibility to authorize and fix the initial fund ceiling for drugs and medicines. Furthermore, Section 3.2.2 of said circular requires the Minister of Health to advise the Office of Budget and Management of the ceiling established, for control/reference purposes. 
	In view thereof, we are returning this request for your appropriate action.
