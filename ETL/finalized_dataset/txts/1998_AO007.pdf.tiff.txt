<subject>SUBJECT: CONDUCT OF LIPOSUCTION WITHIN LICENSED HOSPITALS OR AMBULATORY SURGICAL CENTERS ACCREDITED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH</subject>

<body>

Liposuction is an invasive surgical procedure for the reduction of fat tissues in the body. This procedure involves pre-operative preparation and post operative care.

There has been a reported case of a patient who died while liposuction was being performed in an out-patient clinic.

To ensure safety of the patient it is hereby ordered that liposuction shall be performed only within a hospital or in an ambulatory surgical center licensed or accredited by the Department of Health. In addition, such hospital or surgical center shall ensure that the necessary pre-operative procedures, including but not limited to cardiopulmonary clearance, shall be performed as part of the liposuction. 

This Order shall take effect immediately.

</body>
