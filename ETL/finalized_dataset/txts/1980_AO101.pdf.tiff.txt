TO: Regional Health Directors, Provincial Health Officers and Municipal Health Officers concerned

<subject>SUBJECT: Survey of Drug Utilization and Needs for Primary Health Care</subject>

<body>
The Office of the Minister procures drugs and medicines for the Rural Health Units based on a List of Essential Drugs for Primary Health Care drawn up by the Therapeutic Committee in August 1979. To evaluate the effectiveness of the use of this list and the distribution system of MOH, a survey shall be made of a representative sample of Rural Health Units. For this purpose, a form has been designed to obtain data on drug utilization and actual need in the field.

The following procedures are hereby established for the survey:

1. Upon recommendation of the Provincial Health Officer, the Regional Director shall select 4â€”5 Rural Health Units in each province that shall be representative of the province concerned.

2. Survey forms shall be sent to the Municipal Health Officers of selected Rural Health Units on or before September 30, 1980.

3. The MHO shall fill up the form in duplicate and submit the original to the Provincial Health Office on or before October 10, 1980.

4. The Provincial Health Office shall collate the forms and submit them to the Regional Health Office on or before October 20, 1980.

5. The Regional Health Office shall submit the survey forms from RHU's in the region to the Office of the Minister on or before October 31, 1980.

6. The data from the survey forms shall be consolidated and analyzed and a report shall be submitted to the Minister on or before November 15, 1980.

For the interest of the service, compliance with this order is hereby enjoined.
</body>
