<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines in Payment of CY 2002 Hazard Pay of Ofﬁcials and Employees of the Department of Health</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to the provision of Sec. 7.1.5 of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of RA 7305 otherwise known as the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers, which prescribes the schedule of implementation of the hazard allowance rate for the period 1999 to 2003, for CY 2002 health workers shall receive 20% of the monthly basic salary.

In line with the provision of the above IRR, this Department has issued the following guidelines for the computation and payment of the current year’s hazard pay of its officials and employees, chargeable against savings of respective ofﬁces/hospitals:

1. All permanent, including contractual and casual employees with Salary Grades 19 and below shall receive a monthly hazard allowance equivalent to 20% of their monthly basic salary.

2. Those with Salary Grades 20 and above shall receive the equivalent of 20% of a preset benchmark amount based on SG—19 Step 8 per month.

3. Part time employees are entitled to one half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full time employees.

4. Consultants are not entitled to this beneﬁt.

This Order shall be implemented effective January 1, 2002.
