<docnum>No. 202 s. 1973</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: BANKING THE USE OF JEQUIRITY BEANS</subject>

<body>
Jequirity beans known locally as Saga-saga (Abrus Precatorius Linn) <missingtext> </missingtext> are <missingtext> </missingtext> in the manufacturer of rosaries, necklaces, fancy bags and other ornamental articles.

These seeds contain an intensely toxic substance and when accidentally ingested can cause serious poisoning, which can be fatal, especially to children.

For the protection of the consumer, the manufacturers and exportation of any article made from these seeds shall be prohibited.

This regulation shall take effect 30 days after publication in the Official Gazette.
</body>
