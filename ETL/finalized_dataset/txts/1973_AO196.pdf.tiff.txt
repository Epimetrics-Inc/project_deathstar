<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to the Chief. Personnel and Records Division and the Chief. Quirino Memorial Hospital.</subject>

<body>
Authority is hereby delegated to the Chief, Personnel and Records Divisi on, Office of the Secretary, to approve, By authority of the Secretary of Health, applications for leave of absences, coming from the different units of the Department of Health, When such application either sick, or vacation, do not exceed sixty (60) days.

Likewise, authority is hereby delegated to the Chief, Quirino Memorial Hospital, to approve applications for leave, either vacation or sick leave, of the hospital employees, when such applications do not exceed sixty (60) days.

The herein delegatees shall sign in the following manner: By authority of the Secretary of Health:
