<subject>Further amending Administrative Order No. 32-B, s. 1982, providing the guidelines to implement the provisions of Batas Pambansa 337, as amended by Administrative Order No. 80, series of 1984</subject>


<body>  

	Paragraph 2 of the guidelines to implement the provisions of Batas Pambansa 337 as embodied in Administrative Order No. 32-B dated June 29, 1982, as amended by the Administrative Order No. 80, dated February 7, 1984, is hereby further amended to read as follows.

	2. Free medical core shall cover the following aspects:
	a. Free medicines subject to its availability in the hospital pharmacy.
	b. Free room and board.
	c. Free diagnostic services as requested by the attending physician depending on the facilities of the hospital.
	d. No professional fee.
	This Order takes effect immediately.
