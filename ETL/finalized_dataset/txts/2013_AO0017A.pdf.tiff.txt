<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to A.O. No.2013-0017 Re: Revised Roles, Functions and Responsibilities of the DOH Representatives (DOH Reps) in Support of National Health Thrusts 
</subject>
<body>

The Department of Health (DOH) issued Administrative Order No. 2013-0017 dated May 9, 2013 to provide guidelines for the revised functions, roles and responsibilities of the DOH Representatives in support of national health thrusts. The section on Implementing Mechanisms, however, need to include the renaming of Provincial Health Team Office (PHTO) and the DOH Representatives in the provinces to reflect the name of the Department, and emphasize the level of responsibility and authority of the Provincial Health Team Leader (PHTL) in their respective area of assignment. 

Thus, Section V. Implementing Mechanisms, under A. Structure will now be read as follows: 

3. Provincial DOH Office — The DOH Office in the province is composed of DOH retained personnel of vertical health programs, city and municipal DOH Representatives, Food and Drug Regulation Officer and other detailed staff from the CHD. The Provincial DOH Office is the official workstation of the DOH Team in the Province. 

4.Provincial Health Team Leader (PHTL), hereby renamed as Provincial DOH Officer — the DOH Representative in the province designated as the team leader and whose function is to oversee the day to day operations of the Provincial DOH Office. 

All other provisions of AO. 2013-0017 shall remain in effect and provisions/issuances inconsistent or contrary to this Order are hereby rescinded or modified accordingly. 

This Order shall take effect immediately.
