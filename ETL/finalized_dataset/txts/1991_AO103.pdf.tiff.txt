<subject>SUBJECT : Additional delegation of authority for the Director. Research Institute for Tropical Medicine, to approve regular leave applications of RITM personnel</subject>


<body>
In view of the request of the Director IV, Research Institute for Tropical medicine, in her letter than February 22, 1991, authority to approve ordinary vacation and sick leave applications as well as those involving one (1) day or more but not exceeding 60 days is hereby delegated to the Director concerned;

This Order shall take effect immediately.
