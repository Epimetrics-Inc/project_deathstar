<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 71, s. 1980, relative to Preparation of Payrolls by the Personnel Division, Administrative Service.</subject>

<body>
To expedite processing of payrolls, paragraph 2 of Administrative Order No. 71, s. 1980 is hereby amended to read as follows:

"Payrolls prepared shall be sent to the Chief, Administrative Service for certification of paragraph 1 thereof which shall then be forwarded to the Financial and Management Service for approval by authorized officials."

Immediate implementation of the provision of the above-mentioned Administrative Order is hereby directed.

This Order being issued in the interest of the service, is made of record.
</body>
