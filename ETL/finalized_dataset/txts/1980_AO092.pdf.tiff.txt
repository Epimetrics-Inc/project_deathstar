<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 60 s. l979</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 60 s. 1979 specifically Part VI No. 1 is hereby amended as follows:

1. Residents of accredited departments of Teaching and Training Hospitals who willfully refuse to go on Community Service or fail to render post-residency service or those who resign from his residency training during the second half of training without very strong reasons beyond his control shall be asked to reimburse the amount spent for his training including salaries received after the lst half of residency. The 3rd and 4th year of a 4-year residency program and the 3rd year of a 3—year residency program and 2nd year of a 2—year residency program shall be covered by a contract with stipulation that he will reimburse the amount spent for his training.

The Contract of Agreement executed by all Chiefs of Teaching and Training Hospitals shall be one of the documents of appointment.

As amended, Administrative Order No. 60 s. 1979 stands in effect.

This Order, being necessary in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
