2. MAJOR FUNCTION/S
	Prepare and manage he Medical Specialist Qualifying Examination scheduled on March 28, 1987. Review the Medical manpower Development programs and project of the Department of Health.

3. EXPENSES
       The above-mentioned officials of the Department of Health shall be entitled to collect transportation, per diems and other incidental expenses, pursuant to EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 53, DATED OCTOBER 29, 1986, chargeable against their respective offices/hospitals.

 

 4. TENURE

4.1 The Committee members shall hold office forte period of SIX (6) MONTHS with no prejudice to reappointment.

4.2 The invited members from the private organization may be changed every SIX (6) MONTHS, depending on the respective organization concerned.

This Order repeals/amends previous Orders and/or parts thereof which are inconsistent herewith.
