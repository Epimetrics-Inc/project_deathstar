<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 1 S. 1995</subject>


<body>
   
Administrative Order No. 1, s. 1995 is hereby amended to provide that the National Program Manager of the National Voluntary Blood Services Unit (NVBSU) shall function as the Disbursing Officer of the NVBSU and shall be responsible for all financial transactions of the Unit.

This order shall take effect immediately.
