<subject> SUBJECT: Free Medical Care for Barangay Health Workers under the Ministry of Health Effective December 16, 1985, in any RHV or Hospital Facility of the MOH</subject>
 
	
<body>
        Barangay Health Workers perform a vitally important role in the effective implementation of the Primary Health Care approach of health programs at the levels of this basic political unit. They represent the community’s voluntary response to the call for public service in the field of health.
	For this selfless contribution to the improvement of their community’s health at no added cost to the government. Barangay Health Workers deserve to be entitled to certain medical benefits.
	Accordingly, effective December 1, 1985, all Barangay Health Workers and their dependents shall be granted from medical care in any main health center or hospital facility under the Ministry of Health;

	The term “Free Medical Care” shall mean the following benefits:
	1. From medicines subject to the availability thereof in the hospital pharmacy or RHU,
	2. Free room and board.
	3. Free diagnostic services as required by the attending physician depending on the facilities of the hospital/RHU facility.
	4. No Professional Fee
