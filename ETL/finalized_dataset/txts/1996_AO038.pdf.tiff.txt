<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Sections B and E of Administrative Order No. 16 s1993 re: Reporting Scheme in  accordance with the Department of Health Streamlining per Memorandum Order No. 27 dated August 13, 1992 of the Office of the President</subject>

<body> 

To implement a streamlined process of budget approval from both chambers of
Congress and to implement the necessary coordination of internal policy coordination and development performed by the Health Policy Development Staff and congressional liason and networking performed by the Department Legislative Liason Office (DLLO) of the Department of Health, the DLLO is hereby placed under the supervision of the Office for Management Services.

Thus, “Congressional Liason Group” in Section B, item 4 of Administrative Order No. 16 s. 1993 will now be listed under Section E, “Office of the Undersecretary for Management Services.”

This order will take effect November 4, 1996.
