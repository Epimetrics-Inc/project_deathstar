<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines on the Grant of Anniversary Bonus for Department of Health Officials and Employees</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to Administrative Order No. 263 dated 28 March 1996 by the President of the Philippines Authorizing the Grant of Anniversary Bonus to Officials and Employees of Government Entities and inasmuch as the DOH will be celebrating its 105th year anniversary on June 23, 2003, the grant of AnniversaIy Bonus to DOH ofﬁcials and employees in the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, centers for health development and hospitals including specialty hospitals is hereby authorized in order to improve and enhance employee’s morale and deepen their commitment and dedication to public service.

I. Coverage

All employees under Department of Health regardless of salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, contractual or temporary) who are in the service as of the occasion of the DOH milestone anniversary.

II. Exemption

a. Any DOH employee who has been found guilty of any offense in connection with his/her work during the ﬁve-year interval between milestone years

b. Consultants andlor experts who are hired for speciﬁc purposes

III. Rules and Regulations

a. The Anniversary Bonus shall be granted only during DOH milestone years. Milestone years refer: to the 15th anniversary and to every fifth year thereafter;

b. Payment of the Anniversary Bonus shall be in the-amount not exceeding P3,000.00 each employee provided that the employee has rendered at least one (1) year of service in the Department as of the date of the milestone year;

c. An employee may receive the Anniversary Bonus only once every ﬁve years, regardless of transfer from one agency to another; and,

d. Part-Time employees are entitled to one half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full-time employee.

IV. Funding Source

Payment of Anniversary Bonus shall be charged against any available savings from released allotment for current operating expenditures provided that all authorized mandatory expenses shall have been paid ﬁrst, subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

V. Effectivity

This Order shall take effect immediately.
