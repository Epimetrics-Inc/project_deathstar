<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 20, s. 2000 “IMPLEMENTATION ARRANGEMENT FOR THE UNIFIED PROJECT MANAGEMENT TEAM OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH (UPMT - DOH)”</subject>

<body>
 

Administrative Order no. 20, s. 2000which describes the implementation arrangement for the Unified Project Management Team of the Department of Health (UPMT—DOH) is hereby amended such that Section III, Item H is superceded by the following:

III.H. Regional Uniﬁed Project Management Team (RUPMT)

Every Regional Field Health Office (RHFO) shall create a Regional Uniﬁed Project Management Team which shall be under the local health systems cluster of the RFHO. The team shall be composed of a minimum of four (4) organic RFO staff namely : a Team leader, an Assistant Team Leader, a Monitoring and Evaluation and Project Operations staff. 

h.1 The team Leader shall be responsible for the overall management and administration of Foreign Assisted Projects (FAPs) in the Region, and shall supervise the team.

h.2 The Assistant Team Leader shall assist the team leader in the management and administration of FAPs in the Region and shall be responsible for coordinating with the Regional finance Unit as well as with the Regional  Procurement Committee to facilitate procurement and financial management relative to FAPs.

h.3 The Monitoring and Evaluation start shall be tasked to monitor FAPs implementation at Regional and LGU level. She / he shall be responsible for accomplishing the RUPMT report quarterly and for submitting the same through the Regional Director (RD) to the Central UPMT.

h.4 The Project Operation staff shall oversee the day to day operations of FAPs at the regional level and shall be responsible for the coordination of FAP’s key players and for providing technical assistance to LGU's relative to FAPs.

The Regional Unified Project Management Team shall be under the direct supervision of the head of the Regional Local Health Systems Cluster and it shall closely coordinate with the Management Support Cluster and the Health Operation Cluster of the Region. The RUPMT shall directly supervise contractual Regional Project Staff.

As thus amended, all other provisions of Administrative Order no. 20, s. 2000 not inconsistent with the foregoing shall remain in effect.
