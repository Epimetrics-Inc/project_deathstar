<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 118 dated December 2003 re: Payment of Salary Increase for Public Health Workers (PHWs) after completion of Graduate Studies for a Degree Course</subject>


<body>

Administrative Order No. 118 dated, December 2003 is hereby amended such that No. 2 of the first paragraph shall be read as follows:

2. In case a PHW's present salary is at the 8th step of the salary schedule, he shall be entitled to a raise in pay equivalent to two percent (2%) of his present salary.

As thus amended Administrative Order No. 118 dated 1 December 2003 still stands in effect.
