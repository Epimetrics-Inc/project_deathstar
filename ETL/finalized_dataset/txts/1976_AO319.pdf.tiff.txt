<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

TO: The Technical Staff, Bureau Directors, Regional Health Directors, Chiefs of Offices and Hospital, Heads of Projects and Units, and all others concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Inclusion of Hospital Dentists as member of the Medical Staff for purposes of sharing with the Medicare Medical Staff Common Fund.</subject>

<body>
Upon the recommendation of the Ad Hoc Committee, created by the Undersecretary of Health, in a Memorandum dated December 13, 1976 and pursuant to the provisions of Department Circular No. 262, s. 1974 relative to the disbursement of professional fees paid by Medicare patients, hospital dentists who are involved in the care of Medicare patients are entitled to share in the Medicare Medical Staff Common Fund.

All concerned are advised to be guided accordingly.

This Order shall take effect October 1, 1976.
</body>
