<subject>SUBJECT: Placing the Leprosy Research Laboratory and Training Center under the Dermatology Research and Training Project.</subject>

<body>
In view of the fact that the Bureau of Health and Medical Services is a staff agency, and following the concept that leprosy is principally a disease of the skin, the Leprosy Research Laboratory and Training Center is hereby placed under the administrative direction and control of the Dermatology Research and Training Project, effective immediately.

This transfer shall be reflected in the budget of the Department of Health for CY 1979.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
