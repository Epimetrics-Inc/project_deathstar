<subject> Subject: Implementing the Procurement Policy Board Resolution on the use of (A4) size 210x297mm paper by member-agencies</subject>
<body>
In the Procurement Policy Board Meeting No. 90-05 held on December 19, 1990, it was resolved that PBB member agencies should initiate the use of Metric (A4) size 210 x 297 mm paper for all communications consistent with existing laws and regulations.

To implement the above—cited resolution, all offices, units and agencies, including Regional Health Offices, Integrated-Provincial Health Offices, District Health Offices and Hospitals are hereby directed to use Metric (A4) size 210 x 297 mm paper for all communications.

All Directors and Chiefs or the Offices and hospitals concerned are-hereby required to submit a Compliance Report to the Office of the Secretary after one month from receipt of this Order.

This Order takes effect immediately.
