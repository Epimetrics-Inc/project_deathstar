<subject> 
SUBJECT: Establishment of the Ofﬁce of the DOH —Center of Excellence
on PPPs in Health (CEP3H) at the NKTI Diagnostic Center
</subject>

<body>

In pursuance of Department Administrative Order No. 2012-0004 dated March 1, 2012 establishing the DOH — Center of Excellence on Public-Private Partnerships in Health (DOH-CEPBH) under the Office of the Secretary, and designating the Undersecretary of Health for National Capital Region and Southern Luzon as head of the Center of Excellence on Public — Private Partnerships in Health(DOH-CEP3H) who shall exercise administrative supervision and control over its activities, and mandating the functions of the said Office, it is hereby ordered the DOH—CEP3H shall:

a. Establish its office at {ij the 3rd Floor of the National Kidney and Transplant Institute (NKTI) Diagnostic Center, East Avenue, Quezon City.

b. Ensure the effective and efficient implementation of PPP projects of the DOH; and

c. Act as the clearing house for collecting experiences, analyses and dissemination of best practices;

Funds to carry out the activities and projects of the CEP3H shall be taken from the Ofﬁce of the Secretary of Health until such time that a separate fund allocation for the Operations of the DOH—CEP3H is incorporated as a line item in the GAA under the Office of the Secretary.
</body>

This Order shall take effect immediately.
