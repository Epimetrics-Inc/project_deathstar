<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 22 s. 1987</docnum>
<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority to Mrs. TERESITA A. DELA CRUZ to approve Clearance Certificates for purposes of separation from the service</subject>

<body>
	To expedite the approval of Clearance Certificates for purposes of resignation and retirement, or for separation from the service for any other cause, Mrs. TERESITA A. DELA CRUZ IS HEREBY DELEGATED THE AUTHORITY TO APPROVE SUCH CLEARANCE CERTIFICATES.
	Accordingly, Mrs. Dela Cruz shall approve clearance certificates as follows:


“APPROVED By the authority of the Secretary of Health”
TERESITA A. DELA CRUZ
Assistant Secretary for Management Services”

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
