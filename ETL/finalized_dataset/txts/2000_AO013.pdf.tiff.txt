<subject>SUBJECT: Amendments of Administrative Order No. 21 Particularly on the Provision of Personnel Transactions Bearing on Delegation of Authority to Approve Appointments</subject>

<body>

In the light of the approval from the Office of the President of the DOH request for exemption from Administrative Order No. 100 and in consonance with the anticipated finalization and submission of the DOH Rationalization and Streamlining Plan (RSP) for approval by the Department of Budget and Management the following amendments of the above mentioned Administrative Order are hereby made:

1. All appointments for purposes of fillingâ€”up of vacant positions at various levels which are duly exempted as stipulated in Department Memorandum No. 22 s. 2000 (hereto attached), shall be approved by the Regional Director, while still 1n the process of DOH Re-engineering, or until rescinded both for the Regional Field Office positions and appointments from hospitals directly under the region concerned.

2. Likewise all appointments of the Special Hospitals in Metro Manila shall be approved by the Regional Health Director, National Capital Region for Health.

For the evaluation of applicants for the positions of Division Chief or its equivalent rank under the Region, there is a need for one (1) representative from the Central Office who shall be duly designated by the Secretary to seat with the Regional Selection and Promotion Board during their deliberation.

For original appointments for the position of Medical Officer III in Teaching and Training Hospitals requirements as prescribed by the Health Manpower Development and Training Service, this Department which includes QUERT shall be required as a pre-requisite for appointments.

All other provisions under the Administrative Order No. 21 which are not covered by these amendments shall still be in effect.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
