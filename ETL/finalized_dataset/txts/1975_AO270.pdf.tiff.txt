<body>
The Rules and Regulations implementing the provisions of Republic Act No. 4226, otherwsie known as the Hospital Licensure Act, prescribed under Burea Administrative Order No. 1, series of 1965, dated August 16, 1965, are hereby amended to include provisions relating to dental services, pursuant to Section 15 of Republic Act No. 4226.

PART II
THE BASIC HOSPITAL SERVICES

The following provisions shall be inserted under Part II:

3. Dental Health Service

Each hospital shall have a dental health service which shall be organized as a department, section or unit depending on the type, size and facilities of the hospital and administered in accordance with established hospital administrative procedures. The type of scope of the service, the duties, responsibilities and qualifications, and surgical privileges of the dental staff shall be governed by the pertinent rules and regulations prescribed by the Bureau of Dental Health Services under Administrative Order No. 118, s. 1970 dated June 16, 1970. (Refer to PART IV, Minimum Standard Requirements for the Hospital Dental Health Service, Standards Manual, Bureau of Dental Health Service, 1970)

PART III

HOSPITAL FACILITIES

The following provisions shall be inserted under Part III.

5. DENTAL

Each hospital shall provide the dental department, section or unit with the necessary facilities in accordance with the standard requirements prescribed by the Bureau of Dental Health Services under Administrative Order No. 118, series of 1970 dated June 16, 1970. (Refer to Part IV, Minimum Standard Requirements for the Hospital Dental Health Services Manual, Bureau of Dental Health Services, 1970)

5. X-ray, Laboratory and Medical Records under Part III shall be renumbered as 6 and shall read as follows:

6. X-ray, Laboratory, Medical Records and Dental Records

The following provisions shall be inserted after the last paragraph of Medical Records under 6.

Dental Records

In addition to the hospital records, the dental department, section or unit shall keep records and submit reports in accordance with the standard method of recording and reporting prescribed by the Bureau of Dental Health Services under Section 7, of the Policies, Guidelines and Standards on Dental Health Services, Administrative Order No. 255 series of 1975, dated August 7, 1975.
