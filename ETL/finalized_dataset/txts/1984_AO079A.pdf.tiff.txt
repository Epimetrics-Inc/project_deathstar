<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 52, s. 1984</docnum>

To	: The Regional Health Directors and Chiefs of Special Hospitals/Medical Centers/Regional, Provincial and District Hospitals and all concerned.
<subject>Subject: Delegating authority  to the Regional Health Directors to enter into an agreement with the Regional Population Offices re sterilization subsidy.</subject>
<body>In connection with this Offices approval of the Commission on Population’s proposal to decentralize sterilization subsidy at the regional level, authority is hereby delegated to the Regional Health Directors to enter into the Memoranda of Agreement with their respective Regional Population Offices regarding this matter. Further, you are enjoined to make provisions that subsidy claims for reimbursements as well as corresponding payments of these claims by the Regional Population Offices be coursed through their respective Regional Health Offices.
	A copy of the regional Memorandum of Agreement as well as a repot of quarterly performances should be furnished this Office through the Family Planning Service, for purposes of coordinating the program.

</body>
