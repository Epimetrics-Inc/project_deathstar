OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY
Manila

<date>09/19/1975</date>

<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 257 s. 1975</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to Dr. Jesus C. Azurin, Undersecretary of Health, to sign certain papers previously prepared for my signature, effective September 19, 1975.</subject>

<body>
In view of the acceptance by the President of the Philippines of my retirement to take effect at his pleasure and for purposes of expeditious action on certain papers not otherwise delegated to other officials of the Department under previous administrative orders, effective September 19, 1975, Dr. JESUS C. AZURIN, Undersecretary of Health, is hereby authorized to sign appointments to positions within the WAPCC Salary Range 32 - 50 (₱2880 to ₱6888 per annum), transmittal letters of special budgets, treasury warrants, checks and other communications heretofore prepared for the signature of the undersigned. He shall sign thus:

By authority of the Secretary of Health:

J. C. AZURIN, M.D., M.P.H.
Undersecretary of Health

This authority shall be in effect until the President of the Philippines shall have designated or appointed the next Secretary of Health.

All previous orders inconsistent herewith are hereby rescinded.
</body>
