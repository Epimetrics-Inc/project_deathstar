<subject>SUBJECT: authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that
can be kept in stock by Municipal Hospitals.</subject>

<body>
In consonance with Betas Pambansa Blg. 879 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, the following 'hospitals are hereby authorized to maintain ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is chargeable against allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses;

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321, authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed

1. San Narciso Municipal Hospital - P25,000.00
San Narciso, Quezon

2. Prieto-Diaz Municipal Hospital – P12,000.00
Prieto—Diaz, Sorsogon

The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached requests.
