<subject>SUBJECT  : Delegation of authority to chiefs of special hospitals participating in the use of the C.T. Scanner to approve referrals of their indigent patients to the Makati Medical Center by authority of the Minister of Health.</subject>


<body>In the interest of the service, chiefs of special hospitals participating in the use of the C.T. Scanner under memorandum of agreement entered into by the Ministry of Health and the Makati Medical Center, are hereby delegated the authority to approve the referral of their indigent patients to the said medical institution subject to the conditions specified in the agreement.

       Approval made under such delegation shall bear the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health" above that of the signing official.


       This Order, which supersedes Ministry Order No. 83 dated April 30, 1982, takes effect immediately
 

</body>
