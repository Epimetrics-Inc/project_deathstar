<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 182 s. 1972</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT : Further delegation of authority to certain officials under the Department of Health.</subject>

<body>
Effective November 16, 1972, the Chiefs of the Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital, Dr. Jose Fabella Memorial Hospital, San Lazaro Hospital and the National Children's Hospita; are hereby authorized, subject to the provisions of Rule XVI, of the Revised Civil Service Rules, to approved all applications for leave of absence of officers and employees under their respective jurisdiction, provided their services can be spared without detriment to the interest of the public service.

Request for commutation of salary incident to leave of officials and employees in the above-mentioned hospitals shall continue to be acted upon by this Department, and applications for leave without pay in excess of sixty (60) days shall likewise be submitted to this Department for appropriate action.

In connection with the foregoing authority, the Chiefs of the  aforementioned hospital shall secure from the Personnel and Records Division of this Department the leave credits of each and every official under their respective jurisdiction.

Application for leave of absence of officers and employees of the said hospitals received in the Department before November 16, 1972 shall continue to be acted upon in this Office, but those that may be submitted after that date shall be returned to the office of origin for action conformably with the terms of this Order.
</body>
