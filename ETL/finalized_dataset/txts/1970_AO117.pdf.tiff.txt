<subject>SUBJECT: Fusion of Tagum and Sta. Cruz Malaria Units and Transfer of its headquarters to Davao City.</subject>

<body>
In the interest of the service in line with the austerity program of the government but with intent to keep intact the objective of the malaria eradication activities in the region involved, the following changes in Malaria Area "F" are hereby promulgated:

1. The Malaria Units in Tagum, Davao Del Norte, and Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur, are hereby fused into one (1) Unit.

2. The Unit Headquarters shall be established in Davao City which shall have territorial jurisdiction over the places covered by the former two (2) Units.

3. The movement of the personnel of both Units shall depend upon the technical assessment to be done by the Central Headquarters in Manila with the assistance of the Area Supervisor.

4. An inventory of the properties and supplies involved shall be handled by the Area Supervisor who shall effect the transfer of responsibilities to the Head of the Unit assigned thereat.

5. Other matters affecting the previous two (2) Units shall be handled by the Central Headquarters on the
recommendat1on of the Area Supervisor.

This Order, to take effect on July 1, 1970, is hereby made of record.
</body>
