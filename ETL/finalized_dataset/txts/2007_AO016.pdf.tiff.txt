<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of the Information Management Service from. Internal Management Team to the Sectoral Management and Coordination Team.</subject>

 
<body>
 
In relation to Administrative Order 2005-0023, “Implementing Guidelines for Fourmula One for Health a Framework for Health Reforms” and as a transition arrangement pending the approval of the DOH Rationalization Plan, the Information Management Service (IMS) is hereby transferred to the Sectoral Management and Coordination Team (SMCT) from the lntemal Management Support Team.

The IMS is primarily responsible for the management of knowledge and information coming from the sector and thereby requires more interaction and interfacing with the existing bureaus under SMCT.

This order takes effects August 1, 2007.
