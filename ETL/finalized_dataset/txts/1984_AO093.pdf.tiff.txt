<subject> Subject: Submission of Quarterly Report of Operations of the Integrated Provincial Health Offices</subject>

<body>

 As a monitoring and supervision instrument, Regional Directors are hereby instructed to consolidate the 1) financial report and 2) physical report of accomplishments of the IPHO’s into Quarterly Reports of Operations.
	The reports shall show the annual work targets and allotment as well as the quarterly work targets and allotments. The manpower used each month for the quarter under review shall also be shown therein in terms of man-months. The authorized budgetary units of work measurements for field health services and hospital services, as well as those of field health services and hospital services, as well as those of the decentralized special projects, shall be utilized until modified through the usual process.
	Deviations from quarterly work and financial plans shall be shown in percentages. Significant deviations (10% or more), whether negative or positive, shall be explained.
	The quarterly /report of Operations shall be submitted to the Office of the Minister within twenty (20) days after the close of each quarter. The provincial Health Offices shall be furnished copies thereof with observations of the Regional Directors thereon.
	The attached form shall be utilized as the Report of Operations of the integrated Provincial Health Offices.
	This shall take effect during the Calendar year 1984.
