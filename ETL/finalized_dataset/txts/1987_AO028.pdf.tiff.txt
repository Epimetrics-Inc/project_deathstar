<subject> Subject: Adoption of the System of Regional Bulk Procurement</subject>

<body>

	Pursuant to the agreement reached during the Monthly Regional Health Director’s Meeting to adopt the system of Regional Bulk Procurement, all Regional Health Directors are hereby directed to conduct immediately in their respective regions the Regional Bulk Procurement for the three-month requirements of agencies under them.
	The implementation of the Regional Bulk Procurement shall be subject to the guidelines/procedures/processes hereto attached as an integral part of this Administrative Order. These are to be used as guidelines only and the Regional Health Directors are authorized to modify the same as they see fit to suit the specific conditions and situations in their particular regions.
	The Regional Health Directors are directed to submit a copy of the results of the Regional Bulk Procurement conducted to the Financial Operations and Frontline Service Audit of the Department of Health within thirty (30) calendar days from the date the same was conducted.
	This Order takes effect immediately.
