<subject>Subject: Provision for free laboratory examinations performed in the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, this Department, for certain cases listed hereunder.</subject>

<body>
This has reference to Administrative Order No. 97, dated November 5I199O.containiIng the laboratory fees for certain laboratory examinations performed in the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, this Department copy attached.
       Free laboratory examination may be granted for the following:
1. DOH employees and the their immediate families
2. Dependents of DOH employees

3. DOH retirees

4. Indigents (as certified)

5. Laboratory examinations done as part of BRL/DOH programs:  surveys, research projects, disease surveillance and for clients of Public Health
Programs

6. Laboratory examinations done for non—profit or charitable institutions, the WHO and University of the Philippines Systems
7. DOH/Government candidates for Fellowships abroad

This order takes effect immediately.
