<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority for regional health directors to sign appointments of personnel paid from local funds effective December 8, 1975</subject>

<body>
In accordance with the policy of the Department to delegate administrative authority to the extent necessary and practicable for economical, efficient and effective implementation of health services and in order to facilitate personnel transactions on the regional level, all regional health directors are hereby authorized, effective December 8, 1975, to sign appointments of personnel to positions fully paid from local funds which in several opinions rendered by the Secretary of Justice is vested in the Secretary of Health. They shall sign thus:

<image>figure_1.png</image>

Appointments thus signed shall be sent to the regional office of the Civil Service Commission for appropriate action under the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 807 otherwise known as the Civil Service Act of 1975. Copies thereof shall be furnished this Department for reference and record purposes.

This Order is hereby declared official, confirmed and made of record.
</body>
