<subject>SUBJECT: Amendments to Administrative Order No. 43 dated September 12, 1979</subject>

<body>
The following provisions of Administrative Order No. 43 dated September 12, 1979, providing guidelines on delegations of administrative authority and the flow of communications in the Office of the Minister are hereby modified as follows:

Item 1 (G) All requisitions and purchase order for drugs and medicines involving the regional health offices except in cases of emergency when they are authorized to purchase in an amount not exceeding ₱10,000 a month.

Item 1 (H) Requisitions and purchase orders for drugs and medicines, equipment, supplies or materials from any of the hospitals directly under the Office of the Minister except in cases of emergency when they are authorized to purchase in an amount not exceeding ₱5,000 a month.

A new provision, Item 1(0) is hereby inserted as follows:

Item 1(0) Requisitions and purchase orders for drugs and medicines of bureaus, offices, services and projects.

As thus amended Administrative Order No. 43 stands in effect.
</body>
