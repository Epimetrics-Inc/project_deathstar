<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines for Medical Care Assistance Program including financial assistance to selected government hospitals, subsidy to confined indigent patients and under-privileged veterans and medical assistance to the away stricken areas.</subject>

<body>

The Medical Care Assistance Program was included in the General Appropriation Act (GAA) to provide assistance to indigent patients and the underprivileged veterans who need the hospital health care service in identified DOH retained medical facility.


I. Guidelines regulating the DOH Meﬁcal Care Assistance Program Fund:

1. The Department of Health (DOH) shall implement the Medical Care Assistance Program in selected government hospitals determined by the Office of the Secretary (OSEC).

2. The following individuals shall qualify for the use of this fund:
2.1 Indigent patients; and 
2.2 Under privileged veterans.

3. Only extremely necessary hospitalization shall be undertaken by this subsidy.


II. Implementing Rules and Regulation:

1. The Office of the Secretary shall undertake the method and the selection process to determine the recipient DOH hospitals.

2. Qualified out-patients and in—patients can avail of the funds.

3. The attending physician shall determine the necessary treatment and management of the patient and recommend to the Chief of Hospital (COH).

4. The Hospital Medical Social Worker shall evaluate and validate the status of the patients applying for the subsidy and shall likewise forward the recommendations to the COH.

5. The COH shall recommend to the OSEC the approval or acceptance of the individual to the program.

6. Said subsidy shall be utilized to cover all hospital charges/ bills accrued during hospitalization.

7. The government subsidy for Medicare Assistance Program shall not exceed the total appropriations for the particular calendar year.


III. Mechanics of Disbursement of Funds.

1. The fund for this program shall be sub—allotted from the DOH to the selected government hospital.

2. The recipient government hospital shall submit a report on the utilization of this fund to the OSEC semi-annually.

This A.O. shall take effect immediately upon approval.
