<subject> Transfer of the IPHO site of Zamboanga del Sur from Aurora General Hospital, Aurora, same province, to the Zamboanga del Sur Provincial Hospital, Pagadian City.</subject>
<body>
	Upon representations of the Provincial Governor and the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of Zamboanga del Sur and the recommendation of the Regional Health Director, Region IX and the Chairman, Lupong Tagapagpaganap of the Autonomous Region, the site of the Integrated Provincial Health Office of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby transferred from Aurora, to the Zamboanga del Sur Provincial Hospital, Pagadian City.
	Accordingly, the Regional Health Director, Region IX, and the newly designated Officer in Charge, IPHO of Zamboanga del Sur, shall secure the nucleus of their supportive staff from the Zamboanga del Sur Provincial Hospital and that of the Zamboanga del Sur Provincial Hospital, shall be transferred from the designated provincial hospital, the Aurora General Hospital, to the new site, the Zamboanga del Sur Provincial Hospital in Pagadian City.
	The technical staffs of the IPHO and locally paid health personnel who is actually assigned in Aurora General Hospital shall report to the new IPHO site in Zamboanga del sur Provincial Hospital.
	This Order takes effect March 18, 1985.
