<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to AOI No.110.e. 1986</subject>

<body>

	In view of the presence of volunteer trainees in certain DOH hospitals and the need to clarify their roles, the Administrative Order No. 110, s. 1980 dated November 21, 1980, entitled Guidelines for Hospitals under the Ministry of Health with Trainees under the Volunteer Training Program, specifically item 2, to wit:
	-Trainees accepted by the hospitals under the Program shall perform their duties exclusively during the morning shift; that is from 7:00 A.M. to 3:00 P.M.;

Is hereby amended to read as follows:
	“Volunteer trainees accepted by the hospitals shall perform the duties and responsibilities of regular and permanent hospital personnel.”

	This Order takes effect immediately.
