<subject> Amending Administrative Order No. 82, dated April 28, 1980, relative to Revised Drug Procurement Procedures for Special Hospitals.</subject>

<body>

       In the interest of the articles, paragraph three, Item No.8 of Administrative Order No. 82, dated April 28, 1980, relative to Revised Drug Procurement Procedures for Special Hospitals, is hereby amended as follows:
       “8. Requisition and purchase orders shall be prepared and approved by the chief of the hospital based on the approved price and quantity authorized by the Minister of Health. A copy of every purchase order issued shall be furnished by the hospital to the Office of the Minister for reference. Approval of such Requisitions and Purchase Orders shall strictly adhere to the authority granted under Section II-2 of Administrative Order No. 74-C, dated January 11, 1984, providing for the delegation of authority to approve Requisitions and Purchase Orders to officials of Special Hospitals and Hospitals directly under the Office of the Minister.”

	All pertinent provisions of Administrative Order No. 2, s.1980, as amended, shall apply to all Special and other Hospitals directly under the Office of the Minister.
	For Strict Compliance.
