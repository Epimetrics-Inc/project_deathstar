<subject>Subject: OBM approval of amendment to Par. 13.2 Section XIII of MOH Administrative Order No. 114 dated April 23, 1985</subject>

<body>
Upon representations of this Office and in View of the current reorganization of the Ministry of Health which has adversely affected the salaries of some rank and file employees, the Minister of the Budget has approved the following amendment to Rule 3 of the Implementing Rules of the MOH Reorganization Plan which Corresponds to Par. 13.2, Section XIII of Administrative
Order No. 114 dated April 23,1985:

When an employee is reappointed to a position in the new staffing pattern which is lower in salary range than the one he occupied in the old setup, he shall continue to be paid his present salary rate, but not exceed the maximum of the salary range of the new position.

For purposes of this provision, the maximum of each salary range shall be determined by adding 20% to the hiring rate and placing the resultind figure at the nearest step of the salary schedule under Executive Order No. 594. The other steps, second, third and fourth, may then be identified on the Salary schedule as/applicable to each case.

   
 
Administrative Order No. 114 as amended by Administrative Order No. 121 dated July HQ 1985 and this Order, stands in effect.
