4.1 -Schedule for submission of application are as follows :

1st batch: February to May 15 (but not latter than 4:00 p.m.)
2nd batch: July to August 16 (but not later than 4:00 p.m.)
3rd batch: October to November 15 (but not later than 4:00 p.m.)

All other provisions including the specific requirements of the above mentioned AO’s remain in effect.
