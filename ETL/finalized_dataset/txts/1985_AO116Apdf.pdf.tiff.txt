<subject>Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by APAYAO DISTRICT HOSPITAL, Calanasan, Kalinga, Apayao</subject>

<body> In consonance with Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa 866, Apayao District Hospital, Calamasan, Kalinga-Apayao, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS (P20,000.00) worth of medicines and drugs in the stock for resale, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.
	It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321 authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be created shall be followed.
