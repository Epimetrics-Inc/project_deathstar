TO: Directors of Department Integrated Regional Offices (DIRFOS) and others concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Authority of DIRFO Directors to purchase drugs, medical and dental supplies for vertical public health programs implemented by their units and as assistance to LGUs and NGOs as partners in national health development effective June 1, 1995</subject>


<body> 

With the devolution of health programs to local government units, one major role of the Department of Health in national health development, is to extend technical and material assistance to devolved health units and accredited NGOs as partners in responsive national health development.

In line with these expanded responsibilities, Regional Directors are hereby granted authority to undertake purchases of drugs, medical and dental supplies for the use of vertical public health programs implemented by their field units, as aid to devolved health units and to NGOs involved in health work subject to the following: 

1. That such procurement shall be based on public bidding, exclusive distributorship and lowest evaluated price advantageous to the government; 

2. That the purchases shall be strictly in accordance with the approved annual procurement programs of their offices;

3. That the funds allocated for such purchases shall not exceed those allocated by the DOH/Regional Office for the purpose;

4. That the procurement of drugs shall be in accordance with the DOH National Drug Formulary; 

5. That pertinent budgetary, accounting and auditing rules shall be properly observed.

This Order, which supersedes any previous order inconsistent herewith, takes effect June 1, 1995.
