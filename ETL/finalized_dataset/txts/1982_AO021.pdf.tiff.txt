<subject>SUBJECT : Authority of Mr. ANTONINO P. SAN JUAN, Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development to sign notices of salary adjustment related to the Career Progression Scheme in the Headquarters Offices</subject>

       <body>Administrative Order No. 1 dated July 14, 1981, insofar as the delegation of authority to the Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development is concerned is hereby further modified to include his authority to sign notices of salary adjustment of personnel entitled to salary increases under the Career Progression Scheme.

       It is understood that documentary evidence, preferably the individual's service record and other relevant data shall support the notices recommended by the respective heads of agencies in the Headquarters Offices.

       Such notices shall carry the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health” above that of the signing official.

       This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
