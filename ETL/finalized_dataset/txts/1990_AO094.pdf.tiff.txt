<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority for the Provincial Health Officers to grant permit to DOH personnel in their respective provinces who are seeking to run and serve as members of the Board of Directors of electric cooperatives.</subject>

<body>
	Considering the time, distance and expense that may be spent by DOH personnel, especially those in the far-flung provinces in securing permit from this Office for them to be allowed to run and/or sit as members of the Board of Directors of electric cooperatives, authority is hereby delegated to Provincial Health Officers to grant such permit to their respective personnel in compliance with the New Election Code adopted and promulgated by the National Electrification Administration (NEA) on January 22, 1990.

	This delegated authority to grant permission to government officers or employees to sit as members in the Board of Directors of electric cooperatives involves employment in a private firm. Said employment is subject to provisions of R.A. 6713, otherwise known as the Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for Public Officers and Employees.
</body>
