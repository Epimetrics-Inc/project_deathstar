TO: The Technical Staff, Bureau Directors, Chief of Offices, Project Directors, Regional Health Directors, Provincial Helath Officers, City Health Officers, Chief of Hospitals and all others concerned

<subject>SUBJECT: Amended Standards on Hospital Dental Service</subject>

<body>
In accordance with Letter of Implementation No. 8 relative to Part XVI on Health and other pertinent provisions of the Integrated Reorganization Plan and for effective supervision, control and coordination of the Hospital Dental Health Program with the total health program of the ministry of Health and the Regional Health Offices, the Standard Requirements for Hospital Dental Services prescribed under Administrative Order No. 118 dated June 16, 1970 is hereby amended and the updated policies, guidelines and standards prescribed by the Bureau of Dental Health Services are hereby promulgated to read as follows:

Section 1. Definition of Terms
Section 2. Type, Scope and Quality of Dental Care
Section 3. Manpower Requirements
Section 4. Standard Operating Procedures
Section 5. Facilities, Equipment and Supplies
Section 6. Records and Reports
Section 7. Budgetary Requirements
Section 8. Inspection, Regulation and Accreditation
Section 9. Technical Supervision
Section 10 Channel of Communication
Appendices

These standard requirements are hereby promulgated as official, for strict compliance by all concerned.

Any provisions of previous orders inconsistent herewith are hereby amended accordingly.

This Administrative Order Shall take effect immediately.
</body>
