<subject>Subject: Amending A.O. No. 54 dated February 10, 1983 regarding qualifications of applicants under the Residency Training Programs</subject>

<body>
 
		Administrative Order No. 54 dated February 10, 1983 which spell out the qualifications of those who will be appointed under the Residency Training Program on the Ministry of Health us hereby amended to include the following:
	1.3.3 The Chief of the teaching and training hospital shall include in the transmittal letter forwarding the appointment of a resident physician covered by P.D. 1424 and the following certification:
	“It is hereby certified that the provisions of Administrative Order No. 54 dated February 10, 1983, regarding the qualification on appointees under the Residency Training Program of this Office have been strictly observed in the preparation of this appointment.”

As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 54 dated February 10, 1983 stands in effect.
