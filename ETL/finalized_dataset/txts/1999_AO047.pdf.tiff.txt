I. RATIONALE

The Expansion of the Gamot sa Presyong DOH is a special prefect of the Office of the Secretary of Health, forerunner of which is ”Gamot sa Presyong DOH”, which was implemented by DOH selected Special Hospitals and Medical Centers in 1991 in accordance with DOH A.O. No. 114-A s. 1991.

The Expansion of the Gamot sa Presyong DOH project pertains to one of the strategies of the government to lower the prices of pharmaceutical products through the procurement of drugs and medicines in their generic names and other supplies and materials for sale in DOH hospital pharmacy to in/out patient and as well as the general public at low and affordable cost.


II. PILOT HOSPITALS

III. DRUGS AND MEDICINES FOR SALE

1.0 These pilot hospitals shall initially procure and sell pharmaceutical products listed in the current edition of the PNDF Volume I.


IV. FUND UTILIZATION AND REPORTING

1. The revolving fund for this Expansion of Gamot sa Presyong DOH shall be used for the purchase and resale of drugs and medicines and other medical supplies and materials at the hospital pharmacy with selling price
equal to acquisition cost plus thirty (30%) mark-up.

2. Participating hospitals are requested to buy their pharmaceutical products from companies duly accredited by DOH and their authorized subsidiaries.

3. Te ensure uniformity in the effective implementation of this Project, the guidelines and procedures prescribed under Joint DBM Budget Circular No. 321 and COA Circular No. 80-128 dated 6 February 1980 are hereby prescribed.


VI. REPEALING CLAUSE

Once this ExpansiOn of the Gamot sa Presyong DOH becomes operational, the following changes are to be effected in the operation and management of the Regular Revolving Trust Fund for medicines established in 1980 and the Gamot sa Presyong DOH implemented in 1992.

1.0 Regular Revolving Trust Fund

Drugs and Medicines procured by the hospitals chargeable against the existing Revolving Trust Fund shall be utilized solely for the indigent in-patients of the hospital, subject to the usual replenishment from the regular allotment for drugs and medicines for the current calendar year.

2.0 Murang Gamot sa Presyong DOH

2.1 The balance of the funds, including stocks of drugs and medicines under the original Gamot sa Presyong DOH shall be integrated with the Expanded Gamot sa Presyong DOH.

2.2 Procurement of drugs/medicines shall be charged against the integrated funds and sold to the in-patient/out patients and the general public with a 30% mark-up.


VI. MONITORING AND EVALUATION OF THE BOTICA NG MASA

1.0 The Technical Working Group of the Integrated Hospital Pharmacy program under D.O. 317-H dated August 5, 1999 shall oversee,
monitor and evaluate the implementation/operation of the Expansion
of Gamot Presyong DOH and shall render report quarterly to the
Secretary of Health.

2.0 The National Drug Policy (NDP) in coordination with the Bureau of Food and Drugs (BFAD) shall monitor drug prices of outlets outside the hospitals to assess and evaluate the effect of one of Department of Health’s strategies to lower the prices of drugs and medicines for the general welfare of the Filipino people, specially the marginalized sector of our society.


VII. EFFECTIVITY

This Order takes effect on November 15, 1999.

For guidance and strict compliance.
