<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 13 s. 2000</subject>

<body>

In View of Department Memorandum No. 52 dated March 22, 2000, lifting, the Moratarium re: Filling-up of Positions not only for medical and paramedical but also for all support service positions in hospitals, Item No. 2 of the Administrative Order No. 13 s.
2000 is hereby amended as follows:

“2. Likewise all appointments of the Special Hospitals in Metro Manila shall be approved by the Undersecretary, Office for Hospitals and Facilities Services (OHFS). Accordingly, all positions as stipulated under Administrative Order No. 11, s. 1999 for Undersecretary, OHFS shall still be followed. However, each appointment shall still bear the initial of the Director, National Capital Region far Health before the same shall be approved.”

As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 13 s. 2000 still stands in effect.
