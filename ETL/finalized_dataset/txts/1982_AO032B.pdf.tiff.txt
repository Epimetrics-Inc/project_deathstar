<subject>Subject: Hospitalization Benefits for Barangay Officials </subject>

 <body> In accordance with a Presidential directive, the following guidelines are hereby issued for reference, guidance and compliance, in implementing the provisions of R.A.  3590 otherwise known as the Revised Barrio Charter, insofar as they pertain to hospitalization benefits of barangay official.
	1. the following barangay officials are hereby entitled, under the provisions of Par. (c) Section 16 of R.A. 3590, to free  hospitalization privileges in any government hospital and given priority in free wards of private hospitals for any injury, disease or sickness incurred in line of duty:
	a. Barangay Captain 
	b. Barangay Councilmen
	c. Barangay Treasurer
	d. Barangay Secretary

2. Free hospitalization privileges shall cover the following elements: 
	a. Free hospital medicines
	b. Free room and board
	c. Free diagnostic services as requested by the attending physician.
	d. no professional fee
	4. Barangay officials seeking hospitalization privileges for any service-oriented injury, disease or sickness as provided under the provisions of Par ©, Section 16, R.A. 3590, shall be properly identified through an ID Card issued by the Municipal Mayor concerned, for the guidance and reference of hospital authorities.
	5. Only incubant barangay officials shall be covered by the above mentioned hospitalization privileges spelled out under these guidelines.
