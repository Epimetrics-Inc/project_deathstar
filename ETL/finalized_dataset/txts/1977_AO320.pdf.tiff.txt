<subject>SUBJECT: De-Activation of the Internal Control Unit, FMS.</subject>

<body>
In view of the fact that the proposed positions to man the Internal Control Unit in the Management Division, FMS, are not included in the budget decree, P.D. No. 1050, and in view of the need of the Accounting Division and of the Management Division for the personnel who are detailed in said unit, the Internal Control Unit of the FMS is hereby de-activated effective immediately until positions therefor can be created.

To take over the function being performed by the Internal Control Unit, the processing unit in the Accounting Division shall be strengthened to minimize errors in financial transactions.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
