<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 4-B, s. 1981 relative to delegation of authority to officials of Special Hospitals to approve financial transactions.</subject>

<body>
In the interest of the service and in order to effect greater efficiency and effectiveness in the operations of special hospitals, Section II-1 of Administrative Order No. 4-B series of 1981 is hereby supplemented as follows:

II. Approval of Requisitions and Purchase Orders - 

Officials authorized to approve requisitions and purchase orders:

1. In Special Hospitals:

a. Chief of Hospital -
- UP TO ₱10,000.00 PER REQUISITION FOR EMERGENCY PURCHASE OF SUPPLIES AND MATERIALS.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
