<subject>SUBJECT: Regionalization of funding UNICEF Assisted Projects</subject>

<body>
1. Regional Health Director shall appoint UNICEF Projects Officer who shall be responsible to him.

2. The Regional Health Director shall submit the budget and schedule of activities which need funding to UNICEF through the Overall Project Director, UNICEF Assisted Project and the Director of the particular project at national level who shall recommend the approval of the budget.

3. UNICEF releases the fund directly to the Regional Health Director, copy furnish the Overall Project Director and the individual Project Director.

4. Regional Cashier shall acknowledge receipt of the check, issue an official receipt to UNICEF, deposit same under a separate checking account in an authorized depository bank as a Trust Fund.

5. Regional Health Director shall designate the Regular Disbursing Officer as the disbursing officer for UNICEF Funds and other signatories of the Project.

6. Disbursement shall follow the usual accounting and auditing regulations.

7. Disbursing Officer shall prepare the liquidation of funds not later than 10 days after the end of each funded activity to the Regional Chief Accountant.

8. Regional Chief Accountant shall submit periodic report to UNICEF, copy furnish the Overall Project Director and the individual Project Director. All documents pertaining to all transactions shall be kept in a separate file ready for examination by UNICEF personnel and auditor at anytime without prior notice.

9. Release of fund for subsequent funded activity shall be made upon receipt of liquidation papers of the previous one. However, if 2 or 3 of these activities are conducted too close to each other, release of funds and liquidation shall be done at one time.
