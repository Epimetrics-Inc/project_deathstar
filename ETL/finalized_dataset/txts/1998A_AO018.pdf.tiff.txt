<subject>SUBJECT: DOH THRUSTS FOR 1999</subject>

<body>

Towards making “Quality health care available to every Filipino especially the poor,” the DOH shall be guided by the following general policy thrusts:


A. Enhancement of Support Capacities for a Devolved set-up

1. Strengthening the Regional and other Field Health Offices, thereby making resources available to the periphery where the real actions are.

2. Streamlining of the DOH Central Office with reduction of staff, reorganization of major offices and integration of services.

3. Setting partnership arrangements with LGU’s through the various hospital and local health boards.


B. Disease Control

1. Creation of more responsive and dynamic Centers of Wellness for health promotion and service delivery (field health units)

2. Installing mechanism to effectively and efficiently respond to recurrent and newly emerging diseases.

a. to strengthen disease control for Dengue, Measles, T.B., Malaria and Schistosomiasis

b. development of new strategies and approaches to prevent and control non-conununicable diseases, early childhood diseases and nutritional deficiencies.


C. Health Facilities Development Program

1. Upgrading of hospitals in the periphery to include provincial and district hospitals (revitalization of the district hospital system).


D. Health Financing

1. Development of a system to increase the scope and access to health institutions through some form of a National Health Insurance.

2. Improvement in the mode of payment of health care.


For guidance.
