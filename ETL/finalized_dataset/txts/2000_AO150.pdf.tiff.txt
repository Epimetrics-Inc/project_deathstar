<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to A.O. No. 39 s.1999 dated 2 September 1999</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 39. S. 1999 regarding the guidelines on the utilization of funds (sub-allotment) for the procurement of TB staining kits for the National TB Control Programs using the 1998 Continuing Appropriations is hereby amended as follows:

* Immersion Oil, sputum cups and glass slides for the National TB Control Program (NTP) shall be included as laboratory supplies requirement

* The funds required for the NTP Laboratory Supplies will be charged against the Center for Health Development (CHD) Funds as approved within the DOH budget for the Current Year 2000.  

All the other provisions that are not affected by this amendment shall remain in effect and in full force.
