<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 65 s. 2001, on the Grant of P500.00 Gift Check or In-Kind for Central Office Health Officials and Employees for Calendar Year 2001.</subject>


<body>

Due to unavailability/shortage of gift check and time constraints for the preparation of in-kind items for distribution to all DOH officials and employess this forthcoming Christmas party, Administrative Order No. 65 s. 2001, is hereby amended wherein Non-Cash Benefit of Gift Check or In-Kind items equivalent to P500.00 each is changed to Cash Bonus amounting to P500.00 each officials and employees of the Central Office.

All other provisions of AO No. 65 s. 2001, not affected by this amendment still stand in effect.
