<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 35 entitled “Requirements for the Control of Radiation Hazards from Clinical Diagnostic X—ray Facilities".</subject>

<body>

Provision 4.1.1 of the Section of Manpower Requirement of Administrative Order No. 35 is amended by adding the following provision:

4.1.1.9 The head of a free standing chest x-ray facility may be a fellow of the Philippine College of Chest Physicians (FPCCP) if he has had at least twenty (20) years of clinical practice in chest x-ray diagnosis.

Other provisions that are not affected by this amendment shall remain in effect.
