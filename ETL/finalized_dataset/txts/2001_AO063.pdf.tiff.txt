<subject>Subject: Implementation of Payment of Two Percent (2%) Salary Increase after Post Graduate Studies for a Degree Course.</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to the provisions of Rule XV, 6.2 of the revised IRR of RA 7305, public health workers who have earned post-graduate degree course, shall be entitled to two percent (2%) of their basic salary.

Payments shall be made effective January 01, 2001 in accordance with the governing guidelines as prescribed in the revised IRR, November, 1999 but subject to availability of funds.

For the guidance of all concerned.
