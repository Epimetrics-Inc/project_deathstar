<subject>SUBJECT: Addendum to Administrative Order No.14 s.1998</subject>

<body>

In addition to the working structure and reporting scheme identified under Administrative Order No. 14 s.1998, which is the basis of this Departmentâ€™s operations, all Retained Hospitals including Medical Centers in the various Regions, Provinces and Districts are placed under the direct supervision and control of the DOH Regional Health Offices.

Under this Order, the Regional Directors are to supervise all DOH Retained Hospitals within their administrative regions and shall exercise authority over the administrative and financial transactions of these hospitals. 

In addition to the foregoing the functions of the Regional Health Offices shall be guided by Section 16 of Executive Order No. 119 as applicable. All other previous Department Orders and issuance inconsistent with this Order are hereby rescinded.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
