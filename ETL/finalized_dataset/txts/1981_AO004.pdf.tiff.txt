<subject>SUBJECT: Revoking certain provisions of Administrative Order No. 114, s. 1980, relative to delegations of authority to Ministry Officials on approval of financial and other transactions, signing/countersigning of TCAA checks and Treasury Warrants of the Philippine Population Project I and II (MOH) and delegating authority to certain Ministry Official.</subject>

<body>
Pending the result of the assessment in the implementation of the programs under Philippine Population Project I and II (MOH), authorities delegated to the following Ministry Officials under paragraph I-IV and VI-VIII of Administrative Order No. 114, s. 1980, are hereby withdrawn:

a. Deputy Minister of Health
b. Asst. Secretary for Finance and Management
c. Executive Director for Philippine Population Project I
d. Project Coordinator for Philippine Population Project II
e. Asst. Project Coordinator for Philippine Population Project II
f. Chief, Administrative Service
g. Department Budget Officer III
h. Project Coordinator

As such, approval of disbursements, requisitions, purchase orders, travel orders, countersigning of TCAA checks/Treasury Warrants and other transactions shall be prepared for the signature of the undersigned.

The following delegation of authority are hereby granted to the Assistant Secretary for Finance and Management:

1. To sign Treasury Warrants without limit where the Minister of Health is countersigning.
2. To approve salary payrolls, vouchers and cash advances, in the absence of the Minister of Health, provided, that a report thereof shall be submitted to the Minister.

This Order supersedes all other orders inconsistent herewith and shall take effect immediately.
</body>
