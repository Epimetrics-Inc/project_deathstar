<subject>SUBJECT: DELEGATION OF PROCUREMENT FUNCTION TO DOH RETAINED HOSPITALS</subject>

<body>

Based on the recommendations of the DOH Task Force on Modiﬁed Procurement System, the following procurement of goods and services shall be handled by retained hospitals:

1. Foodstuffs 
2. Drugs and Medicines 
3. Contractual Services such as laundry, security and janitorial

These hospitals are further directed to create the following committees: Supplier’s Accreditation, Prequalification, Bids and Awards, and support committees which will undertake the technical evaluation, inspection and acceptance and price monitoring. The process of procurement should follow the legal and accepted policies on procurement.

The hospitals shall submit to the Secretary of Health a documentation of their operating procedures for procurement through the Ofﬁce of Hospitals Facilities and Standards and Regulations.

All preceeding orders and other issuances inconsistent with this order are hereby rescinded.

Except for goods and services with pricelists or contracts until December 1996, this order takes effect immediately.
