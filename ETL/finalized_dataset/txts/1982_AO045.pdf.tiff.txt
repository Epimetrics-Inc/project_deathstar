<subject> SUBJECT: Guidelines and related procedures for Accounting of funds allotted for the construction of Barangay Health Stations</subject>

<body>
To ensure smooth flow of transactions relative to disbursement/accounting of funds allotted for the construction of Barangay Health Stations under the amended Loan Agreement No. 923 PH (Population Project II) this Administrative Order is hereby issued for the guidance of all concerned. 

1.0 General Guidelines

1.1 Releases of allotments for the construction of Barangay Health Stations shall be taken up in the Accounting Division, Ministry of Health.

1.2 The Budget Division of the Ministry of Health shall prepare the request for Obligation of Allotment (ROM) to cover the cost of construction of Barangay Health Station.

l.3 Disbursement Voucher in favor of the Provincial/Emergency Hospital nearest to the chosen location of BHS shall likewise prepared by the Budget Division(MOH), to cover the remittance of the allotted fund for the construction of the Barangay Health Stations.

1.4 The Accounting Division of the Ministry of Health shall obligate the funds and process the disbursement voucher. The transfer of cash allotment shall be treated in the books as "Prepayment."

1.5 Check shall be issued to the credit of the account of the provincial/Emergency Hospital. The deposit shall be treated as trust fund in the books of said Provincial/Emergency Hospital.

1.6 Incurrence of expenditures out of the funds shall be in accordance with the disbursement procedure, discussed in 2.2 hereof

1.7 Expenditures incurred for the construction of the
Barangay Health Stations shall be paid thru checks drawn by the cashiers concerned against trust deposits.

2.0 For disbursement of funds for construction material.

 

2.1.1 The Barangay Captain prepares requisition and issue voucher to cover all materials needed in the construction based on the MOH specifications.

2.1.2 The Provincial Health Officer/Municipal Health Officer approves the requisitions and forwards papers to the-Municipal Development Council.

2.1.3 The Municipal Development Council Authorized representative prepares the canvas papers and purchase orders for the materials needed to be approved by the PHO/MHO.

2.1.4 The Barangay Captain receives construction materials in the presence of a Commission on audit Representative and signs Cert. No. l of the disbursement voucher prepared by the MDC.

2.1.5 The MDC authorized representative recommends the approval of the voucher by signing certificates’

2.1.6 The voucher is forwarded to the Provincial/Emergency Hospital accountant who signs Cert. No. 4
2.1.7 The chief of the Provincial/Emergency Hospital approves disbursement voucher by signing certificate No. 5.

2.8 The Provincial/Emergency Hospital cashier prepares check in accordance with existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations to pay claimants.

2.2 Procedures for disbursement of funds for payrolls of construction workers.

2.2.1 The Barangay Captain and the NBC authorized representative Sign labor contracts and forward contracts to PHO/MHO for approval.

2.2.2 The PHO/MHO forwards contract to the MDC payroll preparation.-

2.2.3 The Barangay Captain certifies in the payroll that services have been duly rendered. Authorized representative recommend the payment thereof.

2.2.4 The payment is forwarded to the Provincial/Emergency
Hospital to be approved by the chief of Provincial/Emergency Hospital.

2.2.5 The Provincial/Emergency Hospital prepares the check to pay the laborers.


2.2.6 Provincial/Emergency cashier prepare a report of disbursement on a quincena basis to the Project Coordinator. Said disbursement shall likewise be supported by voucher and supported by check stubs, three copies each of invoices, purchase orders, requisition issue voucher and contracts with suppliers if there are any, Creditors’ portion of the voucher shall be signed by payee.
2.2.7 The Municipal Developer shall submit to the Project Coordinator, Ministry of Health, the certification of completion of Barangay Captain, technical inspection by COA Representative and certification of the Municipal Development Council on the expenditures incurred in the construction within 15 days after the turnover. These documents as verified by the Auditor hall support the adjustment to the pre-payment account so as to charge the expenditure against the proper account.

    

This Order shall take effect immediately. Strict compliance with the above guidelines is enjoined of all.
