<subject>SUBJECT: Amendments to Administrative Order No. 293, s. 1976</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 293, series of 1976, concerning the revised Supplementary Rules and Regulations Governing Collection, Processing and Sale f Human Blood and Establishment and Operation of Blood Banks and Blood Processing Laboratories, is hereby amended insofar as Sections 5 and 7 thereof are concerned:

SECTION 1: Section 5 of Administrative Order No. 293, seris of 1976 is amended to read as follows:

"Section 1 - Human blood shall not be the subject of public bidding. It may be procured from licensed blood banks at cost."

SECTION 2 - Section 7 of Administrative Order No. 293, series of 1976, is amended to read as follows:

"Section 5 - an "Honorarium Fee" of at least P.35.00 shall be given a qualified donor to cover his meal transportation and other expenses. The operator of the blood bank shall not deduct any amount from the honorarium fee."

SECTION 3 - These amendments shall take effect upon approval and publication as required.

As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 293, s. 1976 stands in effect.
