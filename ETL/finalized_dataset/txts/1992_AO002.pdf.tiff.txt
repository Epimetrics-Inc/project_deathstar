<subject>SUBJECT : INSTITUTIONALIZATION OF THE FIELD EPIDEMIOLOGY TRAINING PROGRAM (FETP) IN THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH</subject>


<body>
In consonance with the directives of Administrative Order (A0) 118—D dated March 08, 1992, series of 1992, which states that "FETP has reached that point of maturity where it is appropriate to institutionalize it within the Department of Health as part of the Health Intelligence Service (HIS), the DOH Task Force on Reorganization is hereby directed to ensure that FETP becomes an operational unit of the Health Intelligence Service. As such, it must ensure that the functions of FETP as outlined in Administrative Order 118—D series of 1992, becomes 21 part and parcel of the total mandate of Health Intelligence Service.

To ensure that the FETP becomes an Integral part of the Health Intelligence Service, the Task Force on Reorganization is hereby directed to align available permanent position-items to absorb the contractual staff of FETP and provide other necessary items so FETP can fulfill its mandate.

All concerned are hereby instructed to observe this Order.
