<subject>SUBJECT: Further Amendement to Administrative Order No. 2006—0034 “Delegation of Authorities for Certain Transactions in the Ofﬁces Under the Department of Health” dated October 10, 2006</subject>

<body>

In line with the new functional clustering to achieve Universal Health Care and in the interest ofthe service, Administrative Order No. 2006-0034 dated October ID, 2006 regarding Delegation of Authorities for Certain Transactions in the Oﬂices under the Department of Health is hereby further amended such that the signing of orders/issuances pertaining to training and development of ofﬁcers and staff of this Department shall be approved by the Cluster Head of the Office who initiated the activity and favorably recommended by the Director/Medical Center/Chief/Chief of Hospital concerned.

This Order shall take effect immediately and shall only be rescinded/repealed upon issuance of the new delegation of authority.
