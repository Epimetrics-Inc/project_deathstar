<subject>SUBJECT: Payment of Salary Increase for Public Health Workers (PHWs) after Completion of Graduate Studies for a Degree Course</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to Rule XV, Section 6.2 of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers, the following guidelines on the grant of a salary increase for Public Health Workers (PHWs) after completion of Graduate Studies for a degree course are hereby promulgated:

1. PHWs who have earned graduate degrees shall be entitled to a raise in pay equivalent to one-step increment of the salary grade of their present position; and,

2. In case a PHWs present salary is at the eighth step of the salary schedule, it shall be adjusted to the next salary grade higher than his/her present salary grade.

The PHW shall submit to his/her respective HRMO/Administrative Officer the following authenticated documents to prove completion of graduate degree:

1. Diploma;
2. Transcript of Records from the school where the degree is obtained;
3. Certificate of Service for at least two (2) continuous years;
4. Latest appointment of permanent status; and,
5. A satisfactory performance rating during his/her employment.

The Personnel Services Division/Section shall evaluate the submitted documents and once approved a Notice of Salary Increment shall be issued for approval by the head of office concerned.

This Order shall cover employees under this Department as well as all PHWs in the government.

This Order shall take effect January 1, 2004. All other issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed/rescinded.
