<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT OF ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 2-A DATED FEBRUARY 23, 1997 RE: PRESCRIBING FEES FOR CERTIFICATE OF AUTHENTICATION FOR COPIES OF BFAD OFFICIAL DOCUMENT AND AMENDED CERTIFICATE OF PRODUCT REGISTRATION</subject>

<body>

In the interest of the service, Administrative Order No. 2-A dated February 23, 1997, Subject: Prescribing Fees for Certificate of Authentication for Copies of BFAD Official Document, and Amended Certificate of Product Registration, is hereby amended to include the following provision:

1. The company/clients shall pay P20.00 for lost Claimed Copy.

For this purpose, “Claimed Copy” is hereby defined as the third copy of the application form being filled up by the company representative when applying for authentication to the Records Section, Bureau of Food and Drugs, this Department. This copy also serves as tracer or locator slip for fast retrieval of the company’s document.

2. Certificate for Exemption from BFAD Product Registration - P100.00

All other provisions of aforestated Order remain valid and effective.
