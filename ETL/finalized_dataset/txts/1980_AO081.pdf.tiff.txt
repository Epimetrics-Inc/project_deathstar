<subject>SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling of medicines and drugs that can be maintained in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
To implement Section 9, Special Provisions of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa Blg. 40, hospitals and sanitaria are hereby given authorized ceiling for drugs and medicine that shall be in stock at any time including undelivered purchases.

The cost of medicines and drugs for initial stocking purposes shall be funded out of appropriation for maintenance and other operating expenses of the hospital/sanitaria.

The stock inventory as of December 31, 1979 shall form part of the initial ceiling of medicine and drugs for stock and resale.

The ceiling hereby fixed for the following hospitals are based on the submitted average monthly/quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines plus the current government inflation rate of 20%.
