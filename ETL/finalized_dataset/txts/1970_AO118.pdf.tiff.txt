<subject>S U B J E C T : Statement of Policies on Dental Public Health and Establishment of Minimum Standard Requirements for Dental Health Services in accordance with the provision of Republic Act No. 3814 and Executive Order No. 34 of the President, Effective July 1, 1966.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to the general powers and functions of the Bureau of Dental Health Services as contained in Section 2 of Republic Act No. 3814, to wit:

"SECTION 2. The Bureau of Dental Health Services is charged with the protection of the dental health of the people of the Philippines and the maintenance of adequate standards of dental health services and the improvement of such standards. Its general powers and functions shall extend to and comprehend integration, among other things, of the following: (Underscoring supplied).

a) The coordination, control and supervision of all dental health services in the government except those of the Armed Forces of the Philippines and dental infirmaries for the training of dental students in dental schools and dental colleges and those under the Department of Education.

b) Supervision of such dental health services in private firms, companies, corporations, and private educational institutions except dental infirmaries for the training of dental students in dental schools or colleges",

and pursuant to Executive Order No. 34 of His Excellency, President Ferdinand E. Marcos, providing for the implementing details of this law effective July 1, 1966, the following policies on Dental Public Health and minimum requirements governing professional and technical standards for dental health services of the above-mentioned agencies as contained in the manual entitled "STANDARDS ON DENTAL HEALTH SERVICES", prepared and published by the Bureau of Dental Health Services, 1970, are hereby promulgated as officially established.

In order to maintain, control, and improve the levels of quality of dental services provided to the public by rural health units, in hospitals, in private educational institutions, and in industrial establishments, strict compliance with these minimum standard requirements by all concerned is hereby enjoined.

All previous orders, circulars, memoranda, or parts thereof, inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are hereby rescinded.

This Order, consistent with public health policies and being issued for the good of public service, shall take effect immediately.
</body>
