<subject>SUBJECT: Line of Authority in the UNICEF National Committee created under R.A. 6130.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to the offioe set-up of the UNICEF National Committee in accordance with R.A. 6130, and in consonance with efficient personnel management, it is hereby directed that effective immediately the Manager of the UNICEF National Committee in the person of Mrs. GUILLERMINA E. RODRIGUEZ is authorized to assume full responsibility over the affairs and personnel of the said Office. As such, she shall be directly responsible to the Chairman of the Committee. She shall render periodical reports and recommendations to the undersigned.

All personnel concerned are hereby enjoined to extend full cooperation with the Manager in the interest of public service.
</body>
