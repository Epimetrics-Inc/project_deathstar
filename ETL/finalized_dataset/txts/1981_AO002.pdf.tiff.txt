<subject>SUBJECT: Additional delegation of authority to Mr. ANTONINO P. SAN JUAN, Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development to act on administrative matters</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 1 dated July 14, 1981 insofar as the delegation of authority to the Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development is concerned is hereby modified as follows:

a. "To sign appointments up to Range 55 of the Career Service except those of the Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan and those previously delegated to Regional Health Directors and to sign notices of salary adjustments except those of CES officers."

The Order, as amended, takes effect immediately.
