Administrative Order No. 2007-009 entitled Operational Framework for the Sustainable Establishment of the Mental Health Program, dated March 9, 2007, is hereby amended as shown below:

V. IMPLEMENTING MECHANISMS

A.1. National Program Management Committee

a. Composition

To ensure coordination and sustainability of the National Mental Health Program, a National Program Management Committee (NPMC) shall be organized in the Department of Health. The Chair of the Committee shall be the Assistant Secretary for Special Concerns, Co—Chaired by the Medical Center Chief, National Center for Mental Health.

All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 2007-0009, still remain in effect.
