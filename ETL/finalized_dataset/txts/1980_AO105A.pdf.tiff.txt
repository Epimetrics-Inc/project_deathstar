<subject>SUBJECT: National Primary Health Care Committee</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to the implementing guidelines for Primary Health Care, a National Primary Health Care Committee is hereby organized to be the coordinating body on Primary Health Care. The Committee shall be composed of the Minister of Health or his representative as Chairman and senior officials of the following ministries/agencies as members:

Ministry of Agriculture (BAEX)
Ministry of Education and Culture
Ministry of Human Settlements
Ministry of Local Government & Community Development
Ministry of the Budget
Ministry of Public Works
Ministry of Social Services & Development
National Economic & Development Authority
National Nutrition Council
Population Commission
Council of Health Agencies Chairman
Philippine Medical Association
Ministry of Health PHC Coordinator

The National Primary Health Care Committee shall have the following functions:

1. Set the guidelines and provide policy directions in the implementation of PHC;
2. Coordinate health and healthâ€”related activities of the various ministries/agencies;
3. Insure that PHC policies are translated to appropriate PHC projects by partner ministries/agencies;
4. Discuss issues/problems in the implementation of PHC and arrive at solutions;
5. Act on the recommendations of the Technical Working Group on PHC projects;
6. Facilitate the implementation of PHC projects; and
7. Submit periodic reports on the status of PHC implementation including results of evaluation of PHC projects to the Social Development Committee of the NEDA Board.

The National Primary Health Care Committee shall meet at least twice a year (once every six months) and shall avail of the services of resource persons from international and local health agencies, educational institutions and other government and non-government agencies as the need arises.
