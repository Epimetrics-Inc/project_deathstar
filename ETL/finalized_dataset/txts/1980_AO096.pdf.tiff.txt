<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority governing the signing and countersigning of Treasury Warrants.</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to COA Circular No. 80-26 (G) providing for the implementation of COA Circular No. 80-26 (F) concerning the amendment to the partial withdrawal of pre-audit, the following delegations of authority are hereby granted to certain officials, this Ministry, to sign and countersign Treasury Warrants covering claims which are not subject to pre-audit.

I. Officials authorized to sign Teasury Warrants -

Asst. Secretary for Finance and Management - No limit.
Department Budget Officer III - Up to ₱10,000.

II. Officials authorized to countersign Treasury Warrants -

Minister of Health or Deputy Minister - No limit.
Chief, Administrative Service -  Up to ₱10,000.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
