<subject>Subject: Authority for the Director, Regional Health Office No. 8 to approve Leave Applications and Travelling Expenditures of the personnel of the Schistosomiasis Research and Training Division of the Schistosomiasis Control Service, Department of Health.</subject>

<body>  

	In addition to Administrative Order No. 151 dated January 21, 1986 and Administrative Order No. 17-A dated March 11, 1987 which defines the funding scheme and accounting of funds for the Schistosomiasis Research 
Training Center at Palo, Leyte, the Regional Health Director is hereby authorized to approve Leave applications and travelling expenses of the personnel of the Schistosomiasis Control Service at Palo, Leyte subject to the usual accounting and auditing regulations.
