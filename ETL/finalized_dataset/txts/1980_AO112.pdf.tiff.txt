<docnum>No. 112 series of 1980</docnum>

<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority to Dr. Antonio N. Acosta, Assistant Secretary of Health, to approve and sign permits to operate/manage clinical laboratories and blood banks.</subject>

<body>
Effective immediately, the authority to approve and sign Permits to Operate/Manage clinical laboratories and blood banks is hereby delegated to Dr. Antonio N. Acosta, Assistant Secretary of Health. Under this delegation, Dr. Acosta shall sign as follows:

By Authority of the Minister of Health:

ANTONIO N. ACOSTA, M.D.
Assistant Secretary of Health

All administrative issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby rescinded.
</body>
