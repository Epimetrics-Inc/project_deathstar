<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to officials of the National Schistosomiasis Control Commission.</subject>

<body>
In order to establish responsibility and accountability for the most efficient operation of the National Schistosomiasis Control Commission, the Schistosomiasis Executive Director is hereby given the authority and shall be held responsible and accountable for the administration and supervision of the implementation and operation of the National Schistosomiasis Control program. To carry out the functions of the Commission, authority is hereby delegated to the following officials of the Commission, pursuant to Section 4(c) and 68 of Executive Order No. 288, series of 1958:

Section 1. - Approval of Disbursements.

a. Schistosomiasis Executive Director, for disbursement in any amount.
b. Administrative Assistant II, for transactions involving amount not exceeding ₱500.00 in any disbursement.

Section 2. - Signning of treasury warrants, request for obligations of allotments, and/oir countersigning of checks.

a. The officials who are authorized to approve disbursements within specific amounts, as provided for in Section One shall likewise be authorized to the same amounts when signing treasury warrants requests for obligations of allotment, and/or countersigning checks.

Section 3. - In the absence of the Schistosomiasis Executive Director, the officials duly authorized in the Office of the Secretary of Health shall approve disbursements, sign warrants and countersign checks above ₱500.00.

Section 4. - The authority delegated in this Order may not be redelegated. Consideration for any amendments in this order or proposal for authorizing other officials may be initiated by the Schistosomiasis Executive Director.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
