I. BACKGROUND/RATIONALE

Pursuant to Administrative Order No. 183 s. 2004, "Rules and Regulations Governing the Licensure and Regulation of Ambulatory Surgical Clinics" and Administrative Order No. 163 s. 2004 "Revised Rules and Regulations Governing Registration, Licensure and Operation of Dialysis Clinics in the Philippines", the Department of Health shall charge fees for the application for Permit to Construct and License to Operate Ambulatory Surgical Clinics and Dialysis Clinics. And as stated in Executive Order (EO) No. 197 dated January 13, 2000, these fees are collected so that persons receiving services for which fees and charges are imposed are made to share the burden of expenses incurred by the government. However, according to the same EO, these fees shall be uniform for similar or comparable services and functions.

II. OBJECTIVE

This Administrative Order prescribes the schedule of fees for the application for Permit to Construct and License to Operate an Ambulatory Surgical Clinic and a Dialysis Clinic, in order to share the cost incurred by the government in the issuance of permit and license to these health facilities.

III. SCOPE

This schedule of fees shall apply to all government and private Ambulatory Surgical Clinics and Dialysis Clinics. They shall be imposed by the Bureau of Health Facilities and Services (BHFS) in the issuance of Permit to Construct and License to Operate to these health facilities.

In the event that any section, paragraph, sentence, clause or word of this Administrative Order is declared invalid for any reason, other provisions thereof shall not be affected thereby.
