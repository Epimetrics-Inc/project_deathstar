<subject>SUBJECT: Delegating the authority to approve Requisitions and Issue Vouchers for Drugs and Medicines given to Regional Health Offices and other agencies to MRS. ISABEL R. NOLLEDO, Acting Chief, Administrative Services.</subject>

<body>
Effective immediately Mrs. ISABEL R. NOLLEDO, Acting Chief, Administrative Services is hereby delegated the authority to approve requisitions and issue vouchers for drugs and medicine issued to Regional Health Offices and other agencies by the Ministry of Health.

All such requisitions and issue vouchers signed under this delegated authority shall bear the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health" above the name of the signing official.
</body>
