<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81, 88 and 94 s. 1980 dated March 26, May 22 and July 10, 1980: Re-authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>
This order is being issued to supplement Administrative Order No. 81 dated March 26, 1980, Administrative Order No. 88 dated May 22, 1980 and Administrative Order No. 94 dated July 10, 1980, fixing the ceiling of drugs and medicines that can be maintained in stock by hospitals at any time including undelivered purchases to include the hospitals named hereunder.

The ceiling hereby fixed are based on the submitted request/average quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines.
