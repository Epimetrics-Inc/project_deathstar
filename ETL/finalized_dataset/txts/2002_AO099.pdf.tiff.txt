<subject>SUBJECT : Amendment of Adm. Order No. 47, s. 2001 on Delegation of Authority for Centers of Health Development and Retained Hospitals.</subject>

<body>
Upon representations of the Chiefs of Hospitals, local travels of hospital personnel for a period not exceeding ten (10) days shall be approved by the Chief of Hospital concerned. Travels in excess of ten (10) days shall be approved by the Director of the Center for Health Development of the Region.

For this purpose, Item A.2.2.8 of Adm. Order No. 47, s. 2001 is amended accordingly. Ail other provisions of the same AO stand in effect.

Travels of Chiefs of Hospital of less than 30 days shall be approved by the Director IV of the Center for Health Development (CHD) concerned, while travels of 30 days or more shall be approved by the Secretary of Health/Undersecretary (as designated), also under Adm. Order No. 47, s. 2001.
</body>
