<subject>SUBJECT: Authority for the Regional Health Director, Regional Health Office No. II, Tuguegarao, Cagayan, to exercise administrative control and supervision over the Major Ferdinand E. Marcos Veterans Regional Hospital effective December 1, 1979</subject>

<body>
Under Batasang Pambansa Bldg. I, the Major Ferdinand E. Marcos Veterans Regional Hospital has been included among the offices falling under the jurisdiction of Regional Health Office No. II although P.D. No. 376 dated January 14, 1974, placed the hospital under the Central Office. Due to its distance from the Central Headquarters however, the Chief of Hospital has made representations that it be placed directly under the Regional Health Office in order to expedite action on hospital operations.

Accordingly, the Major Ferdinand E. Marcos Veterans Regional Hospital in Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya is hereby placed under the administrative control and supervision of Regional Health Office No. II, The Regional Health Director, therefore, shall exercise all authority delegated under previous administrative issuance including those an appointment of personnel of the said hospital.

This Order takes effect December 1, 1979.
</body>
