Pursuant to Budget Circular No. 2001-02 dated November 12, 2001 in relation to the Presidential Instruction and Pronouncement as embodied in the Memorandum dated June 26, 2001 from the Head, Presidential Management Staff relative to the Granting of Extra Christmas Bonus or Similar Cash or Non-Cask Beneﬁt for Government Personnel as long as the Department/Agency has available funds to pay the said beneﬁt.

In this connection, all officials and employees of this Department who previously received their Bonus and Cash Gift for CY 2001 in accordance With the issued guidelines under National Budget Circular No. 2000-18 dated September 6, 2000 are hereby entitled to receive Additional Cash Gift equivalent to P10,000.00 each. However, the ofﬁcials or employees who have rendered less than four months of service shall be entitled to the said benefit pro-rated as follows:

<image>table_1.png</image>

In case of part time employees, they shall be entitled to one half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full-time employees.

The full requirement needed to implement this benefit shall be charged against any available savings of their respective Ofﬁces/Agencies, Centers for Health Development and Hospitals concerned subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

SUBJECT: CLARIFICATION ON THE USE OF AVAILABLE AGENCY FUNDS TO PAY EXTRA CHRISTMAS BONUS OR SIMILAR CASH OR NON-CASH BENEFIT FOR GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL

1.0 Purpose

This Circular is being issued to clarify tte use of available funds in relation to the pronouncement of Her Excellency, President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo, as contained in Memorandum dated June 26, 2001 of the Head of Presidential Management Staff (PMS), to wit:

“She would not issue a prohibition an extra Christmas bonuses for government employees as long as the Department/Agency had the funds, or the Department/Agency head should ook for a substitute program for the beneﬁt of their employees.

2.0 Coverage

Entities which are covered by or following Republic Act No. 6758 or the
Salary Standardization Law (SSL).

3.0 Funding Source

For this purpose, available funds shall mean savings which refer to such portions or balances of the agency’s budget, free of any obligation or encumbrance and which are no longer intended for specific purpose/s:

3.1 After completion of the work/activity for which the appropriation is authorized; or

3.2 Arising from unpaid compensation and related costs pertaining to vacant-positions; or

3.3 As a result of improved systems and procedures, cost saving measures, and. eff money where the agency was able to meet and deliver the required or planned targets, programs and services approved in the annual budget at a lesser cost.

4.0 Priority in the Use of Savings

Heads of entities are reminded to observe priority in the use of savings as provided under Section 56 of the General Provisions of the FY 2000 General Appropriations Act, as respected in FY 2001, to wit:

"In the use of, savings, priority shall be given to the augmentation of the amounts set aside for compensation, bonus, retirement gratuity, terminal leave, old-age pension of veterans and other personnel benefits authorized by law and those expenditure items authorized in agency Special Provisions and in Section 16 and in other sections of the General Provisions of this Act."

5.0 Reporting Requirement

Heads of entities may grant the herein benef t without need of prior approval/clearance from the DEM, SL bject to the rules provided in this Circular as well as to existing budgeting, accounting and auditing rules and regulations and submission of report utder Annex “A” hereof. Those who did not grant the some benefit are likewise required to inform the DBM thereof.

6.0 Additional Requireinentsmonditions

The grant of the herein benefit is further subject to the following conditions:

6.1 That no additional amount over the approved budgets shall be released by the DBM for the purpose; and

6.2 That no program or services shall be sacrificed as a result of the grant/payment of said beneﬁt.

7.0 Applicability to GOCCs and Lens

GOCCs and LGUs may adopt the herein guidelines, the funding requirement thereof to be charged against their corporate and local funds, respectively.

8.0 Responsibility of Head of Entity

The Head of Entity concerned shall be held personally liable for any payment of benefit not in accordance with the provisions of this Circular, without prejudice, however, to the refund thereof by the employee concerned.

9.0 Saving-Clause

Cases not covered by this Circular shall be referred to the DBM for resolution.

10.0 Effectivity

This Circular takes effect immediately.
