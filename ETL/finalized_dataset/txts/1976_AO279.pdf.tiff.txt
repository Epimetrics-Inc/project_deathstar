<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to officials of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service, this Department.</subject>

<body>
For purposes of carrying out among others, the functions of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service, this Department, and for the execution of its various programs, the following service officials are hereby delegated to exercise financial authority in accordance with Section 3-c, Article II, Chapter I Part II, of the Integrated Reorganization Plans as implemented under Letter of Instruction No. 8, dated September 24, 1972, as follows:

Section 1. - Approval of Disbursements.

a. Schistosomiasis Executive Director, for disbursement in any amount.
b. Administrative Officer I, for transactions involving amount not exceeding ₱2,000.00 in any disbursement.

Section 2. - Signing of treasury warrants, request for obligations of allotments, and/or countersigning of checks.

a. The officials who are authorized to approve disbursement within specific amounts, as provided for in Section 1 shall, likewise, be authorized to the same amounts when signing treasury warrants, request for obligatios of allotment, and/or countersigning checks.

Section 3. - In the absence of the Schistosomiasis Executive Director, the Administrative Officer I, shall approve disbursements, sign warrants and countersign checks in amounts up to ₱5,000.00.

Section 4. - The authority delegated in this Order may not be redelegated. Consideration for any amendments in this Order or proposal for authorizing other officials may be initiated by the Schistosomiasis Executive Director.

Section 5. - Accountability

The Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service officials delegated to exercise financial authority shall be accountable for:

1. Proper utilization of funds including:

a. Incurrence of obligation to conform with the purpose of the allotment, and in accordance with approved procurement programs;

b. The incurrence of expenditures not to exceed authorized allotments; and

c. Provision of funds to adequately finance all activities of the service.

2. For the proper interpretation of related existing fiscal policies, rules and regulations.

3. Attainment of results in accordance with the planned levels of work to be accomplished.

Section 6. - Rescinding Provisions.

All orders inconsistent with this Order are hereby rescinded. This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately.
