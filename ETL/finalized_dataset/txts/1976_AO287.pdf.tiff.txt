<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 287 s. 1976</docnum>

TO: Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors and Officers-in-Charge of Regional Health Offices

<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority governing the signing of certain appointments and the approval of requests of personnel to teach outside of office hours.</subject>

<body>
In line with the Department's present policy to provide responsive leadership in public health administration at all management levels, in particular at the regional level, the regional health offices being the Department of Health's extension arm in the rural areas throughout the country, and following further the declaration of policy under P.D. No. 1, the following delegation of authority are hereby granted to Regional Health Directors/Acting Regional Health Directors/Officers-in-Charge of Regional Health Offices:

Section 1. To sign the renewal of appointments of temporary health personnel with salary ranges up to Range 50 in their respective regional jurisdiction, except those of physicians covered by R.A. 1243 (Residency Training Law) provided that their services for the previous grading period have been rated satisfactory and that the pertinent provisions of P.D. 807 and other civil service laws, rules and regulations are duly complied with.

Section 2. To sign appointments by change of status from temporary to permanent of health personnel with salary ranges up to Range 50 in their respective regional jurisdiction in cases where the civil service eligibility of the incumbent is appropriate and his services are rated satisfactory and also in those cases where the NISA clearance has already been received by the regional office.

Section 3. To approve requests of health personnel for permission to teach outside of office hours subject to compliance with departmental and civil service laws, rules and regulations.

Section 4. Actions taken by the official concerned on these personnel matters shall invariably carry the words "By authority of the Secretary of Health" which shall appear over the signature of the signing official.

Section 5. A monthly summary report of the actions taken on the above personnel matters shall be submitted to the Department for review, together with certified true copies of the appointments acted upon and the approval of requests for teaching outside of office hours.

Section 6. This Order shall take effect on August 1, 1976.
</body>
