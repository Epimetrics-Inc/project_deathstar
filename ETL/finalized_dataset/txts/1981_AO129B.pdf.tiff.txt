<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 129-B s. 1981</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Additional delegation of authority to Dr. J. C. AZURIN, Deputy Minister of Health</subject>

<body>
In order to expedite action on administrative matters in the Office of the Minister over which policies and guidelines have already been established during my term of office and to enable the undersigned to devote more attention to the policy and implementation aspects of major health programs of the Ministry, Dr. J. C. AZURIN, Deputy Minister, is hereby authorized to sign all communications previously prepared for my signature, except the following:

l. Appointments of chiefs of hospitals and sanitaria, provincial and city health officers.
2. Administrative Orders which involve implementing regulations.
3. Ministry Orders creating committees; assignment of physicians for community service and RHPP; designation of Officer in Charge of bureaus and offices.
4. Decisions on administrative cases involving key officials in the Central Office and in the field offices.
5. Memoranda of agreements with other ministries and international agencies.
6. Other matters that I may wish to act upon personally.

The Deputy Minister shall inform the undersigned from time to time of action taken on important matters delegated under this authority for a more effective implementation of health policies and guidelines of this Office.

This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
