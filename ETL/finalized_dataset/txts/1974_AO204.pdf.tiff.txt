<missingtext> </missingtext> changes in the designation of officials who are <missingtext> </missingtext> under Administrative Order No. 61, series of 1959, as amended, to approve disbursements, Section 1, Paragraph I of said Administrative Order No. 61, as amended, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

Section 1. - Approval of Disbursements

I.-Officials Authorized to Approve Disbursements

1. The authorized representatives of the Secretary of Health are the following:

a. The Undersecretary of Health
b. The Chief, Financial and Management Service
c. The Department Budget Officer III, for transactions not exceeding ₱6,000 in any disbursement.
d. The Assistant Department Budget Officer III, for transactions not exceeding ₱2,000 in any disbursement.

Provided, that in the absence of any of the above officials, the officer following him in the foregoing line-up shall approved disbursements up to the amount the absent official is authorized to approve.
