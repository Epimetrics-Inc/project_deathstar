<subject>SUBJECT: Transfer of headquarters of Regional Health Office No. I from its present location in Dagupan City to San Fernando, La Union in accordance with the Integrated Reorganization Plan of the Executive Branch of the National Government</subject>

<body>
Under the provisions of the Integrated Reorganization Plan of the Executive Branch of the National Government, which was approved under Presidential Decree No. I dated September 24, 1972, the regional center for Region I is San Fernando, La Union.

Accordingly, Regional Health Office No. I which is at present located in Dagupan City, is hereby directed to be transferred to San Fernando, La Union within ten (10) days upon receipt hereof.

The Regional Health Director, Regional Health Office No. I is hereby directed to transfer all headquarters offices, together with their personnel, records, supplies, equipment to the Regional Health Training Center in San Fernando, La Union, which is hereby authorized to be utilized as the regional headquarters until such time that more adequate office becomes available.

Proper notification shall be made with other offices, both government and private, as well as the transacting public by regional officials in the interest of the public service.

The Regional Health Director shall inform this Office of the date the transfer has been effected and ready to transact official business at its new location.
</body>
