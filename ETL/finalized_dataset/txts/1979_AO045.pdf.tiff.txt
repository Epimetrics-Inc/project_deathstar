<subject>SUBJECT: Additional Delegation to Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors, Officers in Charge of Regional Health Offices Designated by the Minister of Health, To Exercise Appointing Authority Over Positions from Range 57 to Range 66 Subject to Certain Conditions</subject>

<body>
In order to further improve on the set of Administrative authority delegated to Regional Health Directors under existing administrative issuance in pursuance of LOI 448 and LOI 895. the following additional powers are hereby delegated to Regional Health Directors, Acting Regional Health Directors and Officers in Charge of Regional Health Offices duly designated by the Minister of Health:

1. To exercise appointing authority over original and promotional appointments and reinstatement to positions including supervisory positions below the level of chief of division, from Range 57 to Range 66, subject to the following conditions:

A. That general policies of this Ministry governing the selection, appointment and promotion of personnel embodied in previous administrative issuance as well as applicable civil service rules and regulations shall be strictly observed;

B. That certified xerox copies of all appointments issued under this delegated authority which have been approved by the corresponding CSC Regional Office, together with the sworn personal data sheets and personal history statements of the appointees shall be submitted in summary form at the end of every month to this Office for personnel review and control purpose.

All appointments signed under the delegated authority shall bear the words "By authority of the Minister of Health" above the title of the signing official.

Nothing in this Order shall be construed as a limitation on the power of the Minister of Health to review and/or overrule any action taken by the official concerned which does not conform with the existing policies of the Ministry.

The Regional Committee on evaluation and Protests shall undertake the resolution of protests Cases of appoinments signed under this delegated authority.

Any and all orders to the contrary are hereby supersedes or revoked.

This Order takes effect October 1, 1979.
</body>
