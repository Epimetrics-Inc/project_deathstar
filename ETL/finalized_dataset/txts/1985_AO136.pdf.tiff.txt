<subject>SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81 dated May 26, 1982, Re-authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by CAVITE MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Maragondon, Cavite. </subject>
 
<body>
       In consonance with Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa Blg. 866, CAVITE MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Maragondon, Cavite, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30,000.00) per quarter worth of medicines and drugs in its stock for resale, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.
	It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321 authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be created shall be followed.
