<subject>SUBJECT : Guidelines on the Issuance of Department of Health Identification Cards (ID)</subject>

<body>
In order to expedite the issuance of Identification Cards to all officials and employees of the DOH Central Office, the following guidelines are hereby adopted:

1. All DOH officials and employees should wear their IDs during office hours inside the DOH compound as well as: outside the DOH in the exercise of their official functions.

2. New employees shall accomplish the Application for ID Issuance form and submit it to the Personnel Services Division-Administrative Service, together with one (1) colored ID picture, for processing. The new ID may be claimed three (3) days upon receipt of the form by the PSD—AS

3. In case of loss, the employee concerned shall report it immediately to the PSD—AS and a notarized affidavit of loss shall be submitted thereupon, before a replacement ID can be reissued.

4. Upon an employee's' separation from the service, the ID should be surrendered to the PSD-AS.

All other Orders inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed/ rescinded.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
