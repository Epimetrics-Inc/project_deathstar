<subject>SUBJECT: PRESCRIBING FEES FOR CERTIFICATE OF AUTHENTICATION FOR COPIES OF BFAD OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS, AND AMENDED CERTIFICATE OF PRODUCT REGISTRATION</subject>

<body>

By virtue of article 17 (d) of R. A. 7394 Or the Consumer Act of Philippines, the following fees are prescribed for the following certificates issued by the Bureau of Food and Drugs upon the reqUest of any party other than the Department of Health or any of its agencies:

1. for certificate of authentication for copies of BFAD Official documents, such as certificate of product registration, license to operate, or Reports of Analysis: PHP 20.00 under regular lane; and PHP 30.00 under express lane.

2. amended certificate of product registration for food, drugs, devices, cosmetics and household hazardous substances: PHP 100.00

3. certificate of provisional permit to market food products locally produced for domestic consumption: PHP 100.00.

This Order shall take effect immediately after fifteen (15) days  following its publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
