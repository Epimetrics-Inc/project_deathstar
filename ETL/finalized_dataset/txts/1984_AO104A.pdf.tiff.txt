<docnum>No. 104-A, s. 1984</docnum>
<subject>Subject: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 54 s. 1983 to exempt participants in the NOH residency training program on Psychiatry, Radiology, EENT and Tropical Medicine from the provisions thereof.</subject>

<body>
 
	In view of the need to have more medical specialists in Psychiatry, Radiology, EENT and Tropical Medicine (Infectious Diseases) in MOH hospitals, those participants for residency training in above-mentioned specialties are exempted from the provisions of Administrative Order No. 54 s. 1983.	
       As thus amended, the provisions of Administrative Order No. 54 s. 1983 stands in effect.
</body>
