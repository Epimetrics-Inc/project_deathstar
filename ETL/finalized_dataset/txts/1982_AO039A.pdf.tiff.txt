<subject>SUBJECT: Amending Section I.2 of Administrative Order No. 29, s. 1982, relative to signing of checks</subject>
       <body>In the interest of the service and to expedite financial transactions affected by the withdrawal of the countersigning of checks and treasury warrants by representatives of the Commission on Audit under COA Circular No. 82-185 dated May 18, 1982, Section I.2 of Administrative Order No. 29, series of 1982, is hereby amended to read as follows:
	I. Signing Official
2. Special Projects (National Family planning Office, National Nutrition Service, Research Institute for Tropical Medicine, Dermatology Research and Training, Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service, and National Cancer Control Center)and line bureaus and office (Bureau of Research and Laboratories, Bureau of Quarantine, and Food and Drug Administration):
		Cashier – No limit
	Administrative Officer – No limit, only in the absence of the Cashier
This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
