<subject> Subject: Inclusion of the personnel of the Regional Mental Hospital and the Regional Laboratories absorbed by Medical Centers, Regional Hospitals or Provincial Hospital Components of Integrated Provincial Health Offices in the sharing of Professional fees derived from Medicare Patients</subject>

<body>
Under Executive Order No. 851 dated December 2, 1982, reorganizing the Ministry of Health, regional mental hospitals and regional laboratories shall be absorbed by medical centers and regional hospitals.

In at least two cases, the regional mental hospital in Region IV, and the regional laboratories in Region V, have been absorbed by the Provincial Hospital component (Andres Bonifacio Memorial Hospital) of Cavite, and by the Provincial Hospital component (Albay Provincial Hospital) of Albay, for reasons of expediency.

Accordingly; their personnel shall be included in the sharing of professional fees derived from Medicare patients confined in these hospitals.

 This Order which supersedes any previous order to the contrary takes effect July l, 1984.
