<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of Amelioration Assistance to Ofﬁcials and Employees of the Department of Health (DOH) for CY 1996</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to Section 35 and 36, Book V of Executive Order 292, the grant of payment of fringe beneﬁts (Amelioration Assistance) is authorized to DOH ofﬁcials and employees of the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, regional health ofﬁces and hospitals including specialty hospitals to help them overcome present economic difﬁculties, boost their morale and in recognition of their contributions and continuous efforts and to strengthen their commitment to public service

This beneﬁt shall not be more than FIVE THOUSAND PESOS (P5,000.00) which shall be given to each ofﬁcial and employee of the DOH regardless of salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, temporary, or contractual) subject to actual availability of savings of that agency either from Personal Services, MOOE or sub-allotments (except from CIA, CDF and FAPs) and the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations. Augmentation by the Central Ofﬁce and Regional Health Ofﬁces is authorized to their respective retained hospitals including sanitaria subject to the availability of funds.

To be entitled to this beneﬁt, an employee shall have been employed for at least four (4) months for CY 1996 as of November 30, 1996, provided that an ofﬁcial or employee who has rendered less than four (4) months of service shall be entitled to an amount proportionate to actual service rendered as follows:
