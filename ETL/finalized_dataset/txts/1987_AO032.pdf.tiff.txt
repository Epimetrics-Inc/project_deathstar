<subject> Subject: Further Amending Administrative Order No. 101 s. 1969 Relative to Registration of Drugs and Pharmaceutical Specialties. </subject>


<body>
In compliance with Memorandum Order No. 133 of the President dated 18 November 1987, and in accordance with Section 3 (b) and (c) of R.A. 3720, as amended by Executive Greer Re. 175, the Department of Health, through the Bureau of Hood and Drugs shall undertake a review of all registered drugs and pharmaceutical products. The policies and procedures for this review shall be provided for in a separate Administrative Order.

       In order to facilitate this review, Section 9.12 of Administrative Order No. 101, s. 1969, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

"9.12 The initial registration of a pharmaceutical specialty shall be valid only for one (1) year from the date of release. Subsequent renewal of registration shall be valid only for one (1) year, hence, renewal shall be annually to be charged a fee of thirty pesos (P30.00).”

 All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 101, s. 1969 remain in force. This regulation shall take effect immediately.
