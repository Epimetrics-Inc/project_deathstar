<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

TO : All Regional Health Directors, City, Provincial and Municipal Health Officers. 

<subject>SUBJECT : Survey on Hospitals, Clinics with beds regardless of number operating on 24—hour basis and Dental Prosthetic Laboratories.</subject>


<body>
       The Bureau of Licensing and Regulation under the Office of Standards and Regulation has intensified its drive to identify unlicensed hospitals, clinics with beds regardless of number operating on 24—hour basis and dental prosthetic laboratories in order to regulate them. These facilities should comply with certain standards to ensure the public of at least a minimum quality of care.

       Pursuant to Item No. 16.4 of Section 16 of Administrative Order No, 68—A s. 1989 dated April 3, 1989 entitled Revised Rules-and Regulations Governing the Registration, Licensure and Operation of Hospitals in the Philippines, ”The Director of the Regional Health Offices, Provincial,
City and Municipal Health Officers are hereby empowered to report the existence of unlicensed hospitals or any violation of these rules and regulations".

       All those concerned are hereby instructed to submit the names of unlicensed hospitals, clinics with beds regardless
Of number operating on 24~hour basis and dental prosthetic laboratories within their areas of jurisdiction, their addresses and names of their Medical Directors/Owners/Administrators to the Bureau of Licensing and Regulation through the Regional Health Offices not later than ninety (90) days from the date of this order. 

 

       Attached is the format of the survey which may be reproduced as necessary.

For strict compliance of all concerned.

 

</body>
