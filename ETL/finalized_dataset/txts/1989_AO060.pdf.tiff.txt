<subject>Subject: DOH Policies on R.A. 6683 on Early Retirement Law</subject>


<body>
Application for early retirement under R.A. 6683 on the Early Retirement Law shall be approved by the Secretary of Health on the basis of the following standards:

1. if the application is made by an official or employee who has previously been separated from government service not for cause but as a result of the reorganization pursuant to E.O.119

2. if the application is made  by an official or employee hose position would be abolished after his retirement. In these cases, the head of the agency in recommending approval of the application shall certify that (a) the position occupied by the early retirement applicant shall be abolished upon the retirement of the incumbent and (b) the normal operation and function of the agency shall not be impaired with the abolition of the position. If the head of the office cannot certify to this effect, then the application should be disapproved.
3. if the application is made by an official or employee whose position would be occupied by the promotion or transfer of an incumbent DOH employee whose position in turn would be abolished upon his promotion or transfer to the position vacated by the early retiree. In these cases, the head of agency in recommending approval of the application shall certify that (a) the position occupied by the early retirement applicant shall be filled by promotion or transfer of an incumbent DOH employee (b) the position occupied by the promoted or transferred employee would be abolished (c) the usual operation and function of the agency shall not be impaired with the abolition of the position. If the head of the office cannot certify to this effect, then the application should be disapproved
 
The above standards are established in order to achieve equitable treatment of all applications without compromising public services provided by DOH agencies.
