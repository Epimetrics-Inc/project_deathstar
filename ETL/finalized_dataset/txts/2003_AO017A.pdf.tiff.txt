<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines Implementing the Recommendation of the Oversight Committee on DOH Re-engineering (Resolution No. 002 dated June 19. 2002</subject>


<body>

In view of Resolution No. 0002 dated June 19, 2002, of the Oversight Committee on DOH Re-engineering, deferring the further implementation of the Re-engineering of the National Quarantine Office now Bureau of Quarantine and International Health Surveillance (BQIHS) for being premature and in excess of authority granted under OP MC No. 62 s. 2000, the following guidelines governing the effective operations of the BQIHS quarantine stations are hereby prescribed:

1. Operations of the quarantine stations shall now be under the jurisdiction of the Director IV, BQIHS, Health Operation Cluster.

2. Appointments of BQIHS employees whose positions were transferred to the different Centers for Health Development (CI-ID) per NOSCA issued by the Department of Budget and Management with the implementation of E0 102 will be considered at large and their assignment shall be done or effected by Department Order to provide ï¬‚exibility based on their services.

3. The BQIHS Selection and Promotions Board shall evaluate applicants to the vacant positions in the quarantine station per DM 50 dated April 4 2002 and their appointments shall be approved by the Cluster Head Health Operation Cluster.

4. Employees under the quarantine stations shall still be receiving their salaries and other benefits from the CHDs.

5. Maintenance and other operating expenses (MODE) of personnel under the quarantine stations shall still be drawn to the CHD pending approval by the DBM, of the DOH request to transfer the said MOOB from CHD to the BQHIS as part of the reprogramming as it submits its Agency Budget Matrix (ABM).
