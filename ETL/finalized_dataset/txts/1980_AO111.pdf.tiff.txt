<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 108 dated November 10, 1980.</subject>

<body>
For a most effective control, Administrative Order No. 108 dated November 10, 1980 is hereby amended such that the second paragraph thereof shall read as follows:

"Payrolls prepared shall be sent to the respective chiefs of services or bureaus concerned for their certification which shall then be forwarded to the Chief of Administrative Service for approval".

Accordingly, the chiefs of services/offices and bureau directors must promptly and strictly comply with this Order.

Any order inconsistent herewith is hereby rescinded.
</body>
