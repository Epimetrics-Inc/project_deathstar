<subject> Subject: Clarification: A.O. No . 74-C dated January 11, 1984 with Regard to the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine</subject>

<body>

	For the information and guidance of all concerned, it is hereby informed that the authority delegated to the Chief of Special Hospitals and other hospitals under the Office of the Minister include the authority delegated to the Director of the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine and the corresponding subordinate officers. This follows the Program/Project structure in Batas Pambansa Blg. 640, where the RITM is listed as 3.10 under Program 3: Operation of Special Hospitals, Medical Centers, and Research Institute.
