<subject>Subject: Banning and withdrawal of MERITAL 25/50 mg CAPSULES (NOMIFENSINE) from the market.</subject>
<body>
	Nomifensine which is marketed a MERITAL is an anti-depressant drug and has gained widespread clinical usage.
	However, the drug has been reported to cause severe cases of hemolytic anemia as its side effect which led to the voluntary recall of the product worldwide from the market.

ORDER

1. in the interest of consumer protection MERITAL 25/50 mg CAPSULES (NOMIFENSINE) is no longer registrable.
2. henceforth, the registration of the above-mentioned product is hereby recalled and cancelled.
3. existing stocks from drugstores, hospitals etc. should be recalled by the importer/distributor and destroyed in the presence of BFAD inspectors.
This order takes effect immediately.

</body>
