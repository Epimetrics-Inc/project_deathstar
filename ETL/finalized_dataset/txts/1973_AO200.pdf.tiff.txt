<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

TO : Heads of Bureaus, Chiefs of Offices, Regional Health Directors and Chiefs of Hospitals

<subject>SUBJECT : Supplementary measures to Administrative Order No. 198, current series.</subject>

<body>
These measures provide for the payment of salaries of dental personnel from the Bureau of Dental Health Services who are assigned in the hospitals and sanitaria and are to be integrated under Part II of Administrative Order No. 198, series of 1973, as follows:

II. Payment of salaries from November 1, 1973

"4. Hospitals and sanitaria will assume payment of salaries of all dental health personnel from the Bureau of Dental Health Services whose salaries are provided in their respective allotments."

Accordingly, the Bureau of Dental Health Services shall notify and furnish the necessary payroll deductions and such other records to the Chiefs of Hospitals concerned to enable then to effect the payment of salaries."

This order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
