These SARS Referral Hospitals are responsible for the following:

1. Appropriate management of SARS cases including referral and transport of patients, clinical
management, and disposal of bodies;
2. Ensuring availability of standard isolation facilities and procedures in all identified government .
and private hospitals;
3. Ensuring hospital preparedness and response; and
4. Coordinating with other infectious disease specialists at the national and regional levels.

This repeals previous issuance on SARS REFERRAL HOSPITALS.

This Order takes effect immediately.
