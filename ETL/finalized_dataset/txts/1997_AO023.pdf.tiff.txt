A. PURPOSE

To prescribe / implement standard guidelines for the procurement of drugs and medicines for devolved health facilities as provided for under section 7 of number 2 under Project Policy. 


B. POLICY 

This operation and implementation in the purchase of drugs and medicines by LGU under PAF 2 shall follow Executive Order No. 49 directing the mandatory use of the Philippine National Drug Formulary (PNDF) Volume 1 1996 Edition as the basis for procurement of drug products by the government and also A.O. No. 51, s. 1988 Section 11.1 - 11.4 (R.A. 6625). 


C. SCOPE AND COVERAGE

1. PAP II shall augment the regular budget of 5th and 6th class municipalities on items pertaining to drugs and medicines only. 


D. GENERAL GUIDELINES

1. Criteria for drug procurement 

a) Purchase of medicines and drugs shall be in accordance with the Generics Law and E.O. No. 49, s. 1993 .

b) The condition on the procurement of drugs and medicines shall be provided for in a MOA for PAF II. Basis for the procurement of drugs and medicines shall be on: 

* Morbidity cases and drug consumption reports of the previous years prepared by the Rural Health Unit.
* The Municipal Health Office shall prepare the procurement and distribution plan for endorsement by the Local Health Board to the Regional Health Office.
* Primary Medical Care Drugs to RHU with or without a physician (attachment A-1 and A-2) shall be used as the Official reference list.

c) Only DOHâ€”accredited suppliers are allowed to join the bidding process.

d) Only DOH Regional Field Office approved price list shall be used as basis for abstract quotation.


E. SPECIFIC PROCEDURES

Fund Utilization

a) Quarterly reports using PAF 2 Form 4 as per A.O. No. 291 On Physical and Financial Targets for Component Program shall be submitted by the Local Government Unit to the Regional Health Office with the distribution list,  vouchers, purchase order, official receipts, business permit and license to operate of DOH-accredited suppliers / dealers. The RHO shall likewise consolidate all PAF 2 Form 4 using the Consolidation Form on Resource Tracking and shall submit the same together with Form 4 to the Office of the Chief of Staff thru the Community Health Service, DOH, Manila.

b) In case of ARMM, the LGU shall submit the PAP 2 Form 4 together with the required documents to the ARMM Integrated Provincial Health Office which in turn shall submit to the DOH-ARMM for consolidation. The DOH-ARMM shall submit the consolidated report together with the PAF 2
Form 4 and liquidation report duly audited to the Office of the Chief of Staff thru the Community Health Service.

c) Reports submitted should be verified by COA.


F. EFFECTIVITY. This Order shall take effect immediately.
