<subject>SUBJECT: Addendum to Administrative Order No. 122 dated March 3, 1981.</subject>

<body>
An addendum to Administrative Order No. 122 dated March 3, 1981, which amended Administrative Order No. 83 dated April 22, 1980 designating Medical Centers, Regional and Provincial Hospitals in the twelve (12) regional offices of the Ministry of Health, is hereby authorized with the inclusion of the Severo Verallo Hospital among those categorized as a Provincial Hospital for the province of Cebu, Regional Health Office No. 7.
