<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 21 s. 1999 re: Deletion of Authority for Administrative/Personnel Transactions</subject>

<body>

In the exigency of the service and in order to facilitate the processing of appointments of physicians under the Medical Pool Office of the Secretary) and Doctors to the Barrios Program Administrative Order No. 21 s. 1999, dated June 25, 1999 is hereby amended such that the appointments of the physicians in the said programs shall be approved by the Director III, Health Manpower Development and Training Service upon the recommendation of the Chairman, Medical Pool Committee.

All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 21 s. 1999 still stand in effect.
