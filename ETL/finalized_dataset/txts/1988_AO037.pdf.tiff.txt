<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 37 s. 1988</docnum>

To: The Procurement and Logistic Service Chief and Other Service Chiefs
<subject>Subject: Subject: Increased Number of Suppliers to be Canvassed.</subject>

<body>
	In order to ensure that the Department of Health (DOH) procures its requirements from the cheapest source at the best quality, all items to be purchased that do not pass thru the bidding process must be canvassed from at least five (5) known/legitimate suppliers. Exceptions to this rule may be made under the following cases/circumstances.
1. Emergency situations whereby doing the canvas may cause delay and adversely affect public service or cause injury/death to people.
2. Purchases from the Procurement Service.

This Order takes effect immediately.
