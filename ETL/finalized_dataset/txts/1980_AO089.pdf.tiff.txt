<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 89 s. 1980</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Approval of transactions in Regional Health Offices that were unfinished by the former Regional Directors.</subject>

<body>
To expedite the liquidation of claims left unfinished by former regional directors when the rotation was implemented, the present regional directors are hereby directed to process and take appropriate action on such claims to enable them to close their books, subject to the availability of funds and to the usual audit.
</body>
