<subject>SUBJECT: Guideline in Securing Authority by Field Personnel Coming to Manila</subject>

<body>
In order to eefect greater economy in the expenditure of public funds broughtabout by the present fiscal restraints and to restrict official travel between the Central Headquarters Offices in Manila only to what is deemed urgent and necessary for the requirements of the service, the following guidelines are hereby issued for the information, guidance and reference by all officialsand employees concerned in field offices including hospitals:

1. Field personnel who will transact official business in Manila shall first secure prior authority from the Minister of Health or the Regional Health Director. In the case of regional officials, chiefs of hospitals, Minister of Health shall first be secured, except in case of emergency.

2. For lower echelon personnel in any field office including hospitals, prior approval by the Regional Health Director is necessary before coming to Manila to transact official business in any of the Headquarters Offices or other agencies.

3. Prior authorization to come to Manila from either the Minister of Health or the Regional Health Director will not be necessary in exceptional cases where there is eminent danger to life, property or threat of epidemics when urgent action is imperative.

4. All existing authority granted to MOH-affiliated associations to hold regular meetings on official business are considered revoked. New request may be submitted for study and decision by the Minister of Health.

5. Only the prescribed form of certificate of appearance used in the Office of the Minister duly signed by the corresponding officials authorized under exisiting administrative orders shall be recognized by the office concerned in support of official travel by those authorized.

Any and all orders to the contratry are hereby revoked, or superseded.

This Order takes effect upon approval.
</body>
