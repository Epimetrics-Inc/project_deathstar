<subject>SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock-by CAUAYAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Cauayan, Negros Occidental.</subject>


<body>

In consonance with Batas Pambansa Blg. 879 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, the CAUAYAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Cauayan, Negros Occidental, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Twenty Thousand Pesos (P20,000.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321,authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed.

The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached request.
