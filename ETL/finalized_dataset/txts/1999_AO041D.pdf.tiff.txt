<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment on Administrative Order No.118 s. 1992 “Approval of Resolution of BFAD Administrative Cases Recommending Administrative Cases Recommending Administrative Sanctions for Violations of RA 3720, as amended.”</subject>

<body>

WHEREAS, Section 26 (a); Chapter XI, of RA 3720, as amended, empowers the Secretary of Health to issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to enforce effectively the provisions of RA 3720, as amended.

WHEREAS, the existing rules and regulations in BFAD administrative cases need to be updated to make them more responsive to the demands for speedy and just disposition of administrative cases.

NOW THEREFORE, the Department of Health hereby adopts the following rules that shall govern BFAD administrative cases:


Section 1. The party adversely affected by the Resolution of BFAD administrative case may file a motion for reconsideration with the disciplining authority who rendered the same within fifteen (15) days from receipt thereof.

Section 2. A motion for reconsideration sent by mail shall be deemed filed on the date shown by the postmark on the envelope which shall be attached to the records of the case and in case of personal delivery, the date stamped by the proper office.

Section 3. Only one motion for reconsideration shall be entertained.

Section 4. The filing of the motion for reconsideration within the reglementary period of fifteen (15) days shall stay the execution of the Resolution sought to be reconsidered.

Section 5. All other office circulars and memorandum circulars inconsistent with this Administrative Order are hereby modified and repealed accordingly.

Effective immediately upon approval.
