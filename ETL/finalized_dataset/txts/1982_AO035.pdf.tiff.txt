<subject>Delegation of authority to sign all pertinent papers regarding applications for modification of Radio Station License and additional frequencies to the Acting Chief, Administrative Service, Ministry of Health.</subject>

<body> Upon recommendation of the technical staff of the General Services Division, Administrative services; the authority to sign all pertinent papers regarding applications for modification of Radio
Station License and for additional frequencies with the National Telecommunication Commission is hereby delegated to the Acting Chief, Administrative Service, this Ministry.


       Accordingly, the Acting Chief of the Administrative Service Shall be the authorized representative in all matters pertaining to Radio Stations in the Ministry and shall sign all papers relative thereto.
       	The following phrase shall appear above the signature of the said official: “By authority of the Minister of Health”.
</body>
