<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 35 s. 2000 dated April 12, 2000</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 35 s. 2000 dated April 12, 2000 is hereby amended to include the following:

1. authority for one Regional Smoking Control Program Coordinator to attend the Children and Tobacco Congress on May 31, 2000;

2. the transportation expenses and per diems of the Regional Smoking Control Program Coordinators, per diems of one day before and after the activity for the children delegates and their guardians shall be charged against the Regional Health Offices. The three days board and lodging for the Regional Smoking Control Program Coordinators in Manila will be charged against the DOH-Central Office’s Family Health Cluster; and

3. the snacks and lunch for the guests, NCR children delegates and their guardians shall be charged against the DOH —Central Office’s Family Health Cluster.

All other provisions in Administrative Order No. 35 s. 2000 still stands in effect.
