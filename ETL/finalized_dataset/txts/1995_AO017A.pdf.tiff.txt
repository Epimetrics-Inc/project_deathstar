<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines for the Procurement of Equipment for Hospitals</subject>

<body>

The life of any asset covers the processes of. acquisition, utilization, custody and disposal which in every step connotes responsibility and account ability as far as the requisitioning agency or the end-user is concern. 

Realizing its importance, the Department of Health formulated guidelines on procurement of hospital equipment in accordance with the existing laws and regulations that will ensure economy, effectiveness, and efficiency in the operations of the hospital.

Guidelines for the procurement of equipment for hospitals

1. Hospital equipment shall be bidded at the hospital.

2. Equipment to be procured shall be among the approved list from DBM.

Any modification shall be duly approved by the DOH-OHFSR and DBM.

3. All procurement of hospital equipment shall follow the standard specifications set by DOH-CO.

Any modification from the standard shall be subject to the approval of DOH-OHFSR.

4. Suppliers shall have branch office in the regions with personnel to maintain and repair the equipment.

Spare parts of the equipment shall be available at the regional branch office.

5. The DOH-CO shall circularize a price list of hospital equipment not later than April of the current year.

6. Only suppliers accredited by DOH/Regional Health Office/concerned hospital shall qualify to participate in the hospitaL bidding.

7. Acceptance testing of selected medical equipment shall be done by DOH-CO.

b) Other highly specialized medical equipment
