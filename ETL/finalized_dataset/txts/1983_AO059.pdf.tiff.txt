<subject>   SUBJECT : Designation of Dr. ANTONIO'N. ACOSTA as Acting Deputy Minister, Ministry of Health.</subject>

       <body>In view of his designation as Acting Deputy Minister by the
President of the Philippines on March 15, 1983, Dr. ANTONIO N. ACOSTA shall, in addition to the regular duties of his position, continue to perform the functions delegated to him under Administrative Orders
No. 1 dated July 14, 1981, No. 36 dated July 15, 1982, and No. 38 dated July 30, 1982 issued by the undersigned.

       Such communications prepared for his approval under delegated
authority shall be signed by him “By authority of the Minister of Health“.
