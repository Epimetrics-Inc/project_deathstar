<subject>SUBJECT: Further delegation of authority to the Chief, Financial and Management Service.</subject>

<body>
To facilitate the financial transactions in the interest of the service, further authority is hereby delegated to the Chief, Financial and Management Service, as follows:

1. Countersign treasury warrants covering remittances of national aid to regional offices, hospitals and other subordinate offices of the Department of Health.

2. To sign and approve vouchers and requests for obligation of allotments involving remittances of funds to the subordinate units of the DOH, when such payments merely involve allocations of funds previously approved and authorized by the Budget Commission and do not need exercise of discretion.

3. To sign details of expenses of special budgets of hospitals, provided the covering indorsements or letters recommending their approval shall be for the signature of the Secretary of Health.

4. To sign plans of works and requests for allotments, covering the programming of appropriations, provided the covering letters of indorsements recommending their approval shall be for the signature of the Secretary of Health.

5. To sign and approve Traveling Expense Vouchers, provided the corresponding iteneraries of travels have been previously approved and authorized by the Secretary of Health.

The Chief of the Financial and Management Service shall sign in the following manner:

CLEMENTE S. GATMAITAN, M.D., M.P.H.
Secretary of Health

By:

VIRGILIO R. FLORES
Chief, Financial & Management Service

Administrative Orders No. 164 dated February 1, 1972 and Administrative Order No. 175 dated October 16, 1972 are hereby rescinded.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
