<subject>SUBJECT: Guideline in booking up deposits to the National Tseasury by regional offices and national hospitals/sanitaria following accounting prescribed under B.C. No. 246.</subject>

<body>
Regional offices and national hospitals/sanitaria under this ministry that are following the accounting procedures prescribed under B.C. No. 246 and therefore do not maintain Treasury Account Current (70-700) ndividually, are hereby advised to book up deposits portaining to their respective offices thru a journal voucher entry beginning July 1, 1978.

The total deposits as shown in the Account Current at the back of the report of collection shall be taken up as a debit to the reciprocal account (81-71-100-199) an a credit to the account of the collecting officer (8-70-400) concerned. The remittance advice numbers with the corresponding dates and amounts shall be indicated in the journal voucher drawn. A copy of the journal vouchershall be furnished the Field Head Office Accounting Section of the Department Accounting Division, Financial and Management Service, this ministry, to facilitate responding entry in the reciprocal account (8-81-100-199) maintained therein.

The office mentioned shall also be furnished with copies of journal vouchers involving adjustments on the stated accounts such as debit memos.

A list of all remittances to the National Treasury from January 1 to June 30, 1978 indicating ermittance advice numbers, date and amount, that have been taken up in the bookds thru entries in the Journal of Collections and Deposits shall be furnished the Field Head Office Accounting Section for accounting purpose.

Please be guided accordingly.
</body>
