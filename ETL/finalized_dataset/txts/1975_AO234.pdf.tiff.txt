<docnum>No. 234 s. 1975</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of Administrative Order No. 229, series of 1975 renewing the designation as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspectors of certain law enforcement officers whose deputeation as such expired on December 31, 1974.</subject>

<body>
Item B (4) under the ultimate paragraph of Administrative Order No. 229, series of 1975, renewing the designation as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspectors of certain law enforcement officers whose deputation as such expired on December 31, 1974, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"B. Guidelines:

They shall submit an after-operation report of every activity and accomplishment to the Secretary of Health, copy furnished the Food and Drug Administration and the Dangerous Drugs Board, provided that if there are more than one activity or accomplishment performed in one day, a single integrated report of all such activities or accomplishments in one day will suffice."

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
