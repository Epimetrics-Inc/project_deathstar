<subject>SUBJECT: Revised Membership of the Bids and Awards Committees Created Under Administrative Order No. 33 dated July 17, 1979</subject>

<body>
In order to insure a more effective and expeditious implementation of the non-infrastructure program of the Ministry of Health, Item No. VI-2 of Administrative Order No. 33 dated July 17, 1979, entitled "Establishment of Pre-Qualifications Bidding and Awards Committees" is hereby modified as follows:

"2. Composition of Bids and Award Committees shall be as follows:

A. For the Hospital Non-Infrastructure Committee, the members shall be the following:

a) Chairman - Provincial/City Health Officer
b) Members - Chief of Hospital-
- Regional/Provincial Sanitary Engineer
- Hospital Administrative Officer

B. For Main Health Centers, Baranggay Health Stations Non-Infrastructure Committee, the members shall be the following:

a) Chairman - Regional Health Director or his duly authorized representative
b) Members - Provincial/City Health Officer
- Regional Administrative Officer
- Regional/Provincial Sanitary Engineer

The Chairman of these Committees should see to it that the deliberations thereof shall be witnessed by the Regional Auditor or his duly authorized representative in accordance with existing laws and regulations.

In cases where the Regional Health Director has entered into a memorandum of agreement with his regional counterpart in the Ministry of Public Works providing assistance on the technical aspects of the construction of hospitals, main health centers and baranggay health stations in his area, the duly authorized representative of the regional director of public works shall sit in place of the regional or provincial sanitary engineer.

Administrative Order No. 33 as thus modified stands in effect.
</body>
