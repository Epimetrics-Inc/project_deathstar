<subject>SUBJECT: Authorizing Dr. JACINTO J. DIZON, Director, Bureau of Health Services and Chairman, Committee on Biddings and Awards, to conduct a public bidding on janitorial services for certain offices under the Ministry of Health.</subject>

<body>
Dr. JACINTO J. DIZON, Director, Bureau of Health Services and Chairman, Committee on Biddings and Awards created under Department Order No. 88-D dated March 10, 1978, is hereby delegated the authority to conduct at an appropriate time a public bidding for janitorial services for the following offices under the Ministry of Health:

1. Nutrition Service - Approved contract will expire on 5/15/80
2. Schistosomiasis Control & Res. Service - Approved contract will expire on 6/30/80
3. Ministry of Health Main Building - Approved contract will expire on 12/10/80
4. Bur. of Research & Laboratories - Approved contract will expire 12/31/80
5. Dermatology Research & Training - Approved contract will expire 1/31/81
6. Food and Drug Administration - Approved contract will expire 1/31/81

Approval of the bidding made under this delegated authority shall bear the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health" above the name of the signing official.
</body>
