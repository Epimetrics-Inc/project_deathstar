<subject>SUBJECT: MORATORIUM ON ALL SENTRONG SIGLA ASSESSMENT UNTIL AFTER TRAINING OF ASSESSORS ON JANUARY TO FEBRUARY 2000.</subject>


<body> 

In view of the forthcoming structured training program for assessors which was proposed during the last Sentrong Sigla Re-orientation Workshop in Tagaytay City, all facility assessment is hereby put on hold until after the said training for Regional Assessors have been conducted.

The services of a firm shall be hired for the said purpose. Four representatives per region are enjoined to attend the said training.

For immediate and strict compliance.

By Authority of the Secretary of Health
