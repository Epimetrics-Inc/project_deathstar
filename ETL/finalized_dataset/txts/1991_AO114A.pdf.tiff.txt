<subject>

SUBJECT : Weekly Report of Sales on the Project “Gamot sa Presyong DOH”</subject>

<body>

To facilitate monitoring of the progress of the Project "Gamot Sa Presyong DOH", all hospitals concerned through its Project Manager/Chief Pharmacist, shall prepare and submit to this Office thru Asst. Secretary Teresita A. Dela Cruz, Office of Management Services, a Weekly Report of Sales of the ten (10) essential drugs/medicines in a sample form attached.

The report shall cover the period of one (1) week from Sunday to Saturday.

The report should reach this Office on Mondays, no later than 10:30 AM.

Compliance is hereby enjoined.
