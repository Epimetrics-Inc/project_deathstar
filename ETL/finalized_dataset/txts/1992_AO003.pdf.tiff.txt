<body>
In consonance with the directives of Memorandum Circular No.13 dated October 12, 1992 from the Office of the President, entitled “REQUIRING ALL DEPARTMENTS, AGENCIES, BUREAUS, OFFICES,INCLUDING GOVERNMENT—OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO POST IN CONSPICUOUS PLACES INFORMATION REGARDING THEIR FUNCTIONS, PROCEDURES AND'FEES RELATIVE TO TRANSACTIONS
WITH THE PUBLIC", all offices under this department are hereby directed to publish/post in conspicuous places in their respective establishments, as part of official duties of administrative heads, the following informative materials:

1. A brief and concise summary of the functions and services it renders to the public;

2. An easily understood work—flowchart indicating a step-by-step procedure to be followed by the public in transacting official business, with the names or designation of the particular government personnel tasked to attend to the public in each of the steps to be taken, being specified together with the time (hours/days) estimated to be consumed by the public in completing a particular transaction;

3. A listing of all applicable/imposable fees to be paid by the public for each particular transaction;
 
4. “A Bill of Rights" enumerating the rights and remedies of the transacting public in relation to the services or transactions with a particular government agency. Specific information as to who in each particular government agency the public could report the existence of fixers or cases of arrogance, malfeasance/misfeasance or neglect of duties of government personnel, should be also posted in a conspicuous place.

All concerned are hereby instructed to comply with this Order within thirty (30) days from receipt of a copy hereof. Non-compliance will subject the concerned government official or employee with appropriate disciplinary action under Section 46(b), (3), (25) and (27) of Book V of the Civil Service Commission of the Revised Administrative Code.
