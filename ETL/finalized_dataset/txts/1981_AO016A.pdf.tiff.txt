<subject>SUBJECT: Clarification of the provisions of Administrative Order No. 93, series of 1980, dated July 9, 1980, amending Section 4 of Administrative Order No. 87, series of 1980, dated April 28, 1980 regarding the apportionment and distribution of honorarium</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 93, series of 1980, dated July 9, 1980, was issued pursuant to the provisions of the Budget Program and Personal Services Itemization for 1980, page X-B-5, on Honorarium for Professional and Affiliation Fees, which covers only hospitals. On the basis of this, therefore, what was intended to be amended in Section 4 of Administrative Order No. 87, series of 1980 by Administrative Order No. 93, series of 1980 were those portions pertaining to hospitals more specifically sub-paragraph 4.1 and 4.1.1. This being the case, the provisions of Section 4, sub-paragraphs 4.1.2, 4.2, 4.2.1 and 4.2.2 of Administrative Order No. 87, series of 1980, remain in force and full effect.

All concerned shall be guided accordingly.
