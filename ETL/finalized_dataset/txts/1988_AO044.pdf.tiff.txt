<subject>Subject: Accreditation and Licensing of Private clinics & hospitals for overseas employment purposes to be discharged by the Bureau of Licensing and Regulation effective June 1, 1988.</subject>


<body>
	

Pursuant to the Provisions of E.O. 119, the Bureau of licensing and Regulation is vested with the responsibility of licensing & regulatory functions over hospitals, clinics, and other health facilities. In addition, the accreditation and licensing functions of private clinics & hospitals for overseas employment purposes are to be discharged by the Bureau of Licensing and Regulation effective June 1, 1988
</body>
