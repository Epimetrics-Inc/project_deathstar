<subject>SUBJECT: Control Cash Disbursement Ceiling Allocations by Deputized Disbursing Officers.</subject>

<body>
The responsibilities of Deputized Disbursing Officers in the control of Cash Disbursement Ceiling Allocations are hereby defined in this Order pursuant to the provisions of Section 3.3 and 3.3.1 of National Budget Circular No. 312, Finance Circular No. 9-79 and COA Circular No. 79-107, all dated June 11, 1979. Also provided are the responsibilities of heads of other units to provide the necessary support to the Deputized Disbursing Officers.

I. Responsibilities of Deputized Disbursing Officers:

a. The Deputized Disbursing Officer is primarily responsible for maintaining his check disbursements within the limits of his Cash Disbursement Ceiling Allocations; and

b. In carrying out his responsibility, the DDO shall use and maintain the CDC Control Worksheet to ensure that he does not exceed his CDC allocation. The DDO's Control Worksheet, however, shall not be used as the basis of journal entry in accordance with Section 5.1 of the stated Circular.

II. Heads of Accounting Units:

All heads of accounting units which prepare and issue treasury warrants shall furnish the Deputized Disbursing Officers concerned with daily summaries of the amount of tresury warrants issued.

III. Heads of Finance or Budget Units:

The heads of finance and busget units shall furnish the Deputized Disbursing Officers concerned with copies of their respective Cash Disbursement Ceiling Allocations.

This Order shall take effect immendiately.
