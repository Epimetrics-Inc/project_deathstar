<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 21 s. 1999</subject>

<body>

In the exigency of the service and until such time that the DOH Rationalisation and Streamlining Plan (RSP) shall have been approved by the Department of Budget and Management where Administrative Order (AO) No. 21 s. 1999 shall then also be fully revised, this Office is partially/temporarily amending the provision of AO No. 21 s. 1999 particularly Number 10 of page 4 of 11 pages, bearing on authority to approve Notice of Salary Adjustments (NOSI) and Notice of Salary Increments (NOS), hereunder is the amendment:

“The authority to approve NOSA/NOSI) which previously where to be signed by the Secretary and Undersecretaries concerned is hereby delegated to the Director III of Administrative Service”

All other provisions under Administrative Order No. 21 s. 1999 which have not been affected by this amendment shall still be in effect.
