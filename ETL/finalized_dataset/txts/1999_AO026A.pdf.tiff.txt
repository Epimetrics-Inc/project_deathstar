<subject>SUBJECT: Security Measures for the Central Office</subject>

<body>

A. VISITORS

1. Walk-in visitors who wish to enter the DOH Central Office compound shall use the Main Gate ( Rizal Avenue ). Only employees shall be allowed to use the San Lazaro and Tayuman Gates.

2. The Guard-on-Duty must screen in a very cordial manner all incoming guests and require them to register the following information: 

-Printed Name
-Signature 
-Address 
-Person/Ofﬁce to be visited 
-Purpose of visit

in the Visitor’s Logbook.

3. The Guard—on-Duty shall be responsible for recording the time of entry and exit.

4. Prior to entry each individual shall have to surrender a identification card (ID) or reasonable facsmile thereof to the Guard-on-Duty and shall be issued a Visitor’s Pass

5. Upon leaving the compound, the Visitor’s Pass shall be surrendered in exchange for the individual’s ID.

6. Employees should wear their respective ID’s at all times. Otherwise, they shall be subject to the above procedure.


B. VEHICLES

1. Upon entry of any vehicle, not displaying the DOH Sticker, the guard shall issue a Vehicle Pass upon surrender of the driver or passenger of proper ID.


2. The guard shall record the following information on the Vehicle’s Logbook:
-Time In
—Plate Number
-Vehicle Pass 
-Time Out

3. Upon leaving the compound the Vehicle Pass shall be surrendered in order to claim the ID

4. Government-owned and compound the Vehicles properly displaying the DOH Sticker need not secure a Vehicle Pass to gain entry.

5. Properly identified vehicles of members of the Diplomatic Corps, Houses of Congress and Cabinet Members are exempted from this restriction.

All concerned are enjoined to be guided accordingly.
