<subject>SUBJECT: Relationship between the Food and Drug Administration and the Regional Health Offices with respect to certain functions.</subject>

<body>
To clarify the relationship between the Food and Drug Administration and the Regional Health Offices, and to obviate overlapping of activities, the following guidelines are hereby established:

The Food and Drug Administration shall issue original permits to operate food, drug, and cosmetic establishments; the Regional Health offices shall issue renewals of such permits.

The Regional Health Offices, subject to policies set by the Secretary of Health, shall administer the food and drug services in their respective areas, except in the cities of Manila, Caloocan, Quezon and Pasay and in the Provinces of Bulacan, Rizal, Cavite, Batangas and Laguna, which shall be administered directly by the Food and Drug Administration.

All concerns are enjoined to be guided accordingly.
