<subject>SUBJECT: Designation of Department Heads in the Medical Services of Teaching and Training Hospitals in this Ministry</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to policies and procedures being undertaken by this Office in upgrading medical services in hospitals thru improvement of various residency programs, the assignment of heads of medical departments/services shall be made to the most qualified physician. During his tenure, the designated Head of a department shall evaluate, assess and recommend to the Chief of Hospital, one medical specialist of commendable technical and academic capabilities from the group of full time or part time specialists I, II, III for the position of Head of the Department/Service, with concurrence of the Medical Credentials Committee or any equivalent organized group.

Therefore in observing the above procedures, the following policies shall be followed:

1. Henceforth the Chief of Hospital shall recommend the appointment of Medical Specialists of all levels and categories without specifying the title as "Hospital Department Head".

2. The Chief of Hospital shall thereafter designate the Department Head chosen and recommended by the Medical Credentials Committee from the list of Medical Specialists. The Department Head shall have a tenure of not more than three years, but which may be subsequently renewed if so recommended to the Chief of Hospital. During his designation as Department Head, the Medical Specialist shall receive an additional honorarium of 30% of his original basic salary. Upon completion of a tenure for an appointee, said honorarium shall be transferred automatically to the new designated head upon entrance to duty.

Strict compliance is hereby enjoined.
</body>
