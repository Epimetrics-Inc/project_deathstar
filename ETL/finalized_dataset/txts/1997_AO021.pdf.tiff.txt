<subject>SUBJECT: Requirements for the Procurement of Equipment under the National Voluntary Blood Services Program (NVBSP).</subject>

<body>

Various implementation problems have been identified over the past three (3) year-operation of the National Voluntary Blood Services Program (NVBSP). One problem area is on the use and maintenance of highly technical equipment in the Blood Services Facilities of the program. Problem such as lack of skills for preventive maintenance and repair, improper use of equipment, fluctuations in electrical current especially in the rural areas, and the lack of health human resource to handle preventive maintenance and repair continue to impede efficiency of operation. In order to address these, procurement procedures need to be systematized.

In view of the above, the following requirements, aside from those required under existing rules and regulations, shall be stipulated in all requisitions and issue vouchers relation to the procurement of equipments under the NVBSP:

1. A listing of available service centers with their complete addresses, phone and facsimile numbers and the Head of the Service Centers. In cases where the highly technical equipments are not available for sale in the Philippines as determined by a certification to that effect, this requirement may be waived.

2. Commitment to provide training for selected personnel of this department as identified by NVBSP Program manager to form a core group of trainers for the use of these equipments.

3. Commitment to provide trainings for selected personnel of this Department as identified by NVBSP Program Manager to form a core group of trainors for preventive maintenance an repair of these equipments.

4. Provision of automatic voltage regulator, appropriate/compatible with the equipments procured which cost is already included in the total amount per unit of the said equipments. 

5. No cost warranty. In the event that equipments become out of order and/or non-fictional for whatever causes, no amount shall be charged to the party who had procured the equipments for service fees if the said equipments are still within the warranty period.

This order shall take effect August 1,1997.
