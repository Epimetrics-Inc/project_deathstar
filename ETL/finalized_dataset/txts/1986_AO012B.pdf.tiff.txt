SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by ELVIN MASIGAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Sta. Maria, Isabela.</subject>


<body>
In consonance with Batas Pambansa Big..879 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health; the ELVIN MASIGAN MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Sta. Maria, Isabela, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30,000.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases, the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for  maintenance and other operating expenses.

       It is understated that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321, authorized ceiling of drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed.
        
The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached request.
