<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

<subject>SUBJECT: Authority for Dr. Casiano M. Flaviano, Jr., Chief of RPS Hospital ng Tulungan, to approve requisitions for supplies and materials and to certify to availability of funds.</subject>

<body>
During the tour of duty of the RPS Hospital ng Tulungan, Dr. Casiano M. Flaviano, Chief of Hospital, is hereby authorized to approve requisitions for Supplies and Materials for the use of the hospital. He is likewise authorized to certify to the availability of funds for said requisitions. All transactions of the hospital shall be subject to review by this Office.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
