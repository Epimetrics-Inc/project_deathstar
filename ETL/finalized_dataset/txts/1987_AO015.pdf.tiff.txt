<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

T0 : The Chiefs of Hospitals, Sanitaria and all others concerned.
<subject>Subject: Amending Authorized Ceiling of Medicines and Drugs that can be maintained in Stock by hospitals/medical centers and Sanitaria under A.O. No. 81 series of 1980.</subject>


<body>
You are hereby authorized to increase the Fund Ceiling of Drugs and Medicines in your Hospital/Medical Center/Sanitarium (H/MC/S) to three (3) times provided that:

l. the increase shall be applied on the original fund ceiling authorized in 1980;

2. Hospitals/Medical Centers/Sanitaria that already increased their
Fund Ceiling after 1980 may increase their ceiling again as long as the resulting Fund Ceiling does not exceed three times 1980 levels.

       It is understood that the authorized ceiling for drugs: and medicines shall cover the value of all drugs and medicines in stock at the hospital at any one time plus the value of undelivered purchase orders.

       Attached is a copy of the letter of the Minister of Budget and Management regarding this authority to increase the ceiling of drugs and medicines for your information, guidance and compliance.

       In this regard, please make a report to this Office re the following:

1. If your new ceiling is still inadequate given your respective situation. Indicate the fund ceiling desired and the justification.

2. The result of the implementation of this Administrative Order, i.e. your new Drug Ceiling.

       Strict compliance with the guidelines and procedures prescribed under Joint National Budget Circular no. 521 and COA Circular No. 80—128 are hereby enjoined.
