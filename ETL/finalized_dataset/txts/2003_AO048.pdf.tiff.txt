<subject>SUBJECT: Classiﬁcation 0f Clinical Laboratories Based On Institutional Character</subject>


<body>

Administrative Order No. 59 s. 2001 “Rules and Regulations Governing The Establishment, Operation And Maintenance Of Clinical Laboratories In The Philippines” Section 5.2 states:

Section 5.2 Classiﬁcation by Institutional Character:

5.2.1 Hospital-based laboratory-a laboratory that Operates within a hospital
5.2.2 Non-hospital based laboratory-a laboratory that operates on its own

In response to the need for a more comprehensive and appropriate classiﬁcation of clinical laboratories suited for the prevailing setting, Section 5.2 of Administrative Order No. 59 s. 2001 is hereby amended as follows:

Section 5.2 Classiﬁcation by Institutional Character:

5.2.1 Institution-based laboratory— a laboratory that operates within the premises and operates as part of an institution (e.g. hospital, multi-specialty clinics, schools, medical facilities for overseas workers and seafarers).

5.2.2 Freestanding laboratory- a laboratory that operates outside the premises of an institution and operates independently.

All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 59 s. 2001 shall remain in full force and effect.
