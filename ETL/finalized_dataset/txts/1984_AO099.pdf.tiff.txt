<subject>
SUBJECT : : Additional Delegation of authority to Dr. ANTONIO N. ACOSTA, Acting Deputy Minister, to sign contractual appointments.</subject>
<body>
In order to further expedite action on contractual employment, the authority to sign contracts of services with this Ministry is hereby delegated to Dr. ANTONIO N. ACOSTA, Acting Deputy Minister, in addition to those duties-previously delegated to him.

In the exercise or this delegation, he shall be guided by existing Ministry policies relevant thereto as well as pertinent regulations issued by other offices.

Contractual appointments signed under this delegated power shall bear the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health above that of the signing official.
This Order takes effect immediately.
