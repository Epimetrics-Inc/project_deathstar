<subject>SUBJECT: Merger of Selection Board and Promotion Board into Selection Board and providing for the Guidelines Thereof</subject>

<body>


To ensure uniformity and simplicity in the implementation of policies, standards and procedures on personnel selection and promotion systems, the Civil Service Commission hereby prescribes the following Guidelines in accordance with CSC Resolution No. 87-405 dated October 23, 1987 and No. 68-520 dated August 1, 1988:

1. There shall be created/established in each department/ agency of the government only one Board to be known as the Selection Board to be composed of personnel therein. The functions of the Selection Board shall cover both the recruitment and selection of employees in the first and second level of the career service who are entering the civil service for the first time or are being re-employed/reinstated, and the screening of employees who are considered for promotion. The Board shall have the following composition:

a) An official of the department/agency directly responsible for personnel management;

b) A representative of management;

c) A representative of the organizational unit which may be an Office, Department or Division, where the vacancy is;

d) Two representatives of the rank and file employees, one from the first level and one from the second
level who shall both be chosen by the duly registered/accredited employee association in the depart ment or agency. The former shall sit during the screening of candidates for vacancies in the first level, while the latter shall participate in the
screening of candidates for vacancies in the second level. In cases where there are no employee associations in the department or agency, the representatives shall be chosen at large by the employees through a general election to be called for the purpose.

At the regional level, the Board may have three (3) members: Chief/Head of the Administrative Office/Unit, a representative of the rank and file employees who may be chosen by the employees thereat, and a representative of the organizational unit where the vacancy is.

2. The CGC shall no longer appoint representatives to sit as members in said Selection Board. However, the
CSC Field Office may be consulted to clarity relevant issues on Civil Service law and rules.

THIS MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR SHALL TAKE EFFECT IMMEDIATELY.
