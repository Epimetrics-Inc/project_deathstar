<subject>SUBJECT: Addendum to Item 1 - re. Operational Guidelines in the conduct of the National Staff Meeting</subject>


<body>  
 
Item No. 1 of Administrative Order No. 12-B s. 1995 regarding the composition of the National Staff Meeting is hereby amended to include the following:

H. Medical Center Chiefs of Medical Centers and Specialty Hospitals and Executive Directors of Corporate Hospitals who shall join the National Staff Meetings once avery quarter to be announced by the Secretariat.

As thus amended, AO No. 12 - B s. 1995 stands in effect.
