<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 244 s. 1975</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of Administrative Order No. 240 dated April 7, 1975.</subject>

<body>
To effect greater efficiency and effectiveness in the conduct of governmental transactions in the regional health offices, and inasmuch as the regional health offices are now fully staffed with trained fiscal and administrative personnel, Administrative Order No. 240 dated April 7, 1975, is hereby amended effective immediately to increase from ₱5,000 to ₱50,000 the amount of purchase of supplies, materials, and equipment that may be approved by the regional directors. The regioal directors shall establish budgetary controls to ensure that allotments are judiciously allocated to various items of expenditures under maintenance and other operating expenses, to prevent the incurrence of overdrafts.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
