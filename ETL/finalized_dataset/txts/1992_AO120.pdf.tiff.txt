<subject>SUBJECT: Security Measures for Tayuman Side Gates</subject>

<body>
In order to improve the security, as well as foster measures shall be observed effective April 20, 1992.

On regu1ar working days

1. The gates fronting Tayuman street, adjacent to better control within the DOH Compound, the following the Crematory and Malaria Buildings shall be closed to vehicular traffic from 9:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M.
5:00 P.M. to 5:00 A.M.

2. Vehicles on official business shall use the and main gate (Rizal Avenue) as their entry and exit point;

On-holidays and other non—working days
1. All gates shall remain closed to vehicular traffic for the entirety of the day

2. In case of emergency situation access to the compound shall be thru the main gate.

For strict compliance.
