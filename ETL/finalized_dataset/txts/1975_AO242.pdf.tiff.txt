<subject>AMENDING SECTION 5 OF ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 184, SERIES OF 1972 CONCERNING THE COST OF BLOOD</subject>

<body>
SECTION 1 - The provisions of Section 5 of Administrative Order No. 184, series of 1972, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"Section 5 - Human blood shall not be subject of public bidding. It may be procured from licensed blood banks at cost. The costs of the different types of human blood are as follows:

<image>table_1.png</image>

If plastic bags are utilized, an additional cost of ₱5.00 may be added. An additional cost of ₱25.00 for each type of human blood may be charged if all the following tests are undertaken (1) microhematocrit; (2) Spectrophotometric determination of hemoglobin; and (3) detection of Australian antigen.

An "honorarium" fee of at least ₱30.00 shall be given a qualified donor to cover his meal, transportation, and other expenses."

SECTION 2 - This amendment shall take effect upon approval and publication as required.
