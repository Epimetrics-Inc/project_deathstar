<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to A.O. No. 76 s, 2002 dated February 5, 2002 RE: Additional roles and functions of the National Infectious Disease Advisory Council (NIDAC)</subject>


<body>

In line with the Administrative Order No. 76 s., 2002 dated February 5, 2002, additional roles and functions of the newly organized National Infectious Disease Advisory Council (NIDAC) is hereby amended to fully function as working committee as indicated hereunder:

1. Function as the Country Coordination Mechanism (CCM) for the Global Fund to fight AIDS, TB and Malaria.

2. Authorize to provide technical consultancy in the development of proposals related to TB, Malaria and AIDS and other infectious diseases.

3. Designate a competent trustee in charge for the overall ﬁnancial/program accountability which includes proper utilization of funds for the intended purpose; disburse funds to partners efﬁciently and transparently from the disbursing trustee chosen by the committee to implementing partners.

4. Monitor the implementation of the fund’s programs that deal with the three diseases and other infectious diseases.
