<subject>SUBJECT : IMPORTATION AND DONATION 0F RELIEF COMMODITIES FOR USE IN THE RELIEF AND REHABILITATION PROGRAMS OF THE GOVERNMENT
FOR CALAMITY—AFFECTED AREAS</subject>


<body>
Memorandum Order No. 350 dated March 14, 1991 issued by the Office of the President lays down the guidelines on the importation and donation of food, clothing, medicine and other relief commodities for use in the relief and rehabilitation programs of the Department of Health and the Department of Social Welfare and Development, for calamity-affected areas.

In this connection, the Foreign Assistance Coordination-Service (FACS) is hereby designated as the office responsible for coordinating Department of Health actions regarding the efficient and effective implementation of the provisions of said Memorandum, a copy of which is attached, for information.
