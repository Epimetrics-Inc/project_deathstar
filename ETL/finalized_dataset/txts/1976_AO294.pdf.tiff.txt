<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to sign Notice of Minimum Salary Increase under Budget Circular No. 262, dated August 11, 1976.</subject>

<body>
In order to effect the early release of the Notices for the minimum salary increase authorized under Budget Circular No. 262, dated August 11, 1976, of full-time employees whether permanent, temporary, emergency or casual, the following delegation of authority is hereby ordered:

1. The "Notice of Minimum Salary Increase" for employees of the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, and the Bureau of Quarantine, shall be signed by the respective Directors of the said Bureaus;

2. The "Notice of Minimum Salary Increase" for employees under the Malaria Eradication Service, National Nutrition Service, and National Family Planning Office, shall be signed by the respective Directors of the said Projects;

3. The "Notice of Minimum Salary Increase" for employees of the Food and Drug Administration and the Schistosomiasis Control and Research, shall be signed by the Administrator and Executive Director, respectively, of the said Offices;

4. The "Notice of Minimum Salary Increase" for employees under the Regional Health Offices shall be signed by the Regional Health Directors concerned. In the absence of the Regional Director, the Administrative Officer III of the Region concerned shall sign the notice; and

5. The "Notice of Minimum Salary Increase" for employees in the Special Training Hospitals and those of the Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital and Medical Center and of the Quirino Memorial General Hospital operating under the immediate supervision and control of the Secretary of Health, shall be signed by the Chief of Hospital concerned.

In authorizing the payment of the minimum salary or wage increase, the provisions of Budget Circular No. 262 dated August 11, 1976 shall be strictly observed and complied with. It is understood that the basis of computation for the said increase is the present salary or wage of the employee concerned as of April 30, 1976.

All notices issued in compliance with this delegation of authority shall invariably carry the words "By authority of the Secretary of Health" which shall appear over the signature of the signing official.

Sufficient copies of the Notice shall be prepared, one copy to be retained by the Office or Agency concerned, one copy each to be furnished the Accounting Division and the Personnel Division, this Department, one copy for the Budget Commission, one copy for the Civil Service Commission, one copy for the Commission on Audit, one copy for the Government Service Insurance System and one copy for the WAPCO.

This delegated authority shall be effected and good only for the current fiscal year.
