<subject>SUBJECT: Approval by the Minister of Purchases of Drugs, Medicines, and Equipment of the Central Luzon Sanitarium</subject>

<body>
To attain a more effective utilization of the resources of the Central Luzon Sanitarium, all its purchases of drugs, medicines, and equipment shall be submitted to the Office of the Minister for review and approval, effective immediately. The Review Committee of the Office of the Minister shall receive the pertinent papers direct from the Sanitarium for immediate review and recommendation, to avoid delay.

Any provision of existing issuances contrary to this Order is hereby rescinded.
</body>
