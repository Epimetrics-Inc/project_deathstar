<subject>SUBJECT: Authorizing the Budget Officer in Charge of Fiscal Control, Budget Division, Office of the Secretary, to Approve Disbursements and Sign Checks and Treasury Warrants.</subject>

<body>
To relieve the Department Budget Officer III of too much routine transactions and allow him to devote more time to fiscal planning and analysis, the Budget Officer in Charge of the Fiscal Control Section of the Budget Division, Office of the Secretary, is hereby authorized to approve disbursements not exceeding ₱1,000 in any transaction. He is likewise authorized to sign checks and treasury warrants covering transactions of the same amount.

This order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
</body>
