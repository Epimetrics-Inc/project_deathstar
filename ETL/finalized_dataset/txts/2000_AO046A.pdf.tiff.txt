<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines in Payment of CY 2000 Hazard Pay</subject>

<body>


Pursuant to the provisions of Sec. 7.1.5.b of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations of R.A. 7305, otherwise known as the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers, which prescribes the schedule at implementation of hazard allowance rate for the period 1999 to 2003, following are the guidelines for computation and payment of the current year's hazard pay:

1. All health workers with Salary Grades 19 and below shall receive a monthly hazard allowance equivalent to 10% of their basic monthly salary.
2. All Health workers with Salary Grades 20 and above shall receive the equivalent of 10% at a preset benchmark amount based on SG-19 Step 8 per month.

This Order shall take effect as of January 01, 2000.
