To facilitate the utilisation of the Bed Subsidy for Private Hospitals, the following provisions stipulated in the aforesaid order are hereby amended as follows:

I. On Section III. A, items 1 and 2:

A. Hospital facilities and beds

1. All private hospitals, duly licensed, shall be eligible for the program subsidy;

2. a fixed number of beds not exceeding 5 for primary hospital and 10 for secondary hospitals shall be subsidized depending on the socio-economic condition, morbidity status and population size of the area.


II. Section III-B, items 1, 1.1:

B. Patients and Entitlements


1. Only the following patients shall be subsidized under the program

1.1 Indigent as certified by a municipal Social Worker, in his or her absence, either the barangay chairman, municipal health officer, medical social worker or chief of the nearest district or provincial hospital.

Other provisions that are not affected by this amendment shall remain in effect.

This Order takes effect immediately.
