<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 154 dated 16 November 2000 re: Revised Guidelines for Delegation of Authority for certain Administrative Transactions in the Central Ofﬁce, Department of Health (DOH)</subject>


<body>

In order to facilitate the transactions of the two line bureaus, Bureau of Food and Drugs and Bureau of Quarantine and International Heaith Surveillance which are located in Alabang City and Port Area, Manila, respectively, Administrative Order No. 154 dated 16 November 2000 is hereby amended such that the two Directors of the said line bureaus are given authority to signlapprove the foitowing administrative transactions below Division Chief positions:

1) Appointments in the ﬁrst and second level positions

2) Certiﬁcation of assumptionlentrance to duty

3) Oath of office

4) Notice of Salary Adjustments/Increments (NOSI/NOSA)

5) Permission to teach, exercise, profession, or engage in private practice outside ofﬁce hours pursuant to RA 6713 Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards, Section 7 on prohibited acts and transactions item B No. 2.

6) Sick, vacation leave regardless of the number of days, special privilege leave, maternity, terminal leave of 30 days or more, including monetization and commutation of money value

7) Acceptance of resignation

8) Dropping from the rolls of the service

9) Appiication for retirement

10) Request for the investigation of administrative complaints and the submission of appropriate recommendations

11) DOH clearance

12) Authority to render overtime services

As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 154 dated 16 November still stands in effect.
