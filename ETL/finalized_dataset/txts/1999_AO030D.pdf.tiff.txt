<subject>SUBJECT: AMENDMENT TO THE GUIDELINES ON THE MANAGEMENT OF THE 1999 HEALTH DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM (HDP) AND COMMUNITY HEALTH SERVICE PROGRAM (CHSP) FUNDS FOR THE INTER-LOCAL GOVERNMENT HEALTH ZONES</subject>

<body>


Guidelines on the Management of the 1999 Health Development Program (HDP) fund
and Community Health Service Program (CHSP) Fund for the Inter—Local Government
Health Zones is hereby amended as follows:

1. Release of funds to ARMM shall be by way of Transfer of Fund instead of sub-allotment.

2. Amendments to the intended Beneficiaries by Regions of the sub—allotment (Annex "A") is as follows :

* Region X - to exclude Surigao Norte and Cagayan de Oro
- replace (1) Misamis Or. with Misamis Occ

* Region XI - to include Campostela Valley

* Region XII - to exclude Iligan City

* ARMM - to include the following provinces in the transfer of fund:
	* Sulu
	* Tawi-tawi
	* Lanao del Sur
	* Maguindanao

2. The Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (MOOE 200-10) stipulated in the Guidelines, also include appropriations for 200-02,07,17 and 29;
and3. CHSP and HDP fund grant disbursements to be provided to NGOs/POs, and LGUs, shall be in accordance with the established PHC/PCHD for HDP and LPP fund grant mechanism for LGUs.

4. CHSP fund shall be utilised to support the inter-LGU work planning and cooperation activities nationwide. Since CHSP Funds were taken from the 1998 Budget (CONAP), it shall only be utilized until December 31, 1999.

5. HDP funds shall be used to support the inter-LGU activities for the pilot district health system within the province as selected by the Governor/Mayor concerned.

All other provisions in the amended Guidelines remain in effect.
