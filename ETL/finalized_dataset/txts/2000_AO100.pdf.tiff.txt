<subject>SUBJECT: ADDENDUM TO ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 16-A S. 1999 “GUIDELINES ON THE ISSUANCE OF CERTIFICATION OF DISABILITY TO PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES”</subject>
