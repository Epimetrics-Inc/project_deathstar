Administrative Order No.2005-0022 dated August 25,2005 (Subject:Amendment to Administrative Order No. 164 s. 2002: Revised Guidelines on Management of Animal Bite Patients) on the Immunization Program Policies is further amended such that all the words "Free Cell" used in the said A.O. shall be changed,

From: Free Cell

To : VeroCell

All other provisions stipulated in Administrative Order. 164,. s, 2002 & AO No. 2005-0022 not affected by this Order still stand in effect.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
