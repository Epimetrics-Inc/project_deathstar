<subject>SUBJECT: COMPLIANCE WITH THE DOCUMENTARY AND PROCEDURAL REQUIREMENTS FOR FELLOWSHIPS AND OTHER TRAVEL GRANTS</subject>

<body>

In order to ensure that appropriate and deserving health personnel are given the opportunity to avail of foreign fellowships and other travel grants, and to facilitate the processing and approval of nominations and travel documents, the relevant provisions of the following administrative issuances should be strictly observed:

* Executive Order No. 248, dated 29 May 1995, as amended by E.O. 248-A, dated 14 August 1995 (Prescribing Rules and Regulations and New Rates of Allowances for Official Local and Foreign Travels of Government Personnel)

* Administrative Order No. 13-A,s. 1997 (Guidelines on Local and Foreign Fellowships)

* Administrative Order No. 21,s. 1999 (Delegation of Authority for Administrative/Personnel Transactions)

* Administrative Order No. 25,s. 1999 (Lead Time and Requirements for Approval of Department Orders, Activities and Communications)

For purposes of securing EXECOM concurrence to the nomination and/or travel authorization of DOH personnel, the attached EXECOM Certificate of Clearance shall serve as the official endorsement for any nominee or travel grantee to be issued a nomination letter and/or travel authority to be prepared by the Foreign Assistance Coordination Service (FACS), and a Department Order to be prepared by the Health Manpower and Training Service (HMDTS), to be signed by the Secretary of Health or by
the Undersecretary of Health and Chief of Staff, or by the designated Officer-inâ€”Charge, as the case may be. 

Beginning September 15, 1999, non-complying nomination and travel papers shall no longer be entertained or processed by the Fellowships Committee, HMDTS, FACS, and EXECOM except in special, meritorious cases as determined by the DOH management.

Strict compliance by all concerned is hereby enjoined.
