<subject>SUBJECT : Authority of the Procurement Division to Undertake Alternative Methods of Procurement</subject>

<body>
Pursuant to Rule XVI - Alternative Methods of Procurement of the Implementing Rules and Regulations of Republic Act No. 9184 (IRR-RA 9184), which provides for the use of appropriate alternative methods of procurement, in the interest of economy and efficiency, subject to prior approval of the head of the procuring entity, effective 01 January 2004, the Procurement Division is hereby authorized to take charge of the following procurement modes:

• Goods up to an aggregate amount of P 1,000,000 for purchase through:
1. Limited Source Bidding
2. Direct Contracting
3. Negotiated procurement, during times of emergency, subject to conditions specified in Sections 53 - a) and b) and 54.2 — b) of lRR-RA 9184, i.e., bids are received from at least (3) suppliers

• Goods up to an aggregate amount of P 250,000 per purchase through:
1. Shopping

• Infrastructure Projects, up to an aggregate amount of P 1,000,000 through:
1. Negotiated procurement, during times of emergency, subject to conditions specified in Sections 53 - a) and b) and 54.2 —— b) of IRR-RA 9184, i.e., bids are received from at least (3) contractors

• Goods procured from the Procurement Service — Department of Budget and Management (PS—DBM), regardless of amount

• Goods procured from other government agencies, such as the Philippine International Trading Corporation, subject to the provisions of their respective Memoranda of Agreement (MOA) with the DOH

All other purchases not covered by the above conditions shall be referred to the Central Office Bids and Awards Committee for appropriate disposition.
