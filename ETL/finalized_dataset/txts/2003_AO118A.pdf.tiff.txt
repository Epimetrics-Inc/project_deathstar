<subject>SUBJECT : AMENDMENT TO A.O. NO 10-A s. 2001</subject>

<body>
For purposes of consistency of official actions, Administrative Order no. 10-A s. 2001 dated April 26, 2003 re: Implementation Guidelines for Foreign Assisted Projects of the Department of Health is hereby amended, such that the following items on the roles and responsibilities of key officials involved shall be included, thus:

Provision No. III (A)(3) paragraph I, on the Cluster for External Affairs shall be read to provide :

"The head of this Cluster shall be the designated Director of all on going Foreign Assisted Projects and as such he shall be responsible for the review and endorsement of all policy recommendations relative to FAPs prior to raising them to EXECOM and for the Secretary of Health. Likewise, he/she shall have direct supervision over the designated Technical Coordinators of respective projects in the performance/accomplishment of their inherent and assigned roles and responsibilities in project implementation."

Provision no. III (A)(9) paragraph I, shall be re-stated as follows :

"The designated Project Technical Coordinator shall provide technical leadership for the project, ensuring the technical soundness of the strategies, initiatives, activities to be carried out to attain Project/Program objectives and ensure sustainability and relevance of Project inputs. He/She shall report directly to the. Head of the Cluster for External Affairs on all Project concerns".

Provision no. III(B) which spells out the roles of the CHD Directors in the project implementation is hereby modified to include:

"CHD Directors are hereby mandated to report directly to the Head of the External Affairs Cluster on all concerns pertaining to Foreign Assisted Projects implemented at the CHD level".

As amended all other provisions of A.O. 10-A s. shall remain in effect and binding.
