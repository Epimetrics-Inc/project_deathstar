<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 65 dated January 5, 1980</subject>

<body>
The Chairman, Medical Manpower Development Committee, in a letter dated March 10, 1980, has endorsed favorably the proposal of the Chief, Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital and Medical Center to exempt the medical staff of hospitals from the provisions of Administrative Order No. 65 dated January 5, 1980 in order to avail of critically needed medical skills for teaching and training as well as service hospitals.

In view of the reasons given, the appointments of the medical staff in teaching and training as well as service hospitals are hereby exempted from the provisions of Administrative Order No. 65.

As thus modified, Administrative Order No. 65 stands in effect.
</body>
