<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

<subject>Subject: Direct supervision by the Office of the Minister over the Rizal Medical Center and the Quirino Memorial General Hospital effective January 1, 1984 per Section 10, E.O. 851</subject>

<body>
	In accordance with the provisions of Section 10, Executive order No. 851 dated December 2, 1982, the following hospitals previously under Regional Health Office No. 4 are hereby placed under the direct supervision and control of the Office of the Minister:
	1. Rizal Medical Center – Pasig, Metro Manila
	2. Quirino Memorial General Hospital – Quezon City

	Accordingly, technical, financial and administrative matters previously acted upon by Regional Health Office No. 4, shall be transacted with the Office of the Minister.
	This Order takes effect January 1, 1984.
By authority of the Minister of Health
