<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 93 s. 2003 on Allowable Rates of Payment for Health Human Resource Development Activities of the Department of Health</subject>
<body>
Due to the increasing cost of Board and Lodging, supplies and materials, reproduction and documentation, the maximum allowable rate of Php 450.00 per day per participants for non—residential training and development activities is no longer reasonable. Hence Section III No.1 pertaining to allowable rates of payment of Administrative Order No. 93 s. 2003 is hereby amended as follows:

FROM: Non — residential
P 450.00 per day per participant

TO : Non — residential
P 600.00 per day per participants

All other provisions of Administrative Order No. 93 s. 2003 shall remain valid and in effect.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
