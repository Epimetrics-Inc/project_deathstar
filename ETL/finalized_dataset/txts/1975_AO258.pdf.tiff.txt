<subject>SUBJECT: Authority for the Chief, Financial and Management Service, to approve Sub-allotment Advices and Cash Disbursement Ceilings.</subject>

<body>
For the purpose of implementing Budget Circular No. 246 dated June 30, 1975, the Chief, Financial and Management Service, is hereby authorized to approve the sub-allotment advices and cash disbursement ceilings that are required to be issued under said Budget Circular.

This Order, issued in the interest of the service, is hereby declared official and made of record.
