<subject>SUBJECT: GUIDELINES FOR THE PAYMENT OF NIGHT SHIFT DIFFERENTIAL AND “ON CALL" STATUS OF PUBLIC HEALTH WORKERS UNDER R.A. 7305</subject>

<body>
1.0 RATIONALE

       R.A. 7305 was enacted in the country primarily to promote and improve the social and economic well—being of health workers. Specifically it aims to develop their skills and capabilities; encourage those with proper qualifications and excellent abilities to join and remain in the government-service; attain uniformity in granting payment of additional compensation for night Shift differential and “on call” pay, the following guidelines are hereby prescribed.

2.0 COVERAGE

       Public Health Workers as defined in Section 3 of R.A. 7305 and Section 1. Rule II of its implementing Rules and Regulations shall be entitled to receive the above benefits.


3.0 PAYMENT OF NIGHT SHIFT DIFFERENTIAL
       3.1 Definition

       Work rendered between 10:00p.m. and 6:00 a.m. also known as the "graveyard shift". 

       3.2 .Payment of Nights shift Differential Public health Workers on a night shift duty shall be paid an additional Compensation equivalent to ten percent (10%) of their basic daily pay for each hours of work performed during the night-shift customarily adopted by hospitals/health institutions.

4.0 PAYMENT OF “ON CALL” STATUS
4.1 Definition
	A condition under which public health workers are required to be in their place of work before and/or after their regular working hours, and are within call/reach of their agencies and establishments to respond to emergencies brought about by natural or man—made calamities and massive casualties due to typhoons, floods, volcanic eruptions and accidents, during which they are not free to use their time for their own use. A Public Health Worker "On- Call" as defined in these rules may be called upon to render immediate health/medical assistance or relief work.

4.2 Payment of "on call" status:

Public health workers Who are on "on call" status shall be compensated fifty percent (50%) of their basic daily pay.

4.3 Supporting Documents for Payment

4.3.1. Office Order from the Head of Agency.

4.3.2: Certification from the head of agency that public health worker was "on call status" for I such a period.

5.0 FUNDING SOURCE
       Funding for the payment of night shift differential and "on call" pay status shall be included in the agency’s regular budget.

6.0 EFFECTIVITY

This Order shall take effect upon approval.
