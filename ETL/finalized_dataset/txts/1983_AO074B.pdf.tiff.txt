<subject>SUBJECT	: Use of 101 Reserve Fund for Provincial Health Offices</subject>

<body>In accordance with the financial management system developed for the reorganized Ministry of Health, part of the programmed  appropriation for field health services is withheld as reserve in the regional offices. This MOH reserve allotment is set up to provide for contingencies in the operation or the provincial health offices. It may be made available to said provincial health offices through sub-allotments, upon recommendation of the regional director and approval of the Minister of Health.
