<subject> SUBJECT: MONITORING EXPENDITURES INCURRED IN IMPLEMENTING THE PHIL. HEALTH DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS.</subject>

<body>

	In the implementation of the Philippine Health Development
Project, all expenses incurred chargeable against the funds of the project are to be reported in the attached form("Annex A"). This report shall be prepared monthly in three copies to be submitted to the Project Coordinating Office (PCU) which shall be the basis in preparing the Application for Withdrawal of Loan Proceeds.

	Failure of agencies to report will delay the remittance of
subsequent funding to implement the project.

	The Project Coordinators are directed to supervise and monitor compliance of the Regional/Provincial personnel to this
order.
