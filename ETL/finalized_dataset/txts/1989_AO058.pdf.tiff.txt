<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 58 s. 1989</docnum>

<subject> Subject: Authorizing the Provincial Health Officer of Zamboanga del Sur to allocate resources to operate a 5-bed hospital facility in the Main Health Center of Dinas, in anticipation of the approval of a 10-bed municipal hospital in Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur.</subject>

<body>
	
	The Provincial Health Officer, Zamboanga del Sur is hereby directed and given the authority to operate a 5-bed hospital facility at the main Health Center, in Dinas, in anticipation of the approval of a 10-bed municipal hospital in Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur.
	The appropriate number of staff and the necessary equipment and supplies shall be determined and arranged by the Provincial Health Officer from the resources available at the Provincial Health Office and its catchment hospitals.
	A report on compliance with this order is expected within one month.


</body>
