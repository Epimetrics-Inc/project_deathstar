<subject>SUBJECT: Authority to reimburse expenses made for the emergency purchase of life saving medicines, gasoline, supplies, spare parts, etc.</subject>

       <body>Mr. ROMAN M. ARROYO, in his capacity as Supply Officer I, of the Supply Section, this Ministry, is hereby authorized to reimburse all expenses made for the purchase of life saving medicines, gasoline, supp1ies, spare parts, etc. 
       
       Mr. Arroyo, in this connection, is also being authorized to use government vehicle in the canvassing and procurement of supplies.
