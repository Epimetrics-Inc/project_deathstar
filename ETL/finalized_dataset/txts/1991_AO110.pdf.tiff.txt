<subject>SUBJECT: Re-scheduling of Mandatory Use of Generic Labels for Pharmaceutical Products Containing Four of More Active Ingredients under the Coverage of A.O. 99 s. 1990</subject>


<body>
Upon the request of the affected sector of the Pharmaceutical Industry during the dialogue held with BFAD on June 18, 1991, the implementation of the change 1x1 generic labels of products covered by A. O. 99 s. 1990 is hereby re—schedu1ed as follows:

1. The mandatory use of BFAD approved generic labels in production shall start not later than October 1, 1991; 
2. The products with old labels without generic remedial labeling may be allowed in the market only until December 31, 1991.

This Order supersedes prior orders or issuances.
