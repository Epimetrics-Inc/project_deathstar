<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority to sign Notice of Salary Adjustment/Increase under the National Compensation Circular No. 22 dated June 17, 1982</subject>

<body>


In order to effect the early preparation of the notices for salary adjustments increases authorized under Executive Order No. 801 dated May 21, 1982, as implemented by the National Compensation Circular No. 22 dated June 17, 1982, of incumbents of physician classes of positions in the Ministry of Health, following delegation of authority is hereby
Ordered:   

1. The “Notice of Salary Increase” for Incumbents of physician classes of positions in the Office of the Minister, Bureau of Health Services, Bureau of Medical Services, Bureau of Dental Health Services, Office of Health Education and Training, Disease Intelligence Center, Radiation Health Office, National Cancer Control Center, and Dermatology Research and Training, shall be signed by the Acting Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development;

2. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for physicians is the Bureau of Research and Laboratories and the Bureau of Quarantine shall be signed by the respective Directors of the said Bureau.
3. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for physicians in the Malaria Eradication Service, National Nutrition Service, and the National Fmaily Planning Office shall be signed by te respective directors of the said Projects

4. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for physicians in the Schistosmiasis Control and Research Service and the Schistosomiasis Control Council shall be signed by the Executive Directors of the said Offices;

5. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for physicians in the Regional Health Offices shall be signed by the Regional Health Directors/Acting Regional Health Directors concerned. In the absence of the Regional Health Directors/Acting Regional Health Directors, the Administrative Officer III of the Region concerned shall sign the notice.
6. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for physicians in the Special Training Hospitals operating under the immediate supervision and control of the Minister of Health, shall be signed by the Chief of the afore-cited hospitals shall be signed by the Minister of Health and



7. The "Notice of Salary Increase" for Directors of the National Cancer Control Center, Dermatology Research and Training and the Radiation Health Office shall be signed by the Minister of Health.

The Notices of Salary Adjustment/Increase shall invariably carry the Words “By authority of the Minister of Health" which shall appear over the signature of the signing official. 

It is understood that the basis for cementing the salary increase/ adjustment shall be the annual salary of the chin] eyes as of December 31, 1981, which includes salary adjustments under the National Compensation Circular No. 16 but exclusive of merit increase, salary increase under the Career Progress on Scheme, incentive pay, allowances for transportation/representation, cost of living, bonus, honorarium and/or any other forms of additional compensation


Sufficient copies as the Notice shall be prepared, one copy to be retained by the Office or Agency concerned, one copy each to be furnished the Accounting Division and the Personnel Division, this Ministry, one copy for the Commission, on audit one copy for the GSIS and one copy for the OCPC. 
       This delegated authority's shall be affected and good only for salary increases/adjustments under the Nation Compensation Circular No. 22 effective January 1, 1982.
