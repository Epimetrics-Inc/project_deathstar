<subject>SUBJECT: DESIGNATION OF MS. GRACIELA B. ELEAZAR AS O-I-C, OFFICE OF THE DIRECTOR-GENERAL</subject>


<body>

In an appointment issued by Her Excellency, the President of the Republic of the Philippines, Ms. GRACIELA ELEAZAR was designated Officer-in-Charge, Office of the Director-General, of the Philippine Institute of Traditional and Alternative Health Care (PITAHC).

All officials, personnel and staff-of the agency, or of any person and entity having any lawful transactions or business with the agency are enjoined and directed to comply only with the lawful orders and issuances emanating from the duly designated Officer-in-Charge.

For compliance.
