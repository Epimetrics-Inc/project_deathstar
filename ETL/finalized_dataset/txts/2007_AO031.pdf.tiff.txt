<subject>SUBJECT: Strict Observations of the Managewt Structure of FOURmula One for Health.</subject>


<body>

To fully implement the health policies, programs / projects through adherence to lines of authority, channels of communications, inter—intra ofﬁce coordination and collaboration, all Centers of Health Development and DOH Hospitals are directed to observe the Management Structure of the FOURmula One for Health.

Centers of Health Development and Metro Manila DOH. Medical Centers and Special Hospitals except specialty hospitals and East Avenue Medical Center are directly under the Field Implementation and Coordination Ofﬁce (FICO) while DOH Medical Centers and hospitals outside of Metro Manila including Sanitaria are directly under the Centers for Health Development.

Moreover, Valenzuela General Hospital, Las Piﬁas District Hospital & Satellite Trauma Center and San Lorenzo Ruiz Women’s Hospital are directly under the Center for Health Development Metro Manila.
