<subject>SUBJECT: Fusion of Koronadal and Gen. Santos City Malaria Units with main headquarters in Koronadal, South Cotabato.</subject>

<body>
In the interest of the service and due to fiscal restraint but with intent to keep intact the objective of the malaria eradication activities in the region involved, the following changes in Malaria Area "F" are hereby promulgated:

1. The Malaria Units in Koronadal, South Cotabato, and General Santos City are hereby fused into one (1) Unit with principal headquarters in Koronadal, South Cotabato, and shall have territorial jurisdiction over the places covered by the former two (2) Units.

2. The movement of the personnel of both Units and the inventory of the properties and supplies involved shall be handled by the Area Supervisor who shall effect the transfer of responsibilities to the Head of the Unit assigned in the fused headquarters, and report this matter accordingly to the Central Headquarters in Manila.

3. Other matters affecting the previous two (2) Units shall be handled by the Central Headquarters on the recommendation of the Area Supervisor of the region.

This Order, is being made in the interest of the service, is hereby made of record.
