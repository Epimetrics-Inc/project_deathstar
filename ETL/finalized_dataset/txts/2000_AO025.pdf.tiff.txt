<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment of Administrative Order No. 85-A series 1990 particularly Section 25 — Violations and Penalties, Subsection 25.1.8 —</subject>

<body>

All accredited medical clinics/hospitals authorized to conduct medical examination of overseas workers and seafarers are hereby directed to note the following stipulated amendment of Administrative Order No. 85-A s. 1990. particularly Section 25 - ”Violations and Penalties - Sub—section 25.1.8,

from .......... "Failure to correct deficiencies within reasonable time aﬁer due notice From the BLR ......... "

to ............."Failure to correct deficiencies within fifteen (15) days after inspection shall cause the clinic’s/hospital's accreditation to be automatically terminated or lapsed."

Rationale : These changes are formulated in order to facilitate compliance for a faster processing of all documentary requirements that were found deficient on inspection.

This order shall take effect upon approval.
