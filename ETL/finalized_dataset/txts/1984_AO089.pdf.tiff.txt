<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

<subject> Subject: Amending Adm. Order No. 85-A dated March 15, 1984 regarding Residents who have completed or will complete their Residency Training from February to December 31, 1984 which will constitute the last batch of one year Post Residency Service as per A.O. 60 s. 1979</subject>

<body>
	In view of the urgent need of regional and provincial hospitals for the utilization of the services of skilled medical manpower under P.D. 1424, Administrative Order No. 85-A dated March 15, 1984, is hereby amended such that Residents who have completed or will complete their Residency training from February 1 to December 31,1984, shall constitute the last batch of Residents who will undertake Post-residency service of one year in accordance with the provision of Administrative Order No. 60, s. 1979.
	With regards to the Residency Service embodied in Administrative Order No. 85-A dated March 15, 1984, service assignments for a period of six months shall commence on July 1, 1984, for residents completing their training after December 31, 1984.
	As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 85-A dated March 15, 1984, stands in effect.
