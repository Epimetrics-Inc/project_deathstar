<subject>SUBJECT: GRANT OF AMELIORATION ASSISTANCE TO OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH (DOH) FOR FY 1997</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to Section 35 and 36, Book V of Executive Order 292 and as approved by the Office or the President in its letter of the Executive Secretary Ruben D. Torres dated August 15, 1997, the grant of payment of fringe benefits (Amelioration Assistance) is authorized to DOH officials and employees of the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, regional health offices and hospitals including specialty hospitals to help them overcome present economic difficulties, boost their morale and in recognition of their contributions and continuous efforts and to strengthen their commitment to public service.

This benefit shall not be more than SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED PESOS (P7,200.00) which shall be given to each official and employee of the DOH regardless of the salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, temporary, or contractual) subject to availability of actual savings of that office/hospital either from Personal Services, MOOE or Sub-Allotment (except form CIA, CDF, PAF and FAPs) and the usual accounting rules and regulations. Payment should not be earlier than September 15, 1997 and not later than December 31, 1997.

To be entitled to this benefit, the employee shall have been employed for at least four (4) months for FY 1997 as of August 31, 1997, provided that an official or employee who has rendered less than four (4) months of service shall be entitled to an amount proportionate to actual service rendered as follows: 


Actual Length of Service 			Amount 

Less than four (4) months but not		50% of P7,200
less than three (3) months

Less than three (3) months but not 		30% of P7,200
less than two (2) months 

Less than two (2) months but not 		10% of P7,200 
less than one (1) month 


In case of part-time employees, they shall be entitled to one half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full time employees. 

Exceptions to this benefit are (1) the consultants and/or experts who are hired for specific purposes and services; and (2)PSYP trainees.
