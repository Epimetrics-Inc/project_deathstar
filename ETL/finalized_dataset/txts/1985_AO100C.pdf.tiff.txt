<subject>Authority to reimburse expense made for the emergency purchase of life-saving medicines, gasoline, miscellaneous/office supplies and material spare part and repair of office equipment for the office of the minister of health proper</subject>
<body>
	Mr. ROMAN H. ARROYO, in his capacity as senior buyer of the supply section, general services division, the ministry, is hereby authorized to reimburse all expense made for the purchase of life-saving medicines, gasoline, office/miscellaneous supplies and material spare parts and repair of office equipment, etc.
	MR. ARROYO, in this connection is also being authorized to use government vehicle in the canvassing and procurement of supplies.

</body>
