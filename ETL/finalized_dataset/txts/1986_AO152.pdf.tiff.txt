<subject> Subject: Withdrawal of GRAVIGARD IUD and CU-T from the market.</subject>


<body>

	On the basis of a formal notification on the Manufacturer’s decision to discontinue the sale of GRAVIGARD IUD and CU-T due to reports of its questionable safety, these contraceptive devise are hereby ordered to be withdrawn from the market.
ORDER

1. in the interest of consumer protection, GRAVIGAD IUD and CU-T are no longer registrable and hereby ordered phased out effective immediately.
2. henceforth, the registration of the above-mentioned devices are hereby recalled and cancelled.

3. all existing stocks from drugstore, hospitals, etc. should be recalled by the importer/distributor and destroyed in the presence of BFAD Inspectors.

This Order takes effect immediately.
