<subject>SUBJECT: Logistics System for Grant -in -Aid from KfW (Germany) under the Women's Health and Safe Motherhood Project</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to the Financing Agreement executed between the Republic Of the Philippines and the Kreditanstalt fur Wiederaufbau dated 02 November 1995, the Guidelines for Procurement and Distribution of all grant— in- aid from Germany is hereby laid down. Thus,


I. PROCUREMENT

All procurement procedures shall be done in Germany with 30% of goods originating from the New Federal Lender.


II. DISTRIBUTION
Import duties, levies and other fees as well as all transportation costs from port of Manila to the WHSMP covered provinces shall be borne by the Department of Health as GOP counterpart to the Financing Agreement;

Delivery of medical equipment for Provincial Hospitals, District Hospitals, Rural Health Units and Barangay Health Stations shall be effected only where the construction under the ADB project.

Equipment/drugs shall be provided only to WHSMP-covered provinces with signed Project Implementation Agreement with DOH.

 
III. SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

The awarded German supplier will undertake the proper packing and labeling pursuant to the specific conditions of the Lender documents/contract for convenient delivery of goods to the destined local facilities.

The Philippine forwarders to be identified by Department of Health shall be responsible in undertaking the direct delivery of goods from the port of Manila to the destined provinces  within 15 days in Luzon and 30 days in Visayas and Mindanao.

Local Government Units (LGUs) shall be responsible for the distribution of goods and equipment to the selected District Hospitals, Rural Health Units and Barangay Health Stations and shall bear the costs of local distribution.

The Training—related equipment such as kits for midwives, TBAs and first referral doctors shall be distributed in coordination with the Australian Aid and the Health Manpower Development and Training Service.

The Procurement and Logistics Services, the Finance Services, the PMO (WHSMP) and all  departments concerned shall be responsible for the smooth implementation of this distribution system in accordance with existing parallel distribution systems, rules and regulations. The appropriate forms and detailed procedures shall be designed and implemented by the concerned offices to safeguard the grant-in-kind from KFW. The PMO—WHSMP shall particularly be responsible for the preparation and submission to PLS-DOH of the allocation list and grant/donation documents while the PLS-DOH shall be responsible for the preparation of the invoice receipt.


IV. EFFECTIVITY:

This Order shall take effect immediately.

For strict compliance of all concerned.
