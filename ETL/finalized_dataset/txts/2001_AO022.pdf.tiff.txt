<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations of the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers (R.A. No. 7305)</subject>

<body>
 

In connection with the National Health Workers Consultative Council (NMHWCC), the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations of the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers (RA. No. 7305) dated November 1999, is hereby amended such that Section 6.2, Rule XV shall read as follows:

“6.2 Public Health Workers who undertook postgraduate studies in a degree course shall be entitled to an upgradng in their position or raise in pay equivalentto two (2) percent of their basic salary but payment shall not be made retroactive.”

All other provisions of the above-mentioned Implementing Rules and Regulations still stand in effect.
