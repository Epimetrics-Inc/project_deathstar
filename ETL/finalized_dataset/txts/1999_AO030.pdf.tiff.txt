<subject>SUBJECT: Circulation of Administrative Issuances through the Department of Health Intranet Billboard</subject>

<body>

For an efficient and speedy circulation of all administrative issuances to all offices/services/units concerned, the following guidelines are prescribed:

1. Copies of administrative issuances (i.e. Administrative Orders, Department Orders, Department Circulars and Department Memoranda) shall be made available through the DOH Intranet billboard. Specifically, these can be accessed at this address: http://home.doh.gov.ph either onscreen or through print-out.

2. It shall be the responsibility of the offices, services and units to regularly check the billboard for updates.

3. Starting September 1, 1999, the Records Section of the Administrative Service shall no longer send copies of the issuances through the old routing system or the postal/mailing system.

4. Certified true copies (CTCs) of issuances which are required for certain financial/legal transactions may be made available/furnished by the Records Section only upon request of concerned units.

5. Fur systematic and quick posting of such issuances on the Intranet, all concerned offices/units are required to submit diskette copies/soft copies of their issuances to the Records Section.

6. The Intranet billboard shall he maintained by the Records Section in coordination with the Management Advisory Service and the Health Policy Development Staff.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By Authority of the Secretary of Health
