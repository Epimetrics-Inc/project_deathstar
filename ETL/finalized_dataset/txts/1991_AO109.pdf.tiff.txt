<subject>SUBJECT : Amendment to Department Order No. 200 s. of 1991 to provide the funding source for the operation of the 15—bed extension hospital.</subject>

<body>
Department Order No. 200 series of 1991 dated June 20, 1991 was issued to establish a 15- bed extension hospital in Olongapo City during the duration of the emergency brought by the eruption bf Mt. Pinatubo.

       The operating requirements of said extension hospital shall be charged against the calamity fund releases of Regional Health Office No. III, San Fernando, Pampanga. The Regional Chief Accountant is hereby directed to facilitate the disbursement of funds to meet the urgent needs of the hospital and personnel who will be dispatched (from central office, region I etc.) to assist in the emergency operations in RHO#3 and neighboring provinces.
