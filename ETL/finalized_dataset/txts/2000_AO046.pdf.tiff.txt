<subject>SUBJECT: Addendum to Administrative Order No. 13 s. 2000, Particularly Paragraph 2.</subject>

<body>

In the light of the constraints encountered in the implementation of paragraph 2 of Administrative Order No. 13 s. 2000 which requires one (1) of the members of the Selection and Promotion Board (SPB) front Central Office to seat with the Regional SPB in the evaluation of positions for Division Chiefs, Chiefs of Hospitals or its  equivalent rank, hereunder is an addendum:

“In the event that some of the Central Office SPB members are not available for the above-mentioned purpose, Assistant Regional Directors (ARD) are hereby constituted as a pool from where the Secretary may designate one from among said pool, to seat as a representative of the Central Office SPB together with the Regional Health Offices SPB during the evaluation for the above-mentioned positions. Concerned ARD who will be designated by the Secretary shall be chosen from the nearest Region to where the vacancy under process/evaluation exists.”
