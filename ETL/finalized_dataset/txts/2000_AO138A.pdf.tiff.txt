<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Sub-sections 2, 3, 4, 5 under Section III providing for the Specific Guidelines of AO #34-A s, 2000, Adolescent and Youth Health Policy</subject>


<body>    
  
Sub-sections 2, 3, 4, 5 under Section III Specific Guidelines as stated in Administrative Order #34—A s 2000 are hereby amended, as follows:

SPECIFIC GUIDELINES;

# 2- Adolescent and youth stress reduction shall be an important activity under AYH strategy. Various adolescent and youth stresses such as peer, economic and family pressure are major factors that increase their vulnerability to risky behaviors.

# 3- The DOH through the Family Health Cluster shall assist and / or provide technical assistance to the DECS when necessary in reviewing and updating the school health services, standards and curriculum for implementation.

# 4— A comprehensive quality health care services which include counseling and medical consultation should be provided to all adolescents and youth with respect to ethnic, cultural and religious differences by a trained, as much as possible young health care providers such as doctors, dentist, nurses, nutritionist, social worker, with privacy and confidentiality at all levels of health care. The services should also be directed towards gender specific concerns.

# 5- All DOH retained hospitals shall provide an adolescent and youth - friendly designated area. They should likewise adopt schools near them to provide health services within the school.

All other provisions in AO # 34-A s 2000 remain in full force and effect.
