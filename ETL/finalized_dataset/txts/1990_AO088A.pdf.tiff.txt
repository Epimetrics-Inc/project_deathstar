<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority for the implementation of the Department of Health Decentralization Policy.</subject>

<body>

I. Rationale

	1.1 Expedite the implementation of administrative and financial functions in the various offices of the Department of Health (DOH) which has been reorganized under Executive Order No. 119 dated January 31, 1987.

	1.2 Streamline and simplify the existing procedures in the processing of administrative and financial transactions in order to improve the productivity in DOH operation.

	1.3 Delineate properly the duties and responsibilities of the recommending officials and approving authorities.

II. DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY TO CENTRAL OFFICE INCLUDING THE SPECIAL HOSPITALS WITHIN METRO MANILA

IV. RESPONSIBILITIES

	1. In the exercise of this delegated authority on administrative and financial functions, responsible officials shall observe with diligent care and uphold standards of protecting the integrity of the Department and of the primacy of promoting and preserving the health of the community. Adherence to the Department's priority programs shall be demonstrated in undertaking these transactions.

	2. The persons/officials to whom this authority has been delegated are primarily responsible and accountable for any violation of existing budget, accounting, auditing and other applicable rules and regulations.


V. Effective June 1, 1990.
