Subject: Accounting for funds transferred by the Central Office (Office of the Secretary of Health) to its operating units out of the lump sum releases to implement the various health activities and in funding the operations of the Schistosomiasis Hospital, Schistosomiasis Research and Training Division at Palo, Leyte.</subject>

<body>



To streamline the accounting and disbursement system of funds being transferred by the Office of the Secretary of Health to the various agencies under the Department of Health, the following shall be the accounting system to be used by recipient agencies:

1. Funding checks will be issued by the Office of the Secretary to transfer the funds to its implementing agencies.

2. The Budget Division, Office of the Secretary will prepare a notice of transfer addressed to the agency head of the implementing unit, indicating the purpose of the fund transfer and the function/activity code of the fund where it is taken.

3. Receiving agencies will issue official receipt to the Cashier, Office of the Secretary for the total amount received.

4. Accountants of the Office of the Secretary will record the cash transfer as sub-allotment to Operating units. Operating units or receiving agencies will record the cash transfer as allotment in their books.
<image>table_1.png</image>

5. All unobligated allotment will be reverted to the general fund.
	This supersedes previous orders issued in accounting for cash received from the central Office in 1986 and the administrative order in funding the operations of Schistosomiasis Hospital, Research and Training Division.
