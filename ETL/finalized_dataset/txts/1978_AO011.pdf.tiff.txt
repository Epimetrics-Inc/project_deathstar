<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority to the Chief, Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan</subject>

<body>
This Order is issued to authorize the officials of the Hospital ng Bagong Lipunan to approve disbursements, requisitions, and other transactions in connection with the performance of their fiscal responsibilities.

I. Approval of Disbursement

A. Official authorized to approved disbursements and sign treasury warrants and requests for obligation of allotments:

1. Chief of Hospital - no limit
2. Administrative Officer - up to ₱1,000

II. Signing of checks

A. Officials authorized to sign checks:

1. Cashier - checks up to ₱500
2. Administrative Officer - checks above ₱500 up to ₱5,000
3. Chief of Hospital - checks above ₱5,000

B. Officials authorized to countersign checks:

1. Chief of Hospital - checks above ₱500 up to ₱5,000
2. Administrative Officer - checks up to ₱500

III. Approval of Requisitions and other Fiscal Actions

The hospital Chief shall:

A. Approved requisitions as follows:

1. Requisitions thru DOPS Contracts not to exceed ₱10,000 per requisition;

2. Purchase Orders covering:
a. Emergency purchase of supplies and materials not to exceed four per centumof the annual appropriation for supplies and materials.

b. Emergency repairs of motor vehicles and equipments not to exceed ₱10,000 per order if the repair is to be performed by the exclusive distributor of the particular equipment and the advantage of the government; and not to exceed ₱5,000 per order if the repair services can be readily rendered by several workshops or contractors.

B. Limitations:

As a general rule, emergency purchases are to be discourged. Splitting of requisitions in order to avoid review by higher officials is prohibited. Requisitions must be based on the approval procurement program and itemized in accordance with the commodity groupings prepared by the Bureau of Supply Coordination.

IV. Responsibilities of Approving Officials

Approving officials shall be accountable for the following:

A. Proper utilization of funds, including;

1. The incurrence of expenditures not to exceed authorized allotmens;

2. The provision of adequate funds for all the activities of the Hospital.

B. Proper interpretation of related exisiting fiscal policies, rules, regulations; and observance of prescribed preocedures; and

C. Accomplishments of results in accordance with approved financial and work program.

V. Limitations of Authority

All financial transactions that exceed the authority delegated to the officials designated in this Order shall require prior approval of the Chairman, Board of Governors.

VI. Rescinding Provisions

Any or all orders or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are rescinded.

VII. Effectivity

This order takes effect immediately.
