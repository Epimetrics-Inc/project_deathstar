<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Section E of Administrative Order No. 16, s. 1993: Reporting Scheme in Accordance with the Department of Health Streamlining per Memorandum Order No. 27 dated August 13 1992 of the Office of the President</subject>

<body>


Pursuant to the changes in assignments and reporting contained in AO No. 16, s. 1993, the Management Advisory Service and Health Policy Development Staff upon effectivity of this Order, shall be under the supervision of the Office for Management Services (OMS)

The functions of the two offices which are concerned with management information systems and systems improvement in the areas of finance procurement, organizational methods and policies is considered to be supportive of the overall function of the OMS. This arrangement is consistent with the Executive Order 119 structure.

Thus, Section E of AO No. 16, s. 1996 titled “Office of the Undersecretary for Management Services” shall include other than the 3 existing services identified, the Management Advisory Service and Health Policy Development Staff.

This Order shall take effect on 16 May 1996.
