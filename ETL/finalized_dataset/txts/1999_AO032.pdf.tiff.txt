<subject>SUBJECT: Payment of Personal Long Distance Calls</subject>

<body>

To prevent unnecessary delays in the payment of DDH telephone bills and/or disconnection of DOH telephone lines, the following policies will he adopted for the payment of personal long distance calls:

1. Offices/Bureaus/Services shall restrict use of DOH telephone lines to official calls. Per government policy, personal calls should not be allowed.

2. Upon receipt of the telephone bill, the administrative officer (AO) or AO  designate of the Office/Bureau/Service/Unit shall determine which are personal or official calls. Within ten (1) calendar days the following must he undertaken:

2.1 Secure certification of official calls from official concerned.
2.2 Notify personnel with a personal call of the amount to be paid with in two days upon receipt of the bill.
2.3 Process the disbursement voucher for payment.

3. All personal calls shall have to be paid and settled by the concerned personnel with the Cashier Section, Finance Service within seven (7) calendar days upon notification of the AO/designate. The corresponding official receipt (OR) issued shall be submitted to the AO/designate with in the day the payment was made.

4. On the basis of an official report of the AO/designate, unpaid or unsettled bills shall be automatically deducted within ten days from the receipt of the bill by the Cashier from the salary of the personnel concerned in the next salary payment period.

This order takes effect immediately.

</body>
