<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 11-A s. 1998 re: Guidelines in the Utilization of the Regional Primary Health Care (PHC) Funds as Approved in the General Appropriations Act (GAA) Republic Act No. 8522 of CY 1998.</subject>

<body>

Sections III.B.3 and III.B.3.b on the Assistance to Barangay Health Workers of Administrative Order No. 11-A s. 1998 are hereby amended to include as follows:

III.B.3, - Medical Assistance to BHWs and/or immediate family member. This fund shall support the medical needs for BHWs requesting assistance from DOH Hospitals. The concerned RHO shall sub-allot to the Regional Hospital/ Regional Medical Center/ Provincial Hospital the amount of P200,000.00 in the form of trust fund to subsidize the medical needs of the BHWs requesting assistance. The Regional Health Office could also facilitate the release of funds directly to Barangay Health Worker on a reimbursement basis upon submission of required documents as deemed necessary by the Regional Health Office.

b) - The Chief of Hospital shall see to it that an audited annual fund utilization report is submitted to the RHO, copy furnished CHS, DOH.

Other provisions stipulated in AD. No. 11-A still stand in effect.
