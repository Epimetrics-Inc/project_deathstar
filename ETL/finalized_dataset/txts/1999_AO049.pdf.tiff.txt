<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of Agency Incentive Allowance to all Officials and Employees below Director Level</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to Sect1on I and II, Rule X of the Omnibus Rules, Book V of Executive Order 292 and other pertinent Civil Service Laws, the grant of Agency Incentive Allowance as employee welfare beneﬁt is hereby authorized to all Department of Health (DOH) officials and employees below Director level at the Central Office and other attached agencies, bureaus, regional health offices and hospitals.

This benefit shall not be more than SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED PESOS (P 7,200.00) which shall be given to each official and employee of the DOH below Director level regardless of salary and appointment status (whether permanent, casual, temporary or contractual) chargeable against savings realized from released allotment of continuing and current appropriations- current operating expenses (PS and MOOE) or sub-allotments (except from CIA, CDF, FAPs and Calamity Fund) of the agency and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

To be entitled to this benefit, an employee must have rendered at least four (4) months of continuous service from January 1, 1999 to November 15, 1999, provided that  an official or employee who has rendered less than four (4) months of service shall be entitled to an amount equivalent to the actual service rendered as follows:

In case of part-time employees, they shall be entitled to one-half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full-time employees. 

Exemptions to this beneﬁt are (1) the consultants and/or experts who are hired for specific purposes and services, and (2) the PSYP trainees. 

Strict compliance by all concerned with the provisions of this Order is enjoined.
