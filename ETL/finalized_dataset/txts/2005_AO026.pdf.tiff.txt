<subject>SUBJECT: Moratorium on Accreditation of new Medical Facilities authorized to conduct Medical Examination of Overseas Filipino (OFW) Workers and Seafarers.</subject>


<body>

To date, there are 114 OFW accredited clinics operating in Metro Manila. This number is enough to service the requirements of the departing OFWs. In View of this, a moratorium on the accreditation of new medical facilities is hereby imposed.

This moratorium is only applicable to Metro Manila.
