<subject>SUBJECT: Constitution of the Selection/Promotion Board for positions in the first and second level of the Career Service in the DOH Central Office</subject>

<body>

In compliance with Memorandum Circular No. 18, dated November 9, 1988 of the Civil Service Commission, each of the Offices of the Undersecretaries shall constitute its own Selection/Promotion Board for positions in the first and second level of the career service including supervisory positions up to the level of Division Chiefs. The Board shall composed of the following: 

Chairman	-	The Undersecretary concerned

Members		-	a) Director III or a representative of the organisational unit where the vacancy is located

			b) One Medical Key Official who shall sit with the Board Meeting insofar as the deliberation/evaluation of candidates for appointment to medical positions in the Central Office is concerned.

			c) Representative of the rank—and—file employees for second level positions

			d) Representative of the rank—and—file employees for first level positions


A secretariat of at least two (2) members will be identified.

The Selection/Promotion Board is tasked to objectively assess the qualifications or competence of those entering the government service for the fist time, as well as those moving up the career ladder or those being considered for advancement.

Each Service/Bureau will undertake initial screening of applicants for submission to the Selection/Promotion Board;

Corollary with this, Item 1.1 of Administrative Order No. 17, dated June 25, 1993, is hereby amended in the sense that appointments to positions in the second level of the career service including supervisory positions up to the level of Division Chiefs shall be delegated to the Undersecretary concerned.

Accordingly, the Undersecretaries are hereby directed to submit to the Office of the Secretary their constitutedSelection/Promotion Board for subsequent issuance of Department Order.

This Order takes effect immediately.
