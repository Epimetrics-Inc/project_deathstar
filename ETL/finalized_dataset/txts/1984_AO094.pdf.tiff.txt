<subject> Subject: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 26-C dated May 25, 1982, which amended A.O. Nos. 87 and 93 s. 1980, revising the distribution scheme of Incentive Fees and Directing the Payment of Honorarium and Incentives Fees every June and December each year</subject>

<body>
	Administrative Order No. 26-C dated May 25, 1982, which amended Administrative Order Nos. 87 and 93, s. 1980, regarding the policies, procedures and guidelines that will govern the affiliation and training of students in the various hospitals, offices and units of the Ministry of Health, is hereby amended to include the following provisions:
	1. the incentive fee portion of the Affiliation shall be distributed equally to all employees in the particular unit, office or hospital;
	2. The distribution and payment of the Honorarium and incentive fees shall be made twice a year by the agency, office, hospital concerned, that is, before the end of June and during the first half of December of every year.
	As thus amended, Administrative Order No. 26-C dated May 25, 1982, stands in effect.
