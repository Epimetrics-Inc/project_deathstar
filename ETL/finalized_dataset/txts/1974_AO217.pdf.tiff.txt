<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 217 s. 1974</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT: DESIGNATING MR. EFREN TESORERO, A CIVILIAN RASAC AGENT, WHO HAS COMPLETED THE NARCOTICS COMPLIANCE INVESTIGATIVE COURSE CONDUCTED BY THE DRUG ENFORCEMENT ADMINISTRATION OF THE UNITED STATES IN COOPERATION WITH THE UNITED STATES AGENCY FOR INTERNATIONAL AID AS DEPUTY DRUG INSPECTOR.</subject>

<body>
In the interest of the public service, and pursuant to the authority vested in me as Secretary of Health, concurrently ex-officio member and; Chairman of the Dangerous Drugs Board, to enforce the provisions of Section 27 of Republic Act No. 3720 and Section 12 of Republic Act No. 953 in relation to the effective implementation of the pertinent provisions of Republic Act 6425 for the purpose of securing more effective control of illegal possession, sale, manufacture of and traffic in dangerous drugs, Mr. Efren Tesorero, a civilian RASAC agent, who is presently the team leader of the Anti Narcotics Unit of RASAC Eastern Mindanao with station in Davao City and who had finished the Narcotics Compliance Investigative Course is hereby designated as Deputy Dangerous Drugs Inspector.

Under this designation, Mr. Tesorero shall have the authority:

1) to enter, at reasonable hours, any medical, dental and veterinary clinic, drugstore, factory, warehouse, or other drug establishment in which drugs, are manufactured, processed, packed, or held, for introduction into commerce or are held after such introduction, or to enter any vehicle being used to transport or hold such drugs, in commerce; and

2) to inspect, in a reasonable manner, such medical, dental and veterinary clinic, drugstore, factory, warehouse, establishment, materials, containers, and labeling therein.

While performing the above functions, the following guidelines shall be strictly followed:

1) His area of jurisdiction shall be limited within the city/municipality where they are regularly assigned as police officer.

3) If he made such inspection of a medical, dental and veterinary clinic, drugstore, factory, warehouse or other establishment has obtained any sample in the course of the inspection, upon completion of the inspection and prior to leaving the premises, he shall give to the owner, operator, or agent in charge a receipt describing the samples obtained.

4) He shall submit an after-operation report of every activity and accomplishment to the Secretary of Health, copy furnished the Food and Drug Administration, provided that if there are more than one activity or accomplishment performed in one day, a single integrated report of all such activities or accomplishments in one day will suffice.

The authority herein granted shall take effect immediately and shall continue only up to December 31, 1974, unless sooner revoked.
