<subject>SUBJECT: REDEMARCATION OF MALARIA AREAS</subject>

<body>
For a more effective implementation of the current malaria strategies which calls for a closer liaison coordination and cooperation with the general public health service, the present Malaria Areas are hereby redemarcated into Malaria Regions such that the areas of jurisdiction shall coincide as much as possible with the areas of coverage of Regional Health Offices as follows:

<image>figure_1.png</image>

The Director of the Malaria Eradication Service is hereby authorized to redemarcate the malaria field units under each of these Health Regions and to align and assign such technical personnel and staff as maybe necessary to effect the new demarcation from the existing malaria personnel except in Malaria Regions IX and XII, which are under the administrative supervision of the autonomous regions.

All orders inconsistent herewith are hereby amended.
</body>
