<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of Inspection Units in all agencies and hospitals under the direct supervision of the Office of the Minister, in line with COA Circular No. 81-26(K) dated July 2, 1981.</subject>

<body>
In line with the policy of this Ministry to institute internal control particularly on the procurement system and pursuant to the provisions of COA Circular No. 81-26(K) dated July 2, 1981, Inspection Units shall be established immediately in all agencies and hospitals under the direct supervision of the Office of the Minister and shall perform the following functions, among others:

a. Inspect deliveries of supplies, materials and equipment, when payment thereof is not subject to pre-audit by COA representatives, as to quality, quantity and compliance to specifications.
b. Prepare reports for all inspection conducted.
c. Submit recommendations to higher authorities to correct deficiencies disclosed by such inspections, and
d. Perform such other related functions that maybe assigned from time to time.

In the performance of these functions, the Inspection Unit staff shall be guided by the COA Manual on Property Inspection and other related policies and guidelines prescribed by the Minister of Health.

Agency heads concerned shall see to it that the principles of check and balance shall be adhered to in the selection of personnel to be assigned to Inspection Units. The Unit shall be composed of at least three (3) qualified personnel tapped from the different divisions or units within the agency who are not connected, one way or the other, in the procurement activities of the office. These personnel shall perform the functions assigned to the Inspection Unit in addition to their regular duties and responsibilities.

This Order supersedes all other orders inconsistent herewith and shall take effect immediately.
</body>
