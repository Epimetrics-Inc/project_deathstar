Administrative Order No. 21-A, s. 1993 is hereby amended with the following corrections and addendum:

Section 4.2		Functions of RCATS:
       4.2.3 (Formulate guidelines/policies on affiliation and training appropriate to their area of Jurisdiction with basis on the general guidelines formulated by NCATS.

       4.2.4 Approve Contracts of Affiliation from the Department of Health hospitals in the region through the Chairman of the committee.

Section 6	Procedures in Applying for Affiliation with the DOH Hospitals and other health units
       6.3	For newly established schools, colleges or universities with College of Medicine, Nursing and Midwifery, their course curriculum should be approved first by the Council on Alternative Health Science Education prior to acceptance by any DOH facility for affiliation and training.
              6.7 Affiliation Contract shall be renewed yearly for the first two years and every two years subsequently. 
 
Section 8	Distribution of Affiliation Fees
	8.2 The collected a affiliation fees shall be divided on the following manner:
	8.2.1 Five percent (5%) of the total co11ection shall be remitted by Metro Manila hospital and agencies to the National Committee on Affiliation and Training of Students (NCATS) and by Regional Hospitals and other DOH facilities to the Regional Committee on Affiliation and Training of Students (RCATS) respective1y and shall be used in the following manner:

1% - meetings and conferences
2% - supplies, materials and equipment
2%- Honorarium/incentive for Committee members

Other than the amendment/addendum stipulated herein, Administrative Order No. 21-A, s. 1993 still stands in effect.
