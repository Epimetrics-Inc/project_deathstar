<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority to sign Notice of Salary Adjustment under Budget Circular No. 256 dated December 29, 1975.</subject>

<body>
In order to effect the early release of the Notices for the salary increase authorized under Budget Circular No. 256 dated December 29, 1975 of all officials and employees under the Department of Health, the following delegation of authority is hereby ordered:

1. The "Notice of Salary Adjustment" for those from Chiefs of Divisions and up in the following: Office of the Secretary, Bureau of Health Services, Bureau of Medical Services, Bureau of Dental Health Services, Office of Health Education & Personnel Training, Disease Intelligience Center, and Dermatology Research Training Project, shall be signed by the Undersecretary of Health; while those from below the aforementioned Chiefs of Dvisions shall be signed by the Chief, Administrative Service;

2. The "Notice of Salary Adjustment" for the officials and employees of the Bureau of Research and Laboratories, and the Bureau of Quarantine, shall be signed by the respective Directors of the said Bureauus;

3. The "Notice of Salary Adjustment" for the officials and employees of the Food and Drug Administration and the Schistosomiasis Control & Research, shall be signed by the Administration and Executive Director of the said Offices;

5. The "Notice of Salary Adjustment" for the officials and employees under the Regional Health Offices shall be signed by the Regional Health Director concerned. In the absence of the Regional Director, the Administrative Officer III of the Regional concerned shall sign the notice;

6. The "Notice of Salary Adjustment" for officials and employees in the National Training Hospitals operating under the immediate supervision and control of the Secretary of Health, shall be signed by the Chief of the Training Hospital concerned.

In authorizing the payment of additional salary adjustment, the provisions of the Budget Circular No. 256 dated December 29, 1975 shall be strictly observed and complied with.

It is understood that the basis of computation for the adjustment is the present salary of the official or employee concerned as of December 31, 1975.

Sufficient copies of the Notice shall be prepared, once copy ot be retained by the Office or Agency concerned; once copy to be furnished the Accounting Division, this Department, one copy for the Budget of Commission, one copy for the Civil Service Commission, one copy for the Commission on Audit, one copy for the Government Service Insurance System and one copy for the WAPCO.

This delegated authority shall be effected and good only for the fiscal year 1975-76.
