<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of authority for the Chief, Baguio General Hospital, to approve applications for leaves of absence of hospital personnel.</subject>

<body>
Effective September 1, 1971, the chief Baguio General Hospital, is hereby authorized, subject to the provisions of Rule XVI of the Revised Civil Service Rules, to approve after the words, "By authority of the Secretary of health" all applications for leave of absence of officers and employees of the said hospital, provided their services can be spared without detriment to the interest of the public services, except as follows:

a. Requests for communication of salary incident to leave and

b. Applications for leave without pay in excess of sixty (60) days, both of which shall be submitted to Regional Health Office No. 1 for appropriate action.

In connection with the foregoing authority, the Hospital Chief concerned shall secure from the said Region at Dagupan City the leave credits of each and every personnel of his hospital. Applications for leave of absence of officers and employees of the Baguio General Hospital received in Regional Health Office No. 1 before September 1, 1974 shall continue to be acted upon by the said Region, but those that may be submitted after that date should be returned to the hospital for action conformable with the terms of this Order.
</body>
