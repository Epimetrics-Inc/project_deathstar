<subject>SUBJECT: Participation of Community Hospital and Health Centers in the allocation of local hospital funds.</subject>

<body>
The Department of Health had been receiving inquiries as to whether or not Community Hospital and Health Centers established in isolated communities as a joint project of this Department and the Philippine Medical Care Commission can have a share in the contributions of local governments to the hospital fund in accordance with the provisions of the Hospital Financing Law. (R.A. 1939).

In this connection, it is hereby directed that said Community Hospital and Health Centers shall henceforth participate in the allocation of local hospital funds in accordance with the provisions of Department Circular No. 130, series of 1966.

All concerned are enjoined to properly observe this Order which takes effect immediately.
</body>
