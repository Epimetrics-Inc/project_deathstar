<subject> Amending paragraph nos. 1.3. 1.5 and 2.1.2 of Administrative Order No. 45 series 1982 on guidelines and. related procedures for accounting of funds allotted for the construction of Barangay Health Stations.</subject>


<body>
       To ensure smooth flow of transactions relative To disbursement/accounting of funds allotted for the construction of 915 barangay hea1th stations under the amended Loan Agreement No. 923 PH (Population Project II) the folloW1ng paragraphs are hereby amended to read as follows:

1.0 General Guidelines

1.3 Disbursement voucher in favor of the Integrated Provincial Health Office (IPHO) indicating the location of BHSP shall likewise be prepared by the Budget Division (MOH) to cover the remittance of the allotted funds for the construction of the barangay health stations.

1.5 Check shall be issued to the credit of the account of the Integrated PHO for deposit to the nearest branch of PNB. The deposit shall be treated as trust fund in the books of said Integrated Provincial Health Office. IPHO shall in turn issue funding check to the district hospita1 nearest the chosen location of the HHS. In case the BHS is located near the IPHO office, fund shall be disbursed by the IPHO in accordance with 2.2 of Administrative Order No. 45.

1. 5(a) Recipient District Hospital shall in turn deposit said check.to nearest PNB branch and such deposit shall be treated as trust fund in its books. Funds shall be disbursed in accordance with 2. 2 of Adm. Order No. 45.

1.5(b) Both IPHO and District Hospital shall submit reports as provided in Adm. Order No. 67 series of 1983.

2.0 For disbursement of funds for construction material.
	2.1.2 The IPHO/Chief of District Hospital as the case may be shall approve the requisition.


This order shall take effect immediately. Strict compliance with the guidelines is enjoined of all.
