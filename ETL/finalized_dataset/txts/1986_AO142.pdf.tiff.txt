<subject> Subject: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by DON LEOVILGIDO DIAPO SR. MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Madalag, Aklan. </subject>


<body>
In consonance with Batas Pambansa Blg. 866 Section 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, DON LEOVIGILDO DIAPO SR. MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Madalag, Aklan, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30, 000.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time- including undelivered purchases, the amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.
 

It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 321, authorizing ceiling -for drugs and medicines from which a revolving fund is to be maintained shall  be followed.

The ceiling hereby fixed 15 based on the submitted request
