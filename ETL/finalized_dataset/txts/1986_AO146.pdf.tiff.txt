<subject> SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by BAROTAC NUEVO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo.</subject>

<body>
In consonance with Batas Pambansa Blg. 866,5ection 2, Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, BAROTAC NUEVO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of Thirty Thousand Pesos (P30,000.00) worth of drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock at any given time including undelivered purchases the initial amount of which is chargeable against its allotment for maintenance and other operating expenses.

       It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of Budget and Management, National Budget Circular No. 32l, authorizing ceiling for drugs and medicines from which a trust fund is to be maintained shall be followed.

The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the attached request.
