<subject>SUBJECT: Qualification Standards and Criteria for Evaluation of all Applicants for Director III and Above and for Chiefs of Hospital</subject>

<body>

RATIONALE

In order to ensure the selection and appointment of the most suitable and qualified senior staff of the Department of Health (DOH), the following Qualification Standards and Evaluation Criteria for applicants to positions of Director III and above (including Chiefs of Hospital) are hereby established for the guidance of the DOH Selection and Promotions
Board. 


I. MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS

1. Masteral degree or equivalent
2. Five (5) years experience in health service with at least three (3) years in an administrative or managerial capacity

II. SUPERIOR RATING FOR:
1. Past Performance 
2. Leadership qualities 
3. Communication skills

The SPB will design the appropriate evaluation tools and rating system in conformity with the above.

All issuances inconsistent with this Order are hereby rescinded.

This Order shall take effect upon approval.
