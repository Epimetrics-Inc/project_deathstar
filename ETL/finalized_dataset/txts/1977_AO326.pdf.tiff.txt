<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of the Special Projects Unit, Bureau of Dental Health Services</subject>

<body>
Upon the recommendation of the Director of the Bureau of Dental Health Services there is hereby created a Special Projects Unit to revitalize, develop and oversee the implementation of policies, guidelines, standards and programs covering dental health education, dental personnel training and community fluoridation which will result in more effective delivery of dental health education, standardize training of dental personnel and initiate and expand community fluoridation.

The following are relieved of their present duties and assignment to constitute the Special Projects Unit which shall be directly under the control and supervision of the Director of that Bureau who will prescribe the definition of the Unit's functions, goals, and duties of each personnel:

1. VICTOR R. VALDEZ, DDM, CPH, DPHD
(Supervising Staff Dentist)
Designate Chief of Unit

2. VENUS T. GAMBOA, DDM, CPH, DDPH
Senior Staff Dentist

3. FISENCIA C. MACARAEG, DDM, CDPH
Staff Dentist

4. ESTHER N. MARTIN, DDM
Staff Dentist

5. Mr. WILFREDO S. SANTOS
Illustrator II

6. Mrs. PERLITA G. RAVARA
Clerk II

This Order shall take effect immediately upon its issuance. The designated Chief shall organize the Special Projects Unit immediately and submit reports on organization and function according to guidelines laid down by the Director.
