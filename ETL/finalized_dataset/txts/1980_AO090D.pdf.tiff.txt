<subject>SUBJECT: Extension of Authority to the Designated Officials of the Philippine Population Project I (MOH).</subject>

<body>
PURPOSE: This Order extends the authority of the designated officials to approve financial transactions and communications related to the liquidation operation of the Philippine Population Project I (MOH).

I. Officials authorized to approve disbursements:

a. Executive Director - ₱100,000.00 per transaction.
b. Deputy Director - Not to exceed ₱20,000.00 per transaction.
c. Minister of Health - No limit.

II. Officials authorized to obligate funds and sign ROA's:

a. Executive Director - ₱100,000.00
b. Deputy Director - Not to exceed ₱20,000.00
c. Minister of Health - No limit.

III. Officials authoried to sign TCAA checks and Treasury Warrants:

a. Executive Director -
TCAA Check - up to ₱100,000.00 per check
Trasury Warrant - No Limit
b. Deputy Director -
Treasury Warrant - up to ₱100,000.00
c. Head, Disbursement and Collection Section-
TCAA Check - up to ₱50,000.00 per check
d. Disbursing Officer -
TCAA Check - up to ₱5,000.00 per check
e. In the absence of the Project Coordinator -
The Deputy Director shall sign:
- TCAA Check up to ₱100,000.00
- Treasury Warrant up to ₱100,000.00
f. Minister of Health -
- Treasury Warrant - No limit.

IV. Officials authorized to countersign TCAA Checks:

Executive Director)
Deputy Director   ) - Both to countersign up to ₱20,000.00 per check in accordance with COA Circular No. 76-26 (E)

V. Approval of Requisitions, Purchase Orders and Other Transactions:

- Minister of Health and/or Executive Director - Any of all requisitions, purchase Orders and Contracts.

VI. Issuance, Distribution of Supplies, Materials and Equipment in Stock:

- Executive Director -  To approve all issues or schedules to distribution of the various items in stock in accordance with the Project Plan.

VII. Officials to Approve Travel Orders:

- Executive Director - To approve any of all travel orders not to exceed a period of fifteen (15) days related to the liquidation operations of the Project, chargeable against the said Project Funds.

IX. Re-Delegation:

The authority herein delegated shall not be re-delegated.

X. Effectivity:

This Order shall take effect from July 1 to December 31, 1980.
