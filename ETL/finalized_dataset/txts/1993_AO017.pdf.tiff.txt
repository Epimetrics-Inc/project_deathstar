3. RESPONSIBILITIES
	(1) In the exercise of this delegated authority on Administrative and financial functions, responsible officials are enjoined to observed with diligent care and always uphold the standards of protecting the integrity of the Department and the primacy of promoting and preserving the health of the community. Adherence to the Department’s priority programs shall be demonstrated in undertaking this responsibility.
	(2) The persons/officials to whom this authority has been delegated are primarily responsible and accountable for any violation of existing budget, accounting, auditing and other applicable rules and regulations.
	(3) The exercise of powers herein provided shall in all cases be subjected to such existing department policies as may have been or maybe promulgated.
	(4) It is understood that in the absence of the Regional Director, the Asst. Reg’l Director shall automatically take over the functions and powers granted to the Reg’l Director.
	*Personnel Office DOH Central shall be furnished a copy of all appointments issued.
	This order supersedes all other Orders inconsistent herewith and shall take effect immediately.
