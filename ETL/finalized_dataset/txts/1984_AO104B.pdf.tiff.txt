<subject> Subject: Amending the provisions of Administrative Order No. 5-B s. 1981 to liberalize the regulations implementing bed subsidy to private hospitals</subject>
       <body>The following regulations are hereby issued by this Office to amend the provisions of Administrative Order No. 5-B dated August 31, 1981 to liberalize the implementation of bed subsidy to private hospitals:
       1. That private hospitals which are located not less than twenty (20) kilometers distant from the nearest government hospital shall be qualified for government subsidy;
       2. That private hospitals located in municipalities with at least 20,000 population shall be eligible
