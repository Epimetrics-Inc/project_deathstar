<subject>SUBJECT: Grant of Amelioration Assistance Differential to Officials and Employees of the Department of Health for CY 1996</subject>

<body>

Administrative Order No. 42 s. 1996 is hereby amended to increase the amount of Amelioration Assistance granted in CY 1996 from P5,000.00 to P7,200.00.

An additional amount of P2,200.00 shall be paid to DOH officials and employees of the Central Office and other attached agencies, Bureaus, Regional Health Offices and Hospitals including Specialty Hospitals chargeable against the 1996 Continuing Appropriations of their respective offices.

To be entitled to this benefit, an employee must have been employed for at least four (4) months for CY 1996 as of November 30, 1996. However, an official or employee who has rendered less than four months of service shall be entitled only to an amount is proportionate to actual service rendered as follows:

In case of part—time employees, they shall be entitled to one-half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full—time employees.

Exemptions to this benefit are the consultants and/or experts who are hired for specific purposes and services and the PSYP trainees.
