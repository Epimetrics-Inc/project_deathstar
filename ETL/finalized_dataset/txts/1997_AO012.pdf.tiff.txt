<subject>SUBJECT: Rules and Regulations Implementing Republic Act No. 7632 “An act increasing the bed capacity of Manay District Hospital from 10-25 beds, and appropriating funds thereof.”</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to Republic Act No. 7632, passed by the Senate and "the House of Representative and approved by His Excellency, PRES FIDEL V. RAMOS on July 17 1992, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution, the following Rules and Regulations are hereby adopted. 


Section 1. Title:

These rules shall be known as the “Rules and Regulations Implementing Republic Act No. 7632, An act increasing the bed capacity of Manay District Hospital from 10—25 beds and appropriating funds thereof.”


Section 2. Purpose:

These Rules and Regulations are adopted prescribing the guidelines, procedures for the implementation of R.A. No. 7632 to facilitate compliance therewith and achieve the objectives thereof. 


Section 3. Scope:

This rules shall apply to Manay District Hospital in the Municipality of Manay, Province of Davao Oriental.

Section 5. Guidelines for Implementation of Republic Act No. 7632

1. Upon receipt of Republic Act No. 7632, Manay District Hospital shall send its present plantilla of personnel and inventory of equipment to Hospital Operations and Management Service (HOMS). HOMS shall prepare the staffing, equipment and maintenance and other operating expenses (MOOE) requirement of the Manay District Hospital. These shall be, based on the DOH standard.

2. Upon receipt of the R.A., the Health Infrastructure service shall prepare the budgetary estimates for non-infrastructure requirements of Manay District Hospital.

3. HOMS shall consolidate all requirements and send a copy to Office for Health Facilities, Standards and Regulation and Local Government Assistance and Monitoring Service for their comments and/or approval. 

4. Upon approval by OHFSR, the requirements for staffing, equipment, MOOE and non-infrastructure are sent to the Department of Health-Finance Service (DOH-FS) and a copy ﬁmﬁshed to HOMS for filing. A copy shall also be sent to Manay District Hospital.

5. Any changes for the budgetary requirements must follow step 1 to 4. 

6. The Local Government Unit shall be responsible for seeking funding to implement the law.

7. Upon approval of the staffing, equipment and non-infrastructure requirements, commissioning of the Manay District Hospital begins.

8. The Provincial Health Office, Local Government Unit in Davao Oriental and the Chief of Hospital of Manay District Hospital shall organize the commissioning team. The composition of the team shall be designated by the Provincial Health Office and Local Government Unit. The team should be composed of the following:

a) Local Government Team Leader or his representative
b) Local Goverment Assistant Team Leader or his representative
c) Members
- District Health Officer
- Provincial Engineer
- Chief Nurse 
- Administrative Officer 
- DOH Representative in the province and municipality

9. The functions/responsibilities of the Commissioning Team include the following: site assessment; project implementation; recruitment and appointment of additional personnel and staff training; procurement of needed equipment, furniture and supplies and inauguration of the new building.

Section 6. Repealing Clause:

These Rules and Regulation shall supersede all previous Administrative and Bureau Orders and Circulars of the Department. The provisions of any Department of Health, Office for Health Facilities, Standards and Regulation order and circulars, or other issuances inconsistent with these Rules and Regulations are hereby repealed and modiﬁed accordingly. 


Section 7. Effectivity Clause:

These Rules and Regulations shall take effect immediately.
