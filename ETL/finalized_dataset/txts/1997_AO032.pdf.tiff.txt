<subject>SUBJECT: PRESCRIBING FEES FOR CERTIFICATES, CLEARANCES, PERMITS AND LICENSES ISSUED BY THE BFAD IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE CONSUMER ACT OF THE PHILIPPINES, REPUBLIC ACT 7394</subject>

<body>

Article 17 of Republic Act 7394 reads:

"Powers, functions and duties. In addition to their powers functions and duties under existing laws the concerned department shall have the following powers, functions and duties:

x x x 

a) to administer and supervise the implementation of this Article and its implementing rules and regulations;

x x x

d) to levy, assess collect and retain fees as are necessary to cover the cost of inspection, certification,  analysis and tests of samples of consumer products and materials submitted in compliance with the provisions of  this Article;

x x x

Further, Section 13 (b) of Executive Order 119 provides that the BFAD "Shall act as the policy formulation and sector monitoring arm of the Minister (Secretary of DOH) on matter pertaining to goods, drugs, traditional medicines cosmetics and household products containing hazardous substance and the formulation of rules, regulations and standards

x x x

In accordance and consistent with the foregoing provisions of law, the undersigned Secretary of Health hereby prescribes fees for certificates, clearances, permits and licenses that will be issued by BFAD in the conduct of its functions in the amount that shall be fixed by the BFAD in an appropriate Bureau Order. In fixing the amount of fees, the BFAD shall be be guided by Executive Order No. 159 setting the standard for fees and charges.

This Order shall be effective immediately upon approval.
