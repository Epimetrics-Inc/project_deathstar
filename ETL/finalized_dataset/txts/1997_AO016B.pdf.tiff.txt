<subject>SUBJECT: Creation of a Hospital Infrastructure Committee (INFRACom) for all Hospitals under the jurisdiction of the Department of Health</subject>

<body>
 
1. Rationale:

a) Decisions made pertaining to the development of infrastructure projects of hospitals should be participatory in approach since infrastructure projects follow criteria and protocol for identification and prioritization, evaluation and approval, as embodied in A.O. 9-A s. 1997

b) The needs and opinion of the project end users which reflect the requirements for design, quality assurance and medical care delivery, among others, must be ensured of representation with hospital management. In this manner, the end users dissatisfaction over completed infrastructure projects wi11 be avoided.

c) In view thereof, each special/specialty hospital, medical center, regional hospital and sanitarium of the DOH shall form its own Hospital Infrastructure Committee 


2. Functions: 

The Hospital Infrastructure Committee (INFRACom) shall primary be responsible for assisting the Director/Chief of Hospital in:

a) The prioritization and identification of infrastructure projects in accordance with the hospital medium term development plan and the hospital indicative site development plan;  

b) The evaluation of the design brief of every hospital infrastructure project. The design brief, which indicates the project’s space activities, estimated area needs, equipment requirements and other technical concerns, will be prepared by the project’s end user/s and shall be the reference in the preparation of plans; 

c) The analysis and concurrence to project schematic designs and plans prior to approval by the Undersecretary of Health, OHFSR, in accordance with AO 40-A s. 1996.


3. Composition:

The Hospital INFRACom shall be composed of the following hospital-staff:

a) Regular Members

i) Director/Chief of Hospital 		- 	Chairman
ii) Chief of Clinics  			-	Vice Chairman
iii) Administrative Officer 		-	Member 
iv) Chief Nurse 			- 	Member 
v) Chief of Maintenance 		- 	Member

b) Provisional Members (Project to Project Basis)

i) Department Head Concerned 
ii) Project End—User Representative 

c) Resource Persons

The following may be called upon by the Hospital INFRA Com to make presentation and provide further explanation, on project schematic designs and plans.

i) Hospital Architect—in—Charge from DOH - Health Infrastructure Service (INFRA)
ii) Hospital Engineer-in-Charge from DOH - Health Infrastructure Service (INFRA) 
iii) Regional Civil Engineer

 
4. Requirements: 

a) All Hospitals are required to form their INFRACom Membership list for submission to the Office for Health Facilities, Standards and Regulations (OHFSR), on or before 30 October 1997

b) Every INFRACom is required to prepare its Standard Operating Procedure Manual for submission to the OHFSR on or before 30 November 1997.

This administrative Order shall take effect 01 October, 1997
