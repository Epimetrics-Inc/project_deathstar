To	: All Medical Clinics and Hospitals Accredited to Conduct Medical Examination of Overseas Contract Workers and Seafarer, the Maritime Industry Authority, Philippines Overseas Employment Administration and all others concerned.


<subject>  Subject: Authority of the Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) from the Department of Health to authenticate medical certificates of seafarers.</subject>

<body>
In order to facilitate authentication of medical examination certificates of seafarers the authority to authenticate such certificates granted by the Department of Health to MARINA since September 19?0 is hereby extended pursuant to the Memorandum of agreement between DOH and MARINA consistent with Administrative Order No. 65—A s. 1990.

       The policies on authentication of medical examination certificates, embodied in Bureau Circular No. B-A, s. 1992, dated March l9, 1992, shall be strictly followed such as, among others, the requirement of a referral slip for medical examination from the manning agency or shipping company. For direct hire or name hire seafarers the MARINA shall issue a referral slip addressed to an accredited medical clinics.

       The MARINA shall continuously furnish the Bureau of
Licensing and Regulation, DDH, with a monthly report of authenticated medical examination certificates.

	This authority shall be in effect until such time that it is revoked by the Department of Health.

	For the information and guidance of all concerned.

   
</body>
