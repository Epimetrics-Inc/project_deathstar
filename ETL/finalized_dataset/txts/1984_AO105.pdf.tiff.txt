<subject>SUBJECT: Subject: Simplification of the Salary Payment Procedure, Office of the Minister, Staff agencies, and Special Projects.</subject>



<body> To expedite the payment of salaries of employees in the
Office of the Minister and special projects under it, the procedures therefor are hereby simplified as fo11ows:

1. It shall be the responsibility of the Collection and Disbursement Section to prepare the payrolls. However, the assistance of agencies may be availed of whenever necessary.


2. The payro11 shall be signed by the head of each agency and sent directly to the Financia1 and Management Service for processing and approval. The payroll shall be acted upon immediately in the agency, in the budget division, and in the Accounting Division. If one authorized to sign is not in the office for any reason, the one next in rank shall immediately take action on the payroll, including the certification of funds available.

3. The cash voucher and check covering the salary shall be prepared by the Collection and Disbursement Section. They shall be sent directly to the Financial and Management Service for processing and approval in accordance with authority delegated under existing issuances. The cash advance voucher and check shall be acted upon immediately. If the official authorized to sign is not in the office for any reason, the official next in rank who has the authority to sign shall immediately take action thereon.
	The Chief of collection and disbursement section shall see to it that salaries are ready for payment on paydays so employees are paid promptly.
	This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
