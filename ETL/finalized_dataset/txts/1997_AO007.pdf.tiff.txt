<subject>SUBJECT: Assignment of the Field Epidemiology Training Program (FETP), Department Legislative Liaison Ofﬁce (DLLO), and the Media Relations Unit to the Ofﬁce of the Secretary</subject>

<body>   

In the interest of the service the following programs/Unit are hereby assigned to the Ofﬁce of the Secretary:

1. Field Epidemiology Training Program (FETP)

2. Department Legislative Liaison Ofﬁce (DLLO)

3. Media Relations Unit of the Public Information and Health Education Service 

Accordingly, the Heads of the above—mentioned programs/Unit shall report directly to the Secretary of Health. 

This Order takes effect immediately.
