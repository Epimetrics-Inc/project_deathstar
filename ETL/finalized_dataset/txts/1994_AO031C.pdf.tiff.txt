<subject>SUBJECT; GUIDELINES IN THE GRANTING OF HONORARIA AND ALLOWANCES IN RENDERING MEDICOLEGAL SERVICES</subject>


<body>
1.0 RATIONALE
       In order to promote the social and economic well—being of our physicians to develop their skills and capabilities and to encourage them to remain in the government service, the following guidelines in granting of additional honoraria and allowances are hereby prescribed:


2.0 COVERAGE
        Rural health physicians/mun1c1pal health officers and resident physicians of non—training hospitals who shall be required to render medico-legal services.

3.0 PAYMENT OF MEDICO-LEGAL HONORARIA AND ALLOWANCES
       3.1 As a rule, the Secretary of Health or the proper government officia1 shall assign a medico-legal officer in every prov1nce
       3.2 In places where there is no medico—legal officer, rural physicians and resident physicians in non-training hospitals who are required to render medico-legal services shall be entitled to additional honorarium and allowance not less than five hundred pesos (P500. 00) for each service performed and not more than two thousand pesos (P2000. 00) for each case completed chargeable against the funds of the agency concerned.

4.0 FUNDING SOURCE
	Agencies concerned shall prov1de funds in their regular budget for this purpose.

5.0 EFFECTIVITY

	This Order shall take effect upon approval.
