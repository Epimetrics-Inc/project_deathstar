<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines in Payment of CY 2003 Hazard PaY of Ofﬁcials and Employees of the Department of Health</subject>


<body>

On the fifth year of payment of hazard allowance as prescribed under Sec. 7.1.5 of the Revised Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of RA 7305, the rate of said beneﬁt shall now be computed at 25% of basic monthly salary starting 2003 and onwards to effect ﬁill implementation of the provision as contained in the Magna Carta of Public Health Workers.

In line with the provision of the above IRR, this Department has issued the following guidelines for the computation and payment of the current year’s hazard pay of its officials and employees, chargeable against savings of respective offices/hospitals:

1. All permanent, including contractual and casual employees with Salary Grades 19 and below shall receive a monthly hazard allowance equivalent to 25% oftheir monthly basic salary.
2. Those with Salary Grades 20 and above shall receive the equivalent of 25% of a preset benchmark amount based on SG-19 Step 8 per month.
3. Part time employees are entitled to one half (1/2) of the amount enjoyed by full time employees.
4. Consultants are not entitled to this benefit.

This Order shall be implemented effective January 1, 2003.
