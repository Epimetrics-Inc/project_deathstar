<subject>SUBJECT: Submission of Weekly Situationer Briefs</subject>


<body>

To enable the Secretary of Health, the Chief of Staff and other key DOH officials to effectively manage the Department of Health and immediately respond to issues if necessary, all Regional Directors, Chiefs of Retained Hospitals and Bureau and Service Directors shall submit a Weekly Situationer Brief.

The process flow for the Weekly Situationer Brief will be as follows:

1. All Regional Directors shall submit via facsimile, the Weekly Situationer Brief to the Chief of Staff, Attention: Director, IPS every WEDNESDAY no later than 4:00 pm. The reporting period is the week from Wednesday of the previous week Tuesday of the present week. Please refer to Annex “A” for the format. 

The DIRFOs submission is a consolidated report which will include the report of the region proper and the retained hospitals in its area.

2. Likewise, Bureau and Service Directors of the Central Office shall submit the Weekly Situationer Brief every WEDNESDAY to the designated receiving units indicated in Annex B.

3. By Thursday, the designated receiving units indicated in Annex B shall consolidate the submitted reports and prepare a one (1) page summary. This one page summary shall be forwarded to the Chief of Staff and the concerned Undersecretaries/Assistant Secretaries on the same day.

4. Based on the submitted summary report, the concerned Usecs/Asecs shall decide on matters that need their action not later than Friday morning.

5. The USECS/ASECS shall likewise give the Chief of Staff and the originating office a feedback of the status / actions taken.

6. The OCS shall then consolidate the Weekly Situationer Briefs including actions taken.

7. By Monday morning of the following week, the OCS shall forward the consolidated report to the Secretary of Health.

8. The OCS shall also prepare the report to be presented by the Secretary to the President and the Cabinet.

9. At the EXECOM, all Usecs and Asecs shall provide the Secretary an update/status report of the actions taken.

10. Following the EXECOM discussion, the Usecs and Asecs shall give the sender a feedback on the status of actions taken.

11. The Usecs/Asecs shall also have the responsibility of monitoring and keeping track of the issues/concerns.


In periods where no significant concern is to be reported, the same Weekly Situationer Brief will be submitted with the notation — NONE.

The Submission of the Weekly Situationer Brief however, must not prevent the various Offices/Units/Hospitals to submit reports as often as needed if circumstances so require. 

The Chief of Staff may also require additional information with regard to the reported items and/or other items as may be deemed necessary.

This Order takes effect immediately.

For strict compliance.
