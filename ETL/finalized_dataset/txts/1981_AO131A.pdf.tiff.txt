<subject>SUBJECT: Standard operating procedures on requests for repair, installation and maintenance of Ministry of Health hospitals' x-ray machines and other electro-medical equipment.</subject>

<body>
For purposes of saving time and government expense, the Radiation Health Office hereby issues the standard operating procedures on repair jobs, maintenance and installation of Ministry of Health hospitals' x-ray machines and other electro-medical equipment.

These shall apply when request for repair and installation of x-ray machines and other electro-medical equipment from regional health hospitals/clinics and others concerned would be necessary and urgent.

The procedure for seeking technical assistance from the Radiation Health Office engineers and technicians shall be as follows:

l. For regions V, IX, X and XI, requests should be directly addressed to the Radiation Health Office, Ministry of Health, San Lazaro Compound, Rizal Avenue, Sta. Cruz, Manila.

2. Requests from other government hospitals should be addressed to their respective regional health director:

<image>figure_2.png</image>

Travel Order of the technician concerned shall be prepared by the regional health office. Per diem allowance and travel expenses of the above-mentioned technicians shall be charged against the funds of their respective requesting agency. 

3. Indicate the following:

a. Make and model of unit and manufacturer
b. Type of service required: installation, maintenance or repair.

4. If repair is requested, indicate symptoms observed by the operator. The technician concerned should visit the requesting hospital and rectify the fault, if possible. Accomplishment reports will be forwarded to regional health directors and the Radiation Health Office to monitor progress of work done.

5. If the technician cannot handle the repair by himself, he should ask through the director of the hospital, request for the assistance of the Radiation Health Office, describing fully the observations made during the visit.

For the Radiation Health Office assistance, an airplane ticket must be made available together with the certificate of funds availability to cover the travel expenses of the engineer or technician.

6. If request is for installation, enclose the approved floor plan of the x-ray room and the adjacent rooms and send it directly to the Radiation Health Office.

7. If request is directly referred to the Chief, Bio-medical Engineering Division, Radiation Health Office, assignment of job orders will be on first come first serve basis.

8. An assignment office order will be issued only upon receipt of transportation allowance. After completion of the job order, a report of accomplishment and certificate of appearance must be submitted.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
