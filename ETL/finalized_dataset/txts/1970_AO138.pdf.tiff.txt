<subject>SUBJECT: Amending Administrative Order No. 110, series of 1963</subject>

<body>
In line with the recommendation of the Special Committee created under Department Order No. 99-B, series of 1969, and pursuant to the powers vested in this Office under the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, and the pertinent provisions of other existing laws, the following provision is hereby inserted in Part I. (Chiefs of Hospitals) of Administrative Order No. 110, series of 1963, as an amendment in the manner of filling vacant positions of Chiefs of Hospitals under the Department of Health:

"D. Procedure in Filling Vacancy:

xxxxxxxxxx

3. In the designated medical centers, namely: Baguio General Hospital, Dr. Jose R. Reyes Memorial Hospital, Southern Islands Hospital, Davao General Hospital, and special hospitals, namely: National Mental Hospital, National Orthopedic Hospital, San Lazaro Hospital, Dr. Jose Fabella Memorial Hospital and National Children's Hospital, Chiefs of Clinics (or Supervising Resident Physicians where applicable) and Department Heads of the hospitals concerned shall also be considered, together with the Chiefs of Hospitals who are in line, for promotion to the position involved: Provided; That said Chiefs of Clinics or Supervising Resident Physicians and Department Heads possess the qualification requirements prescribed for such position and that they are employed as such for at least 5 years."

This Order shall take effect on January 1, 1971.
</body>
