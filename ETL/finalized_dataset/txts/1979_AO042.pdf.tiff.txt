For the purpose of further clarifying Administrative Order No. 38 s. 1979 and in order to make it more specific and descriptive of the  objective for which it has been drafted or formulated, the second paragraph and items 1, 2, 3, and 4 are hereby amended as follows:

1. The second paragraph is deleted and replaced as follows:

"FOR THE PURPOSE OF REGULATING THE USE OF SEVERAL BRAND NAMES FOR PRODUCTS OF THE SAME FORMULATION AND PHYSICAL SPECIFICATIONS, THIS REGULATION IS HEREBY PROMULGATED:"

2. Item 1 of Administrative Order No. 38 shall read:

"A EVERY drug or pharmaceutical specialty shall be registered with this Office and henceforth marketed only under its generic name or THE brand name of the manufacturer, REGISTERED DRUG COMPANY, exclusive distributor or licensee, OR UNDER ITS GENERIC NAME."

3. Item 2 of Administrative Order No. 38 shall read:

"Thebrand name of a formula owner, the WHOSE product of which is produced by contract manufacturer, shall be accepted for registration REGISTERED only if it can be established that the product is definitely different from the product of the manufacturer, IN CHEMICAL FORMULATION OT PHYSICAL PRESENTATION TO AVOID PUBLIC CONFUSION, if any, otherwise, item 1 applies. Among other things, contract of agreement, protocol of production and technical AND PHYSICAL specifications will have to be submitted."

4. Item 3 of Administrative OrderNo. 38 shall read:

"The registration of drugs and pharmaceutical specialties produces by a manufacturer or imported by an exclusive distributor/licensee and distributed by different persons under different BRAND names WHICH DO NOT CONFORM TO ITEMS 1 AND 2 ABOVE shall be allowed to continue until he expiration of the validity of registration. Henceforth, renewal OR of registration OF SUCH PRODUCT shall only be allowed under condition of item 1."

5. Item 4 of Administrative Order No. 38 shall read:

"Any manufacturer, importer or wholesaler who, UNDER ITEMS 1, 2, AND 3, permit allows registration of a drug or pharmaceutical specialty under different names by the distributor is subject to Section 19 (a) of RA 3720 for misbranding, Section 11 (a) of the same Act and PD 280 for violation of the rules and regulations."

This regulation amends Administrative Order No. 38 s. 1979 and supersedes any other administrativev order regulating the use of brandnames and shall take effect immediately upon approval.
