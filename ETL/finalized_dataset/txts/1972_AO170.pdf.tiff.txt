<body>
In View of the implementetion of Progrem I of the Philippine Medicare Act of 1969, which involves the health insurance and benefits of members of the GSIS and the SSS, and in view of the desire of this Depertment that rural health physicians under the Department of Health participate in the successful implementation of the said law, the rules and regulations of this Department restricting private practice is hereby relaxed so as to allow rural health physicians to engage in the private practice of their profession to help realize the aims and purposes of the Med1care Law.      

Under the terms of this Order, the private practice of rural health physicians shall be done after office hours, using for the purpose their own private cl1nics, subject to the condition that no government facilities, property or equipment shall be used in such private practice.

This Order takes effect on April 1, 1972.
