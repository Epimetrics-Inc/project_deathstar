<subject>SUBJECT: Revised Guidelines for Delegation of Authority for certain Administrative Transactions in the Central Office, Department of Health</subject>

<body>

In view of the implementation of the DOH Rationalization and Streamlining Plan per Executive Order No. 102, and in order to streamline operations and fast-track action on official documents and communications, authority for certain officials to approve transactions is hereby delegated. This delegation is within the framework of EO 102, other executive orders and the General Accounting and Auditing Manual. Further, this Order thereby supersedes stipulated in all other Orders on “Delegation on Authority” and all other Orders inconsistent herewith.
