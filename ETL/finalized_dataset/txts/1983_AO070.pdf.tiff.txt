<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>No. 52,s. 1983</docnum>


To	:A11 Importers of Food and Food Products

<subject> SUBJECT: Strict compliance with the provisions of Ministry of Health's Administrative Order No. 37, s. 1979.</subject>

<body>In View of the reports of our FDA Inspectors that there are still imported food and food products not registered With the Food and Drug Administration, in violation of Administrative Order No. 37, S. 1979, all importers concerned are therefore enjoined to comply strictly with the provisions of said regulation not later than September 15, 1983.

       Thereafter, all non-registered imported food and food products shall be subject to seizure in all outlets where they may be found, in addition to other legal action which this office may deem necessary in accordance with existing laws and regulations.
       
       Strict compliance therewith is hereby required of all concerned.

</body>
