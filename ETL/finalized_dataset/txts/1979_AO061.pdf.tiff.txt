<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 61 s. 1979</docnum>

TO: The Regional Health Directors, Chiefs of Teaching and Training Hospitals and other Agencies concerned.

<subject>SUBJECT: Authority of the Regional Health Directors to approve Contracts of Affiliation Between Schools of Midwifery and Teaching and Training Hospitals under the Ministry of Health.</subject>

<body>
In order to facilities the processing and affiliation of Schools of Midwifery with Teaching and Training Hospitals under the Ministry of Health, the Regional Health Directors are hereby authorized to approve said affiliations in their respective regions provided the rules and regulations under DOH Administrative Order No. 275 s. 1976 are strictly complied with.

All contracts on affiliations between the teaching and training hospitals and the schools of midwifery which ae approved by the Regional Health Directors under this delegation shall bear the words "By authority of the Minister of Health" above that of the signing official.

This Administrative Order supersedes all issuance on this matter and shall take effect immediately.
</body>
