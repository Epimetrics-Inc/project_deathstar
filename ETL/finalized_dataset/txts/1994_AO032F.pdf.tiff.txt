<subject>Subject: GUIDELINES IN GRANTING OF QUARTERS/HOUSING ALLOWANCE OF R.A. 7305</subject>


<body>
1.0 RATIONALE

	In order to provide policy which shall serve as basis in claiming for housing and quarters allowance, the following guidelines shall be prescribed:

2.0 DEFINITION

	Tour of Duty shall be defined as the period of assignment in a hosp1tal Sanitaria or health infirmaries.

3.0 COVERAGE
	Public health workers as defined in Section 3 of R. A. 7305 and Section 1 Rule III of its Implementing Rules and Regulation (IRR) shall be entitled to receive the following benefits.

	3.1 Public health workers who are on tour of duty and those who because of unavoidable circumstances are forced to stay in the hospital sanitaria or health infirmaries shall be entitled to free living quarters within the hospital sanitaria or health infirmary premises;

	3.2 If such quarters are not available, the Head of agency concerned shall make available a monthly housing or quarters allowance at the rate of five hundred (P5OO.00) a month in accordance with the schedule under section 5.2

4.0 FUNDING SOURCE
	It shall be included in the agency’s regular budget beginning 1995.
 
5.0 EFFECTIVITY
	5.1 Quarters/housing allowance shall be implemented upon the effectivity of the Act
	5.2 Quarters/housing allowance at the rate of P500.00 per month shall be implemented by 1995 subject to annual review by the National Management Health Workers Consultative Council to determine the need to increase the rate of such allowance.
       5.3 This Order shall take effect upon approval.
