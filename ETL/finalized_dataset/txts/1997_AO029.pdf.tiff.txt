<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Item no, 8 Section V of the 1997 Poverty Alleviation Fund (PAF2)</subject>

<body>

Pursuant to the COA Circular No. 94—013 item no. 8 Section V (Fund Access) of the 1997 Poverty Alleviation Fund (PAF 2) Guideline of this department is hereby amended as follows:

“Upon release of the NCA The DOH Regional Field Ofﬁce shall forge a Memorandum of Agreement with the concerned LGU and, subsequently, shall transfer the cash/money to the LGU, as the implementing agency, and be taken as Inter-agency Transferred Funds and as a trust liability by the LGU. The DIRFO shall require the LGU to submit fund utilization report (Form 4) duly signed by the LGU’s resident COA auditor. COA Circular No. 94-013 shall serve as the guiding principle in the utilization and liquidation of funds transferred to LGU.”

This amendment shall take effect immediately.
