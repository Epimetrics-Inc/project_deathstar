<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines governing ﬁnancial matters in cases of transfer of assignments of Directors IV/III.</subject>


<body>

Changes in the assignment of Directors IV/III are made in the interest of public service. To assist ﬁnancially those affected, the following guidelines are hereby issued:

1. Payment of travel expenses authorized under Sections 341 and 342 of the Government Accounting and Auditing Manual (GAAM), Volume 1, January 1992;

2. Payment of salaries, regular allowances and other beneﬁts chargeable against the funds of the new ofﬁce provided, however, that they are transferred to an identical position where there is no incumbent. In cases of transfer to an ofﬁce where there is an incumbent of the position, the salaries, regular allowances and benefits shall continue to be paid by the ofﬁce of origin;

3. Payment of straight per diems for the ﬁrst 30 days in their new ofﬁce as part of relocation expenses. Travels, however, made during the same period shall no longer entitle them to per diems;

4. The Representation and Transportation Allowances (RATA) of those transferred to a lower position shall at their original rate, while those transferred to a higher position shall receive the higher rate. In either case, payment of RATA is chargeable against the funds of new ofﬁce.

5. The funds for the extra—ordinary expenses shall be disbursed by the ofﬁcial designated to the position which carries with it funds for the purpose.

Previous issuances inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are hereby rescinded and/or amended accordingly.
