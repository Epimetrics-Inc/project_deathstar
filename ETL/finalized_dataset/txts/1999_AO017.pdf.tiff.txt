<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to A.O. No. 12 s. 1999</subject>

<body>

Section 4 of Administrative Order No. 12 s. 1999 dated March 26, 1999 is hereby amended to read as follows:

“Pursuant to existing accounting and auditing rules and regulations, relocation for permanent reassignment requiring the relocation of the family of the deployed employees, necessary expenses for the transport of his/her personal belongings not exceeding P20,000.00 and transportation expenses of his/her minor children below 21 years of age through the most economical means, shall be paid by the DOH - C.O. on reimbursement or prepayment basis provided however that the prepayment shall be liquidated within thirty (30) days after the arrival of concerned official or employee at his/her new official station.”

Liquidation report should be submitted to Finance Service together with the original invoices, receipts, bus/boat tickets. Failure of the accountable official or employee to liquidate his/her prepayment within the prescribed period shall constitute a valid cause for the withholding of subsequent sub-allotment corresponding to his/her salary and other emoluments Finance Officers/Accountants of the Regional Health Offices shall assist in the implementation of this order.

All other provisions of the above-mentioned Administrative Order shall remain and continue 10 stay in effect.
