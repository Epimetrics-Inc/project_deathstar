<subject>SUBJECT: Designation of the Procurement Division of the Procurement and Logistics Service PLS as the Central Ofﬁce Bids and Awards Committee COBAC Secretariat and the Director of PLS as Head of the Secretariat</subject>


<body> 

Pursuant to Section 11 of Executive Order 40 “Consolidating Procurement Rules and Procedures for all National Government Agencies, Government-Owned or Controlled Corporations and Government Financial Institutions, and Requiring the Use of Government Electronic Procurement System," the Procurement Division (PD) of the Procurement and Logistics Service (PLS) is designated as the organic ofﬁce to act as the Secretariat of the COBAC. The Director of PLS shall act as the Head of the Secretariat with the Chief of the PD as permanent alternate. As stipulated in the Section 11.1 (page 8) of the Implementing Rules and Regulation(IRR) of E0 40 the Procurement Division in addition to its usual functions and responsibilities shall have the following functions as COBAC Secretariat:

1. Provide administrative support to COBAC;

2. Organize and make all the necessary arrangement for the COBAC meetings;

3. Attend COBAC meetings as Secretary;

4. Prepare minutes of the COBAC meetings; -

5. Take custody of procurement documents and be responsible for the sale and distribution of bidding documents to interested bidders;

6. Manage procurement process for COBAC;

7. Monitor procurement activities and milestones for proper reporting to relevant agencies when required;

8. Consolidate the Project Procurement Management Plans (PPMP) from the various units of DOH to make them available for review as indicated in Section 4 of EO 40 IRR;

9. Make arrangement for the pre-procurement and pre-bid conferences and bid Openings; and

10. Be the central channel of communications for COBAC with end users PMOs and other units of the line agency, other government agencies, providers of goods, works and consulting services and the general public.

The head of the COBAC may designate other staff from her ofﬁce to provide additional secretariat support.

This Order takes effect immediately.
