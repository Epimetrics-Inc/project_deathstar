<subject>SUBJECT: Location of the Housekeeping, Laundry and Linen Sections in the Organizational Structure of the Department of Health Hospitals.</subject>

<body>

Housekeeping, Laundry and Linen Sections / Services in the organizational structure of DOH retained hospitals shall be under the Administrative Service. The responsibility on the general supervision and management of the services is vested on the Administrative Officer who reports directly to the Chief of Hospital (COH).

This Order shall take effect immediately.

FOR STRICT COMPLIANCE.
