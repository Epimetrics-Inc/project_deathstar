<subject>Subject: GUIDELINES IN GRANTING PAYMENT OF REMOTE ASSIGNMENT ALLOWANCE UNDER R.A. 7305.</subject>


<body>
1.0 RATIONALE
	R.A 7305 was enacted in the country primarily to promote and improve the social and economic well-being of Public Health Workers, their living and working conditions in terms of employment; to develop their skills and capabilities; and to encourage those with proper qualifications and excellent abilities to join and remain in the government service, the following guidelines in requesting payment of additional compensation for remote assignment allowance of public health workers concerned sha11 be prescribed.

 
2.0 COVERAGE
	Public health workers, as defined in Section 25 of R. A. 7305 and Rule VIII Section 3, Letter D of its Implementing Rules and Regulations, shall be entitled to

3.0 DEFINITION

	3.1 Remote/Depressed. Area - refers to a location accessible by regular public air, water, or land transportation, and where basic infrastructure and services are not available. An area that is considered remote is to be certified by the local Chief Executive or his/her duly authorized representative.

	3.2 Hard to reach/Isolated - a location where any or all of the follow1ng conditions exist:
	(a) No roads or trails
	(b) No roads but trails only
	(c) Reached by hiking, animal or animal-drawn vehicle
	(d) Unpredictable and irregular schedule of transportation service
	(e) Lack and/or inadequate communication facilities
   

 
4.0 PAYMENT FOR REMOTE ASSIGNMENT
	4.1 Public health workers ass1gned in work areas, stations, units considered remote, isolated or depressed, or areas wherein existing position has not been filled-up for two years prior to the approval of this Act shall be entitled to a remote assignment allowance equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of their basic pay.
 
	4.2 They shall also be entitled to reimbursement for actual cost of transportation to and from the remote areas upon assumption to and leaving such position and during official trips.

	4.3 Public health workers who are on remote assignment shall be given priority in promotion or' assignment to better areas, except when there are no position for their transfer or they prefer to stay in such post in excess of two years.

5.0 FUNDING SOURCE.

	5.1 Funding for the payment of remote assignment allowance shall be included in the budget of the agency concerned.

6.0 EFFECTIVITY
	This Order shall take effect upon approval.
