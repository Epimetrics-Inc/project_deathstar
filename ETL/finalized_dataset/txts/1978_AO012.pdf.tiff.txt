<subject>SUBJECT: PLACING THE ACCOUNTING AND CASHIERING SERVICE OF THE SCHISTOSOMIASIS CONTROL TO THE ACCOUNTING DIVISION AND THE COLLECTION AND DISBURSEMENT SECTION, OFFICE OF THE MINISTER OF HEALTH</subject>

<body>
In view of the expansion of the field operations of the Schistosomiasis Control and Research Service, the saidOffice cannot cope with the voluminous work attendant to the Accounting and Cashiering needs of the Schistosomiasis Control Council, In order not to adversely affect the essential activities of the Council, the Accounting and Cashiering Service is hereby transferred to the Accounting Division and the Collection and Disbursement Section, respectively, Office of the Minister of Health.

All concernced are advised to be guided accordingly.

This Order shall take effect December 1, 1978.
</body>
