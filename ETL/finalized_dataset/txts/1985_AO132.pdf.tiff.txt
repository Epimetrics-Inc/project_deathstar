       This order is being issued to further supplement
Administrative Order No. 81 s.1980 and the supplementing orders thereto issued, implementing section 2 Special Provision of the Ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa Blg. 866 fixing the ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria at any given time including undelivered purchases so as to include,
ALCALA MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, Alcala, Cagayan in the amount of P3l,000.

       The ceiling hereby fixed is based on the submitted quarterly consumption of drugs and medicines. ‘
