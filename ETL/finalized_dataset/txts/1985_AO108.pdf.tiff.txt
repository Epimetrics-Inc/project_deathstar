<subject>
SUBJECT: Authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by Ragay District Hospital, Ragay, Camarines Sur.</subject>

<body>
       In consonance with Section 2, Special Provisions of the ministry of Health, Batas Pambansa 640, Ragay District Hospital, Camarines Sur, is hereby authorized to maintain a ceiling of P23,OO0.00 worth of drugs and medicines in its stock for re-sale chargeable against the releases for its maintenance and other operating expenses.

       It is understood that the corresponding rules and regulations issued by the Office of the Budget and Management and COA regulating the use of revolving fund to be created out of this authorized ceiling shall be followed.
