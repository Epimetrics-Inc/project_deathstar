<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 152 s. 1971</docnum>

TO: Regional Health Directors, Chief Training Officers, Provincial and City Health Officers

<subject>SUBJECT: Affiliation with Public Health Centers</subject>

<body>
In view of the request of Provincial and City Health Officers, it is hereby directed that Colleges and Schools of Nursing and other disciplines affiliating with health centers shall deal directly with the Provincial or City Health Officers concerned, and all affiliation fees paid in connection with training in said health centers shall be remitted to them.

The Provincial or City Health Officers concerned, shall apportion their respective affiliation fees in accordance with the provisions of Administrative Orders No. 87, series of 1968, No. 103 and No. 110, both series of 1969, as amended by Administrative Order No. 133, series of 1970, and shall submit their respective budget and plantilla for this purpose for approval by the Secretary of Health, as basis for disbursement of said fees.

All fees collected by the Regional Health Officers previous to this Order and accruing to the Provincial or City Health Offices concerned shall be remitted to the said offices. It is understoor that a formal Contract of Affiliation has been entered into by representatives of the affiliating institution and the Department of Health agency concerned and approved by the Secretary of Health.
</body>
