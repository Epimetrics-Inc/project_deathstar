<subject> Subject : Designation of Teaching and Training Hospitals of the Ministry of Health for the Residency Training Program for specialties under P.D. 1424.</subject>

<body>
To : The Bureau Directors, Regional Health Directors, Project Directors, Chief of Offices/Services/Hospitals directly under the Office of the Ministry of Health, Unit Heads and others concerned.</body>
<body>

	These hospitals in addition to present standards of training and skill development shall also serve as the Medical center referral from various regions of the country for patients requiring highly management and services.

	All other provincial hospitals currently rendering tertiary care shall upgrade and comply with required standards to serve as centers for in-service training and referrals. In-service training for health personnel shall cover medical, paramedical and nursing discipline. The Ministry of Health shall allocate the required hospital items and the respective management of the hospital shall veer administration to meet the necessary supportive technical and physical requirements. The officials of these hospitals concerned shall direct their efforts, resources towards implementation of this objective. 
	This circular takes effect April 1, 1984 to coincide with the provisions of Administrative Order No. 85-A dated March 15, 1984.
