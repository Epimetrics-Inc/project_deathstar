<subject>SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority to the Actg. Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development.</subject>

<body>
To facilitate action on matters invoving the Rural Service Program for theMinistry of Health in compliance with Letter of Instructions No. 559 (Pagliligkod: Bagong Lipunan) of the Office of the President dated June 23, 1977, authority is hereby delegated to the Actg. Assistant Secretary for Personnel Management and Development to handle and take charge of the activities and work involved in the said Program in coordination with the Honorable Deputy Minister of Health, who is the MOH member in the National Secretariat Sub-Committee, Paglilingkod: Bagong Lipunan.

Mr. San Juan is further delegated the authority to activate the Buklod ng Kabataan Kawani in the Ministry of Health as required by the Civil Service Commission, and to take charge of all matters and activities relative to the BKK.

This Order takes effect immediately.
</body>
