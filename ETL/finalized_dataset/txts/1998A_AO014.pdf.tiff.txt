<subject>SUBJECT: Working Structure and Reporting Scheme for the DOH</subject>

<body>

In accordance with the legal mandate and general framework of Executive Order 119 (Reorganizing the Ministry of Health) and Executive Order 292 series of 1987, the following working structure and reporting scheme shall be the basis of this Department’s operations: 


OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY (OSEC§

I. Executive Committee for National Field Operation composed of the Regional Directors, Chiefs of Medical Centers and Regional Hospitals thereat
II. Attached Agencies/Corporations
* Philippine Health Insurance Corporation
* Dangerous Drugs Board
* Philippine Heart Center
* Philippine Children’s Medical Center
* Lung Center of the Philippines
* National Kidney and Transplant Institute 
III. Office of the Chief of Staff (OCS) headed by Undersecretary Antonio S. Lopez
* Office of Legal Affairs
* Financial Operations and Frontline Service Audit
* Community Health Service
* Health Intelligence Service
* Internal Planning Service
* Foreign Assistance Coordination Service
* Local Government Assistance and Monitoring Service
* Essential National Health Research Program
IV. OFFICE FOR PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICES (OPHS) headed by Undersecretary Susan P. Mercado
* Malaria Control Service
* Schistosomiasis Control Service
* TB Control Service 
* Communicable Disease Control Service 
* Non-Communicable Disease Control Service 
* Maternal and Child Health Service 
* Family Planning Service
* Nutrition Service
* Environmental Health Service
* Dental Health Service
* Public Information and Health Education Service
V. OFFICE FOR HOSPITAL AND FACILITIES SERVICE (OHFS) headed by Undersecretary Margarita M. Galon
* Hospital Operations and Management Service
* Radiological Health Service 
* Hospital Maintenance Service
* Health Infrastructure Service
* Metro Manila Hospitals
VI. OFFICE FOR STANDARDS AND REGULATION (OSR) headed by Undersecretary Milagros L. Fernandez
* Bureau of Licensing and Regulation
* Bureau of Research and Laboratories
* Bureau ofFood and Drugs
* National Quarantine Office
* Biological Production Service
VII. OFFICE OF MANAGEMENT SERVICES headed by Undersecretary Teresita A. dela Cruz
* Administrative Service
* Management Advisory Service
* Procurement and Logistics Service
* Finance Service
* Health Manpower and Development Training

The reporting system for the various programs that are either foreign or locally funded and other units not included herein shall be prescribed under a separate Order.

All other orders and issuance inconsistent herewith are hereby rescinded.

This Order takes effect September 21, 1998.
