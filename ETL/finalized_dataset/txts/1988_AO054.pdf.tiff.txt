<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

<subject>SUBJECT : Delegation of authority to Chiefs of Regional
Medical Centers/Regional Hospitals/Sanitaria regarding leave applications</subject>

<body>

For purposes of facilitating action on routine personnel matters, Chiefs of Regional Medical Centers/Regional Hospitals/Sanitaria in the Regional Health offices are hereby delegated the authority to act on and approve all applications for vacation/sick/maternity leaves of absences, including terminal leaves of personnel under their respective jurisdictions;

	Orders inconsistent with this delegation are hereby rescinded..

	This Order takes effect immediately.

 
</body>
