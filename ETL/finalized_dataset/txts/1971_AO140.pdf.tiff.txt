<subject>SUBJECT: Delegating authority to Mrs. Guillermina E. Rodriguez, Manager, UNICEF National Committee, to approve disbursments, sign checks and correspondence under certain conditions.</subject>

<body>
Effective February 1, 1971 , Mrs. Guillermina E. Rodriguez, Manager, UNICEF National Committee, is hereby authorized to approve vouchers and sign checks covering transactions involving amounts not exceeding ₱1,000.00 in any disbursements for payment of salaries, traveling expemee of personnel and sundry expenses, chargeable from the allotments of said Program. She is further authorized to sign correspondence of routine nature pertaining to the operation of the Program except those which pertain to policy-determining and international commitments.

A11 issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby superseded and/or modified.
