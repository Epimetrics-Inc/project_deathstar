<subject>Subject: Delegation of authority governing the approval of field trips and travels of certain personnel in the Bureau of Health Services and the Bureau of Disease Control.</subject>

<body>
For purpose of ascertaining the necessity for allowing division chiefs and other ranking personnel, especially medical officers and sanitary engineers of the Bureau of Health Services and the Bureau of Disease Control to undertake field trips or to go on travel, as well as for the purpose of facilitating appropriate action on approval of such trips and travels, authority therefor is hereby ordered as follows:

Section 1. Dr. JACINTO J. DIZON, Chief of the Disease Intelligence Center and concurrently the designated Officer-in-Charge of the Bureau of Health Services and the Bureau of Disease Control, is delegated with authority to provide direction, control and supervision over such matters involving the field trips and/or travels of the officers and personnel herein concerned.

Section 2. Accordingly, unless otherwise specified by the Secretary of Health, all field trips and/or travels to be undertaken by all officers and personnel of the said bureaus shall be authorized and approves by the Officer-in-Charge thereof and effected thru specific Bureau Orders only.

Section 3. All previous Department Orders authorizing the field trips and/or travels and the corresponding claim for traveling expenses and other allowances of the officers and personnel of the said bureaus, including Department Order No. 3 dated January 2, 1974, inconsistent herewith are hereby rescinded or revoked.

Section 4. This Order shall take effect immediately upon its issuance.
</body>
