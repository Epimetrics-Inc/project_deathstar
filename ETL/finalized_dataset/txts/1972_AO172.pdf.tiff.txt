<subject>SUBJECT : Further amendment to Administrative Order No. 41, series of 1967</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 42, series of 1967, relative to they revised schedule of hospital fees and charges, as amended, is hereby further amended such that patients covered by the Philippine Medical Care Act of 1969 (R.A. 6111) are exempted from the provisions of paragraphs D and E under setion 16 thereof, insofar as payment of deposit upon admission as pay patients in hospital under the Department of Health is concerned; If such patients are accommodated in a ward, the rate of which is over the rate payable by system, He may be requiered to make the deposit which shall be based on the estimated excess amount not payable by the System, but payable by the patient.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
