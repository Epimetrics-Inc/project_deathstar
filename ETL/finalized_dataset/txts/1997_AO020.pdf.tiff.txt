<subject>SUBJECT: Guidelines for the Recording, Disbursement, and Liquidation of funds for the Women’s Health and Safe Motherhood Project (WHSMP)</subject>

<body>

1. Purpose

These guidelines are issued for the following purposes and objectives:

(a) To expedite the release of funds to the Regional Health Ofﬁces (RHOs) and the replenishment and liquidation of the same; 

(b) To enable the Project Management Office (PMO) to effectively monitor on time the actual utilization of funds.


2. Responsibilities of Concerned Units/Ofﬁcials

2.1 Finance Service- DOH (Central Office)

(a) Release the loan proceeds requirement of the respective regional health offices out of the ADB Imprest Fund and World Bank Special Account, and GOP Counterpart upon submission of sub-allotment requests by the PMO. These sub allotments requested by the PMO shall be subject to compliance by the regions of the necessary reports by project components/subcomponents accordingly.

(b) Transfer Imprest Fund and Special Account on a bank-to—bank mode.

(c) Consult and advise the PMO regarding the release of funds to the different regions.

(d) Prepare the consolidated Financial Statements i.e. Trial Balance, Income Statement (if applicable) for the Project as required by the Commission on Audit. These Financial Statements are consolidation of all financial transactions for the Project including those transactions done at the ﬁeld level and at the Central Office.

(e) Maintain separate books of accounts for the Project. 

2.2 Project Management Office (PMO) - DOH (Central Office)

(a) Prepare all withdrawal applications to the ADE/World Bank including liquidation and replenishments of Imprest Fund/Special Account.

(b) Consolidate the audited regional annual Statement of Sources and Applications of Funds (SSAF), including SOEs, and integrate these with project expenditures at the Central Office to form the overall for the project.

(c) Monitor the actual accomplishments and release of funds to the regions, and the submission of reports required by the Finance Service and the COA.

2.3 Regional Health Offices

(a) Maintain separate bank accounts for the Imprest Fund of the ADB or Special Account of the World Bank. GOP Counterpart fund could be maintained in existing bank account but should be properly accounted for.
(b) Maintain separate books of accounts for ADB and World Bank loans.
(c) Prepare on a monthly basis statement of expenditures (SOEs) and submit to the PMO - Women’s Health and Safe Motherhood Project, Department of  Health, 3rd Floor, Building 12, san Lazaro Compound, Sta. Cruz, Manila, and to the Regional COA. The RHOs should maintain a one month lag time, meaning SOEs for the month of September should be submitted in the month of October
(d) Prepare and submit monthly report of disbursements to the Finance Service, copy furnished the PMO. 
(e) submit to the COA within three (3) months from the end of each year the project’s annual Statement of sources and Application of Funds for audit.
(f) Submit monthly accomplishment reports to the PMO.

3. Effectivity

This Order shall take effect on September 15, 1997.
