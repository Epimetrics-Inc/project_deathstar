<subject>Subject : Transfer of the Nervous Disease Pavilion from
Davao Medical Center to the Mental Hospital and for other purposes.</subject>


<body>
       For the purpose of consolidation into one agency matter re1ative to mental health,-the existing Nervous Disease Pavilion at the Davao Medica1 Center is hereby transferred to the Mental Hospital, together with all its personnel, equipment and facilities.

The Davao Medical Center is hereby authorized to convert the area vacated by the Nervous Diseases Pavilion into an outpatient Department of the Medical Center.

This Order takes effect immediately.
