<subject>SUBJECT: Authority of the Assistant Regional Director (Director III) to exercise the functions, powers and responsibilities of the Regional Health Director in the latterâ€™s absence from his official station, on leave of absence or on trip abroad</subject>

<body>

In the interest of the service, the Assistant Regional Director, (Director III) is hereby delegated the authority to exercise the functions, powers and responsibilities of the Regional Director when the latter is absent from his official station, on leave of absence or on official or personal trip abroad.

Under this Order, the Assistant Regional Director is hereby authorized to act on all administrative, financial and technical matters within the authority of the Regional Director. Under this delegated authority, he shall be primarily responsible and accountable for any violation of existing budget, accounting, auditing and other applicable rules and regulations of the DOH.

Policy issues and other important matters shall be forwarded to the Office of the Secretary for appropriate action and decision.

This Order takes effect immediately.
