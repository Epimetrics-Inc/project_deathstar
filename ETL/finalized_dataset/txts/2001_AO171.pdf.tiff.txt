<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 164-A s. 2000</subject>


<body>

Administrative Order No. 164-A s. 2000 is hereby amended to include:

“All employees travelling on ofﬁcial business and ofﬁcial time are required to accOmplish “Scholarship Service Contract.”

“All employees attending meetings/conferences/visits and other similar purposes without training/academic component on official time and ofﬁcial business shall submit Travel/Terminal Report, while employees attending training courses/study tour/workshops shall submit Re—Entry Plan.” The plan/reports must be submitted to the Health Human Resource Development Bureau (HHRDB), through the Bureau Director/Head of Agency within ten (10) working days upon completion of the training.

All other provisions of the previous Order are still in effect and full force.
