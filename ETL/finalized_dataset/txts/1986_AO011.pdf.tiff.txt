<subject>  Subject: Authority of the Davao Medical Center to make the necessary changes in the housing of the Infectious Diseases ward and the Nurses Dormitory</subject>


<body>
In order to provide close supervision in the operation of the Infectious Disease Ward of the Davao Medical Center, presently situated at the back of the main building, the same is hereby transferred to the building presently occupied by the Nurses Dormitory and the building to be left vacant by the Infectious Disease were will be utilized as Nurses Dormitory.

The Nurses Dormitory shall, henceforth, be exclusively only for Nurses employed by the Medical Center. The Medical Center Chief of the Davao Medical Center is hereby authorized to issue rules and regulations governing the utilization of the Nurses Dormitory. 

This Order takes effect immediately.

</body>
