<subject>SUBJECT: Implementation of Administrative Orders Nos. 87 and 93 s. 1980</subject>

<body>
In order to assess the activities of the affiliation programs, regional health directors, chiefs of special hospitals and other hands of offices concerned are hereby directed to strictly implement Administrative Orders Nos. 87 and 93 s. 1980.

It is further directed that quarterly reports be submitted to the National Committee on Affiliation and Training of Students, c/o Bu. of Medical Services.
</body>
