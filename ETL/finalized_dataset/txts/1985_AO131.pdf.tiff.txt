SUBJECT: Supplementing Administrative Order No. 81 dated May 26, 1982, Re-authorized ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be kept in stock by hospitals and sanitaria.</subject>

<body>

This Order is being issued to supplement Administrative Order No. 81 dated March 26, 1980 fixing the ceiling for drugs and medicines that can be maintained in stock by hospitals and sanitaria at any time including undelivered purchases so as to include DAGOHOY municipal hospital, Inabanga, Bohol for a ceiling of P16,000.
	The ceiling hereby fixed is based on submitted request.
