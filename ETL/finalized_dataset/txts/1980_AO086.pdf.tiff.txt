<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 86 s. 1980</docnum>

TO: Chiefs of Special Hospitals in the Metro Manila under the Office of the Minister

<subject>SUBJECT: Inclusion of x-ray films in the revised procurement procedures for special hospitals</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. 82 dated April 28, 1980 which establishes guidelines for the revised drug procurement procedure for special hospitals is hereby amended to include the purchase of x-ray films to be covered by the provisions thereof under the same terms and conditions.

As thus amended Administrative Order No. 82 stands in effect.
</body>
