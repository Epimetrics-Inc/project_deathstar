<docnum>No. 178 s. 1972</docnum>

<subject>SUBJECT : Withdrawal of Dihydrostreptomyoin from all pharmaceutical products intended for human use.</subject>

<body>
For the guidance of pharmaceutiacal manufacturers and distributors, dihydrostreptomycin and its salts will no longer be allowed to be sold for human use singly or in combination with other drugs in specially formulations.

For the orderly phasing out of stocks which have been distributed to retail outlets prior to the issuance of this order, all stocks shall have been withdrawn by January 1, 1973.
</body>
