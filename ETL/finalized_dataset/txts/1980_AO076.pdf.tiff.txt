<subject>SUBJECT: Revising the policy in the granting of allowances to the FP Administrator and Medical Coordinator in the Regional Health Office.</subject>

<body>
The supplementary allowance which the Regional Health Director and his Assistant or the Chief of Consultants as Administrator and Medical Coordinator of the Family Planning Program of the Ministry in their health areas of jurisdiction are receiving at ₱150.00 and ₱100.00 a month each, respectively, and chargeable against the regular funds of the National Family Planning Office, shall be treated as honorarium, effective Fiscal Year 1979.

This is in lieu of the representation and/or transportation allowances which they have been authorized to receive since 1972.
</body>
