<subject>SUBJECT	:	Authority of Mrs. ISABEL R. NOLLEDO, Acting Chief, Administrative Service, to confirm the use of taxi by officials and employees prior to July 1, 1981</subject>

<body> Under Administrative Order No. 44 dated September 4,1982, the authority of _Mrs. Isabel R. Nolledo, Acting Chief, Administrative Service, Office of the Minister, to approve the use of taxi by officials and employees under the Office of the Minister is limited to July 1,1981 and prospectively. 
       In the interest of the service and to expedite action on routine matters in the office of the Minister, Mrs. Nolledo is further authorized to confirm the use of taxi by officials and employees in the Office of the Minister prior to the said data subject to the same conditions stated in the said Administrative Order which require the chiefs of sections/divisions/offices concerned to issue a certification justifying the use of taxi by their respective employees before approval is made. 
       As thus amended, the said delegation of authority to Mrs. Nolledo is hereby confirmed.
