<subject>Subject: Requirements for Suppliers Authorized Representatives During Public Bidding and Other Modes of Procurement</subject>


<body>

In addition to the mandatory requirements for suppliers eligibility participate in a public biddIng and other modes of procurement conducted by the DOH and to enter into a procurement contract with DOH, the following certiﬁcations are required from the representative(s) of a prospective bidder. This shall be part of the ﬁrst envelope required for public bidding and for other modes of procurement:

1. For a local company representing a foreign manufacturer, Suppliers and/or distributOrs — A certiﬁcation from the foreign manufacturer, supplier and/or distributor indicating the extent of authority given to a local supplier and/or distributor during the procurement process, duly attested by the Philippine Consulate at the foreign suppliers country. It shall speciﬁcally indicate in what capacity it is affording the local supplier such as, as ofﬁcial representative to tender bid and/or to enter in to contract in its behalf.

2. For a joint venture between a local and foreign supplier — a joint certiﬁcation from both the suppliers. indicating who is/are their ofﬁcial representatiye(s).

3. For a local supplier, that is:

a. Corporation - A Board Resolution signed by the Members of the Board or a Secretary's Certiﬁcate proclaiming who is/are the corporations ofﬁcial representative(s),

b. Sole proprietorship or partnership, choperative- Certification issued by the Company/Cooperatives President or CEO disclosing who is/are their authorized Representatiye(s); 

b. Joint venture— Ajoint certiﬁcation issued by the suppliers’ authorized ofﬁcials making known who is/are their authorized Representative(s)
