<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Section 11.3.3.8 re: Medical Records Service (Submission of Statistical Reports by all Hospitals to BLR)</subject>


<body> 
 

To facilitate reporting and processing of Statistical Reports by all hospitals to the Bureau of Licensing and Regulation, Section 11.3.3.8 of Administrative Order No. 68-A s. 1989 is hereby amended as follows:

1. The submission of hospital statistical reports which is done Quarterly is hereby changed to Semi-annually.

2. The Hospital Statistical Report covering the period from January to June for the current year shall be submitted to the Regional Health Office on the 7th day of July; those from July to December shall be submitted on the 7th day of January the following year.

3. The Regional Health Office shall forwardly hospital statistical reports to the Bureau of Licensing and Regulation, Department of Health, Manila on the 15th day of July and January respectively.


Submission of these reports is one of the requirements for the issuance of hospital license.

This order takes effect two weeks after its publication in two newspapers of general circulation and this supersedes any other issuances inconsistent hereto.
