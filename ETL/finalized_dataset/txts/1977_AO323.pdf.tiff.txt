TO: The Officer-in-Charge, Regional Health Office No. 11, Davao City

<subject>SUBJECT: Authority for the Officer-in-Charge of Regional Health Office No. 11 to act on personnel matters including certain appointments involving the officers and employees of the Davao General Hospital.</subject>

<body>
In accordance with the guidelines set forth in Administrative Order No. 299 dated September 22, 1976 and in line with previous Administrative Order Nos. 287 and 292 dated June 30 and August 4, 1976, respectively, the Officer-in-Charge of Regional Health Office No. 11 is hereby authorized to act on all personnel matters enumerated in the aforementioned administrative issuances, including appointments to 1st and 2nd level positions with salary ranges up to 50, involving the officers and employees of the Davao General Hospital at Davao City.

Actions taken by the official concerned on the above personnel matters shall invariably carry the words "By authority of the Secretary of Health" which shall appear over the signature of the signing official.

Actions taken on these personnel matters shall be included in the monthly report to be submitted to the Department for review, together with certified true copies of appointments acted upon and other pertinent papers approved.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
