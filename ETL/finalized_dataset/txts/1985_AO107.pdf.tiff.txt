<subject>
S U 8 J E C T : Further amendment to Adm. Order No. 543. 1983 and Adm. Order No. 104-A s. 1984 to exempt participants in the MOH residency training program on Pathology and Anaesthesiology from the provisions thereof.</subject>
<body>
       Due to the exigencies of the services, Residency training on Pathology and Anaesthesioiogy are further included in the specialties exempted from the provisions of Administrative Order No. 54 s. 1983.

       Administrative Order No. 54 s. 1983 as amended by this order and by Administrative Order No. 104 s. 1984, stands in effect.
