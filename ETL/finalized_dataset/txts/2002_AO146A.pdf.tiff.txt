<subject>Subject: Quarterly Submission of Historical Procurement Data</subject>


<body>

Pursuant to the ongoing thrust for improvement of the DOH Supply Management System, all Centers for Health Development (CHDs) and Retained Hospitals are directed-to submit, on a quarterly basis, to the Procurement Division, Procurement of Logistics Service, DOH Central Ofﬁce, the following data relative to purchases made by their respective offices:

1. Speciﬁcations of items purchased
2. Country of Origin (for goods produced overseas)
3. Unit Price
4. Unit
5. Date
6. Supplier
7. Mode of Procurement

The prescribed format for the Quarterly Report of Purchases is enclosed for ready reference. To facilitate consolidation, all reports should be prepared using Microsoft Excel and sent, stored in floppy disks, thru the postal system or thru e-mail to jjnlazo@central.doh.gov.ph.

Aforecited data shall be used for purposes of data build-up of the DOH Price Monitoring System.

Strict compliance of all concerned is here enjoined.
