<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>

To: Chiefs of Training Hospitals, Regional Training Centers, Regional Training Laboratories, and other concerned

<subject>SUBJECT: Amendment to Administrative Order No. 162, series of 1971</subject>

<body>
Administrative Order No. l62, series of l971, which amended Administrative Order No. 103 and No. 110, both series of 1969, and No. 133, s.  1970, relative to the guidelines for the implementation of student affiliation with government Training Hospital, Regional Training Centers, Regional Training Laboratories and other agencies of the Department, is hereby further amended in order to implement Section 20 of the Special Provisions under Presidential Decree No. 233 dated June 30, 1973, which includes Pharmacists as quoted:

"Section 20. — Physicians, pharmacists, nurses, medical technologists and other staff members of government hospitals, who participate in the training of student of private medical pharmacy, nursing and medical technology schools are hereby authorized to receive compensation at rates to be fixed by the Secretary of Health upon recommendation of the chiefs of hospitals and/or heads of agencies concerned, the compensation to be payable from funds contributed by the said schools for the purpose not to exceed 70% of the total collection, including the purchase of equipment, supplies and materials not to exceed 25% thereof, and the remaining 3% to constitute the reserve fund. The affiliated, schools, colleges, and universities are required to pay training fees of thirty pesos (₱30.00) per medical interns, twenty-five pesos (₱25.00) for pharmacy interns, twenty pesos (₱20.00) for medical technologists and medical clerks and ten pesos (₱10.00) for nursing interns. All bills incurred in connection with this training shall be rendered directly against the affiliated educational institutions by the chief and/or the head of agencies."

This Order shall take effect as-of January 1, 1974.
