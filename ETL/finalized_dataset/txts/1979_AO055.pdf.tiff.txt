<subject>SUBJECT: Authorizing Dr. JACINTO J. DIZON, Director of Health Services and Chairman, Committee on Biddings and Awards to Approve Open Market Purchases for Foodstuff of Hospitals under the Ministry</subject>

<body>
Dr. JACINTO J. DIZON, Director ofHealth Services and Chairman, Committee on Biddings and Awards created under D.O. No. 88-D dated March 10, 1978, is hereby delegated the authority to approve open market purchases of hospitals under the office of the Minister effective immediately.

Approval of purchases made under this delegated authority shall bear the phrase "By authority of the Minister of Health" above the name of the signing official.
</body>
