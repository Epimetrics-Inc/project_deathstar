<subject>Subject : Further amendment to Administrative Order No. 61, series of 1959.</subject>

<body>
Section 1-I-4 of Administrative Order No. 61, series of 1959, is hereby amended to read as follows:

Section 1. - Approval of Disbursements
I. Officials authorized to approve disbursements.

4. Chiefs of hospitals directly under the Office of the Secretary and Chiefs hospitals and sanitaria which have their own accounting services.

This amended provision repeals Section 1-II-2(d) of the Administrative Order.

Section 2 of the same Administrative Order is likewise amended to read as follows:

Section 2 - Signing of Treasury Warrants.

I. Officials authorized to sign treasury warrants

1. Authorized representatives of the Secretary;
2. Regional Health Officials
3. Bureau of Hospitals directly
4. Chief of Hospitals directly under the Office of the Secretary.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
