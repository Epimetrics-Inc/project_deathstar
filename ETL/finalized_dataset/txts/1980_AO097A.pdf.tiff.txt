<doctype>ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER</doctype>
<docnum>NO. 97-A s. 1980</docnum>

TO: All Chiefs of Hospitals, Maternity Clinics, Maternal and Pediatrics Institutions/Clinics Government and Private and all others.

<subject>SUBJECT: Administrative Order No. 77 dated March 5, 1980 regarding the Referrals of Abandoned Children to the Ministry of Social Services and Development by hospitals, maternity clinics and pediatrics institutions is hereby amended as follows:</subject>

<body>
1. Cause of maltreated children in hospitals must be duly certified and reported to the nearest MSSD Unit Office.

2. Steps should be taken to prevent "simulation" of births in the name of persons other than the biological parents and that cases of "simulated" births should be reported to the nearest MSSD Unit Office.

3. Attach copy of the cerficate of consent form.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
</body>
