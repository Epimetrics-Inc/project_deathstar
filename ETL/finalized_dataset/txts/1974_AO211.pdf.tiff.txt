<subject>SUBJECT: Delegations of Authority to Regional Officials to Approve Disbursements and Issue Requests for Obligation of Allotments.</subject>

<body>
In line with the functions of the Regional Health Offices as reorganized, authority is hereby delegated to the following Regional Health Officials to enable them to perform their new functions and responsibilities as follows:

I. Approval of Disbursements

A. Regional Health Directors — no limit to the amount of any transaction.

B. Budget and Finance Division

1. Regional Budget Officers II — For transactions involving amounts not exceeding ₱2,000 in any transaction;

2. Regional Budget Examiners III - For transactions involving amounts not exceeding ₱500 in any transaction;

C. In the absence of the Regional Health Directors, the Regional Budget Officers may approve salary payrolls in excess of ₱2,000.

II. Issuance of Requests for Obligation of Allotments

a. The above mentioned Regional Health Officials are likewise authorized to approve requests for obligation of allotments not to exceed the amounts which have been delegated to them when approving disbursement.

III. Countersigning of Checks and/or Treasury Warrants

a. The above mentioned Regional Health Officials are also authorized to countersign checks and or treasury Warrants not to exceed the amounts which have been delegated to them when approving disbursement.

IV. Scope of Authority

The authority of the said Regional Health Officials extends to those funds which are sub-allotted to the various programs and projects of Regional Offices.

V. Instructions

a. Said Regional Health Officials shall exercise their authority under the covering phrase;

<image>figure_1.png</image>

b. The authority herein delegated shall not be re-delegated.

VI. Rescinding Provisions

Any of all orders which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Order are hereby rescinded.

This Order shall take effect immediately.
